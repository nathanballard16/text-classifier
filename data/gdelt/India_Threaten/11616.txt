Imran Khan compares India’s seizure of Kashmir to the Nazis

Kashmiris shout pro-freedom slogans before prayers for Eid al-Adha, the festival during which their movement has been restricted

Pakistan’s prime minister compared the Indian government to the Nazis for imposing direct rule on Kashmir as the troubled region celebrated the Muslim festival of Eid al-Adha under military security today.

Imran Khan, who has tried to rally international opposition against India for moving to strip Kashmir of its statehood last week, compared global inaction to the appeasement of Hitler.
