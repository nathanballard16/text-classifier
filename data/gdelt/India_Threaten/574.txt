Kidnap threat to CM Arvind Kejriwal's daughter

ANONYMOUS MAILThe email, sent to the Chief Minister’s Office, threatened to kidnap Arvind Kejriwal’s daughter, following which a personal security officer has been assigned to her. Delhi Chief Minister’s Office received an anonymous mail on January 9 threatening to kidnap Kejriwal’s daughter, after which she had been given a personal security officer. A PSO has been deployed for Arvind Kejriwal’s daughter by the North District Police as the Chief Minister resides at Civil Lines. The CMO received an anonymous mail on January 9, following which the mail was forwarded to the Delhi Police Commissioner. A team of Delhi Police’s special unit is probing the matter.
