Post-Pulwama

With the government now done with the Saudi Crown Prince’s visit, it should now get down to dealing with India. There are multiple fronts that demand the government’s utmost attention. From Iran summoning the Pakistani envoy to Kulbhushan Jadhav’s case hearing and Pulwama attack, a lot needs to be taken care of.
