IMD issues thunderstorm warning for Madhya Maharashtra, Marathwada

The IMD issued thunderstorm and lightning alerts for Madhya Maharashtra on April 12, 13 and 15; for Marathwada from April 12 to 15; and Vidarbha on April 11 and 15. Light rainfall, meanwhile, was recorded at Solapur and Satara in Madhya Maharashtra on Saturday, it said. (Representative Image)Thunderstorm accompanied by gusty winds and lightning is likely to occur over Madhya Maharashtra and Marathwada, including Palghar, Thane and Pune, for some days till April 16, the Indian Meteorological Department has said. AdvertisingMumbai, meanwhile, recorded a spike in maximum temperature on Saturday evening at 35.3 degrees Celsius, which is three degrees above normal. AdvertisingMet department said that the maximum temperature in the city will continue to rise till Sunday.
