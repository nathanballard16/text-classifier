NMDC cautions job aspirants against fake news

NMDC has warned job aspirants against some websites hosting fake recruitment notice on ‘NMDC Executive Trainees Recruitment 2019’.

It said in a statement that it is fake news and it is being spread through websites and social media with malicious intent. People are requested to be wary of such mischievous acts aimed at deceiving the gullible by fraudulent means.

It clarified that NMDC, a Government of India Enterprise, has not issued any such notification pertaining to recruitment of executive trainees in any website, social media or newspapers.

Whenever any recruitment notification is released, it is by default hosted on NMDC’s official website www.nmdc.co.in (career link) and notifications are published in Employment News and also in major newspapers simultaneously.
