NMA to boycott meeting with RN Ravi

Dimapur, Feb. 25 (EMN): The Naga Mothers’ Association has announced that it will boycott the meeting between representatives of Naga civil society organisations and the government of India’s interlocutor to the Indo-Naga talks, RN Ravi, scheduled for Feb. 26 in Kohima.

A statement from the association on Monday informed that the boycott was to protest New Delhi’s ‘insincerity and failure’ to resolve the long drawn Naga political issue.
