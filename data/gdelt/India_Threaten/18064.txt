LoC provocations by India a threat to regional peace: CM Buzdar

LAHORE (Dunya News) – Punjab Chief Minister Sardar Usman Buzdar has on Tuesday said that provocations by India along the Line of Control (LoC) are a threat to regional peace.

The chief minister strongly condemned Modi government’s hostile ambitions in his statement and stressed that India’s warmongering has put the peace of entire region at stake.

CM Usman Buzdar warned that India should not remain in any misunderstanding as the whole nation is standing with Pakistan Army which is fully capable to retaliate against any aggression.

The CM vowed to defend the motherland and its sanctity and said India’s next generations will also remember Pakistan’s response if it resorts to any misadventure.
