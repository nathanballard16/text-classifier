PM Modi meets BJP’s OBC MPs

NEW DELHI: PM Narendra Modi on Wednesday restarted the practice of breakfast meetings with BJP MPs as he met over 40 party MPs from Other Backward Classes at his official residence.This was the first of a series of meetings planned between Modi and BJP MPs during the Parliament session. In the 16th Lok Sabha , he used to meet MPs from different regions but now, groups are being divided on the basis of social groups they represent. On Thursday, he is scheduled to meet MPs from Scheduled Castes.
