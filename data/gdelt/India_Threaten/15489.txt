QChennai: Govt Docs Call Off Strike; Museum for Keezhadi Artefacts

The strike by Tamil Nadu government doctors was called off on Friday, after a week. Health Minister Vijayabaskar and Secretary Beela Rajesh met with the Federation of Government Doctors Association (FOGDA) and discussed their demands.

The government doctors had called for an indefinite strike on 25 October 2019 demanding pay parity, service quota reservation for PG speciality and super speciality courses and fair counselling for job placements after post graduation. Tamil Nadu government had issued an ultimatum to the protesting doctors on Thursday that the posts of the doctors who were missing work, will be filled through fresh recruitment/ transfers.
