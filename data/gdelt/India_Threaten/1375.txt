Hyderabad CP warns rumor mongers of stern action

He also warned that any citizen sending such messages through social media will be prosecuted. In fact, there are no such persons, but some mischievous people were disseminating false information through social media in the form of messages, text, etc. The prohibitory order will be in force for 15 days from January 29 up to 0600hrs on February 12. He also said recently some cases have been registered against certain persons, who are allegedly responsible for attacking and causing death of innocent persons on the ground that they have come from other States and were kidnapping children for illegal activities.
