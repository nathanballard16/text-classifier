Surgical strike on 'Fake News': Indian Army, IAF fight back against false propaganda on social media

Indian Army and IAF have warned against 'false and malicious' information being spread on social media by India's enemies.

India security forces are not only busy fighting terrorists and giving a fitting reply to those who sponsor terror, but they are also fighting against false propaganda and misinformation being spread on social media by such states.

Indian Army and Air Force have called for the public to not trust any 'false and malicious' information being spread on social media by such sponsors of terror. They warned people that such actors are busy in spreading false propaganda, lies, and deception against Indian security forces. They called the Indian public to guard against such activities and not to trust these 'fake news' stories.
