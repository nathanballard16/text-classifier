Moto E6S vs Xiaomi Redmi 7: Specs, features and price in India compared

Moto E6S

Key Highlights The Moto E6S is the latest entry in the Indian budget segment market The Phone directly faces the competition from Redmi 7 Redmi 7 currently retails at Rs 7,499 Moto E6S has been launched at Rs 7,999

Lenovo-owned Motorola which is one of the oldest smartphone manufacturers has announced an affordable E series smartphone in India. Called the Moto E6S, the phone essentially comes as an Indian variant of Moto E6 Plus that was announced at the IFA 2019. Its headline features include a removable battery, a 6.1-inch HD+ display and 10W charging support.

With the launch of the Moto E6S, the company will directly pose a tough fight to Redmi 7 which is one of the leading devices in the segment in the country. Though both the phones significantly differ from each other in terms of battery, display and processor, they come at a similar price tag.

The Moto E6S is priced at Rs 7,999 whereas the Redmi 7 retails at Rs 7,499. Here's how the two entry-level devices compare against each other.

Moto E6S vs Xiaomi Redmi 7

Price

Moto E6S: Rs 7,999 for the only 4GB RAM with 64GB internal storage.

Redmi 7: Rs 7,499 for the 2GB RAM with 32GB storage variant and Rs 8,499 for the 3GB RAM with 32GB storage model.

Display

Moto E6S: 6.1-inch (1560x720 pixels) HD+ 19.5:9 Max Vision IPS display with 420 nit brightness.

Redmi 7: 6.26-inch HD+ display (1520x720 pixels) with 19:9 aspect ratio, 1500:1 contrast ratio, 84 per cent NTSC Color Gamut, 2.5D curved glass display and Corning Gorilla Glass 5 protection.

Processor

Moto E6S: Octa-Core MediaTek Helio P22 12nm processor with 650MHz IMG PowerVR GE8320 GPU.

Redmi 7: Octa-Core Snapdragon 632 processor with Adreno 506 GPU on top.

RAM and storage

Moto E6S: 4GB+64GB storage, expandable up to 512GB memory via dedicated microSD card.

Redmi 7: 2GB RAM+16GB storage, 3GB RAM+32GB storage and 4GB RAM+64GB storage, expandable via microSD card with up to 512GB memory.

Operating system

Moto E6S: Android 9.0 (Pie).

Redmi 7: Android 9.0 (Pie) with MIUI 10.

Cameras

Moto E6S: 13-megapixel rear camera with f/2.0 aperture and LED Flash, 2-megapixel secondary camera. an 8-megapixel selfie camera with f/2.0 aperture.

Redmi 7: 12-megapixel camera with f/2.2 aperture, LED Flash, 1.12um pixel size, PDAF, 2-megapixel secondary rear camera. 8-megapixel selfie shooter.

Biometrics

Moto E6S: Fingerprint scanner.

Redmi 7: Fingerprint sensor.

Battery capacity

Moto E6S: 3000mAh removable battery with 10W charging.

Redmi 7: 4000mAh battery.

Other features

Moto E6S: 3.5mm audio jack, FM Radio, P2i water-repellent nano-coating, 4G VoLTE, WiFi 802.11 a/b/g/n, Bluetooth 4.2, GPS and Micro USB.

Redmi 7: WiFi 802.11 b/g/n, Bluetooth 4.2, Dual 4G VoLTE, and GPS+GLONASS.
