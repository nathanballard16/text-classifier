The Gurdaspur Lok Sabha seat had been represented by late actor Vinod Khanna for four-times--1998, 1999, 2004 and 2014. Jakhar had defeated BJP candidate Swaran Salaria by a whopping margin of 1,93,219.

CHANDIGARH: Actor and BJP candidate Sunny Deol, whose real name is Ajay Singh Deol, filed his nomination papers from the Gurdaspur Lok Sabha seat in Punjab. Deol has declared assets worth over Rs. 81.47 crore. According to the actor's affidavit submitted as a part of his nomination filing process for his maiden fight in elections, his movable assets stand at over Rs 60.46 crore. They include Rs. 25,000 cash in hand, bank deposits of Rs. 26 lakh, investments in mutual funds with a total current market value of about Rs 1.43 crore. As part of his immovable property, the 59-year-old actor has declared that he owns 49.532 acres of agricultural land worth over Rs. 10 crore and non-agricultural land worth over Rs. 8.5 crore. Deol, son of legendary actor Dharmendra, also owns a flat in Mumbai with the current market value of Rs. 2.4 crore.
