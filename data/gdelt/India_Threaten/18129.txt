Anti-CAA stir: Section 144 imposed in Mandi House area

Dec 24, 2019, 02:54PM IST

Source: Times Now

Delhi police have imposed Section 144 in Mandi House area after protesters gather at Mandi House to protest against Citizenship (Amendment) Act. Jamia University students have planned a protest from Mandi House to Home Minister Amit Shah’s residence. Police imposed prohibitory orders under Section 144 of the IPC in Mandi House area to maintain the law and order situation. Protests have erupted across the country since the enactment of the CAA that grants citizenship to Hindus, Sikhs, Jains Parsis, Buddhists and Christians fleeing religious persecution from Pakistan, Afghanistan, and Bangladesh and came to India on or before December 31, 2014.
