19 ultras slain in Jammu & Kashmir till February 3

The killing of terrorists in encounters with the security forces in J&K continue well into 2019 with 19 terrorists neutralised till February 3. Over the past two years, as the J&K police, CRPF and Army turned up the heat on terrorists in the Valley, as many as 470 of them have suffered fatalities.The figure of 19 cited in a written reply by junior minister for home Hansraj Ahir to the Lok Sabha question on Tuesday, has now gone up further with five terrorists killed on Sunday and another on Tuesday.
