Intelligence input warns of terror attack in J&K: Govt sources

New Delhi [India], May 17 (ANI): The intelligence input on Friday warned of a terror attack in the state of Jammu and Kashmir, said government sources.

The sources further stated that the terrorists are planning to carry out attacks in Srinagar and Awantipora air bases.

Security forces are on high alert in and around these bases.

More details in this regard are awaited. (ANI)
