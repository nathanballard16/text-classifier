Hands tied in symbolic protest, farmers’ stir on DND flyway enters second day

Farmers from western Uttar Pradesh continued their protest on the Delhi-Noida-Delhi flyway to demand compensation for land acquired by former state governments for the second day on Saturday, PTI reported. It also brings transparency to the process of land acquisition and promises rehabilitation of the affected land holders. “We are here to demand that we be given compensation in accordance with the law on land acquisition that was passed in Parliament in 2013. Two years after the protests, the Congress government at the centre introduced Right to Fair Compensation and Transparency in Land Acquisition, Rehabilitation and Resettlement Act, 2013 (better known as Land Acquisition Act, 2013). AdvertisingThe farmers had on Friday warned that they will camp at the site until their demands are met, leading to traffic snarls all day.
