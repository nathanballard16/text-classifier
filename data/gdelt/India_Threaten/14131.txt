BJP-AAP clash on Kejriwal’s comment on Bihari patients

Today, someone from Bihar can take a Rs 500 ticket to the capital, get free treatment in our hospitals and return happily. Reacting to this, Delhi BJP president Tiwari on Monday said Kejriwal has been targeting the Purvanchalis, which is another name for people from Uttar Pradesh and Bihar. He asked “why does it pain Kejriwal” if a patient from Bihar or any other state gets treatment in Delhi . “Since I have become the chief of Delhi BJP, Kejriwal has abused me, my father and also my profession. According to Tiwari, “Delhi is not disabled, you (Kejriwal) are not capable.
