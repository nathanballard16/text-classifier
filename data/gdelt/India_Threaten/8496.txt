IMD issues warning of severe cyclonic storm across south India

Thiruvananthapuram: India Meteorological Department has issued warning of severe cyclonic storm across south India. The low pressure developed in Arabian Sea will intensify into a depression in next 12 hours and then to a cyclonic storm within 24 hours, it said.

Kerala State Disaster Management Authority (KSDMA) in a Facebook post said that according to IMD warning, the storm is likely to move north-northwestwards during next 72 hours.

It is very likely to cause adverse impact in terms of wind and rainfall.

IMD has warned Kerala of widespread rainfall with heavy falls (to very heavy falls on June 12) at isolated places from June 10 to June 14.
