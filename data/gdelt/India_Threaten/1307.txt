Arvind Kumar orders suspension of GHMC AE for negligence of duty

Charge memos will also be issued to the concerned Deputy Executive Engineer and the Executive Engineer, he ordered. He also said charge memos have been issued to the concerned Deputy Executive Engineer Venkatnarayana and Executive Engineer Anup Kumar. Hyderabad: Municipal Administration Principal Secretary Arvind Kumar today instructed GHMC Commissioner to immediately place one Assistant Engineer of GHMC under suspension for sheer negligence in his duties. Arvind Kumar instructed the GHMC Commissioner to immediately suspend concerned Assistant Engineer Venugopal. The Principal Secretary has warned that in future any lapses are observed, the concerned Assistant Engineer and Deputy Executive Engineer will be suspended without notice.
