No casualties in Balakot border breach, admits India

India has admitted for the first time that there were no casualties in the Balakot border breach.

Talking to an Indian TV channel, Union Minister Surinder Singh Ahluwalia said the so-called airstrikes by India were just a warning.

He accused Indian media of putting unconfirmed and wrong figures of killings.
