Today’s Top Breaking News LIVE Updates: Vikram Rathour set to become India’s batting coach, Bharat Arun, R Sridhar to continue as bowling and fielding coach

Top Breaking News LIVE: After the re-appointment of Ravi Shastri as the head coach of India, Vikram Rathour is all set to become the new batting coach of the Indian cricket team. Bharat Arun and Ramakrishnan Sridhar are going to again serve the Indian Cricket team as their bowling and fielding coach respectively. The second stint of Ravi Shastri will last till the T20 International World Cup 2021 and after that as per the requirement of the team and the players, the coach will be re-appointed or changed.
