AIMIM: Is the party facing internal squabbles?

A+ A-Hyderabad: All India Majilis-e-Ittehadul Muslimeen (AIMIM) is reportedly facing internal squabbles. Party men were asked to sort out all the issues before civic polls. According to the report published in New Indian Express, President of AIMIM, Mr. Asaduddin Owaisi has issued warning to party men. Mr. Owaisi asked them to sit down with District Presidents and resolve the issues. Talking about Civic Polls, Mr. Owaisi advised them to select candidates who can win and serve people.
