Cops tighten security in Delhi

A day after Pulwama attack, Delhi is on high alert and the senior officials have been asked to tighten up the security in the city. Along with market places, the cops have been asked to keep extra vigil near the headquarters of all the security forces in the Capital.

"Indira Gandhi International airport, New Delhi railway station and India Gate, along with other historical monuments, are among the vulnerable places that could be the target for the possible terror strike," said a senior police official, requesting anonymity.

Since Friday morning, Amulya Patnaik, Delhi Police Commissioner has conducted two separate meetings at police headquarters with the senior police officials.

Highly-placed sources confirmed the specific information about a possible drone attack in Lutyens' Delhi. "Anti-social or terrorist elements may pose a threat to the safety of these Constitutional authorities," a source said.
