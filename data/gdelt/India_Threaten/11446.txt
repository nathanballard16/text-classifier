School bus operators sound caution

Ludhiana: City-based school bus operators have issued a set of guidelines for parents who avail school bus services to ensure the safety of their children. In view of the increased cases of abduction members of school bus operators welfare have appealed to parents of all prominent city-based schools that they should not depend on anyone to get their children picked up from the respective bus stops. Bhupinder Singh, one of the members of the association said, “Parents should constantly counsel their children that they should not accompany any unknown person even if they gave the reference of their parents.” He said parents should also tell their ward not to accept any eatables from such unknown persons.
