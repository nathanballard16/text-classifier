Thunderstorm warning issued for Telangana

Hyderabad: India Meteorological Department has issued thunderstorm warning for Telangana State. However, the trough at 0.9 km above mean sea level now runs from Gulf of Mannar to North Interior Karnataka across Tamilnadu and South Interior Karnataka. The upper air cyclonic circulation extending upto 0.9 km above mean sea level over southwest Bay of Bengal off north Tamilnadu coast has become less marked. Thunderstorm accompanied with Hailstorm likely to occur at isolated places in all the districts of Telangana on 26th and 27th January, the MET Department said. Mainly low level Southeasterly / southerlies prevail over the Telangana State,” the MET Department said in its bulletin.
