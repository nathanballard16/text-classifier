Man held for sending threat message to Maharashtra CM Devendra Fadnavis

Identified as Pankaj Kumbhar, the man allegedly issued threat to Chief Minister Devendra Fadnavis on social media on Sunday. A 34 year-old man has been detained by Satara police crime branch after taking his custody from Agripada police. He was to put up a stall at the venue of CM's rally and was in Mumbai to buy the items. Fadnavis was scheduled hold a rally in Satara on February 4. He would come to Mumbai to buy materials and then sell it in Satara.
