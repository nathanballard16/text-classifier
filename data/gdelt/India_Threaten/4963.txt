Ludhiana visit did gangster in: Cops

LUDHIANA: Amarbir Singh alias Lalli Cheema , a member of the Sukha Kahlon gang who was arrested following a shootout in Ludhiana, had come to the city along his accomplices on Tuesday to stay for a night at a friend’s place in a village nearby. They stopped at a juice vendor when the police tried to nab them. Soon, an exchange of fire ensued between cops and the gang, said police on Wednesday.According to information, after the cross-firing — in which Lalli Cheema also sustained a bullet injury in his leg —the police managed to nab Cheema, who tried to damage his mobile by chewing it. The cops then hit him on his jaw, resulting in a broken jaw.On the complaint of subinspector Kirpal Singh of Organised Crime Control Unit (OCCU), Dehlon police have registered a case under sections 307 (attempt to murder), 353 (assault or criminal force to deter public servant from discharge of his duty), 186 (obstructing public servant in discharge of public functions), and 34 (act done by several persons in furtherance of common intention) of the IPC, and Arms Act against Cheema and Kuldeep Singh. The police on Wednesday also produced Kuldeep in the district court, which has announced three days police remand. On the other hand, Cheema has been admitted in Rajinder Hospital in Patiala for treatment. The police have also recovered .30 bore pistol with three live cartridges and a 7.65mm pistol with five live cartridges.Kunwar Vijay Pratap Singh, IGP-OCCU said: “Lalli Cheema is a member of Sukha Kahlon gang.
