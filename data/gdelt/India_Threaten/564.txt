Kejriwal office receives kidnapping threat for daughter

New Delhi, Jan 12 : The office of Delhi Chief Minister Arvind Kejriwal reportedly received an email from an anonymous account threatening to kidnap his daughter, sources said on Saturday.

The threat was received on the official email of the Chief Minister on Wednesday.

"The Delhi government had forwarded the threat email to Delhi Police Commissioner three days ago. The Delhi Government has not been given any information so far by the police," the CM office said on Saturday.
