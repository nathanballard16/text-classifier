'No challenge for PM Modi, BJP will get 74 plus seats in UP on its own': Highlights of Yogi Adityanath's interview

UP CM Yogi Adityanath's exclusive interview to Zee Media. Here are the highlights.

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath on Tuesday said there was no challenger of Prime Minister Narendra Modi and the Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP) will win more than 74 Lok Sabha seats in the state.

In an exclusive interview to Zee Media, Yogi said Prime Minister Modi is not facing any challenge, neither in Varanasi from where he will contest the Lok Sabha polls or in the country.

The Chief Minister said the BJP had Modi's name and his work as Chief Minister of Gujarat in 2014 and contested the poll on that. But in 2019, there is both Modi's name as the Prime Minister and Modi's work in five years which BJP will bank upon to fight the polls, he told Zee Uttar Pradesh Uttarakhand.
