Arvind Kejriwal alleges 'secret understanding' between Congress, BJP in Delhi

Arvind Kejriwal claimed when the entire country wants to oust the BJP from the power, the Congress is helping the saffron party by splitting anti-BJP votes.

Delhi CM Arvind Kejriwal (Image: PTI)

Delhi Chief Minister Arvind Kejriwal alleged \"some secret understanding\" between the Congress and the BJP.

He said Congress wants to split anti-BJP votes in Delhi in the Lok Sabha polls.

People will defeat this \"unholy alliance\" of the Congress and the BJP, Kejriwal said.
