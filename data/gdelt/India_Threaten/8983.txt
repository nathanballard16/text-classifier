Intelligence warns ISIS attack in Kerala

Thiruvananthapuram : Intelligence agencies have warned ISIS attack in India, especially in the metro cities including Kochi. As per the reports, the letters sent by the state intelligence to top Kerala Police officers hinted at the possibilities.

"Key installations in Kochi, including a prominent shopping mall, could become ISIS targets. According to the senior officials, Kerala, Tamil Nadu, Andhra Pradesh and Kashmir are the states most vulnerable to the ISIS influence in the country', they were quoted as saying.

The Kerala cost has been on alert to prevent the entry of ISIS terrorists from the island nation since May-end.
