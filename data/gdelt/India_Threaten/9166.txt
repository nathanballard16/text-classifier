Trump signs executive order imposing "hard hitting" sanction on Iran

Soon after Iran downed the US drone on Thursday, Trump had tweeted a warning message saying Iran had made a very big mistake.

i: US President Donald Trump signed an executive order on Monday, imposing “hard-hitting" sanction on Iran, days after Tehran claimed to have shot an American drone. Trump also said that the US sanctions on Iran could last 'years' and it will deny the Iranian Supreme Leader and other officials access to financial instruments within US jurisdiction. Quoting officials, news agency AFP reported that the US will block 'billions' more in Iranian assets and it will add Iran Foreign Minister Zarif to sanctions list 'later this week'.
