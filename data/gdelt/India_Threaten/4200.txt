Mayawati's ex-secretary raided over tax evasion worth Rs 100 crores

A retired IAS officer, Netram held a key post in Mayawati's government between 2007 and 2012. (Photo: File)

New Delhi: Bahujan Samaj Party chief Mayawati's former secretary was raided by the Income Tax officials over suspected tax evasions amounting to Rs 100 crore.

The raids are still underway at 12 location in Lucknow and Delhi.

A retired IAS officer, Netram held a key post in Mayawati's government between 2007 and 2012.

This is a developing story.
