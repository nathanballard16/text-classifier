Mehmood Khan's journey from top Unilever exec to fighting Lok Sabha polls

As a child, Mehmood Khan says he would often navigate through waist-deep waters to reach school some distance away from his village of Nai Nangla in Mewat.

From there, he went on to navigate the globe as a Unilever executive -- from seeking business opportunities in Eastern Europe as it came out from behind the Iron Curtain to opening up countries such as Vietnam, Cambodia and Laos for the company.
