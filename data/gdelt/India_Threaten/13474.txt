3 students held for threatening to kidnap child in Ghaziabad

Ghaziabad: Three young students have been arrested by Ghaziabad police for allegedly hatching a plan to kidnap an 11-year-old student of Amity International School and demanding a ransom of Rs 5 lakhs from the family in Indirapuram area of Ghaziabad. Cops said that the victim was cousin brother of one of the accused, who is also the mastermind of their plan.
