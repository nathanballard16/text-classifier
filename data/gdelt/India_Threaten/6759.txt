Heat wave : Red alert in Nagpur, most of Vidarbha

Regional Meteorological Centre, IMD, Nagpur has on Tuesday issued a heat wave warning for Vidarbha, including red alert for many cities including Nagpur. In Akola, Amravati and Chandrapur, severe heat wave condition has been predicted to continue atleast two more days. Nagpur: Even as the heat has engulfed the major parts of India including across Maharashtra, Nagpur and rest of Vidarbha is feeling the bitter pinch. The Met office has also issued similar heat wave warning for other cities of Vidarbha including Akola, Amravati, Chandrapur, Wardha, Washim and Yavatmal for Tuesday and Wednesday. The RMC forecast projects heat wave over many places in Nagpur on Tuesday which is expected to rise on Wednesday.
