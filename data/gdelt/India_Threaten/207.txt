Peace in Afghanistan is important for the region

US President Donald Trump's plan to reduce the number of American troops in Afghanistan to half is definitely a great cause of concern for the region. Whether the decision will lead to increased fighting in Afghanistan, or a power-sharing arrangement with the Taleban is anyone's guess at the moment. But India has reasons to worry whatever the case may be. New Delhi has invested considerable time and resources in Afghanistan, and now it should engaging more with Taleban.

-Ramesh G Jethwani, Bengaluru
