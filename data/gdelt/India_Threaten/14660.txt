Parties slammed for demeaning Phoolka

Ludhiana, October 11Manwinder Singh Giaspura of Hondh Chillar Talmel Committee slammed the SAD, Congress and leaders of other political parties, who are allegedly criticising former AAP MLA HS Phoolka, who had resigned as legislator from Dakha constituency. He warned the political parties against running any “false” campaign against Phoolka. Giaspura said Phoolka had resigned to protest against the Congress for allegedly failing to act against the accused in sacrilege incidents. “HS Phoolka had started a mobile dispensary around two years ago and many people have taken benefit of it. We condemn parties for levelling false allegations against HS Phoolka,” said Ranjit Singh Dhamot of the committee.
