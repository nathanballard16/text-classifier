Protest rallies staged against Indian aggression

HANGU: Protest rallies were staged in Hangu and Orkazai districts to condemn the Indian aggression.

Led by Mufti Hashmat, the protesters marched from Mashti Bazaar to District Headquarters. They were holding placards inscribed with slogans against the Indian Prime Minister Narendra Modi and in support of armed forces.

Speaking on the occasion, the speakers warned the Indian government against aggression, adding that tribespeople would sacrifice their lives for safeguarding the motherland. A rally was organised in Hangu district in support of the armed forces. The speakers including Maulana Abdul Jalil, Maulana Irshad Ali, Malik Sajid and others said the masses would fight against India alongside armed forces.
