LS polls: 'I'm a monk, will curse if you don't vote for me' warns BJP MP Sakshi Maharaj

Sakshi misused his position as a monk and warned people of dire consequences if they didn’t vote in his favour.

Sakshi further threatened the people in the name of ‘mythology’. Sakshi, who is currently a Member of Parliament from Unnao, has made several controversial statement in the past as well, but this time the ‘controversial and threatening ‘statement was addressed to the voters.
