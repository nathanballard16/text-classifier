Internet suspended, curfew imposed, 2 dead amid anti-CAB protests in Assam - 10 points

Anti-CAB protests in Assam | Photo Credit: PTI

Guwahati: People in north-eastern states of Assam and Meghalaya took to streets resorting to vandalism and arson on Thursday. The protests over Citizenship (Amendment) Bill, 2019, claimed two lives in Assam’s Guwahati amid attacks on some politicians and burning down of infrastructure in the state.
