Branches break off trees as dust storm hits city

Ludhiana: Dust storm took the city by surprise on Thursday evening. Commuters faced tough time navigating as branches of several trees broke off on the Ferozepur Road and Chandigarh Road.

An official of the meteorological department in Chandigarh said western disturbance triggered the storm in the state. He said speed of dust storm at Amritsar Airport was measured at 27 nautical miles.

The department official said they had predicted dust storm for Thursday and no such disturbance is expected in the next few days.

Meanwhile, maximum temperature in the city on Thursday was 32.2°C, which is a degree less than normal and minimum temperature was recorded at 21.5°C, which is three degrees more than normal.
