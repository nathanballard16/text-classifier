No tropical depression observed: Oman Meteorology

By: Times News Service

Muscat: The Public Authority for Civil Aviation has announced that there is no tropical depression in the Arabian Sea.

In case the depression is observed, the authorities will notify through its proper channels.

In a statement online, Oman's meteorology authority said:

"The National Multi Hazard Early Warning Centre assures everyone that there is no tropical activity in the Arabian Sea. If a tropical depression is observed, the Center will issue an official report according to the operational procedures followed by the Center."
