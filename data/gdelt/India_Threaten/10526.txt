Group clash triggers firing

Ludhiana: Members of a group allegedly fired in the air during a clash with another group on Machhiwara Road in Samrala on Thursday evening. Police officials said members of two groups had a tiff over some trivial issue. They said after the situation turned ugly and a member of a group and fired twice in the air. Samrala DSP Harinder Singh said they are in the process of registering a case into the matter. He said persons involved in the clash have been identified.
