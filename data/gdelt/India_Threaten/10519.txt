Mumbai: NCP's Sachin Ahir clears Worli for Aaditya Thackeray

Clearing the decks for Yuva Sena chief Aaditya Thackeray to contest the state polls from Worli, the man who would have been his main challenger in the constituency, NCP's Sachin Ahir, joined the Shiv Sena on Thursday. Ahir, until recently the Nationalist Congress Party's Mumbai chief, will fight the upcoming assembly elections from Byculla constituency. Speaking to the media during Ahir's formal entry into the Sena, Aaditya said they had been discussing this for days.
