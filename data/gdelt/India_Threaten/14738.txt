Man robbed of Rs 4L, near Metro station

New Delhi, October 14Four men, who had hid their faces with handkerchiefs and helmets, robbed a businessman of Rs 4 lakh at a busy red light near a Metro station in North-West Delhi after breaking a window of his car with a hammer and threatening him with a gun, said the police on Monday. Anil Arora, who was accompanied by an associate in the car at the time of robbery on Sunday night, was returning home in Panipat from the popular Gaffar Market in Karol Bagh, they said. Arora, 22, runs a mobile phone business. When he and his associate reached the red light near the Majlis Park metro station, four men arrived from behind and broke a window of his car with a hammer and fled with a bag containing Rs 4 lakh after threatening the duo, the police said. The police said they did not receive any PCR call in connection with the robbery.
