Arvind Kejriwal receives letter threatening to kill daughter Harshita

Arvind Kejriwal receives letter threatening to kill daughter Harshita Delhi Police has deployed a Protective Service Officer (PSO) for CM's daughter and the matter has been handed over to Cyber cell. NEW DELHI: Delhi's Chief Minister Arvind Kejriwal received an email threating to kill her daughter in the official inbox. According to Police Sources, the CMO received the email sent by an anonymous sender on the 9th of January. After receiving the alleged threat, the Delhi police has now provided CM Kejriwal's daughter with Protective Service Officers (PSO) for her security. On the matter, Delhi Government says they have not been given any information so far by the Delhi police. Delhi government claims that they had forwarded the threat email to Delhi Police Commissioner three days ago. In the case, as soon as the CM Office received a threatening email, the police commissioner was informed and a complaint was filed by the Chief Minister's office. In view of the seriousness of the situation, the case has now been handed over to Cyber Cell. Aam Aadmi Party (AAP) chief Arvind Kejriwal's daughter Harshita, who scored 96 per cent in the board exams, had joined Indian Institute of Technology.
