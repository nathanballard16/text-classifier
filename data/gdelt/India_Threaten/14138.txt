Hyderabad CP warns of severe action against rumors on Kashmir

A+ A-Hyderabad: The City Police has warned of criminal action against those spreading rumors on social media to instigate trouble. In a message to the citizens, City Police Commissioner Anjani Kumar has appealed to the general public not to forward any unverified posts on social media platforms. The message was in the background of a few quarters spreading pictures and fake news on the situation in Kashmir.
