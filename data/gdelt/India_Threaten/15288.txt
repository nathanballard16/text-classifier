Will fight for justice, says DK Shivakumar

A+ A-Bengaluru: Congress leader DK Shivakumar, who was granted bail by Delhi High Court in a money laundering case, said on Saturday that he will fight for justice. Let the God and law punish me,” Shivakumar said in a press conference here. I don’t think I will ever be able to pay them back,” Shivakumar said.
