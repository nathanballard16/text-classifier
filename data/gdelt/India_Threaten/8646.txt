Sending too many messages on Whatsapp can create trouble for you

The Facebook-owned instant messaging platform, WhatsApp is to take strict action against users who send too many messages. While the messaging company could put a ban on the users for contravening WhatsApp terms and services, the legal action against users is another move in company’s efforts to crackdown on fake news.

With its 1.5 billion active users, WhatsApp has updated its FAQ section announcing that from December 7, 2019, legal actions will be taken by the company against users for activities like “bulk and automated messaging”. Also the company has officially issued a warning to initiate legal actions even if it relates to “information solely available to us off our platform”.

The move will also let the messaging app to put a check on to how users utilise its platform.
