Bomb threat call received at Ahmedabad airport, no flight disrupted

Follow us on Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel International Airport

A bomb threat call for T-2 terminal at Ahmedabad International Airport was received at around 3:30 am through Air India office in Mumbai, reported ANI quoting Director, Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel International Airport.

The Bomb Threat Assessment Committee assesed the call as 'non-specific', added Director.

Security has been beefed up at the airport as precautionary measure.

"No flight has been disrupted and all the operations at airport are being carried normally," Director said.
