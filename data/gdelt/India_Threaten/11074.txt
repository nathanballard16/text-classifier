Woman blackmailed by boyfriend goes to police

Kolkata: A girl from Narendrapur in South 24-Parganas lodged a complaint with the police against her boyfriend who had allegedly blackmailed her using some of her personal photographs. The victim in her complaint also alleged that her boyfriend had established physical relation with her with a promise of marriage. As she demanded an explanation from the accused when he failed to keep his promise, the accused alleged threatened her saying he would upload some of her personal photographs on the social media. Initially, the victim was scared to raise a complaint with the police as the accused threatened her with dire consequences. The victim also told the police that the accused took her personal photographs when they had physical relations.
