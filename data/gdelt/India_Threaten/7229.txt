Heat wave conditions may end today in Telangana

Hyderabad: The heat wave conditions are likely to end on Friday with the Met Department issuing thunderstorm warning for three days from May 11-13.

According to India Meteorological Department, heat wave conditions were likely to prevail over some parts in many districts of Telangana State on Friday. However, it predicted light to moderate rain or thunder showers, accompanied with gusty winds (40 to 50 kmph), at isolated places over Telangana on 11th, 12th and 13th May.

Meanwhile, heat wave conditions prevailed over some parts in the districts of Khammam and Nalgonda on Thursday. Highest Maximum Temperature of 45 Degrees Celsius was recorded both at Nalgonda and Khammam followed by 44 Deg Cels in Adilabad and Ramagundem. The Maximum Temperature in Hyderabad was 42 Degrees Celsius.
