Bomb Threat At Chandigarh's Elante Mall; Shoppers Evacuated, Search Underway

Thousands of people who were present in the mall and nearby areas were informed about the possible threat and were asked to leave the premises.

Panic gripped Chandigarh’s Elante Mall and people were evacuated, after police received a threat call about a bomb allegedly planted in the mall’s premises. The informants contacted Chandigarh Police via an internet call and claimed that a bomb has been planted in the premises of Elante Mall. Police issued an alert immediately after receiving the call and deployed all the force including bomb squad team and other teams, inside the mall.
