Maruti cuts prices by Rs 5,000 on select cars to boost festive demand

Maruti Suzuki, the leading automaker in the country, on Wednesday said it had slashed prices by up to Rs 5,000. This move will boost demand in the festive season when most cars are sold, it said. The lower prices will be effective immediately on models such as Alto 800, Alto K10, Swift (diesel), Celerio, Baleno (diesel), Ignis, Dzire (diesel), Tour S (diesel), Vitara Brezza and S-Cross.
