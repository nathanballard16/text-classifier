We will fight against Modi at Centre & KCR in State: Vijayasanti

Hyderabad: TPCC leader Vijayasanti today said they would campaign for the party in the upcoming parliament elections by portraying it as a fight between Congress president Rahul Gandhi and Prime Minister Narendra Modi. She also said their main target would be to expose failures of the Modi government in the people’s court.

She made these remarks at national capital after attending a meeting of senior party leaders from the State held at AICC war room in New Delhi. TPCC president N. Uttam Kumar Reddy and party MLA Komatireddy Rajagopal Reddy also attended the meeting.

The meeting discussed about the strategies to be devised for the victory of the party in the Lok Sabha elections. Speaking on the occasion, she said they had not yet decided on the fact of launching their election campaign in the State. She said they would fight against Modi at the Centre and Chief Minister KCR in the State in the upcoming Lok Sabha elections.
