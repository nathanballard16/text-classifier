GoAir waives cancellation, rescheduling fees due to Fani

New Delhi: In wake of the cyclone Fani budget airline GoAir is waiving cancellation and rescheduling fees for Bhubaneswar, Kolkata and Ranchi flights for travel between May 2 to May 5, 2019.

The airline in its alert said, "Due to cyclone FANI, passengers traveling to and from Bhubaneswar, Kolkata and Ranchi could be inconvenienced. We are waiving cancellation and rescheduling fees for Bhubaneswar, Kolkata and Ranchi flights for travel between May 2, 2019 to May 5, 2019. Flights must be re-booked within seven days of scheduled departure."

It further said that the customers can contact the Airport Ticketing offices or call the 24x7 customer service center on 18602 100 999.
