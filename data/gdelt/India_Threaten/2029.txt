UKHS to launch new party

Belagavi: Uttara Karnataka Horata Samiti (UKHS) general secretary Nagesh Golshetti said that the forum has planned to form a new regional political party for the overall development of North Karnataka . He said a public referendum is being conducted on launching the new party.Golshetti told reporters on Wednesday that the referendum was launched on February 2 and will continue till February 28. “If we get positive public response, the new party will be announced on March 3,” he said.Golishetti alleged that many political leaders in different parties had raised their voice in favour of North Karnataka region but become silent after they get lucrative posts. The prime aim of the new regional party will be fighting for a separate North Karnataka without any compromise, he said.UKHS district president Adivesh Itagi was present.
