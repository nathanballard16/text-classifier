Follow us on Image Source : ANI Fire breaks out at Directorate General of Health Services Office in Karkardooma on Friday.

A fire broke out at Directorate General of Health Services (DGHS) Office, Karkardooma, on Friday. 22 fire tenders were at the spot.

The fire department received a call at 1.50 pm after which 22 fire tenders were rushed to the spot.

"More than 60 fire personnel have been deployed in the fire fighting operation. No casualty has been reported," PTI quoted Atul Garg, Chief Fire Officer, as saying.

There is no detail about the casualties yet. What led to the fire is yet to be ascertained.
