MEERUT: PM Narendra Modi pays tribute to Choudhary Charan Singh in Meerut rally, says chowkidar does no injustice.

India has made up its mind who to vote for says PM in Meerut rally.

On one side there is a strong ‘chowkidar’, on the other a line of tainted people said Modi in Meerut.

Land, sky or space, my govt has shown courage to conduct surgical strike in all spheres said Modi.

Modi says Lok Sabha elections will be fight between decisive government and indecisive past.
