Water of three rivers common with Pakistan will be diverted to Yamuna: Nitin Gadkari

Baghpat (Uttar Pradesh), Feb 21 (ANI): Transport and River Development Minister Nitin Gadkari issued a stern warning to Pakistan over Pulwama attack and said that rivers water from Sutlej, Beas and Ravi on which India has full rights will be diverted to Yamuna by building projects. “After partition three rivers were divided by India and Pakistan and water from three rivers within India’s rights was flowing to Pakistan. We have constructed projects which will BE diverted to Yamuna and it will have more water”, said Gadkari while addressing in Baghpat. His comments come amid India finding several ways to punish Pakistan over Pulwama terror attack.
