Babri Mosque case: Indian Supreme Court sets final deadline

In India, the Supreme Court has set a deadline of 18th of next month for all stakeholders to complete their arguments in a case pertaining to 1992 Babri Masjid demolition.

A Bench headed by Chief Justice Ranjan Gogoi said that if parties want to amicably resolve the matter through mediation, they can still go ahead with it.
