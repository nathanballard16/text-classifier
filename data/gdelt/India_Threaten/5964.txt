Ganged up political parties will never be allowed by Modi to succeed: Jitendra - Jammu Kashmir Latest News

JAMMU: Union Minister and BJP Lok Sabha candidate from Udhampur-Kathua-Doda Parliamentary constituency, Dr Jitendra Singh on Tuesday said that Congress, its allies and all those political parties and leaders who have ganged up against BJP and Prime Minister Narendra Modi, are feeling so insecure that they have resorted to anti-national and anti-India jargon, but cautioned that Modi will never allow anti-nationals or anti-national designs to succeed.
