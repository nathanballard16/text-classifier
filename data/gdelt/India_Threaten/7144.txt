Heat wave warning extended till May 10

Hyderabad: The Met Department on Tuesday extended the heat wave warming for Telangana State till May 10.

According to India Meteorological Department, heat wave conditions are likely to prevail in many parts of Telangana State till May 10. On Tuesday, heat wave conditions prevailed over some parts in the districts of Khammam and Nalgonda.

The highest maximum temperature of 45.6 Degrees Celsius (DS) was recorded at Khammam on Tuesday, followed by 45 Deg Cel in Nalgonda, 44 Deg Cel in Adilabad, Bhadrachalam, Nizamabad and Ramagundem.
