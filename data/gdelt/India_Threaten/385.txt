Central staff strike hits banks, post offices, coal production

Nagpur: The two-day strike by central government staffers against new pension scheme (NPS) paralyzed work in a number of offices apart from PSU banks and insurance companies. A senior official in the postal department said over 90% of the post offices were closed. Cash transactions could not happen at BSNL offices.Production was partially affected at the central mining PSU Western Coalfields Limited. The federations of employees union in the organization have called for a three-day strike from January 23 on the same issues. There was skeletal staff in PSU banks and the government life insurer Life Insurance Corporation (LIC).
