Nowhera Shaikh’s Heera Gold Group Case: Names of terror suspects surface during probe

Terror suspects had allegedly invested in Heera Gold Group. Hyderabad: New shocking details came to light in Nowhera Shaikh’s Heera Gold Group case. Police suspects that persons who are involved in criminal activates had invested in Heera Gold Group. It may be mentioned that Andhra Pradesh Police on Thursday arrested Nowhera Shaikh, the Managing Director of the Hyderabad-based Heera Gold group, in a ponzi scheme case. According to the report published in DC, Hyderabad Central Crime Station Police also obtained names of some suspects from Central Security agencies.
