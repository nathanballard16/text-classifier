IndiGo Pune-Nagpur flight returns after engine caution message; landed safely

An IndiGo Pune-Nagpur flight after take-off returned to Pune when the pilot observed engine caution message, today. As per sources, the IndiGO aircraft landed safely and it is being inspected by a technical team. Earlier on Friday IndiGo flight operating from Delhi to Istanbul was diverted to Kuwait after getting a snag warning in one of the two Pratt & Whitney (PW) engines powering the Airbus A320 Neo.
