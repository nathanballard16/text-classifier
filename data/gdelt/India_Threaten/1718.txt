Ahead of Lok Sabha elections 2019, WhatsApp warns political parties against misuse of messaging platform

2/5

WhatsApp also said that it has been engaging with parties to emphasise that the misuse will lead to banning of such accounts.

"We have seen a number of parties attempt to use Whatsapp in a way that was not intended and our firm message to them is using it in that way will result in bans of our service," WhatsApp Head of Communications Carl Woog told reporters in New Delhi.

"We are trying to be very clear going into elections that there is an abuse of Whatsapp and we are very working very hard to identify and prevent it as soon as possible," he added
