More trouble for Nowhera Shaikh?

Hyderabad: Enforcement Directorate (ED) sought permission from the local court to interrogate MD of Heera Gold Group, Ms. Nowhera Shaikh. Enforcement Directorate will take Nowhera Shaikh into its custody soon for further interrogation. It may be mentioned that Central Crime Station of Hyderabad City Police had arrested Nowhera Shaikh last year on the basis of the complaints received from various account holders that she had deceived them. According to the press note issued by ED, the manager of the company, Murli Thomas and Biju Thomas will also be taken into custody for interrogation. So far, the investigating agency has identified 81 properties of her group spread over AP, Delhi, Maharashtra, Kerala and Karnataka.
