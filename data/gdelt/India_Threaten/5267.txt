Gondia: Satta Bazaar abuzz with BJP’s return to power

BJP may sweep Maharashtra, MP; close fight in Chhattisgarh:If Satta Market is to be believed, BJP-Shiv Sena may sweep Maharashtra. The satta bazaar, or the illegal betting market, had turned red hot. But according to the Satta Bazaar, Dalit Vanchit Aghadi and BSP-SP combine is likely to dent UPA seats considerably. Satta Bazaar is agog with speculations. Three bookies of Nagpur and Gondia have claimed the Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP)-led National Democratic Alliance (NDA) returning to power.
