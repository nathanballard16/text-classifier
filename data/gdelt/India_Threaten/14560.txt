Hyderabad: Wine shop dealers cash in on Dasara

A+ A-Hyderabad: The Excise Department has warned wine shop dealers against selling liquor at prices more than the MRP taking advantage of Dasara festival. Excise Department has warned wine shop dealers of imposing a fine of Rs 2 lakh if found guilty. According to reports from Sircilla, wine shop dealers have exorbitantly increased the rates on account of the festival. This has been the case in respect of at least 75 per cent of the wine shops. There are at least 1000 belt shops in the district linked to these wine shops and bars.
