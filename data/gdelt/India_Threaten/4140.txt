CBSE cautions parents against fake messages in social media about paper leak

Students and parents should specifically be aware of fake messages of paper leak and paper availability in open. “The mischievous and fake messages create confusion in the minds of students and parents and they face a number of hardships. Nagpur: In view of ongoing Class 10th and Class 12th exams, the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) has cautioned the parents to be aware of mischievous and fake messages being circulated in social media. In its notification, CBSE has said that it has so far conducted 136 exams successfully. An FIR has been lodged in this connection,” CBSE declared and added that Delhi police have been urged to take action against those guilty under IT Act.
