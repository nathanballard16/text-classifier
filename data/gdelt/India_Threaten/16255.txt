Cop nabbed with heroin, dismissed

A drug addict, was peddling drugs for 7 yearsTribune News ServiceLudhiana, November 19Special task force today nabbed a Ludhiana policeman with 51 gram of heroin. The suspect has been identified as Amandeep Singh (27), a resident of Onkar Vihar, Sector 32. The STF set up a naka and nabbed Amandeep with the heroin. The suspect also admitted that he was a drug addict and he had been into drug peddling for the past over seven years. The STF in-charge said they would seek police remand of the suspect so that the entire drug racket could be busted.
