(Image source from: V6velugu.com)

Temperatures in Telangana Touches 47 Degrees:- The heat wave had a huge impact on the people of Telangana. The state witnessed temperatures crossing 47 degrees celsius from the past two days. The Telangana State Development Planning Society (TDSDPS) revealed that Mancherial district's Neelwai witnessed 47.8 degrees yesterday and it is followed by Dharmapuri in Jagtial district touching 47.7 degrees. Endapally Rajarampalli and Jaina witnessed maximum temperatures of 47.7 and 47.6 degrees respectively.

Hyderabad too has been burning with the heat wave and people are scared to come out. Maredupalle in Peddapalli district is at 47.5 degrees and Kothapallegori in Jayashankar-Bhupalpally district touched 47.5 degrees. Medchal-Malkajgiri district which is a part of Hyderabad witnessed 46.6 degrees yesterday. Hyderabad and Rangareddy districts witnessed 44.8 and 44.9 degrees. The Indian Meteorological Department (IMD) has already issued a heat wave warning asking people to be cautious. IMD said that the heat wave would continue till May 30th.
