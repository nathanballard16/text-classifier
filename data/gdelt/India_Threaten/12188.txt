'Sultan & Pehlwaan': Sudeep Poses With 'Dabangg 3' Co-Star Salman Khan! See Pic!

Take a look at his post below:

Kannada superstar Kichcha Sudeep's photograph with Bollywood superstar Salman Khan has taken over social media. Sudeep took to Twitter to post a pic that has the two stars striking a wrestling pose. "Pehlwaan & Sultan", goes the caption of the snapshot, referring to the fact that Salman Khan had played a wrestler in the 2016 film, "Sultan", while Sudeep essays one in his upcoming biggie, "Pehlwaan". "No this isn't another poster.... This is just how he bonds if he luvs. Thank you Salman Khan sir, for the place you have given me in ur life. Im honoured and blessed," Sudeep wrote.
