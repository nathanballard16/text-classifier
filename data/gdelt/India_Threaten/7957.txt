Yogi Adityanath’s Cabinet to meet for the first time after poll verdict

After the Lok Sabha results were out on May 23, it would be the first meeting of the Cabinet of Uttar Pradesh. The Cabinet is likely to congratulate and greet Prime Minister Mr. Narendra Modi for his landslide victory in the general elections 2019. Also the three ministers, who were elected to the Lok Sabha, are to be bid farewell.

The meeting is likely to mark the shift of Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath’s focus back to governance taking up the key issues for deciding on them in the coming days. Other issues likely to be taken up include amendment to the Uttar Pradesh Sugarcane (Regulation of Supply and Purchase) Act 1953, nod to Uttar Pradesh Cow Protection and Development of Cows Rules 2019, amendment to Uttar Pradesh State Universities Act, 1973 and issues concerned with the construction of International Airport at Jewar in Greater Noida region of Gautam Buddha Nagar district.

Yogi Adityanath’s Cabinet, bidding farewell to three ministers, will vacate the seats to 14 that also includes a seat of SBSP leader Om Prakash Rajbhar who was sacked from the cabinet, bringing down his cabinet strength to 43.
