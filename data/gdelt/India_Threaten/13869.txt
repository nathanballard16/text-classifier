Jaish Hit List: Narendra Modi and Amit Shah Targets:- Indian Prime Minister Narendra Modi, Union Home Minister Amit Shah and National Security Advisor Ajit Doval are on the hit list of Pakistan based terrorist organization Jaish-e-Mohammad. Jaish is planning massive attacks over the abrogation of Article 370 in Jammu and Kashmir said Intelligence Department. The Bureau of Civil Aviation Security recently received a letter recently about this. It has been revealed that the attacks are planned in 30 cities across the country including Pathankot, Jammu, Amritsar, Gandhinagar, Jaipur, Lucknow, Hyderabad and Kanpur.

There are also talks that Lashkar-e-Taiba is planning a terror attack in Narendra Modi's constituency Varanasi. LeT, led by Hafiz Saeed is planning the attacks as per the Indian Intelligence. The Centre alerted all the states and their governments ahead of this high alert. The security has been tightened across all the airports in the country. Some of the terrorists used drones to transfer weapons along with explosive materials to Punjab's Tarn Taaran district and the cops arrested a bunch of terrorists recently.
