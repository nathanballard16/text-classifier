More trouble for Nowhera Shaikh? EOW to take her into custody

Hyderabad: After local court’s hearing, the Managing Director of Heera Gold Group, Nowhera Shaikh may face more troubles. The judge has granted five-day police custody of the Nowhera Shaikh.

According to the report published in Telangana Today, she will be taken into custody by the Economic Offences Wing (EOW) of Cyberabad Police.

It may be mentioned that presently, Nowhera Shaikh is lodged in Cherlapally Central Jail.

It may be noted that police wants to acquire information regarding investments made by persons having links with the illegal organizations.
