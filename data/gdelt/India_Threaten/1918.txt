Cash agent robbed of Rs 10L near his office

The incident took place at 9.30am on Monday in front of the builder’s office in Avantika. As Chaudhary resisted the robbery attempt, the miscreants took out a gun and fired at him. GHAZIABAD: Three-bike borne assailants robbed Rs 10 lakh at gun point from an employee of city-based builder. After snatching the bag, the assailants fled towards Chiranjeev Vihar.”The manager subsequently called the police control room to report the crime. The miscreants fired two gunshots in the air to disperse the crowd which started to run after them.
