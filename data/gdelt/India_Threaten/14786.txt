Angry PMC Bank Depositors Stage a Protest in Mumbai

Angry depositors stage a protest against the harsh limits put on the account holders of the PMC bank, which is embroiled in a Rs 3,830 crore scam in Mumbai. The protest went severe after the death of a customer with around Rs 90 lakh in the crisis-hit Punjab & Maharashtra Cooperative Bank Ltd has died.(Image: Sumangal Tambe/News18)
