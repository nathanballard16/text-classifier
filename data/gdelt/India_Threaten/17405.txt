From Rahul to Priyanka: Who said what at Congress' 'Bharat Bachao' rally at Ramlila Maidan

The Congress on Saturday held a huge 'Bharat Bachao (Save India)' rally at New Delhi's Ramlila Maidan against the "anti-people policies" of the Centre. Several Congress leaders - including former party president Rahul Gandhi, interim president Sonia Gandhi, Priyanka Gandhi Vadra, and former Prime Minister Manmohan Singh addressed the people gathered at the rally.

The Congress leaders targeted the government on issues including the state of the economy, unemployment, and farmers' issues. The focus was on the recently passed Citizenship (Amendment) Bill, which the Congress has called 'communal' and 'divisive'.
