Industry must not violate the spirit of law, says Piyush Goyal

The government will take the "strictest of action" against businesses violating the law, said Commerce and Industry Minister Piyush Goyal on Friday, referring to controversies involving e-commerce and audit companies.

Goyal, in an interaction with members of Confederation of Indian Industry in Mumbai, said companies should respect the law and not try to circumvent it through loopholes.
