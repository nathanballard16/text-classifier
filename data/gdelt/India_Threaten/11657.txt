Kashmir will slip out of India’s hands: Senior Congress leader

NEW DELHI - Senior Congress leader in India has warned the BJP government to act thoughtfully otherwise Kashmir is slipping out of our hands.

Referring to an international media report, Digvijaya Singh said the government has burnt their hands in fire by abolishing Article 370.

Communist Party of India-Marxist leader Brinda Karat said that what the Indian government has done in Kashmir is nothing but the murder of democracy.

A political party MDMK, active in Tamil Nadu and other states of India has said that Kashmir will no more be under Indian occupation when New Delhi will celebrate its 100th year of freedom, the Radio Pakistan reported.

In an interview, party chief Vaiko said BJP has tried to smudge Kashmir in the mud.
