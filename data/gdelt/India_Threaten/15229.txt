Smuggler held with 12-kg ganja, accomplice escapes

Tribune News ServiceLudhiana, October 25The Focal Point police claim to have nabbed a notorious smuggler and recovered 12.1 kg of ganja from his possession. The suspect has been identified as Manoj Kumar Shah, a native of Bihar and at present residing at Janta Colony, and his accomplice as Lalita Devi, a resident of Gobindgarh. A naka was set up and the suspects were signalled to stop for checking. Brar said the smugglers brought ganja from Bihar-based smugglers and then sold it to their clients in Ludhiana district. “We will seek police remand of the suspects for their further interrogation,” said the SHO.
