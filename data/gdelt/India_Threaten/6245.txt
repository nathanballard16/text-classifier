Voting for 117 seats to be held on Tuesday in 3rd phase of LS polls

Tuesday's polling will cover all 26 seats from Gujarat, all 20 from Kerala, 14 seats each in Karnataka and Maharashtra, 10 in Uttar Pradesh, seven in Chhattisgarh, six in Odisha, five each in Bihar and West Bengal, four in Assam, two in Goa, and one each in Jammu and Kashmir, Tripura, and the Union Territories of Daman and Diu and Dadra and Nagar Haveli.
