Aamir Khan’s Laal Singh Chaddha to release on Christmas 2020

On his 54th birthday (March 14), Aamir Khan surprised one and all. He announced his next movie, Laal Singh Chaddha, which is an official adaptation of Tom Hanks’ Forrest Gump. We revealed to you how Aamir Khan will be producing the film in association with Viacom 18 Studios. We even mentioned that it was written by his Rang De Basanti co-star Atul Kulkarni. However, we didn’t know the release date of the Advait Chandan-directorial. Aamir had earlier told that they are still finalising the date, but we can now confirm it to you! Aamir Khan’s Laal Singh Chaddha is all set to release on Christmas 2020.

Well, it will surely be a Merry Christmas for all of us! In a press statement, the release date was announced. The film’s spokesperson revealed, “Viacom18 Studios is happy to announce Christmas 2020 as the release date of Aamir Khan Productions’ Laal Singh Chaddha, starring Aamir Khan, directed by Advait Chandan and written by Atul Kulkarni.” What do you guys have to say about this development? Apart from Aamir Khan, no one has been cast for the film yet. The makers are zeroing in on a few actors and actresses for the various parts in the Hollywood adaptation! Aamir Khan, on the other hand, is currently loosing weight for his part. And from what we hear, the movie will kickstart by late 2019.
