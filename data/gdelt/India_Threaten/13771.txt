Friendship call of woman: Police issues advisory

Hyderabad: Cyber Crime Police of Hyderabad issued an advisory warning the citizens of Hyderabad not to respond to the friendship call from an unknown woman.

Police received many complaints that a high profile woman is contacting innocent people from a posh locality and usurping large amounts of money and properties in the name of making friends.

The modus operandi of the woman is that she elicits relevant details from social media and contacts them offering her friendship.

She receives money under the pretext of getting lucrative jobs for the unemployed youth. She collects huge amounts from them and later disappears.
