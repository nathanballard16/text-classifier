Rape allegation: Binoy Kodiyeri appears before police in Mumbai

Mumbai: Binoy Kodiyeri, accused by a Bihar woman for rape, has appeared before the Oshiwara police in Mumbai on Monday. The sessions court in Mumbai had granted anticipatory bail the other day.

Bail was granted on certain conditions. One of the bail condition directed Binoy to appear before the police station every Monday for a month and to provide blood samples for DNA test if required.

Police will interrogate Binoy at the station. Binoy appeared before the police for the first time after the woman lodged the complaint against him. It is assumed that police might interrogate him over the documents she submitted as evidences against him.
