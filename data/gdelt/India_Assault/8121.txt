Middle-aged man kidnapped, threatened to kill for money in Pachpaoli

The abductors reportedly thrashed the man and threatened to kill him if he refused to pay them money. Nagpur: In an audacious act, a group of four persons on Sunday abducted a middle-aged man from his Sindhi Colony residence in a broad day light. Pachpaoli police have booked all the accused under Sections 365, 384, 452, 504, 506, 323, 34 of the IPC and began the probe. However, Ishwar somehow managed to flee from the hut and approached Pachpaoli police. The accused forced him to seat on their motorcycle and took him inside a hut at an isolated place, where all the accused then reportedly beat up Ishwar and threatened to kill him, if he refused to pay them money.
