Rajkot: Raped minor girl gets pregnant, man arrested

RAJKOT: A 14-year-old mentally challenged girl was allegedly raped several times over a period of time in Wankaner town of Morbi district . The matter came to light only on Wednesday when the girl’s parents came to know that she is pregnant , and lodged a police complaint.According to police, the girl was raped multiple times in the last few months by one Sagar Dangar, a resident of Rajkot. The accused has been arrested.Police inspector B D Parmar said, “The accused was living at his maternal uncle’s house in Wankaner where the girl would come to play. The accused allegedly took advantage of the girl’s disability for his heinous crime. We are waiting for the medical report and will take the accused’s statement.”
