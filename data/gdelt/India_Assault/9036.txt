Delhi: Ram Lal Anand College president found dead

New Delhi [India], May 30 (ANI): Utkarsh Sharma, president of Ram Lal Anand College, was found dead at his residence in RK Puram on Thursday, police said.

Sharma, a third-year student, was pursuing BA (Hons) political science.

A case has been registered and further investigation is underway. (ANI)
