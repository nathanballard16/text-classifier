Criminal who committed rape at gunpoint arrested

NEW DELHI: Delhi Police has arrested an accused who committed sexual assault at gunpoint in Rohini. Delhi Police arrested Aarish with a loaded double barrel pistol and averted a planned murder of the victim. Complainant reported that on July 26, 2019, her friend Aarish took her in a flat in Rohini on the pretext of his birthday celebrations where he and his friend sexually assaulted her at gunpoint. A case was registered in the appropriate sections of law and investigation was taken up. Aarish disclosed that he was away from his house for last one and half month as he was wanted in this case.
