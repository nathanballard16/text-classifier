5-year-old speech-impaired girl raped, neighbour held

LUDHIANA: Taking advantage of her speech impairment, a 23-year-old man allegedly raped a 5-year-old girl, who lived in the same building in the labour quarters.Police have arrested the accused.Police said the incident had occurred at 6.30 pm on Friday, when the girl had been playing with other kids outside her house. They thrashed him before handing him over to the police.Police said the accused knew that the girl was speech impaired and would not be able to reveal anything. Police said the accused was being interrogated.A case under Section 376 (rape) has been registered. As her parents started looking for her, they got to know that the 23-year-old man had allegedly taken the girl towards an under-construction building in the nearby locality where he worked as a painter.The parents and other residents of the area rushed towards the building and caught the accused sexually assaulting the girl. Thereafter, she had gone missing.
