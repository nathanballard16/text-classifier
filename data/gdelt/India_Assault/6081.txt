Hyderabad police rescues abducted child within four hours

Four hours of kidnap, the Hyderabad police rescued the eight-month-old child on Saturday here at Chaderghat.

According to the inspector Nagaraju and SI Rajasekhar Reddy, the accused kidnapped his niece on Friday night while her mother was asleep. M Rohini, mother of the 8-month-old is residing with her in-laws at Shankarnagar in Old Malakpet after her husband Aslam's death.

In the early hours on Saturday, Rohini found her child missing following which she lodged a complaint with Chaderghat police. The police who launched an investigation traced the child with Akram. A case was registered by the police.
