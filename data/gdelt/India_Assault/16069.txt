On cam: Parents, relatives thrash teacher who assaulted Class 12 student

SURAT: In a shocking incident, a class XII student was thrashed by a teacher at Ashadeep School in Simada area of Surat on Tuesday.Furious over the assault, the student's parents and friends went to the school next day and beat up the teacher. Both the incidents have been captured in CCTV cameras of the school.The student allegedly suffered minor injuries following the thrashing. While the teacher had to be hospitalised after the assault.On Tuesday, the class XII student was slapped and punched by the teacher Vipul Gajera in school. The school officials claimed that the student was shouting in toilet and when questioned, he abused the teacher. Later, Gajera thrashed him in lobby of the school."My son went to toilet during break and he was badly thrashed by the teacher for mischief done by some other students. The school did not inform us about the incident,” said Paresh Lakhani, father of the student.After reaching home, the student informed his parents who came to make representation of the incident on Wednesday morning and they attacked Gajera. The parents were accompanied by their friends and relatives. They pulled Gajera out of the class where he was teaching and attacked him with sticks."The school had already initiated the process to relieve Gajera of his services when parents attacked him. They used abusive language in presence of students as well. Gajera is currently admitted in hospital due to the attack,” said Mahesh Ramani, chairman of Ashadeep School.
