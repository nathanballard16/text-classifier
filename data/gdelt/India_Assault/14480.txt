Ghaziabad: Two persons, including a minor, have been arrested by Ghaziabad police for allegedly raping a 14-year-old girl in Masuri area of Ghaziabad. Cops said that the accused persons have also shot an obscene video and photos of the victim and continued raping her for nearly a month by threatening her to make them viral. According to police, the arrested accused has been identified as Avdhesh (19), a native of Indragarhi area in Masuri and a juvenile accused.
