Tamil Nadu: Former Tirunelveli mayor, two others hacked to death

Uma Maheshwari was hacked to death at her residence Uma Maheshwari was hacked to death at her residence

Former Tirunelveli Mayor Uma Maheshwari was hacked to death at her residence Tuesday. In Reddiyarpatti town near Tirunelveli district of Tamil Nadu, the former DMK Mayor, her husband, and the housemaid were hacked to death by unidentified persons.
