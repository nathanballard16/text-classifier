Panipat: Arjun Kapoor REVEALS after Kriti Sanon, his horse was his favorite co star; Read on

Arjun Kapoor and Kriti Sanon are coming together for the first time in Ashutosh Gowariker’s Panipat and going by the trailer, and songs, we are sure that Arjun and Kriti are going to set the screens on fire together. That said, as we speak, Arjun Kapoor and Kriti Sanon are busy with the promotions of the film and today, these two were papped at the Mumbai airport as they headed to Delhi for the promotions. As we all know, in the film, Arjun Kapoor plays the role of Sadashiv Rao Bhau, a Maratha warrior, and in order to prep up for the role, Arjun not just lost weight but also, learnt how to ride a horse.

During an interview, Arjun Kapoor recalled his horse-riding training for Panipat which, according to the actor led to inner peace. In the interview, Arjun recalled that he roughly trained for two-months for horse riding and those two months were the best thing to remember from the film's journey. Furthermore, Arjun admitted that more than training, he enjoyed shooting for the scenes. “I took a lot of efforts to get the skill correct. More than the training, the shooting experience was much more enriching,” said Arjun. But when Arjun was asked about his experience of working with Kriti Sanon, he said that after Kriti Sanon, his horse was the best co-star “I gelled so well with the animal and in return he also developed attachment towards me,” shared Arjun.

Talking about the film, Arjun Kapoor plays the role of Sadashiv Rao Bhau, a Maratha warrior who played a major role in the third battle of Panipat and Kriti Sanon plays the role of Parvati Bai. Besides Arjun Kapoor, Panipat stars Kriti Sanon, Sanjay Dutt, Mohnish Bahl and Padmini Kolhapuri in lead roles and the film is directed by Ashutosh Gowariker. Panipat will hit the screens on December 6, 2019.
