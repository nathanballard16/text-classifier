14-yr old girl kidnapped from Imambada

Nagpur: Unidentified person(s) abducted a 14-year old girl from Imambada area here on Friday. PSI A M Chechere is investigating the matter and launched a search to trace the girl as well as kidnapper(s). According a complaint lodged in this connection, the minor girl was lured by the unidentified person and taken her away between 1.30 pm and 7 pm on Friday. Imambada police registered a case under Section 363 of the IPC as the directives of Supreme Court.
