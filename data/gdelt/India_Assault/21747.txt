Police fire teargas shells after protest against CAA turns violent in Seelampur

NEW DELHI: A protest against the amended Citizenship Act turned violent in northeast Delhi's Seelampur area as demonstrators pelted stones at police personnel who fired tear gas shells to disperse the crowd.According to a senior police officer, the protest began around 12 pm and the people from the area gathered at Seelampur T-point.The protesters raised slogans against the Act, the National Register of Citizens (NRC) and the government.
