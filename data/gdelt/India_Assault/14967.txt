Inmate attacks Issewal gang-rape accused in jail

Ludhiana: An inmate attacked an accused in Issewal gang-rape case at Ludhiana Central Jail on Tuesday evening.

Baba hit undertrial Surmu, 30, on head with a utensil. “Surmu was going to his barrack when Baba attacked him without a reason. Surmu, who suffered minor injuries, was taken to civil hospital, where he was administered two stitches,” said jail assistant superintendent Jaswinder Singh.

The jail authorities have written to the police station to file a case against Baba, he added.
