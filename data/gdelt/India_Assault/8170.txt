Delhi: Man shot at by unidentified bike-borne assailant, hospitalised

New Delhi [India], May 15 (ANI): A man was injured after being shot at by an unidentified bike-borne assailant near Nawada metro station, the police said.

The victim, Bobby, was walking on the street on the night of May 14, when a person opened fire at him. The attacker, however, missed the aim and the bullet hit Bobby's elbow.

He was rushed to the Deen Dayal Upadhyaya hospital, where he is undergoing treatment.

The victim's wife has alleged that her brother, who was opposed to their marriage, is involved in the incident.

"I beg my parents to have mercy and let us live peacefully," she said.

A case has been registered and an investigation is underway. (ANI)
