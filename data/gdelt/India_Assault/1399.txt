Delhi: Doctor held for raping minor patient

NEW DELHI: A doctor has been arrested for allegedly raping a minor patient in outer Delhi’s Sultanpuri , police said on Friday. A case has been lodged at Sultanpuri police police station under IPC Section 376 (rape) and POCSO Act. The accused has been identified as Joginder Singh, a resident of Rohini Police said on Wednesday morning the girl went to the doctor’s clinic for treatment, when the crime took place. Cops are probing the case.
