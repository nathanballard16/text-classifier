In the same company his sister Beena Mulchandani, who is pregnant works in the HR department. After the matter was resolved, Beena reprimanded Yagnesh in front of the entire staff for his behaviour. On Friday, Beena intervened to resolve an ongoing dispute between two employees, identified as Yagnesh Nagar and Veerbahadur regarding a personal matter.
