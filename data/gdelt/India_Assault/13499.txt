Man murders neighbour s son makes ransom call days later held

New Delhi: A businessman's son, who was 8-year-old, was kidnapped and murdered by a 19-year-old man in Rohini's Aman Vihar area. Police said that after murder, the accused demanded a ransom of Rs 25 lakh from the deceased's family. Later he (father) received a phone call on his mobile from someone claiming that the body recovered was not of his son. "For two days the accused was searching for a SIM and later made the ransom call," police said. The accused thought that through this SIM he would demand ransom and no one will catch him," police said.
