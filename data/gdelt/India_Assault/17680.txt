Two persons injured in firework explosions in city

Kolkata: Two persons were injured in separate incidents of firework explosions in the city's Kalighat area, police said on Thursday. A 16-year-old boy from Kalighat area lost four of his fingers while an elderly man received head injuries in flower pot explosion on Tuesday night, the police said. The teenager was rushed to the SSKM hospital where necessary medications were provided to him. "The man was treated at SSKM Hospital and his condition is stable. On Sunday during Diwali celebrations, two persons including a five-year-old boy were killed in firework explosions in separate incidents in the city.
