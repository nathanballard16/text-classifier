On duty police officer assaulted at Hiland Park

Kolkata: Police personnel were assaulted during naka-checking on Sunday night in front of Hiland Park. According to the police, on Sunday night, Purba Jadavpur police station and Survey Park police station were conducting naka-checking in front of Hiland Park. At the naka-checking point, the bike was stopped by Sergeant Satyendra Kunwar and other police personnel of Survey Park police station. Seeing the superior officer getting assaulted, a constable and CV tried to resist. Later a case was initiated against him on charges of obstructing public servant in discharge of public functions, voluntarily causing hurt to deter public servant from his duty and assault or criminal force to deter public servant from discharge of his duty.
