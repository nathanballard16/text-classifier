Police probe torture of 2 Dalit youths in custody after NGO report

Jaipur: Jaipur police is investigating an alleged case of torture of two Dalit youths in police custody after a fact-finding report by NGO Human Rights Law Network (HRLN) brought it to their notice. According to the report, one Deepak and his brother from Govindgarh were severely beaten up after being framed in a case. He is also undergoing training at ITI.“We want that an FIR be lodged against the police officials who tortured the two youths. Following this, a team of three government doctors also conducted a medical examination of the two youths. Twenty-three-year-old Deepak was referred to SMS Hospital on Monday.“We have constituted a team to look into the matter.
