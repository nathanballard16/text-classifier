Delhi Twp. man found not guilty on three rape charges

CINCINNATI, OH (FOX19) - A Delhi Township man was found not guilty Tuesday on three rape charges.

Phillip Brown, 26, was arrested in February.

He had been accused of forcing a 17-year-old girl into three sexual encounters.

Brown spent several months in jail before being found not guilty.
