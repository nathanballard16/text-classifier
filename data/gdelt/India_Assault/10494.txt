Advisor Kumar, DGP visit family of slain police inspector

Srinagar

Advisor to Governor, K Vijay Kumar along with DGP, Dilbag Singh on Sunday visited the family of Police Inspector Arshad Khan who was killed in Anantnag attack on June 12.

During the visit, Advisor Kumar met the family members of slain Police Officer. He offered his condolences and expressed sympathy with them.

K Vijay Kumar prayed for eternal peace to the departed soul and expressed sympathy and solidarity with the family members of slain police officer.

The Advisor and other senior police officers remained with the family of a slain police officer for some time.

Police Inspector Arshad Khan was critically injured in the militant attack in Anantnag district on June 12 succumbed to his injuries on June 16 at All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS) in New Delhi.

IGP Kashmir S P Pani and SSP Srinagar Dr Haseeb Mughal were also present on the occasion.
