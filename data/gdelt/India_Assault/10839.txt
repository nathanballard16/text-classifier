Delhi: Two minor boys strangle friend after he accuses them of stealing Rs 10,000

The Delhi Police has detained two minor boys for murdering their friend. The accused were arrested from Anand Vihar area in Delhi.

They are accused of killing their friend, Nitin, by strangulating him. According to the police, the murder took place on June 27.

The motive behind the murder was a dispute between the trio. Rs 10,000 had gone missing from Nitin's belongings.

He suspected the minor boys of the theft which led to a fight between the three. The minor boys decided to murder Nitin and later strangled him.

According to police, Nitin was a known offender in the area.
