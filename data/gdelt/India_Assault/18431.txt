Taxi driver held for rape of mentally challenged woman

Kolkata: A taxi driver has been arrested on the city's southern outskirts in connection with the alleged abduction and rape of a shelter home inmate, a senior police officer said on Sunday. The woman also said that she was beaten up and pushed out of the vehicle near Sonarpur area at dawn. "The accused taxi driver confessed to the crime following a prolonged interrogation. His taxi has also been seized," the senior officer said. The woman is sexually active but there is no sign of gang rape," he said.
