Trio abducts ex-Army man at Majestic, robs him

HM Chaithanya Swamy ByExpress News ServiceBENGALURU: A 58-year-old retired Army man spent a nightmarish evening in Bengaluru on Christmas Day when a gang abducted and robbed him near Majestic. Three men followed him in an autorickshaw, stopped him, covered his face with a cloth and warned him not to raise an alarm. The trio, who demanded that he give them more money, stabbed him on his stomach and abdomen. After getting treated for his injuries, Machaiah returned to the lodge and filed a complaint with Cottonpet police. According to an investigating officer, the trio spoke to Machaiah in the local language.
