Alleged rapist of the minor girl arrested

Hyderabad: Mylardevpally Police arrested the alleged rapist who had raped a three year old girl earlier. He is stated to be the brother-in-law of the victim. The accused Raju had married the victim’s elder sister three years back.

Rubi Devi who knew Raju was instrumental in arranging his marriage with her elder daughter.

After picking up the girl from Auganwadi, Raju telephoned his mother-in-law that the girl is with him.

At 7.P.M. Raju left the girl crying and had fled away.



After receiving the complaint, police arrested Raju and sent the girl to Osmania General Hospital for medical examination.
