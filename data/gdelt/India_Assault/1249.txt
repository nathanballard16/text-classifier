Man held for raping, trying to kill child in east Delhi

NEW DELHI: A 30-year-old man was arrested for trying to kill a seven-year-old girl whom he had kidnapped and raped on the terrace of a Madhu Vihar house in east Delhi . Police suspect the man, identified as Manish, was involved in two other rape cases in Ghazipur and Kalyanpuri. Manish tried to smother her, but she somehow broke free and ran to another end of the terrace from where she called out to the neighbours. He lured her to the terrace of a house on the pretext of showing her something. He was then handed over to the police.A case of rape and under POCSO was registered.
