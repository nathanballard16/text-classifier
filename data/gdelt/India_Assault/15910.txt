Delhi: Man stabs wife to death, chops body into pieces

The incident happened in Prem Nagar area on Saturday. (Representational Image)

A man killed his wife in Delhi after he suspected her of having an affair.

The incident happened in Prem Nagar area on Saturday.

The accused first stabbed his wife to death and later chopped her body into pieces. He then dumped his wife's body, which he chopped into pieces, in a septic tank near their house.

After the act, the accused visited the nearby police station to confess his crime.

Meanwhile, the police have arrested the accused. He is in police custody now and the police are further investigating the case.
