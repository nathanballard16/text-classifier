Partly blind class XI student raped, one held

(The victim's identity has not been revealed to protect her privacy as per Supreme Court directives on cases related to sexual assault)

PALANPUR: A 22-year-old man was arrested for raping a 19-year-old partly blind girl late on Tuesday. The survivor class XI students with 90% vision studied in Mudetha village of Sabarkantha.The accused was identified as Shailesh Katara.According to police, when the girl was commuting by state transport bus from her village Dichniya on Monday, she was abducted by Katara, under the pretext of crossing the road. Katara took her behind the nearby petrol pump and allegedly raped her.After the girl reached home, she narrated the ordeal to her parents. Next day her parents lodged complaint with Idar police on Tuesday. Based on the complaint, police arrested Katara on the same day and charged him with rape and abduction.
