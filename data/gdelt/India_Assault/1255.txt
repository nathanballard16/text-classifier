Delhi: Gang of six who abducted truck driver held, Rs 6 lakhs recovered

Police have recovered cash amounting to six lakhs and stolen items from their possession. On the night of December 27, 2018, two persons dressed as police officers stopped a truck driver, identified as Vipin Kumar, near the Akshardham Flyover. On their instance, further recovery of Pan Masala cartons was made and Rs 6 lakhs cash received through the sale of stolen items was also recovered," a senior police officer said. Over the years they purchased property and cars by selling stolen items, police said. They later abandoned the driver along with the empty truck near Timarpur.
