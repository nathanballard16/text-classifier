Navy officer’s wife found dead

The husband told police he found her hanging and cut the bedsheet to bring her down (Representational) The husband told police he found her hanging and cut the bedsheet to bring her down (Representational)

A Navy officer’s wife was found dead at the couple’s house in South West Delhi’s Tarapore Enclave Wednesday. Police said Parinika (29) was found on the bed. “We found ligature marks on her neck. The couple got married in 2016,” said a senior officer. The husband told police he found her hanging and cut the bedsheet to bring her down. A probe has been ordered.
