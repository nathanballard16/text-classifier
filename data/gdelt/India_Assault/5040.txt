Madhopuri case: 2 accused arrested

Ludhiana: Division 3 police on Saturday arrested the two accused who had set a laundry man ablaze in Madhopuri. According to police, the accused are cousins of a safai karamchari woman who were suspecting the laundry man had teased their cousin. To take revenge, they planned to kill him.The arrested accused were Ramesh Kumar (28) of Mohalla Shivpuri, and Rajinder Kumar (32) of Kila Mohalla. The police have stated that during investigation, it had not been verified if the laundry man Binder Lal (45) had teased the woman. The officials also said the accused and the victim had never met before, and did not know each other.
