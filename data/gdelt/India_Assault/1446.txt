Woman allegedly set on fire by in-laws in Lucknow for dowry

Lucknow (Uttar Pradesh), Feb 03 (ANI): A woman allegedly set on fire by her in-laws in Uttar Pradesh’s Lucknow on Saturday. Victim’s family alleged that dowry is the main reason for this incident. Later, victim was admitted to hospital where her medical treatment is going on. According to the information given by doctors of Civil Hospital (Lucknow) woman is burnt around 70 percent. She got married one year ago and basically hails from Aliganj town of UP.
