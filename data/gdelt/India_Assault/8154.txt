Pune: Woman ‘bludgeons daughter to death’ with rock, arrested

A woman allegedly bludgeoned her 19-year-old daughter to death in a fit of rage over constant arguments at their home in Baramati town on Tuesday. According to the police, the woman had a heated argument with her daughter in the morning, during which she hit her with a large rock on the head. The woman was later arrested.
