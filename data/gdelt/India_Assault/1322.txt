Anti-bill activists thrash BJP’s Tinsukia chief

BJP’s Tinsukia district unit president Lakheswar Moran was assaulted by anti-Citizenship (Amendment) Bill protesters in Tinsukia town on Wednesday.Around 3pm, Moran arrived at a meeting organised by RSS-affiliated Lok Jagran Manch to ‘dispel myths’ about the Bill. On Wednesday, as Moran arrived, he was greeted by black flags. He was accosted by a group of agitators. He was slapped and beaten up while the police allegedly did nothing. Other BJP members were also assaulted. “The protesters rained kicks on Moran but police did not intervene,” an eyewitness said.
