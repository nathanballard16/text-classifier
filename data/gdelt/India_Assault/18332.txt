Mumbai: 25-year-old sailor found dead with bullet injury

The bullet which fatally injured him was shot from his own service rifle. (Representational)

A 25-year-old sailor of the Navy, identified as Akhilesh Yadav, was found dead with a bullet injury to his head on Thursday evening. Yadav, who was posted on security duty at INS Angre, was rushed to INHS Asvini, a military hospital, where he was declared dead on arrival, the Navy said in a statement.

The bullet which fatally injured him was shot from his own service rifle. Serving as a leading sailor in electrical (LEMP), Yadav had completed seven years of service and was from Uttar Pradesh.

Yadav allegedly committed suicide, sources said. The Navy and the Colaba police station are conducting the investigation. ENS
