sexual harassment in up balia: Teacher booked for sexual harassment, suspended in UP's Ballia district

VARANASI: A teacher of a government primary school in Baria area of UP's Ballia district was booked on Monday for allegedly sexually harassing the girls of Class IV and V. Bairia police station has registered an FIR against the accused, who has also been suspended .According to reports, primary school-2 teacher Arvind Kumar of Chakia - who is absconding - used to show obscene video clips to girls on his mobile phone and also indulge in indecent activities with them. Many girls refused to go to school due to this.When the villagers, after having come to know about this, went to school on Monday with their complaint, Arvind fled. Headmaster Kripa Shankar Chauhan informed the higher authorities about the matter.District Basic Shiksha Adhikari Santosh Kumar Rai told TOI that the teacher had been suspended with immediate effect after the charges against him were prima facie found to be true. Block education officer of Revati Awdhesh Kumar Rai has been asked to investigate the matter and submit a report in 15 days.Meanwhile, on the complaint of villagers, the police lodged an FIR against the teacher under section 354 (assault or criminal force to woman with intent to outrage her modesty) of the IPC and the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences (POCSO) Act.
