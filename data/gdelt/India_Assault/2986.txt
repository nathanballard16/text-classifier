Bhopal cops to rescue after youth stabs dad

BHOPAL: Dial 100 received a bizarre call from Jehangirabad locality on Wednesday afternoon when a youth stabbed his deaf-and-mute father in the neck as he felt that the father harboured ill-will against his wife.A bloody scuffle ensued and the youth also sustained injuries. Had the cops being late the father would have bled to death, say doctors. The police vehicle reached the scene within four minutes. He had stab injury in the neck and weirdly his trousers were burning. By that time the father was bleeding profusely.
