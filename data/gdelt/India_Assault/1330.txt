Delhi Elderly woman alleges torture at private shelter rescued

Delhi: Elderly woman alleges torture at private shelter, rescued31 Jan 2019, 12:35PM ISTSource: TNNA 50-year-old woman has alleged that she was tortured at a private shelter home in outer Delhi. Its officials even used to shove chilly powder in her private parts and pour boiling water on her whenever she protested or raised questions, the woman claimed in a police complaint. The complaint is being investigated
