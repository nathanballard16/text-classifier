Teacher’s custodial killing exposes Kashmiris’ helplessness: Mirwaiz

Srinagar, March 19, 2019 (PPI-OT): In occupied Kashmir, Hurriyat forum Chairman, Mirwaiz Umar Farooq, today, said that the custodial killing of a school teacher has once again exposed the helplessness of the people of the territory.

Mirwaiz Umar Farooq taking to the twitter said, “Distraught at the brutal custodial killing of young Rizwan Asad of Awantipora. The brutal killing has once again exposed the helplessness, vulnerability and insecurity of the lives of Kashmiris….”

The school teacher identified as Rizwan Asad, a resident of Awantipora in Pulwama, was tortured to death in India’s National Investigation Agency custody at infamous Cargo camp in Srinagar during the intervening night of Monday and Tuesday.
