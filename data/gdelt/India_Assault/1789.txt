New Delhi: In an appalling case reported from the national capital, a 10-year-old girl was allegedly raped by a sweeper inside a civic body-run school in Shahdara district. However, the police have arrested the accused on Saturday.

According to news agency PTI, the complainant mentioned that she was leaving the school on February 5 when the accused asked her to sit at a place. He then moved her to another seat, then forced her to a wall and raped her.

Meanwhile, the accused then threatened the girl of dire consequences if she reported the matter to anyone.

PTI report claimed that the victim later narrated the incident to her relatives after which they approached the police on Saturday and filed a complaint. However, Deputy Commissioner of Police (Shahdara) Meghna Yadav told the agency that a case was registered under the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences (POSCO) Act and other relevant sections of the Indian Penal Code.

The DCP further added that the 38-year-old accused has been working as a sweeper in the EDMC-run school for the last three-to-four months. The victim's parents live in Bihar and she stays with her relatives in Shahdara.
