Anti-CAA protests: Bengal IPS officer hurt in bomb attack

Kolkata, Dec 17 (IANS) An IPS officer and two other police personnel were injured when bombs were hurled at them during an anti-CAA procession in Sankrail in West Bengal's Howrah district on Tuesday.

Deputy Commissioner (headquarters) of Howrah police, Ajit Singh Yadav, was among the injured, officials said.
