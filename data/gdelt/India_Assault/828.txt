Mumbai: Youth arrested for raping woman for four months

Crime branch unit 12 has arrested a man for raping a woman with learning disabilities for four months. Considering gravity of the crime, the case was simultaneously investigated by Crime Branch Unit 12. Four police teams were formed by crime branch officials and each investigated a different angle of the crime. The incident came to light when the woman complained of intense pain in her abdominal area. On Thursday, police laid a trap in Virar and arrested the youth and booked him for rape (section 376) of the Indian Penal Code, said a police officer.
