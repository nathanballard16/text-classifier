Ludhiana: Issewal gang rape case, next hearing on December 12

(The victim's identity has not been revealed to protect her privacy as per Supreme court directives on cases related to sexual assault)

LUDHIANA: The defence counsels concluded the cross examination of the friend of the Issewal gangrape victim during the hearing of the case in a Ludhiana court on Tuesday.Special public prosecutor for the state B D Gupta said that survivor’s friend has fully supported the prosecution’s case during cross examination. He added that they will get more prosecution witnesses examined in the case in the coming hearings of the case. The counsel said that the prosecution has listed fifty four witnesses but not all will be examined.On February 9, a group of miscreants had allegedly gang raped a 20-year-old student after holding her and her friend hostage in Issewal area.Police had later arrested six accused in the case, who were identified by the survivor. The police filed chargesheet in the case on April 4. The case will now come up for hearing in the court of Additional Session Judge Rashmi Sharma on December 12.
