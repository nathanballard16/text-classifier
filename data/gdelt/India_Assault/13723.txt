5-Year-Old Girl In Telangana Raped By 20-Year-Old Neighbor

Telangana, India: During a press release, the police said that a 20-year-old man raped in Vikarabad District raped his 5-year-old girl neighbor.

The police said that the 20-year-old neighbor brought the 5-year-old girl to their house and raped here there.

Reports suggest that the incident happened on August 9, 2019.

The police arrested the man after the parents and other family members of the girl filed a complaint against the 20-year-old neighbor.

The man is currently facing charges under the Indian Penal Case and under the relevant sections of the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences Act.

The police are also trying to find out of anyone else was involve in the case.
