The ensemble cast of Mission Mangal featuring Akshay Kumar, Vidya Balan, Kirti Kulhari, Sonakshi Sinha, Taapsee Pannu, and Nithya Menen kickstarted the promotions in full bloom. Seen here, Akshay Kumar who is playing the pivotal role of Rakesh Dhawan in the film was captured by the photographer as he promoted the historical drama in Mumbai's Juhu, earlier today. Looking handsome as usual an all-black outfit, the story idea of Mission Mangal was mentioned to the actor while he was working for Pad Man.
