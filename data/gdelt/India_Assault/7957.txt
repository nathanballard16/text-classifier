Mumbai: Man arrested for rape of minor for 3 years

The incident came to light after an NGO alerted the police about the matter. A daily-wager has been arrested for allegedly raping a minor girl, living under his guardianship, over the last three years in Mahim, police said on Saturday. Earlier this week, an NGO working with children received information about the case and alerted the police. “The girl doesn’t have a father and her mother had left her to live with the accused a few years ago.
