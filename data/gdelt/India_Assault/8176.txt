Lucknow: 40-year-old takes minor to a secluded spot and rapes her; arrested

The accused allegedly took the minor to a secluded area and raped her (Representative Image). | Photo Credit: Getty Images

Lucknow: Crime against women has been running rampant all across the country. Another incident of rape surfaced from Uttar Pradesh when a man allegedly raped a minor. The 40-year-old accused allegedly raped the minor when he had come to the latter's village to attend a wedding.

The victim was sent for a medical examination. The police registered an FIR against the accused on the basis of the statement given by her kin. The incident took place in Moradabad.

The news agency, ANI reported that the accused had come to the victim's village to attend a wedding. The accused allegedly took the minor to a secluded area and raped her.

The accused was booked under relevant sections of the Indian Penal Code (IPC) and the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences (POCSO) Act.

This incident came to light just a day after another minor was raped in Uttar Pradesh. The victim was found dead at the residence of the accused. The incident took place in the Mahewa Ganj area of Bareilly. The accused was identified as Rudal.

The deceased had gone to watch a movie with her family but she decided to return home after she started feeling drowsy. The minor did not reach home. When the body was found, the locals beat up the accused and then handed him over to the police. The accused was booked under relevant sections of the IPC and the POCSO Act.
