Rag-picker found murdered in Khamla, cops hunt accused

The motive behind the murder of the rag-picker identified as Dilip Dhanvijay (45) could not be ascertained immediately. Nagpur: A middle-aged rag-picker was stoned to death by unidentified person(s) in Sonegaon police jurisdiction on Sunday night. The body of Dilip Dhanvijay was found lying at railway property near Vidya Vikas Public School. Dilip was found murdered with his head smashed by a heavy stone in Khamla area. It is learnt that the murder could be fallout of a tiff between the deceased and his friends.
