Gang involved in 25 crimes busted

Vadodara: The detection of crime branch (DCB) sleuths busted a gang of thieves that had been involved in 25 different crimes in various districts across the state. The gang, which also pickpocketed, committed crimes in Vadodara, Ahmedabad, Surat, Nadiad, Kheda and Anand. The police said that the gang used to steal passengers who boarded their auto. The Devipujak gang was nabbed when four of its members were travelling in an auto-rickshaw on their way to commit another crime. “One member drove the auto, while the other three would sit behind as passengers.
