Shopkeeper rapes 15-yr-old neighbour

When the girl did not reach home even after an hour, her mother started looking for her. On enquiring from neighbours, she learnt that the shopkeeper was inside. Ludhiana: The police on Tuesday night booked a man for raping his 15-year-old neighbour when she went to his shop for a mobile recharge.It has been alleged that the accused pulled down the shutter of his shop to rape the girl. When she reached near the shop, she found the shutter down. The accused is yet to be arrested.
