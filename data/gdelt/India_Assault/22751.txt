Professor ‘sexually assaults’ engineering student in Telangana

Hyderabad, December 25

A second-year engineering student of a private college here was allegedly sexually assaulted by an assistant professor at the institute, police said on Wednesday.

The 19-year-old woman, in her complaint, said the professor called her to the lab on Monday and bolted its door before committing the crime, they said.

A case under relevant sections of the IPC was registered against the accused, aged around 30, and he has been taken into custody. PTI
