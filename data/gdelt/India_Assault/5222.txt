Hyderabad man charred to death in Ethiopia car fire

A man hailing from Hyderabad charred to death in a car fire along with four others in Ethiopia on Wednesday, according to the early reports coming in.

The car in which the victim was travelling in along with his friends has been set on fire by unidentified assailants. He was identified as Pavani Venata Shasidhar of Ashok Nagar in Musheerabad.

Further details are awaited.
