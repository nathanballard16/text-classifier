Teenage girl abducted from school in Kalamna

Whether the teenage girl was abducted or herself went somewhere due to adverse result could not be verified. The teenage girl left home around 9 am on Thursday and went to her school for collecting result. Nagpur: A 16-year old girl who had gone to school for collecting result has reportedly been kidnapped in Kalamna police jurisdiction on Thursday morning. Cops have launched a search to trace the minor girl. However, she did not return home and has gone missing since then.
