Bandh observed in Kalmeshwar over minor girl's murder

Residents of Kalmeshwar in Maharashtra's Nagpur district on Monday organised rallies and called for a bandh to protest the brutal murder of a five-year-old girl in Linga village.

Main markets remained closed since morning after people took to streets to condemn the incident and demanded speedy justice.

The body of the five-year-old daughter of a farm labourer with her head smashed was found on an agriculture farm in the village on Sunday.

The deceased was missing since December 6 afternoon.

Police have arrested a suspect in connection with the murder.

Superintendent of Police, Nagpur Rural, Rakesh Ola confirmed the arrest with PTI, but refused to elaborate.

Police have not ruled out sexual assault angle.
