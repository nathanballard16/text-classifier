Shocking: Karnataka Cong leader calls for PM Modi's 'assassination', BJP demands Rahul take action

Karnataka’s BJP unit demanded action agains Congress leader Belur Gopalkrishna for asking for PM Modi’s ‘assassination’.

BJP Karnataka’s Twitter handle tweeted an undated video of Belur Gopalkrishna in Kannada in which he makes deeply unparliamentary remarks against the democratically elected head of the state of India.

In the video, Gopalkrishna can be heard saying in Kannada, ‘if anyone needs to be killed it’s Modi’.
