2 minors among 3 gang-raped in Alwar district in 24 hours

JAIPUR/ALWAR: Three different cases of gang-rape, including two of minors , were registered in Alwar district in the span of 24 hours (Saturday-Sunday).A 15-year-old was allegedly abducted by three youths on Saturday night in Alwar’s Thanagazi and gangraped in a forested area. A case under IPC section 376 D (gang-rape) and under the Pocso Act was registered. Preliminary investigations and her medical examination confirmed rape “The villagers caught the accused and handed them over to the police,” said Vishnaram Bishnoi, ASP Alwar (Rural). Two of the accused are in their 20s, while one of them is a minor.
