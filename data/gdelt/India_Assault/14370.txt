Clash between TMC factions at North 24 Parganas

Kolkata: A clash broke out between two factions within Trinamool Congress at Sashan in North 24-Parganas on Saturday night. Both the group members allegedly hurled bombs at their opponents. Some police personnel were also attacked allegedly by the group members. Some of group members allegedly hurled a bomb at the police vehicle. District Trinamool Congress leaders said action would be taken if any Trinamool Congress activists were found to be involved in the incident.
