Hyderabad: Man stabbed to death by friend

Hyderabad: On Tuesday at Sanathnagar, a glass factory worker was stabbed to death by his friend over money.

According to the TOI reports, the deceased, identified as Sanjay Kumar had recently joined Bhongir glass factory, but quit the job and decided to return Gujarat.

He had borrowed a sum of Rs 10,000 from Nagesh, his friend.

On Tuesday, Sanjay went to Nagesh, as his mother informed him that Nagesh’s brother came to meet him. An argument raised between them over the money and during that argument, Nagesh stabbed Sanjay in the stomach with a knife.

ACP of Balanagar, T Govardhan said, Sanjay succumbed to injuries in hospital and Nagesh has reportedly been taken into custody.
