Rajkot: Man who raped minor, held after 3 years

(The victim's identity has not been revealed to protect her privacy as per Supreme Court directives on cases related to sexual assault)

RAJKOT: A man was arrested on Friday for allegedly raping a 20-year-old woman several times when she was minor, in Khambhalia of Devbhoomi Dwarka district and Jamnagar Police said the accused, Anil Rajgor (24), had allegedly raped the woman several times when she was 17 years old by threatening making viral her nude photographs, which he had allegedly clicked while she was bathing.“Rajgor raped the survivor in Khambhalia and Jamnagar. Three years later, after the survivor got married to another man, Rajgor started pressurizing her to walk out on her husband and come to live with him. When the survivor refused, Rajgor obtained her husband’s number and sent him her nude photographs on WhatsApp from an unknown number,” said P A Dekivadia, inspector with Khambhalia police station.The survivor narrated her past to her husband, after which her husband took her to Khambhalia police station and lodged the complaint on Thursday night.The police arrested Rajgor after booking him under Protection of Children from Sexual Offences (POCSO) Act, section 376 of Indian Penal Code (IPC) dealing with rape and under IT Act.
