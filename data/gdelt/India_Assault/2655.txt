Pulwama Attack: Total Dhamaal will not release in Pakistan, says Ajay Devgn

Mumbai:

Ajay Devgn on Monday announced that his upcoming film Total Dhamaal will not be releasing in Pakistan. The decision comes in the wake of a terror attack on a CRPF convoy in Kashmir that claimed the lives of at least 40 soldiers. "In light of the current situation the team of Total Dhamaal has decided to not release the film in Pakistan," Devgn tweeted.
