Vadodara: The J P Road police arrested three persons for circulating obscene pictures of the woman who has filed a complaint of rape against Dr Yashesh Dalal, a leading neurosurgeon in the city. The police also recorded the woman’s statement under 164 CrPC on Wednesday.The woman had filed a complaint of rape and criminal intimidation against Dr Dalal two days ago and told the police the accused threatened her often. The complainant used to work at Dalal’s hospital and, according to her complaint, he took her to several places under the pretext of attending conferences and raped her. The cops have arrested all the three persons and is questioning them.
