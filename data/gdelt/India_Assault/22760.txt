9 students injured after school bus hit sugarcane trolley in Pune

Pune (Maharashtra) [India], Dec 25 (ANI): At least nine students were injured after a bus hit a sugarcane trolly from behind in the wee hours of Wednesday on the Mumbai-Pune National Highway near the Telgoan Khind area.

Forty-four students were on the bus when it hit the trolley from behind.

The injured students were taken to the nearest hospital by the police.

Further details are awaited. (ANI)
