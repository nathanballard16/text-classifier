No action in snatching bid

Amritsar, September 1Even after five days, the rural police are yet to arrest those involved in a snatching bid, in which two government school teachers were seriously injured. Victims Vinita Sharma and Gupreet Kaur lost their purse to three bikers. Gurpreet Kaur sustained severe injuries. School Principal Kulwinder Singh said the police were approached, but no case was registered. SSP Vikram Jeet Duggal said if the cops were found at fault, action would be taken against them.
