4 arrested for assault on woman family members

Kolkata: Four persons were arrested for assaulting a woman and stealing a gold chain on Monday night. Meanwhile another car overtook them. A woman sitting on the front sit beside the driver was dragged from the car by the accused persons. The woman alleged that while assaulting them, one of the accused persons allegedly snatched a gold chain which she was wearing on her neck. Later, he told the names of other three accused persons who were subsequently arrested on Monday night.
