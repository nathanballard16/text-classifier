Two arrested for abducting 10yearold boy

Surat: Police arrested two youths for abducting a 10-year-old boy from Bhatar area of the city on Thursday. Kiran Patil, 20, and Ajay Gawai, 18, were arrested for allegedly abducting Yash, son of contractor Vilas Kharate, a resident of Vesu. However, the boy didn’t reach his mother’s kiosk and went missing. The boy’s parents lodged a complaint at Khatodara police station when they didn’t find him.Yash had met Kiran and Ajay while going to his mother’s kiosk. The accused took the motorcycle on Surat Dhuliya highway.
