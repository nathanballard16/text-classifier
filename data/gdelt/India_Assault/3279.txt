Gold heist: 3 accused nabbed

According to Dinesh Singh, SP, STF, "Sushil is a hardened criminal and was the main accused in the infamous kidnapping of Dr Srikant Gaur in Delhi in 2017. We have also recovered the stolen bike and a car that was used in the robbery apart from 4 kg of gold jewellery. Investigations are still on and we will soon recover the remaining quantity of gold as well. It was revealed that one of the accused, Bhagat Singh, was resident of this very locality." "Asked about the total number of criminals involved in the heist, the ADG said, "Investigations are still on."
