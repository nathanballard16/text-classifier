Class-10 girl raped by minor in Kothrud

Pune: In a shocking incident, a class 10 girl was allegedly raped by a minor boy. The incident took place in Pune's Kothrud.



A complaint was filed by the victim's mother, following which, police filed a case against the minor boy.



"The accused stopped the victim while she was returning home from class on February 23. Later, he snatched her bag and forcibly made her sit on a bike. He then took her to a terrace of building and committed the crime," said police.



The girl narrated the incident to her mother after reaching home. Police have launched the investigation to nab the accused.
