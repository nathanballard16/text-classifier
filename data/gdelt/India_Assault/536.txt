Four suspects being questioned in minor girl rape case

Surat: Four suspects were nabbed by police and being questioned in the case of sexual assault on a three-year-old girl at Hazira near here on Friday.A police officer said, “We are cross verifying the statements of the suspects. A 19-year-old youth brought the girl to her house, saying that someone had handed over her to him. We are also recording statements of residents of Hazira where the girl was found abandoned in the bushes after rape by an unidentified man on Thursday evening. Forensic Science Laboratory team and dog squad visited the spot on Friday. We have got CCTV footage from one place and are examining it.
