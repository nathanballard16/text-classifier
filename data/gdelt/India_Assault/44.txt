Minor gang rape 2 more suspects evading arrest

PATNA: Police are yet to arrest two more suspects in connection with a minor girl’s gang rape and murder. One Anil Kumar (45) was also arrested the next day after the body’s recovery, Bairgania SHO Sujeet Kumar said on Monday. But the minor was then gang-raped and killed by Anil and his friends and her body was dumped in bushes,” the SHO added. He, however, had promised the girl that his neighbour Anil will give her a smartphone. Anil then asked the boy to go home as it was getting late and assured him that he would drop the girl at her home.
