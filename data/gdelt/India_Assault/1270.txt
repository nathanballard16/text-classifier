Hope Hospital doctor, 2 others booked for beating finance co’s manager

Nagpur: A doctor from Hope Hospital Nagpur first defaulted on a loan of Rs 60 lakh taken from a finance company and later thrashed the company’s manager with the help of two of his accomplices. Police sources said Murli had called Raghavendra Jadon, who works as a manager at a finance company, to collect the pending money to the company. On Tuesday, Murli called Jadon to come to the hospital to collect the money, where a quarrel broke out between the two. Pachpaoli police have the booked the doctor, identified as BK Murli, 49, for assault, criminal intimidation and other charges under section of Indian Penal Code (IPC). Murli had taken a loan for around Rs 60 lakh a few months back.
