Police Rescue Girl From Kidnapper In Hyderabad

Hyderabad: Police came to the rescue of a young girl who was kidnapped on Saturday at LB Nagar in Hyderabad.

The police in Nuthankal mandal of Suryapet district sprung into action following the complaint lodged by the girl's parents and rescued the girl a day after the kidnap.

The girl, Keerthana was playing in front of her residence at Dirshanapalli crossroads when she was lured by a man who offered her biscuits. Finding their daughter missing, the parents immediately approached the police and filed a complaint.

The police arrested the accused and reunited the child with her parents on Sunday.
