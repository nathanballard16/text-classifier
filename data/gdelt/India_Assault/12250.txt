2.5 yr old girl child found dead after being abducted in Pune

Pune: In a shocking incident, a two and a half years old girl was found dead after being abducted from her house in Pune’s Sanghavi area on Tuesday.

“A girl who was the daughter of a was abducted from her house late night on Monday from their house based in a camp and was found dead in the morning in a canal,” Prabhakar Shinde, senior police inspector of Sanghavi police station, Pimpri-Chinchwad said.

The family was brought from Chhatisgarh to work for a construction site where they were allotted a construction site. Police have detained four accused on the basis of suspicion and investigating the matter further. The FIR is yet to be registered since post mortem of the girl is still pending,” he added.

FIR registration is in process.
