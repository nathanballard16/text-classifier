Two dead after police opens lathicharge on protesters at Vedanta Odisha plant

Two dead after police opens lathicharge on protesters at Vedanta Odisha plant I Photo credit: ANI | Photo Credit: ANI

New Delhi: Two dead reportedly died while several others were injured after police lathicharged protesters at Vedanta Aluminium Plant earlier Monday. The protesters were demanding employment. Among the dead were a protester and one Odisha Industrial Security Force (OISF) personnel who succumbed to injuries later.
