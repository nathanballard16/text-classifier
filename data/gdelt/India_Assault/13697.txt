House of man accused of murder set ablaze in Rajkot

RAJKOT: Friends of a murder victim set ablaze house of the murder accused in Chunaravad locality of Rajkot on Sunday night.Police said the accused, identified as Gopal Dhuldhoya, Kamlesh Solanki, Jatin Beladha and an unidentified aide of theirs, set afire the house of Rajesh Chawda, who is in jail for the murder of Satish Bharwad.“Chawda had stabbed Bharwad to death over a financial dispute. The accused were friends of Bharwad and had decided to avenge his murder by setting Chawda’s house ablaze,” said an officer of Thorala police station.Meanwhile, the police have launched a manhunt for the accused, who are on the run.
