UP: Married couple found dead at residence in Lucknow

Lucknow (Uttar Pradesh) [India], Dec 13 (ANI): A married couple was found dead at their residence in Lucknow's Sadatganj area on Thursday, police said.

"Police got the information that a couple was found dead in Sadatganj. We found some gloves and tea cups inside the house. We are investigating the case," SP Kalanidhi Naithani told reporters here.

The deceased identified as Ilal Ahmed and his wife hail from Kanpur and were living in the state capital on rent for the past many years. (ANI)
