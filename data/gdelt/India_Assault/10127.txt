Police files FIR after woman raises allegation against Binoy Kodiyeri

Mumbai : A sexual assault and rape case was filed against Binoy Vinodini Balakrishnan, the eldest son of CPM State Secretary Kodiyeri Balakrishnan.

A Bihari woman has filed a complaint of rape and false promise of marriage. According to the complaint, she was sexually assaulted for over eight years and also added that they have an 8-year-old child from the relationship.

She sated that Binoy promised her to marry her. A flat was rented in Andheri and Binoy payed for all their expenses. But later she found that Binoy was married and settled with a family in Kerala. However, Binoy blatantly denied the allegations and termed it as a case of blackmailing.
