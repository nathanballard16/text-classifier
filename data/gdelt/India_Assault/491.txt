Three-year-old girl sexually assaulted, abandoned in Hazira

Surat: A three-year-old girl was sexually assaulted by an unidentified person here on Thursday. Meanwhile, the girl was brought home by the youth in blood-stained clothes at around 5.30pm.“We are questioning the youth who had brought the girl home. Doctors have found blood and also accused’s sperm on the minor’s body, who has suffered serious injuries on her private parts.The minor girl was found a kilometre away from her house in Hazira village. The family then contacted 108 and immediately shifted her to hospital.The girl had gone missing at around 3pm while playing outside her house. The profusely bleeding girl was found abandoned in the bushes.
