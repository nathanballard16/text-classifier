Ahmedabad: Man abducted, beaten up by loan sharks over Rs 30,000

A man was abducted from near Dharnidhar area and beaten up by three men who are supposed to be loan sharks. The incident took place on Thursday night at around 8.30 pm near Dharnidhar Derasar crossroad. On Thursday night, when Vora was near Dharnidhar Derasar crossroad, Rana came to the spot with two others and abducted Vora in his car. He stated in his complaint that around four months ago, he had borrowed Rs 30,000 from the accused identified as Mahesh Rana. A complaint was lodged with the Paldi police against the three accused by Vora and further investigation is on.
