Dispute over money Man abducted 3 arrested

Kolkata: Three persons were arrested by officials of the Ekbalpore police station for allegedly abducting a man on Thursday. The police said the abduction was an outcome of a dispute between the victim and the accused persons over money. According to the police, on November 8, five persons called the victim, identified as Avijit Barua, at Mominpur petrol pump area in Ekbalpore to discuss about the dispute. One of accused sat on the pillion seat of a bike, which was being driven one of Avijit's friend. In the middle of the road, the man, who was on the bike with Avijit's friend, asked him to stop the bike and got down.
