Naked body of 8-year-old girl found in Haryana’s Mewat; rape suspected

Tribune News Service

Mewat, December 27

In a shocking incident, the naked body of an eight-year-old girl was found in Mewat’s Ferozepur Jhirka on Friday.

The girl was found hanging from a tree in the foothills of the Haryana district.

Police suspect the girl was raped before being done to death.

There was tension in the area following the incident.
