Two civilians injured in cross LoC firing in Rajouri

Srinagar

Two civilians were injured after armies of India and Pakistan resorted to cross border shelling and firing on the Line of Control (LoC) in Rajouri district.

“Two civilians were injured in the ceasefire violation by Pakistan in Nowshera sector of Rajouri district, yesterday late night,” news agency ANI wrote on Twitter.

An official said that the injured couple identified as Sanjeev Kumar and his wife Rita Kumari have been shifted to hospital for treatment.
