Rajkot: Couple attacked, robbed

RAJKOT: Four robbers assaulted a couple in a farm on the outskirts of Rewad village of Una taluka in Gir Somnath district and looted cash and jewellery worth Rs 1.41 lakh on Tuesday night.Police said the incident occurred when Bhagwan Ahir and his wife Mali Ahir were asleep in their farm.“The robbers, who are yet to be identified, attacked the couple, tied their hands and feet and beat them up. Then they robbed the owman of her gold and silver jewellery worth Rs 81,500 while stole Rs 60,000 in cash from the man,” said an official from Una police station.“We have booked an offence against the unidentified persons for the loot and started investigation,” added the official.
