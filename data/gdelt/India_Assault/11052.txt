DCW notice to police in domestic help assault case

Tribune News ServiceNew Delhi, July 2The Delhi Commission for Women (DCW) yesterday issued a notice to the Deputy Commissioner of Police seeking action in a case of abuse of a 12-year-old domestic help. A DCW member said the girl was allegedly beaten up and confined for three months by her employer. On June 6, the girl attempted to commit suicide after which the employer took her back to the placement agency and left her there. The incident came to light on June 23 when a person saw the plight of the girl and informed the Delhi Commission for Women. The DCW chief Swati Maliwal in the notice to the DCP (Northwest Delhi) sought a copy of the FIR, action taken in the case, details of the arrest and any other relevant orders in the case, by July 4.
