Mumbai: College student on run after assaulting parents

A college student is on the run after attacking and seriously injuring his parents at their home in Nallasopara on Tuesday morning. The police said Janmesh, a third-year student at a college in the area, had begun investing in stocks last year against his parents’ wishes. On Monday, Janmesh lost a lot of money for which he was scolded by his parents and warned to stop investing further. The police said the accused’s parents had confronted him after he lost a large amount of money he had invested in stocks.
