Dehradun: In yet another rape case reported from Nainital district of Uttarakhand, a 12-year-old girl was found pregnant by doctors and the survivor accused her uncle of rape.

According to a report in the Hindustan Times, Malital police in-charge, Vipin Chandra Pant said that the survivor's father mentioned that she complained of pain in her abdomen. She was immediately rushed to a district government hospital, where the doctors found she was two-and-a-half-month pregnant.

Meanwhile, the family questioned the survivor and she revealed that she was raped by her uncle from Uttar Pradesh. The hospital authorities have a memo regarding the case and accordingly the police officials have started an investigation in the matter.

The police official told the HT that the survivor's family was originally from Uttar Pradesh but her father worked here in Nanital. They often used to visit their hometown, he added.

However, the police have filed a case under section 376 (rape) and various sections of the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences (POCS0) Act. Also, a team has been sent to Uttar Pradesh to nab the accused.
