Hyderabad: Depressed man burns himself to death

Hyderabad: Depressed over losses incurred in business, Hyderabad man set himself ablaze outside his house.

The incident is reported from Sanathnagar’s Snehapuri where Venkatesh Gupta burned to death after he came out from his house doused himself in petrol and later set himself ablaze on road.

Reports stated Gupta was depressed over the losses incurred in his businesses and committed suicide.

Further details are awaited.
