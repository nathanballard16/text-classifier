Minor gangraped near ESI Hospital

New Delhi: A 16-year-old girl was allegedly raped by two men on a vacant plot near ESI Hospital in West Delhi's Moti Nagar on Tuesday, police said. The accused, identified as Ravi (25) and Ankit (24), residents of Basaidarapur, have been arrested, they said. At around 3:30am, the security supervisor of the ESI Hospital informed police and said the woman was left alone near the hospital, they said. The accused approached the girl in a park and offered her a job with a better salary and accommodation. They then took her on the plot located behind the hospital and raped her, said a senior police officer.
