Gazetted audit officer in CAG duped of Rs 2 lakh one held

New Delhi: The Shahdara district police on Friday claimed to have solved the cheating case with Gazetted Audit Officer in CAG of India. Police identified the accused as Pradeep Mandal, a native of Jharkhand. According to police the complainant, Gazetted Audit Officer in CAG of India, alleged that he received a call from mobile number who told him that he is speaking from DRM office railway account department and asked for account details, PIN and OTP. As per the details of accused person bank account, he withdraw more than Rs 59 lakh within a month of July. Before committing the crime, they study the background of the target person through social media platform.
