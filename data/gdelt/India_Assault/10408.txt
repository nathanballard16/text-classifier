Man arrested for raping woman

SURAT: A married woman registered a complaint of rape and blackmail against a man at Amroli police station here on Thursday. Accused Latchubhai Bagada was arrested on Friday.Police said the woman came in contact with Bagada after she returned to her parents’ house after a tiff with her husband. He befriended her by promising to marry her and repeatedly raped her for over two years, blackmailing her with some obscene pictures that he had clicked of her. He threatened to make the pictures go viral on social media and to kill her parents if she dare speak or resisted him.When the woman became pregnant, he forced her to have an abortion . TNN
