Elderly person, his son stabbed to death

RAJKOT: A 75-year-old man and his 48-year-old son were stabbed to death by an assailant near Nagina mosque in Sandhivad locality of Botad on Sunday evening.The deceased were identified as Nura Jokhia and his son Firoz Jokhia and the accused as Javed Jakhada.“The murder was the result of a long pending land dispute between Firoz and Jakhada. In the attack, the daughter of Firoz, Salma (18), was also stabbed. She has been admitted to Sir T Hospital in Bhavnagar ,” said Shaktisinh Zala, inspector with Botad city police station.Meanwhile, the police have launched a manhunt for Jakhada who went absconding after committing the crime.
