Mumbai: 65-year-old man gets 10 years rigorous imprisonment for raping 5-year-old girl

The crime came to light after the victim told her aunt about pain in her private parts. | Photo Credit: Getty Images

Mumbai: A Mumbai court has sentenced a 62-year-old man to 10 years rigorous imprisonment for raping a 5-year-old girl in Kurla area in 2018. The convict Banarasi Gupta has been found guilty under Protection of Children from Sexual Offences (POCSO) Act and section 307 of the Indian Penal Code.

According to a report in Times of India, the incident occurred in 2018 when the victim and her brother were at their aunt's house in Nehru Nagar in Kurla. There the accused, who is a neighbour, took the girl to his home on the pretext of giving her ice cream and raped her.

The crime came to light after the victim told her aunt about pain in her private parts. Later, a case was registered against the accused at the Nehru Nagar police station.

In January this year, a school van driver was sentenced to life imprisonment by a Hyderabad court for kidnapping and raping a 14-year-old girl at Hayathnagar in 2014. According to a report in The Hindu, the convict acquainted the girl, who was a student of class 9, and kidnapped the victim and raped her multiple times.

In December 2018, a POCSO court in Bundi district of Rajasthan sentenced a 35-year-old man to life imprisonment for raping a 16-year-old girl in 2014. The court had also imposed a fine of Rs 20,000 on the man.

Meanwhile on Friday, Sri Ganganagar police in Rajasthan arrested three minor boys in connection with the rape of an eight-year-old girl. Officials familiar with the matter said that the minor boys, who are neighbours of the victim, were addicted to pornography and committed the heinous crime after watching a pornographic film.
