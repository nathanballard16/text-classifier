Police teams formed to arrest absconding men

NEW DELHI: The Delhi Police has formed several teams to arrest the two absconding accused in the Sail Chairman attack case in Hauz Khas. Anil Kumar Chaudhary, chairman of India's largest state-owned steel producer Steel Authority of India ( SAIL ) was assaulted by some drunk men armed with iron rods in the national Capital on Wednesday night. Though initially, it looks to be a road rage case but the police are investigating from all possible angles. Police is now verifying if there was some hidden motive behind the attack or it was a simple road rage case. Teams of the police are raiding locations where the two absconding accused could be hiding.
