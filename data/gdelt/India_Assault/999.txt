Pune: Man rapes, kills 7-year-old girl in Dapodi to take revenge from girl's mother; hangs self later

Pune girl raped and murdered (Representative Image) | Photo Credit: Thinkstock

Pune: In a horrific incident in Pune, a seven-year-old girl was raped and then murdered in Dapodi in Pimpri-Chinchwad. The suspect though in the case also committed suicide by hanging himself from a tree near an engineering college in the area.

The accused, identified as one Vijay Yadav (25), apparently committed the crime after the girl's mother refused his proposal to marry him, according to the police. On Tuesday morning the victim's mother had gone to work and her sister had gone to school. While she was alone in the house, accused Yadav entered the house and raped the minor.

When her mother returned from work, she found the girl unconscious on the ground. While she immediately took her to the hospital, the doctors declared her dead on arrival.
