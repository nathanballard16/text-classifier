35-year-old Turbhe resident gang-raped by five men while out for a stroll at night.A 35-year-old man, who was allegedly sodomised by five unidentified men in Vashi, Navi Mumbai on Monday, is battling for his life.The suspects also allegedly sodomised him with broken coconut shells, the police said. The Vashi police registered a case under section 377 () and 34 (acts done by several persons in furtherance of common intention) of Indian Penal Code on Wednesday, and are on the lookout for the suspects, who are suspected to be drug users.According to the police, the incident took place around 9 pm when the victim, a resident of Turbhe and an employee of, had gone for a stroll inof Vashi near Jagruteshwar lake in sector 8, which is relatively deserted at night.The police said that while he was smoking a cigarette, the suspects suddenly pounced on him and dragged him into the bushes, sodomising him repeatedly. They fled after committing the crime, the police said. The victim somehow managed to reach a nearby hospital with the help of passersby.Doctors at the hospital had to conduct multiple surgeries to remove the shells and save his life. The victim’s family members on Wednesday approached the police, following which an FIR was registered.However, the suspects are still at large.The victim told the police that all of them are between the ages of 25 and 30 years, and seemed to be under the influence of drugs. “We are trying to identify the culprits. We are checking CCTV footage from the area and are questioning,” said Anil Deshmukh, senior inspector of Vashi police station.Doctor Prakash Shendage, who is treating the victim, said he is in a critical condition, and will have to undergo more surgeries. “This is a very critical case, and we are doing everything possible to save him,” Shendage said.
