M Sc student Alleges Rape By Staff At Rashtrapati Bhawan

New Delhi: In a shocking incident, an M Sc student alleged rape by a staff at the Rashtrapati Bhavan staff quarters.

According to police, the accused has been identified as Nishant Yadav. He had lured the girl on the pretext of marriage and called her to the President's residence and raped her on Tuesday night.

Police said an FIR has been registered and the accused is at large.
