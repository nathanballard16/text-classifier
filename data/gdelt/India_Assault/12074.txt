Delhi Police registers case in mysterious death of 9 yr old girl

NEW DELHI: The Delhi Police on Saturday registered a murder case in the unnatural death of a nine-year-old girl who was found dead under mysterious circumstances in Delhi. "The victim was brought to a hospital in east Delhi at around 1pm on Friday where she was declared dead," a senior police officer said. The police said that they are waiting for the post mortem report which will make clear the cause of the death. 'Once the post morterm report comes, it would be easier to investigate the case as the actual cause if death would be known" said a senior police officer. The cops said that investigation from all angles is being done to reveal the actual cause of death.
