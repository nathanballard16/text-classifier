Rajkot: Minor abducted, raped

(The victim's identity has not been revealed to protect her privacy as per Supreme Court directives on cases related to sexual assault)

RAJKOT: A 16-year-old girl was allegedly abducted and raped by a youth in Chhotunagar slums on Raiya Road in Rajkot. Though the crime was committed on last week on Thursday, the complaint in this connection was lodged only on Wednesday. Police said Bharat Devipujak, had allegedly abducted the girl after luring her to elope with him with the promise of marriage and raped her several times in Chhotunagar, Wankaner and Chotila "We have launched a manhunt for Devipujak who has gone absconding after committing the crime," said an official from Gandhigram police station.
