SAIL chairman assaulted

New Delhi, August 8Steel Authority of India Limited (SAIL) Chairman Anil Kumar Chaudhary was assaulted allegedly by some men in south Delhi’s Hauz Khas while he was on his way home, in a suspected case of road rage, police said on Thursday. The incident took place near HUDCO Place on Wednesday and two persons, Amardeep, a resident of Uttam Nagar and Lalit, a resident of Dwarka, were arrested from the spot. The police said a case under IPC Sections 307 and 34 has been registered at the Hauz Khas police station. The police said they were probing the case — PTI
