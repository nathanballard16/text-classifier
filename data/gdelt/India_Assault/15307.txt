Youths throw acid on school student

Kolkata: A Class IX student was seriously injured after a few youths allegedly threw acid on her face on Monday night at Daspur in East Midnapore. According to a source, the girl belonged to Chandrokona in East Midnapore. It was suspected that the accused youths threw acid on her. She was rushed to Midnapore Medical College and Hospital where she is at present under treatment. The police are trying to find out if the girl was being stalked or if any of the youths was known to her.
