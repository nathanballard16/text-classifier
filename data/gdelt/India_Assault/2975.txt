No Kashmiri assaulted after Pulwama attack: Javadekar

NEW DELHI: Union minister of human resource development Prakash Javadekar said on Wednesday that no Kashmiri students had been assaulted following the Pulwama terror attack. He also said that there is no threat for students from Kashmir studying in various institutions in the country.The minister was addressing the media on Operation Digital Board, where he was asked about the claims of Kashmiri students being victimised.Several Kashmiri students from different cities across India have been slapped with sedition charges for allegedly supporting the terror attack on the social media. Also two colleges in Dehradun have decided not to admit those from Kashmir, while many students have been claiming that they are living in a state of fear.
