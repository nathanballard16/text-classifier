Ahmedabad: Three held for abducting, extorting land broker

DETAILS OF CRIMEThe accused took Padhiyar to a deserted place & beat him up. The accused took Padhiyar to a deserted place and started beating him up. The Odhav Police on Monday arrested three accused in relation to a case of abduction and extortion of a land broker in March. The accused have been identified as Amit Rabari, Rohit Rabari and Amrit Rabari, all three residents of Gandhinagar. After the incident took place, a complaint was lodged with the Odhav police station against the five accused by Padhiyar.
