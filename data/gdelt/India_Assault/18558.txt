Hyderabad: 19-year-old rapes five-year-old who bought sweets from his shop

Accused sent to judicial remand

(The victim's identity has not been revealed to protect her privacy as per Supreme court directives on cases related to sexual assault)

HYDERABAD: A 19-year-old grocery shop worker was arrested on Tuesday for raping a five-year-old neighbour who frequented his shop to buy chocolates in Maheswaram mandal.The accused was arrested from Porandla village in the mandal after the girl’s parents filed a complaint on Monday night. The incident happened on Nov 17 when the girl’s parents, who are daily wage workers, were not at home.“During interrogation, he confessed to his crime,” Maheswaram inspector D Venkanna Naik said. “The victim was alone, while her elder sister was playing nearby when the accused sneaked into the house. He raped the girl and fled when she started crying,” the inspector said.The girl narrated her ordeal to her parents, who also came to know about the assault from neighbours.After they lodged a complaint with Maheswaram police on Monday night, a case was registered against the accused under IPC Section 376 ( rape ) and relevant sections of the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences Act.The girl has been sent for a medical examination.“We produced the accused before a local court. He has been sent to 14-day judicial remand,” the inspector said.
