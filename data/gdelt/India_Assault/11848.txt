Police catch Canacona rape-accused in Bengaluru

Rape-accused Ramchandrappa Yellappa who had escaped from police custody on 28th June has been re-arrested in Bengaluru. He was brought to Goa by Goa Police on Tuesday.

Ramchandrappa had escaped through the toilet vent when he was brought to the Margao Sessions Court for a hearing from Colvale Central Jail.

He was arrested for the rape of a British woman on December 20, 2018 in Canacona.

One of the police officers received a burn injury as his leg touched the motorcycle silencer when he jumped to catch the undertrial rape accused .
