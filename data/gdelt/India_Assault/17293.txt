School dropout held for sexually assaulting homeless girl

New Delhi: The rape case of homeless girl near IP park in Sarai Kale Khan area was solved after a person was arrested from South East Delhi. She decided to stay at the bus stand for the whole night as she was not having any shelter in Delhi. At 12 am in the night, she was sleeping at IP park bus stand Ring Road, two men came near her and forcibly committed the offence. Both the accused persons manage to escape from the place of occurrence," police said. They forcibly took her behind the bushes near IP park bus stand.
