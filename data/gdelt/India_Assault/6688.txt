Pleader appointed DRI, Customs prosecutor

RAJKOT: Kutch district government pleader Kalpesh Gowswami has ben appointed as a special prosecutor by the central government to represent Customs, Directorate of Revenue Intelligence and Central Goods and Service cases in courts of Kutch.The state government also appointed him as a special prosecutor in sensitive cases like those of former Kutch collector Pradeep Sharma, Nalia rape case , besides representing government agencies like anti-corruption bureau and crime branch.
