Help abducted sailors: MEA to envoy in Nigeria

HYDERABAD: Union external affairs minister Sushma Swaraj on Tuesday asked the high commission of India in Nigeria to engage in a dialogue with the Nigerian authorities and secure the release of Indian sailors who were abducted by pirates there."I have seen news reports about abduction of five Indian sailors by pirates in Nigeria. I am asking Indian High Commissioner to take this up at the highest level with Government of Nigeria for their release," Sushma Swaraj said in a tweet to the High Commission of India in Nigeria.On April 19, despite the presence of the Nigerian security , pirates entered the ship and abducted six sailors out of which five are Indians.
