4 killed 1 injured in mishap at Dhulagarh

Kolkata: Four persons died and another was seriously injured after a car rammed into the back of a container carrier on the wee hours of Wednesday at Dhulagarh on National Highway 6. The injured person has been admitted at NRS Medical College and Hospital with serious injuries. Locals tried to rescue the people inside the car but failed to break the car free from under the container carrier. The injured person identified as Krishna Rao is also from the same location. Sources informed that after rescuing the five persons, police also found a bottle of alcohol and a few glasses from the car.
