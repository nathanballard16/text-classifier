Hyderabad: Fire breaks out on the sets of Chiranjeevi's Sye Raa

A massive fire broke out on the sets of actor Chiranjeevi's upcoming movie Sye Raa on Friday morning. The mishap occurred at the actor's farmhouse at Kokapet where the film is being shot

Locals who noticed the smoke at the farmhouse alerted the police and fire officials. The property loss of Rs 2 crore is estimated. However, no casualties have been reported as there was no one on the film sets.

The film unit has been shooting a part of the movie on the sets for the last three days. The shooting was also held on Thursday night.

It may be recalled that the sets of Sye Raa were also doused along with Manam movie sets at the fire mishap in Annapurna Studios.

Sye Raa Narasimha Reddy which is based on the life of Uyyalawada Narasimha Reddy is being helmed by director Surender Reddy. The film also stars actors Amitabh Bachchan, Nayanathara, Tamanaah and others.
