Six of family die in road accident

A case has been registered in Nippani town police station. Belagavi: Six persons of a family from Maharashtra died in an accident at Stavanidhi Ghat on the Pune-Bengaluru National Highway-4 on the outskirts of Nippani on Saturday evening. The entire family was coming to Sambra to attend the naming ceremony of the baby.The family’s car has been completely mangled while a major part of the truck is also damaged in the accident. They were residents of Murgod town in Kolhapur district of Maharashtra.The lorry driver and cleaner were also injured in the incident and are being treated at a hospital in Kolhapur. According to police sources, the granite-loaded truck was heading towards Kolhapur.
