Eight-year-old girl raped, culprit remains unidentified

Surat: An eight-year-old girl was sexually assaulted by an unidentified man near Amroli area of the city on Friday. The girl was found lying in a serious condition by passersby from behind Utran railway station on west side.“The girl was taken to Amroli police station first. The girl underwent a medical examination and also surgery for injuries on her body in a hospital. The girl underwent a medical examination and surgery at Surat Municipal Institute of Medical Education and Research. The minor girl’s sister filed a complaint at railway police station, subsequent to which police have launched a manhunt for the culprit.
