TV actor Karan Oberoi arrested in alleged rape case, booked under section 376 of IPC

Karan Oberoi (image courtesy: karanoberoi.in)

Television actor Karan Oberoi who is known for his famous role in the serial Jassi Jaisi Koi Nahin has been allegedly charged with rape. The actor was recently arrested by the police in connection to the alleged rape case, reports ANI. While the case is still under process, further details about the arrest of the actor are being awaited.

Karan who has been a part of many commercials was also known for his involvement with the indie pop band A Band Of Boys which was formed in 2001. According to reports, the actor is being produced at Andheri Court today for the rape charges.

An FIR was registered at the Oshiwara Police Station in Mumbai under sections 376 (rape) and 384 (extortion) of the Indian Penal Code against Oberoi. "Oberoi not only raped the woman and filmed the act but also demanded money from the victim threatening to release the video if she did not pay up," the police said told ANI quoting the FIR. Apparently, the actor-model raped the woman on the intention of marrying her.

Karan was apparently arrested on Monday and was charged allegedly raping and blackmailing a woman, police told ANI. The actor who was arrested has been sent to police custody till May 9 by the Mumbai Court.

On the work front, Karan had started his television journey with Mahesh Bhatt's Swabhimaan, where he played Bobby and later starred in shows like Saaya and Jassi Jaisi Koi Nahi.
