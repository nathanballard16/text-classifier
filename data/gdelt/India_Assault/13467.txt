SAIL chairman attacked by 4 assailants in Delhi; 2 attackers nabbed by cops

Steel Authority of India Limited (SAIL) Chairman Anil Kumar Chaudhary was attacked by four unknown assailants on Wednesday night near HUDCO Place in New Delhi.

Chaudhary was on his way back to his residence from office in his official car. "Chaudhary was brutally attacked at around 10.30 pm when his car was intentionally hit by another car occupied by four people," the company said in a statement on Thursday.
