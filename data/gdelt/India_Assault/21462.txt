Ludhiana: Man booked for rape after he refuses to marry woman

Representative image

LUDHIANA: Focal Point police on Saturday booked a man on the charge of rape for allegedly establishing a physical relationship with a woman by promising to marry her.

According to the woman, the man had agreed to marry her after she had gone to the Punjab and Haryana high court against him. Later, when she withdrew the complaint, the accused refused to marry her, she alleged.

The 25-year-old woman alleged that the accused had been in a relationship with her for three and a half years. Investigating officer ASI Harjeet Singh confirmed that they had registered a case under sections 376 (rape) and 506 (criminal intimidation) of the Indian Penal Code. “He will be arrested soon,” said the cop.

(The victim's identity has not been revealed to protect her privacy as per Supreme Court directives on cases related to sexual assault)
