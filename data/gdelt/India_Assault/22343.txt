BJP worker aides in skull caps held for pelting stones on train

Kolkata: Six persons were arrested on Wednesday morning by the Berhampore police for allegedly pelting stones at a train in the Sealdah-Lalgola section, wearing skull caps and "lungi". One of the accused youths is known to be a BJP worker. Locals informed that Sarkar, a resident of Srishnagar, adjacent to Radhamadhabtala, is a BJP worker. On Friday, a senior police official of the West Bengal Police said: "Locals are saying that one of the accused persons is a BJP worker. Local BJP leadership claimed that BJP has no connection with the incident and Sarkar is not their party worker.
