Narayan Sai's sentencing to be held today

Narayan Sai is in jail since his arrest from Delhi in December 2013. Two Surat-based women had accused Asaram and Narayan Sai of sexual exploitation. A sessions court in Surat will pronounce the sentence of rape convict Narayan Sai, son of jailed self-styled Godman Asaram Bapu, on Tuesday. Lawyers had said that quantum of punishment of Narayan Sai, and three other of his associates who were also convicted, would be pronounced on April 30. Asaram was convicted for life for raping a minor girl in Rajasthan last year and is serving his sentence.
