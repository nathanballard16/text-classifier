Man sentenced to 10 years for sexually assaulting girl

New Delhi

oi-Oneindia Staff

By Anuj Cariappa

New Delhi, Mar 13: A man was awarded 10 years imprisonment by a court here for sexually assaulting a girl three years ago, a police official said.

The court also imposed a fine of Rs 25,000 on Mithlesh Chaudhary who was convicted under the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences (POCSO) Act, he said.
