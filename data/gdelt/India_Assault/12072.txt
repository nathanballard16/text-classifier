Pakistan breaches truce along LoC in J&K’s Poonch, civilian injured

JAMMU: A civilian was injured in a shell explosion as Pakistan violated ceasefire along the LoC in J&K’s Poonch district on Saturday morning.The breach of truce comes on a day when defence minister Rajnath Singh was in the state to commemorate 20 years of Indian Army ’s Operation Vijay.“Pakistan army initiated unprovoked firing of small arms and mortar shelling along the LoC in Poonch’s Mendhar sector,” said Jammu-based defence spokesperson Lt Col Devender Anand. Besides Mendhar, Pakistani troops also targeted forward Army posts at Mankote and Krishna Ghati sectors, and civilian areas near the border, police sources said.Some Pakistani shells landed in residential areas, one of them injuring Zaffarullah Khan, the naib-sarpanch of Baloni village in Poonch’s Mankote tehsil, said sources. Khan was admitted to a nearby hospital for treatment.
