Gurgaon: Woman shot dead, colleague names ‘boyfriend’ in FIR

AdvertisingAccording to the FIR, her colleague, who is the complainant in the case, has accused the victim’s “boyfriend” for the crime. A 23-year-old woman, who worked at a bar in Delhi, was found shot dead near Gurgaon’s Khushboo Chowk Friday morning. Police said the woman, who hailed from Karnal, had earlier registered a case of rape against him and the matter is currently in court. The complainant claims to have rushed to the victim’s mother and sister, and confided in them about the incident, following which they approached police. “The victim had five gunshot wounds on her body… The fatal wound hit her on the back,” the report stated.
