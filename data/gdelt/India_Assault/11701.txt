2 killed, several injured in building collapse in N. India - Xinhua

Source: Xinhua| 2019-07-14 22:42:06|Editor: yan

Video Player Close

NEW DELHI, July 14 (Xinhua) -- At least two persons, including a woman and an Indian army personnel, died and several others were injured when a building collapsed in India's northern hilly state of Himachal Pradesh on Sunday.

The incident occurred in Himachal Pradesh's Solan district at around 15:45 local time, Additional Superintendent of Police Shiv Kumar Sharma told Xinhua via phone.

"Two dead bodies, of a woman and an Indian Army personnel, have been recovered. Besides, we have rescued 27 persons from the rubble of the collapsed building. Many are still feared to be trapped under the debris. Rescue and relief work is going on," said the police officer.

He said the victims were eating there when the tragedy struck.

According to media, the building collapsed due to incessant rains over the past few days.

Besides the local police, personnel of the Indian Army and the National Disaster Response Force (NDRF) are involved in the relief and rescue work. Four medical teams of the Indian Army are present at the spot.
