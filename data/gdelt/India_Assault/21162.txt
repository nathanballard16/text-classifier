Coimbatore: 50-year-old man arrested for molesting 13-year-old girl

(The victim's identity has not been revealed to protect her privacy as per Supreme Court directives on cases related to sexual assault)

COIMBATORE: The all women police RS Puram , on Friday arrested a 50-year-old man for sexually assaulting a 13-year-old girl.Police identified him as P Raju of Range Gowder Street, who was working as a labourer. “He had separated from his wife five years ago and was staying alone. He had way laid the victim when she was on the way to school last month and touched her inappropriately. The scared girl did not reveal the incident to her family. But when Raju targeted her again on Friday morning, she informed her mother. Her relatives identified Raju with her help and informed police,” a police officer said.Police registered a case against him under sections 7 (sexual assault), 8 (punishment for sexual assault) of the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences (POCSO) Act, 2012 and section 506 (i) (criminal intimidation) of the Indian Penal Code. He was remanded in judicial custody and lodged in the Coimbatore Central Prison.
