Cross LoC shelling: Injured army man succumbs

Srinagar

An army soldier who sustained injuries a day before after armies of India and Pakistan exchanged gunfire along Line of Control (LoC) in Poonch succumbed to injuries on Tuesday morning.

On Monday, an army man was killed and another sustained injuries who was subsequently referred to an army hospital for the treatment. However, the injured man succumbed to injuries this morning.

The deceased soldier has been identified as Jawed, a resident of Marrar, Tehsil Morrahi, District Khagaria, Bihar.
