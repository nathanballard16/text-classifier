Delhi horror! 27-year-old neighbour rapes minor, arrested

New Delhi: In another rape case reported from the national capital, a 27-year-old man was arrested for raping a minor on Monday. The accused raped the minor in an isolated place.

Mirror Now reported The accused has been booked under the sections of the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences along with Indian Penal Code. The accused man was the victim's neighbour and he allegedly raped her when her parents were not at home. The accused threatened her of dire consequences if she tells anything to her parents.

Meanwhile, the girl narrated the ordeal to her mother, after which the parents lodged a police complaint.

In a similar case reported from the national capital, a 35-year-old sanitation worker was arrested for allegedly sexually assaulting a four-year-old girl at a public toilet. The incident took place on February 6 and was reported in the Naraina police station. The investigation revealed the survivor had gone to a public toilet when the cleaner allegedly grabbed her and raped her. The accused was on the run and the police had launched a search operation to nab the accused.
