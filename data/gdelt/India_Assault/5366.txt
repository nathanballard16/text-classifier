Mumbai: Four attack civic executive engineer

KALYAN: An executive engineer of Kalyan-Dombivli Municipal Corporation (KDMC), Subhash Patil, was attacked with blades by four masked persons on a busy foot over-bridge in Dombivli (E) on Friday evening.Patil suffered multiple injuries and has been admitted to Icon hospital’s ICU in Dombivli. Ramnagar police have registered an FIR against unidentified persons.
