Telangana: Girl student set ablaze by jilted lover in broad daylight, dies

Hyderabad: Another girl became the victim of crime against women. In Telangana State, a jilted lover set a girl student ablaze in board daylight.

This incident took place at Hanamkonda crossroads, Warangal District. The accused is identified as Avinash.

In the incident, the girl received injuries up to 70%. She was rushed to a corporate hospital in the city where she died last evening.

After the death of the girl, her body was shifted to Gandhi Hospital Mortuary.

Police had arrested the accused earlier.
