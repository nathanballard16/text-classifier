Man hacked to death at Jagraon village

Tribune News ServiceLudhiana, August 4A 45-year-old man was hacked to death in Kothe Sher Jang village at Jagraon last night. The deceased has been identified as Jasbir Singh (45). On Saturday night, Jasbir, along with his wife Daljit and son Arman (8), slept in their room. They first hit her in the head and then hacked her husband to death.
