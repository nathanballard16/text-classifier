President Ram Nath Kovind condemns attack on CRPF convoy

Srinagar

President Ram Nath Kovind on Thursday has strongly condemned the attack on CRPF convoy in Lethpora area of Awantipora in which forty-four Central Reserve Police Force men were killed and several others injured.

Taking to social networking site Twitter, President wrote: “Strongly condemn the terrorist attack in Pulwama, J&K. Thoughts and prayers with the families of martyrs and wishing an early recovery to those injured. The entire nation stands united in the fight against the forces of terror and evil #PresidentKovind.”
