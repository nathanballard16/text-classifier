Man, his horse attacked by landlord in south Delhi; one arrested

A case has been registered under Section 308 (attempt to commit culpable homicide) of the Indian Penal Code. (Representational Image)

A man and his horse were allegedly attacked by a group of people in South Delhi on Sunday, police said.

Angad Sahlot was attacked by the landlord of a farm in Fatehpur Beri where he stays with his family, and horses and cows, the police said.

According to the police, Sahlot has dispute with the landlord for some time now. On Sunday, the landlord and others attacked Sahlot and also injured his horse, they said.

A case has been registered under Section 308 (attempt to commit culpable homicide) of the Indian Penal Code. An accused has been arrested and others will be arrested soon, Deputy Commissioner of Police (South) Atul Kumar Thakur said.

The officer added that the animals are fine in the farm.
