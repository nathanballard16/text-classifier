Delhi: Trader’s employee stages abduction to buy house31 Jan 2019, 09:25AM ISTSource: TNNA day after a businessman’s employee had complained about being abducted and robbed of Rs 50 lakh in Karol Bagh, it was found that he had staged it along with a colleague, who is also a relative, to buy a house. DCP (central) Mandeep Randhawa said Harender and Kamal, who are from Rudraprayag in Uttarakhand, had been arrested. The entire amount has been recovered.
