Man sentenced over minor rape in India

(MENAFN) An Indian court has handed a self-styled religious priest a life term sentence in prison, for rapping a minor, in the most recent sexual assault cases against the alleged "godman".



The preacher, aged 77, has over 400 ashrams worldwide, according to his website, has had bail bids rejected several times.



The establisher of a non-government group which assisted the victim's family seek the lawsuit, Kiran Jha Thakur declared: "The sentencing has come as a big relief,"



He added: "It goes on to show that even if you are a 'godman', if you commit a crime, our judiciary will get you,"
