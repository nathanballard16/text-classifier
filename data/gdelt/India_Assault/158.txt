Man found dead in pumphouse

A 25-year-old contract employee of Mission Bhagiratha was found dead in the Bangarigadda water pump house here on Thursday morning, the police reported. Identified as Chilukuri Chandrasekhar he was an inspector overseeing Mission Bhagiratha works in the Munugode segment.

Family members, who could not trace him since Wednesday afternoon, were on their way to the police station to lodge a complaint on Thursday when they found his motorbike parked near the pumphouse on the outskirts of the village. The police reached the spot and retrieved the body from the water tank and reportedly found a note reportedly written by the deceased person.
