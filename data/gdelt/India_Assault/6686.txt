Mumbai: 18-yr-old held, minor detained for ‘abducting’ 2 school girls

An 18-YEAR-OLD was arrested and a 15-year-old boy detained by the Bhiwandi police for allegedly abducting two minor girls. “The 18-year-old and the minor accused are friends and they had been following the girls for some time. The accused were picked up from the Nashik Road railway station, along with the girls, the police said. While one accused works as a construction worker, the minor is a school dropout,” an officer said. The police said the two girls, students of Class VI, left school during the lunch break on Wednesday.
