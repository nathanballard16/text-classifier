The deadly terror attack in Jammu and Kashmir’s Pulwama district on Thursday (February 14) has left the entire country in a state of shock. The attack, that happened on the fateful night of 14th February, 2019 claimed the lives of over 40 jawans. Celebrities are coming forward to help and support the families of the martyrs. Saath Nibhana Saathiya actress Lovey Sasan , who recently tied the knot, visited martyred CRPF jawan H. Guru’s residence in Hudigere village, Mandya district near Bangalore.The actress was accompanied by her husband Koushik Krishnamurthy . She also extended financial support to the family. She shared the same on Twitter and wrote, “Visited Brave martyred CRPF JAWAN - H. GURU ji’s residence in Hudigere village, Mandya district near Bangalore to pay our shraddhanjali and to our bit of financial aid to GURU’s family, I pray almighty to give them courage and strength to bear this irreparable loss. My deepest condolences to all our bereaved soldiers’ families who lost their precious life for all of us in Kashmir Pulwama terror attack . Amar Jawan.”Recently, TV actors Ravi Dubey and Sargun Mehta had also supported the families of the deceased jawans with monetary contribution.Bollywood stars like Amitabh Bachchan, Salman Khan and others have already offered their contribution.In the worst ever terror attack in Jammu and Kashmir since militancy erupted in 1989, a Jaish-e-Mohammad suicide bomber had rammed his vehicle packed with explosives into a Central Reserve Police Force (CRPF) bus on the Jammu-Srinagar highway in Pulwama district, killing at least 40 troopers on the spot.
