HC Abdul Malik inquires about health of NZ terror attack victims

Pakistani High Commissioner to New Zealand, Abdul Malik visited a hospital in Christchurch to inquire about the health of those injured in the terror attack on two mosques.

The High Commissioner prayed for the early recovery of the injured.

The injured citizens hailed from Malaysia, Palestine, Jordan, Syria, Fiji, Egypt and India.
