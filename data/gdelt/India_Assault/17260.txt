Issewal gang rape: Friend cross examined

Ludhiana: The defence counsels partly cross examined the friend of Issewal gang rape victim during the hearing of the case in a Ludhiana court on Saturday. The next date of hearing of the case in the court of Additional Session Judge Rashmi Sharma is October 23.

One the defence counsels said that the cross examination of the friend of the prosecutrix was done by defence counsels partly on Saturday and they will cross examine him in the next hearing of the case on October 23. He added that during the hearing on Saturday, the defence counsels cross examined the friend of the prosecutrix for about one-and-a-half hour before lunch.

The counsel said that the friend of the prosecutrix fully supported the case. He added that all the accused, including the juvenile, were produced in the court for hearing.

Meanwhile, the counsel for juvenile said the court is expected to announce the order during the hearing on October 30 on their revision petition wherein they have pleaded that juvenile be tried by the juvenile justice board and not with the other accused in the case. He added that they have pleaded as per the matriculation certificate of his client that he was born in April 2002 and hence at the time of commission of offence, he was not even 17 years old.

On February 9, a group of miscreants had allegedly gang-raped a 20-year old student after holding her and her friend hostage as they had gone to Issewal area to celebrate Chocolate Day. Police had booked ten unidentified accused in the case and arrested six accused, including a juvenile.
