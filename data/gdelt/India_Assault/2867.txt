Man hacked to death by unknown assailants in Hyderabad

A man was hacked to death by some unknown assailants on Tuesday here at Borabanda.

Sanjay was brutally attacked with knives at a nearby glass factory where the victims work. His death was instant. The police who inspected the spot said that the accused who were suspected to be in drunk state attacked Sanjay.
