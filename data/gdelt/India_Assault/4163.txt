Woman Set On Fire By A Stalker In Hyderabad After She Rejected His Proposal

A 20-year-old Student was set on fire by a stalker on February 27, 2019, by a stalker, reports say that the 20-year-old woman rejected his proposal.

The 21-year-old man hurled petrol all over her and set her on fire after she rejected his proposal, the man has been arrested by the local police.

The local police said that the 20-year-old woman attained 70 percent burns on her body after the attack happened, the man did this while she was entering the college premises.

The police said that the 21-year-old stalker was harassing the woman for the last 6 months, but she constantly spurned him and rejected his proposals.
