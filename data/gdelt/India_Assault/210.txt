2 brothers held for wife-swapping in Kolkata

KOLKATA: Two brothers were arrested for alleged torture and wife-swapping in the city, police said on Friday. The incident came to light after a woman lodged a police complaint accusing her husband and brother-in-law of forcing her into wife-swapping. "We have arrested the two brothers and have started an investigation into the incident," a police official said.The father of the arrested duo claimed that the allegations levelled against his sons were completely baseless and his younger daughter-in-law was trying to malign his family as his younger son was mulling divorce. She also accused her in-laws of torturing her.The case was lodged on Thursday.When police reached the residence of the accused in Ballygunge on Friday, a scuffle broke out with the accused as other family members tried to resist the arrest, police said.
