Delhi: Cops, lawyers clash outside Tis Hazari court, vehicle set ablaze

A scuffle broke out between police and the lawyers in Delhi’s Tis Hazari Court on Saturday.

Gunshots were heard and a lawyer was injured in the incident. He was taken to hospital for further treatment

According to the sources, parking dispute triggered the clash. However, there was no confirmation from the police.

A police vehicle was also set ablaze.

The exact cause of the incident is yet to be ascertained, officials said.
