Assam State Disaster Management Authority Formed Task Forces To Observe Diwali Regulations

STAFF REPORTERGUWAHATI: The District Administration and the Assam State Disaster Management Authority (DDMA) have jointly formed five task forces to patrol in five zones in the city during the Diwali festivities. Each task force will comprise of five members which will include a magistrate, police and GMC personnel. The District Commissioner (DC) Kamrup (M), Biswajeet Pegu informed that the five task force teams will ensure that crackers are not sold outside of the above mentioned locations.
