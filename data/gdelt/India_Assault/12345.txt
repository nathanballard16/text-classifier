Thief nabbed within 40 minutes of stealing mobile phones cash at New Alipore

Kolkata: A thief was nabbed within 40 minutes of committing the crime, at New Alipore on the wee hours of Wednesday. The accused then managed to steal two high-end mobile phones and some cash from Chatterjee's room. Based on a hunch, police divided themselves into teams and started searching for possible suspects at New Alipore and Majherhat railway stations. At around 3:15 am, a police team noticed a youth at the platform of New Alipore railway station, who was wearing only one slipper. While searching him,police recovered 15 mobile phones, including the two stolen from Chatterjee's house, as well as the cash.
