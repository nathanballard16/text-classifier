Southeast Delhi Schools To Be Closed Today After Clashes Near Jamia

Buses were set on fire and protesters clashed with police near Jamia Millia Islamia.

All schools in southeast Delhi area will be closed today in view of the situation that emerged after violence near the Jamia university, Delhi Deputy Chief Minister Manish Sisodia has said.

"In Delhi's south east district areas including Jamia, Okhla, New Friends Colony and Madanpur Khadar, all government and private schools will remain closed tomorrow. Delhi government has taken the decision in view of the current situation," Mr Sisodia tweeted in Hindi.

Buses were set on fire and protesters clashed with police near Jamia Millia Islamia university on Sunday against the amended citizenship law.
