Blast at school in Pulwama, at least 12 students injured

SRINAGAR; A blast at a private school in Jammu and Kashmir 's Pulwama district left at least 12 students injured Wednesday, police said.The explosion took place inside the school in Narbal village of Kakapora area in the south Kashmir district this afternoon, a police official said.He said at least 12 students of class 10 were injured in the blast that rocked the school when they were taking winter tuition.The injured students have been rushed to a hospital for treatment and there condition is stated to be stable, the official said.He said police officers have reached the spot and are ascertaining the nature and circumstances of the blast.A case has been registered and investigation taken up.
