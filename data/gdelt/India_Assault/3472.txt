Pakistan Vows 'Befitting Response' to India Air Strike

Pakistan Foreign Minister Shah Mahmood Qureshi speaks during a press conference at the Foreign Affairs Ministry in Islamabad, capital of Pakistan, on Feb. 26, after Indian warplanes violated the Line of Control in the Kashmir region. Pakistan Foreign Minister Shah Mahmood Qureshi accused India of committing "grave aggression," saying his country had the right to give an appropriate response. (Xinhua/Ahmad Kamal/IANS photo)
