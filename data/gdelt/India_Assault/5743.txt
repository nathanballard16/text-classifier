Jaipur: Man rapes niece, dies while trying to escape from the police

The complaint was filed on Friday in Hamirwas, Churu (Representative Image). | Photo Credit: Getty Images

Jaipur: An attempt to escape the police costed a man his life as a truck ran over him. The accused had allegedly raped his niece. The incident took place in Churu. The police were trying to nab the accused when he tried to escape and eventually died.

The 14-year-old was lodged a complaint against the accused on account of sexual assault. The complaint was filed on Friday in Hamirwas, Churu. The accused was run over by a truck.

The police stated that it was an accidental death. They added that the accused was trying to escape from them when the incident took place. While talking to the Times of India, Station House Officer, Hamirwas police station, Krishan Kumar said that the accused had allegedly taken the victim to his house on Thursday where he raped her. The accused allegedly kept the victim at her house for a day. Following the incident, the victim narrated her ordeal to her family and filed a police complaint. The police then went to the village of the accused to arrest him but he died before the arrest could be made.

The accused was identified Anil Kumar. The police registered a case against the accused under Section 376 of the Indian Penal Code (IPC). The police said that the accused was not escaping from them. A police official called it an accidental death and stated that he was trying to cross the road when he was hit by a pick-up truck.

The police investigation on the rape is being carried out and the medical examination of the victim has been done. The police will take further action after receiving the medical reports of the victim. After being hit by the truck, the accused was taken to a hospital where he succumbed to his injuries.
