New Delhi: 3-Year-Old Girl Raped By Tuition Teacher’s Father

Police Report: The Delhi Police arrested a man after he raped a 3-year-old girl in New Delhi’s Punjabi Bagh area on Saturday.

The police said that the accused rapist is the father of the 3-year-old girl’s tuition teacher. The father also lived in the same area.

The accused was arrested by the police on Saturday.

An FIR has been registered under several sections of the IPC and the police have assigned a team to investigate the rape case.
