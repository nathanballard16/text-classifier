Chargesheet against BSP MP in rape case

VARANASI: Lanka police on Saturday filed a chargesheet in a case of rape and fraud lodged against BSP MP from Ghosi, Atul Rai by a Ballia girl.Lanka inspector, Bharat Bhushan said on Saturday that police have submitted chargesheet against the BSP MP before judicial magistrate-I court as per the allegations and statements of the complainant. However, hearing on Rai's plea for bail for taking oath as Lok Sabha member could not take place for the second consecutive day due to strike of lawyers' body Central Bar Association. The plea has been listed for hearing on July 1.A week after Rai filed his nomination on April 26, a Ballia girl lodged a complaint against him with Lanka police in Varanasi on May 2, accusing him of raping her repeatedly and threatening to make video of the act viral on social media. She alleged that Rai called her to his flat at Chitaipur, Varanasi in March 2018 on pretext of introducing her to his wife, where he raped her and made a video of the act. After that, he raped her several times, she alleged.Rai was declared absconder by local court on May 20 under section 82 CrPC, following which police served notice at his Varanasi residence.On June 22, Rai surrendered before the court of judicial magistrate Ashutosh Tiwari and was remanded to 14-day judicial custody.
