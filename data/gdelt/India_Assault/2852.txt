Gang rape survivor attempts suicide, battling for life

Kanpur: A 16-year-old rape survivor allegedly attempted suicide by consuming poison in her house in the city on late Monday night. The complaint further stated that she had gone to college with her friend when their common friend Anurag took her to an apartment. However, during investigation, the police allegedly gave a clean chit to three accused — Abhishek, Jacky and Shubham. When she reached there, she found Abhishek, Jacky and Shubham present there. They took turns to rape her.
