Hyderabad: Road accident triggers tension in Saidabad, police force deployed

Hyderabad: In the Saidabad area of the Old City of Hyderabad, road accident triggered tension. This incident took place last night.

According to the report published in Telangana Today, persons belonging to two different communities gathered in large number after a two-wheeler hit a pedestrian. Miscreants also pelted stones at each other.

Soon, Saidabad Police rushed to the spot and brought the situation under control. The senior police officials also visited the area. Sufficient police force was deployed to restore peace in the locality.
