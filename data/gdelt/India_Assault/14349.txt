NGO man fakes kidnapping in vengeance

Tribune News ServiceNew Delhi, August 24Marketing head of a Rohini-based NGO, Anti-Corruption Crime Control Force, has been held for conspiring and kidnapping three girls to falsely implicate two officials of the Kalindi Kunj police station, Delhi. The accused, Bimal Kumar (38), is a resident of Sector 5 at Harola in Noida. Earlier, the Kalindi Kunj police arrested him for breaching peace. Since then, the accused wanted to take revenge from the two police officers of the station. On August 22, complainant Yashpal Singh, a resident of Khadda Colony in Jaitpur, received a call.
