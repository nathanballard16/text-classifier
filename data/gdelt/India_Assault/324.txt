46 Delhi cops promoted on New Year

NEW DELHI: Last January, six-year-old Vihan Gupta was kidnapped for ransom by criminals who intercepted his school bus and shot the driver. The cop, who was alone, opened fire at the criminals and also got into a scuffle which left him severely injured. After reaching there, he saw a woman crying for help on the third story of a burning house . The cop gave an outstanding performance as he arrested 82 absconding criminals between January and October. However, his intervention averted the robbery at the house of an elderly couple who lived alone.Head constable Ajay Kumar from Kirti Nagar police station was assigned the job of nabbing proclaimed offenders of west Delhi.
