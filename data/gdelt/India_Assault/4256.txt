India reaching out to China, other UNSC members to get Jaish chief Masood Azhar banned

Azhar's listing by the UNSC will subject him to global travel ban, asset freeze and arms embargo.

JeM chief Masood Azhar. FILE Photo: AFP

India is reaching out to all the 15 UNSC member countries both in New Delhi as well as in their respective capitals

A proposal has already been moved by the US, Britain and France at the UNSC to ban Azhar

The UN Secy Gen has not spoken to PMs of both countries, but has been in touch with other leaders on both sides
