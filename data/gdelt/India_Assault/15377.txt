Moti Nagar gangrape Victim s clue help cops to nab accused

New Delhi: The investigation in the gang-rape case of a 16 year-old-girl in West Delhi's Moti Nagar area revealed that clue given by victim help cops in nabbing two sexual predators. According to police, on Tuesday two persons identified as Ravi and Ankit Choudhary were arrested from Basaidarapur area of West Delhi for raping a minor girl. "During the investigation, the girl said that one of the accused while committing the crime, was taking the name of his associate. The police officer further said that they started rounding up a few anti-social elements who keep roaming around the area. Later, they once again go near the victim to say sorry and trapped her on the pretext," sources added.
