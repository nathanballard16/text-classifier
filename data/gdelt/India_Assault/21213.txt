Do not support such a statement, AAP over Rahul Gandhi’s “rape in India” remark

Condemning Congress leader Rahul Gandhi’s “rape in India” remark, the Aam Aadmi Party senior leader Sanjay Singh said, “I do not support such a statement because no one should try to use the word rape improperly…”

The remark by Rahul Gandhi came during an election rally in Jharkhand.

Singh said to that a strict law should be brought in to check increasing incidents of crime against women.

“In the floor of the Parliament, I have demanded a strict law against rape incidents in India. People of India are worried about the increasing number of rape incidents and it is high time to make a more stringent law against rape,” said the AAP leader.
