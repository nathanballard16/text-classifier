Drunk man stabs wife to death

NEW DELHI: A man who was reportedly drunk has stabbed his wife to death in Central Delhi's Anand Parbat area of Delhi. Delhi Police apprehended the man after the murder was committed. After some heated arguments, the husband stabbed his wife and she died. It is said that the husband identified as Sonu murdered his wife Payal in a fit of rage over some small argument. Later, the neighbours heard the shrieks of the victim and found that the husband has murdered his wife.
