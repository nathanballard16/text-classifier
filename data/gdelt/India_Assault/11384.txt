Rape allegation: Binoy agrees to DNA test

Mumbai: Binoy Kodiyeri, accused by a Bihar woman for rape has agreed to provide blood samples to police for DNA test. The investigating officers had demanded blood samples from binoy to prove the paternity of the complainant woman’s child.

Earlier, the sessions court while granting the anticipatory bail had directed Binoy to provide blood sample for DNA test.

Binoy was granted bail on certain conditions including appearing at Oshiwara police station on Mondays for a month. Binoy appeared before the police based on this direction.

Oshiwara police interrogated Binoy over the documents that the complainant woman submitted at the court.
