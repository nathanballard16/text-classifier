Angry mob ransacks tantrik s house sets vehicle on fire

Kolkata: Local residents of Swarupnagar in North 24-Parganas on Thursday set a woman's house on fire suspecting her to be a tantrik. The accused woman, Alpana Ghosh, and her husband Nityananda are at present absconding. Local residents alleged that Alpana and her family has been staying in Kabilpur village for the past three years. Local residents said about two months ago, Alpana had prescribed some roots and a chemical to a three-year-old girl to cure some ailment. Once the news reached the village, local residents attacked the house of the tantric and ransacked it.
