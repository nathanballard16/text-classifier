Youth held for rape, murder of widow

Following the murder, Nimbhore left the body and returned home on his motorcycle, said Taitwale. Aurangabad: Within 24 hours of finding the dead body of a woman bludgeoned to death, the Chikalthana police have arrested a youth, who allegedly raped her before murdering her at Sai Hills. The woman, in a fit of rage, threatened to commit suicide if her demand were not met. She rode the motorcycle of Nimbhore, who had reached the area near the aerial water tank in Hanumannagar and the duo went to Sai Hills at Deolai. It was after the accused had allegedly raped her that the duos got into a quarrel.The widow insisted that Nimbhore marry her and he turned down her demand citing that she was 10 years older to him.
