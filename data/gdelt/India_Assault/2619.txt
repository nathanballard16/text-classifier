KSRTC woman staffer alleges assault by senior

MANGALURU: A woman employee of KSRTC has filed a police complaint against the divisional officer of the transport corporation accusing him of assaulting her. An FIR has been registered against Nagaraj Shiroli , divisional controller, Puttur , following the allegations at the women’s police station in Puttur. The complainant is employed with Ugrana branch.According to police, the woman had filed a private case, details of which are not revealed, against Nagaraj at the central office of KSRTC in Bengaluru.On February 15, Nagaraj is alleged to have demanded Shalini to withdraw the case.
