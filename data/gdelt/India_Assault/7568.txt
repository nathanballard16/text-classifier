51-year-old man stabbed to death

TIRUPUR: A four-member gang stabbed A Arockiyasamy, 51, of Thoothukudi, to death in broad daylight in the city on Sunday. He was staying in Angeripalayam and was working as a supervisor at a construction site. Arockiyasamy had come to pick up workers from the corporation park on Perumanallur Road when the gang attacked him. He died on the spot.
