2 Group D staffers held for assaulting minors

Kolkata: Two Group D staffers from SSKM Hospital were arrested for assaulting two minor girls who were undergoing treatment there. The family members of the victims lodged a complaint with the police on the basis of which the accused have been arrested. The duo's job is to shift the patients on trolleys from one ward to the other. The victims' family members alleged that the two Group D staffers were taking the two patients on two separate trolleys while they were being shifted to another ward. The incident triggered an outrage among the family members of the victims who later lodged complaint.
