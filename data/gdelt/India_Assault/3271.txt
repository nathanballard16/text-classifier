Kolkata: Youth suspected of being child-lifter assaulted by mob

In yet another incident of violence, a mob assaulted a man in Phoolbagan area of North Kolkata on Friday, suspecting him to be a child-lifter. There have been a rise in incidents of mob violence fuelled by rumours and several people were injured in different parts of the state. Sources said that in the wake of mob attacks, the state government has also asked police officers to monitor social media. Rattled by the growing incidents of mob violence, Director General of Police, Virendra, Saturday held a meeting with top police officials, including all the superintendents of police (SP), through video conferencing. The DGP has asked the police officials to tighten their sources so that exchange of information is faster, sources said.
