Salman Khan,and Disha Patani-starreris one of the most anticipated movies of the year. After teasing fans with posters and trailer, the makers have released the first song today titled Slow Motion.With Slow Motion,introduced Salman aka Bharat’s life from his time in the circus. Slow Motion features Disha and Salman and is based on the backdrop of 1960s in India. Salman is seen grooving with Disha, who plays a trapeze artist in the film. The song captures the environment of a circus – acrobat, stunts, bike stunt and of course lots of colour. While Salman brought his unique style in the song, it's’s moves that will make you go gaga.Salman looks dashing in a white outfit, while Disha sizzles in a yellow saree. Her look will surely bring you back the memories of Raveena Tandon wearing the yellow saree from the evergreen song Tip Tip Barsa Pani from Mohra.The peppy track is penned by Irshad Kamil and composed by Vishal-Shekhar. Slow Motion, which is sure to rock the chartbuster, is sung by Nakash Aziz and Shreya Ghoshal.Directed by Ali Abbas Zafar, the film also stars Katrina Kaif, Tabu, Jackie Shroff and Sunil Grover in pivotal roles. Bharat is set to hit theatres this Eid.
