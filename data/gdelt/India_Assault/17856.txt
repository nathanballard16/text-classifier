Surat: Teenager held for sexual assault

(The victim's identity has not been revealed to protect her privacy as per Supreme court directives on cases related to sexual assault)

SURAT: A 19-year-old teenager was arrested by Khatodara police on Sunday for sexually assaulting a four-year-old girl. The accused offered a chocolate to the minor girl and lured her to his rented room where he sexually assaulted her on Saturday. When the girl started to scream, the accused escaped from the spot but was nabbed by locals and handed over to police.The minor girl was playing with a friend near her maternal grandparents' house in Althan area when the accused lured her to his room and sexually assaulted her.Police arrested Manish Maurya for alleged sexual assault on the daughter of a domestic help. The accused has been booked under various sections of Indian Penal Code and Protection of Children from Sexual Offences (POCSO) Act for sexual assault and harassment.The accused earns his livelihood by washing the cars of residents in apartments in Althan. His father and brother are watchmen in residential societies. They live in a rented room.Mother of the girl used to leave the survivor at her parents' house before going to work as a domestic help."We are collecting scientific evidence to prove the charges against the accused. We are also registering statements of people who were present at the spot," a police officer said.
