Chargesheet filed against rape accused BSP MP Atul Rai

VARANASI: Lanka police on Saturday filed chargesheet in the case of rape and fraud lodged against BSP MP from Ghosi Atul Rai by a Ballia girl.However, hearing on his plea for appearing in Lok Sabha to swear-in as a member of parliament could not take place for second consecutive day due to strike of lawyers’ body Central Bar Association. Now, it will be heard on July 1.Regarding progress in investigation in Rai’s case inspector Lanka Bharat Bhushan said that police has submitted chargesheet in this case before judicial magistrate-I court as per the allegations and statements of the complainant.A week after filing nomination on April 26 by Rai, a Ballia girl lodged a complaint against him with Lanka police in Varanasi on May 2 accusing him of raping her repeatedly and threatening to make video of the act viral on social media. She alleged that Rai called her to his flat at Chitaipur, Varanasi in March 2018 on pretext of introducing her to his wife, where he raped her and made a video of the act. After that, he raped her several times, she alleged.Rai was declared absconder by local court on May 20 by local court under section 82 of CrPC on May 20, following which police served notice at his Varanasi residence. The same notice was also served at Rai’s residence at Birpur village in Bhavarkole area of Ghazipur district on June 13.Rai on June 22 surrendered before the court of judicial magistrate Ashutosh Tiwari and court sent him to jail on 14 days judicial remand.He had submitted his plea before JM-I court on June 27 but hearing on it could not take place due to on-going strike of lawyers. Court has now fixed date of July 1 for hearing.
