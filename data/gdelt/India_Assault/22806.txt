Murder accused ends life in Bharuch sub-jail

Surat: A 32-year-old murder accused committed suicide by hanging himself in Bharuch sub–jail on Wednesday afternoon sometime between 3 and 4 pm, said cops. Monu Singh, a private firm employee and resident of Padmavati Society in Ankleswar, was convicted for his wife’s murder on December 22, 2019. According to Bharuch police, Singh doubted his wife, Asha Devi, of having extra marital affair and allegedly stabbed her to death in their house. He was caught by the police on the same day and arrested.Cops have sent the body for post-mortem. Once formalities are done, police will investigate the issue to know why Singh took the drastic step of taking his own life.
