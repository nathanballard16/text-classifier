Ahmedabad: Man stabbed to death over pan masala squabble

A 22-year-old man was stabbed to death allegedly by four of his friends over a petty issue on Monday night. The cops have arrested three accused in the case, while one is absconding. The deceased has been identified as Vijay Vaghela, a resident of Vadaj area. Before Jeevi could reach the spot, Poonam pulled a knife from his pocket and stabbed Vijay several times on the chest. Primary investigation revealed that the dispute broke over buying pan masala."
