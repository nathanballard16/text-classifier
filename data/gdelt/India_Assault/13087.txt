Unnao rape survivor”s accident case to be heard in Lucknow for 15 days: SC – Chennaionline

The Supreme Court on Friday ordered that transferring of the accident case of the Unnao rape survivor to Delhi be kept in abeyance for 15 days. The case will be heard in Lucknow till then, the apex court said.

On Thursday, a bench headed by Chief Justice Ranjan Gogoi had shifted the five cases related to the Unnao rape to Delhi and assigned a special judge to conduct day-to-day trials.

Today, the Central Bureau of Investigation (CBI) urged the SC not to transfer the accident case to Delhi.
