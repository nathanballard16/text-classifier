Rape charges against TV actor Karan Oberoi

The police arrested TV actor Karan Oberoi on Monday for alleged charges of rape and blackmailing a woman in Mumbai, Maharashtra, said the police.

An FIR has been registered against him at Oshiwara Police Station in Mumbai under Sections 376 (rape) and 384 (extortion) of the Indian Penal Code.

The actor-model allegedly raped the woman on the pretext of marrying her, said the police.

“Oberoi not only raped the woman and filmed the act but also demanded money from the victim threatening to release the video if she did not pay up,” they said.

‘Jassi Jaissi Koi Nahin’, ‘Inside Edge’ famed actor also appeared in advertising campaigns of FBB, Royal Enfield Riding Apparel, Aldo, Jack & Jones and Lamborghini.
