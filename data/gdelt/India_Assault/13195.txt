CBI sleuths grill truck driver involved in Unnao accident case

AMN

CBI sleuths Saturday grilled the truck driver and cleaner involved in Unnao rape victim’s road accident case.

A special central forensic science Laboratory team today reached the spot in Raebareli district where the accident took place and examined the ground situation and inquired the people.

Another team of the agency visited King George Medical University trauma centre Lucknow where the injured rape survivor and her lawyer are being treated.

Accused of the case, expelled BJP MLA Kuldeep Sengar was interrogated by CBI in Sitapur Jail itself today. As per medical bulletin of KGMU, the condition of both the victim and her lawyer remain critical but stable.
