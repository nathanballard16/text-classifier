49 Students Injured After Bus Falls Into Canal In Maharashtra's Palghar

The accident was reported at about 7 am.

Fourty nine students were injured this morning when a bus they were travelling in fell into a canal in Maharashtra's Palghar, about 100 km from Mumbai.

The bus was travelling on the Pivli-Vada road with 49 children on board when it skidded off the road at about 6:30 am. The driver lost the control as he was trying to save a woman who suddenly emerged in front of the bus, said police.

All the injured were rushed to the hospital.

More details are awaited.
