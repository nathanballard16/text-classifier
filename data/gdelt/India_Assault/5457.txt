Woman patient alleges gang rape in UP ICU

MEERUT: A 30-year-old woman patient has alleged that she was gangraped by three staffers at a private hospital here on Sunday. She was undergoing treatment for a liver ailment and admitted to the intensive care unit of the hospital. She claimed that she was injected with sedatives and then gangraped by three ward boys. All the three accused have been detained for questioning.However, cops are not fully convinced by her tale. According to Kailash Kumar, investigation officer, Medical police station, “Probe is on and prima facie the case seems to be a bit suspicious as the woman was in the ICU where all beds were occupied. We are probing the matter.”
