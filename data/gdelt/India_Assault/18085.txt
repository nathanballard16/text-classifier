Man gets 7-yr jail for raping minor

Ludhiana: A local court has sentenced a 27-year-old man to seven-year rigorous imprisonment for raping a minor girl. The court of additional sessions judge Tarntaran Singh Bindra also imposed a fine of Rs 25,000 on the convict. In default of payment of fine, the convict will have to further undergo rigorous imprisonment for two months. On June 22, 2018, Jodhan police had booked the man under Sections 376 (rape), 506 (criminal intimidation) of the IPC and 3 and 4 of Protection of Children from Sexual Offences (Pocso) Act.
