Alwar gang-rape "not political, but emotional issue,” says Rahul Gandhi after meeting victim

Gandhi along with CM Ashok Gehlot and Dy CM Sachin Pilot, were slated to meet the Alwar gang rape victim and her family members in Thanagazi on Wednesday but the meeting got postponed due to bad weather.

Image courtesy: ANI

Congress President Rahul Gandhi on Thursday met the victim of Alwar gang-rape case and her family, in Rajasthan.

Gandhi was accompanied by Chief Minister Ashok Gehlot, Deputy CM Sachin Pilot and Congress national general secretary Avinash Pandey.

After meeting the victim, Gandhi said that the unfortunate incident was not a political issue for him but an emotional one and that justice will be delivered at the earliest.
