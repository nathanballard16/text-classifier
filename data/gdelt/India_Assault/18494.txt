Boy stabbed to death in revenge killing

NEW DELHI: A 21-year-old boy was stabbed to death by two boys in Madangir in Ambedkar Nagar, South Delhi late night on Sunday. The police said that the argument between the deceased boy Lavi and the accused resulted in violent stabbing of the boy. Police received a call at around 11:12 pm on Sunday night that a boy has been stabbed by three to four people in Madangir. "We have arrested a 21-year-old boy identified as Sooraj and apprehended a CCL in connection with the murder. To take revenge, the accused procured knife from Haridwar and executed the murder by stabbing the deceased.
