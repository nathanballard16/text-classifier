Youth absconds after double murder at Mogra

Kolkata: Two persons including an elderly woman were hacked to death at Mogra village in Bankura Sadar area. The accused youth, identified as Arup Chowdhury, fled after murdering the duo. While he was walking, Arup came and attacked Gunomoy with an axe, stabbing him multiple times. Meanwhile, after a few moments of stabbing Gunomoy, Arup again attacked an elderly woman identified as Lichu Roy (74) while she was walking along the road. Arup stabbed her repeatedly as well and fled the spot.
