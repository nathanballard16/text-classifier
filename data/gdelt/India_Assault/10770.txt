Three peacocks found dead in Jamnagar village

RAJKOT: Three peacocks were found dead near a windmill in Chur village of Jamjodhpur taluka in Jamnagar on Wednesday. Villagers alleged that the birds were electorcuted after it came in contact with livewire and lodged complaint with Jamjodhpur police and the forest department on Thursday.“There are seven windmills being operated there by Gujarat-based and Hyderabad-based firms. We have demanded that these firms should use either coated wire or underground wire, so that such tragedy could be averted,” said Jayendrasinh Jadeja, a retired government teacher and village leader.Forest officials said that the exact cause of death would be known only after postmortem of the carcasses.According to the forest department, Chur village has a population of around 200 peacocks.
