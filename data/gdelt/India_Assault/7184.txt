Delhi: 10-year-old goes missing while playing; found dead a day later

According to the police, the deceased was kidnapped for money (Representative Image). | Photo Credit: Getty Images

New Delhi: The police found the dead body of a boy who had been kidnapped a day before. The boy had allegedly been kidnapped from his house in Modinagar. The body of the 10-year-old was found about in Niwari. Niwari is at a distance of around eight kilometres from the house of the deceased.

According to the police, the deceased was kidnapped for money. The police are also suspecting that the accused murdered the deceased thinking that he would reveal their identity.

The accused was a class 6 student and was playing with his friends when he was kidnapped. The deceased was kidnapped from the Manavtapuri locality. The deceased was kidnapped by two bike-borne men at around 8 pm. The body of the deceased was found in a field.

While talking to the Hindustan Times, Neeraj Kumar Jadaun, superintendent of police (rural) stated that the deceased seemed strangled to death. Initial investigation revealed that the deceased did not resist the kidnapping, which was indicative of the fact that the deceased knew the accused.

Allegedly, the accused told the deceased that they were going to take him to his father's shop. The FIR was lodged by the deceased's uncle. The family of the deceased was expecting a ransom call from the accused but did not receive the same.

The CCTV footage of the area was being scanned to trace the accused. The police rounded up some people to investigate them in relation with the case. The post-mortem report of the deceased revealed that the he had been strangled to death and even had an injury on his leg.
