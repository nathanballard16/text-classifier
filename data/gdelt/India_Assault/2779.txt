Host turns enemy, helps Bf rape birthday girl

Palghar: Friend turns enemy- A shameful incident occurred where a birthday girl was raped in the birthday party organized for her after the accused spiked her drink, police said.

The incident is reported from Maharashtra’s Palghar district where the 27-year-old victim a resident of Kanivali in the northern Mumbai was allegedly raped at her birthday party organised at a friend’s house by the host’s boyfriend after they reportedly spiked her drink, PTI reports.
