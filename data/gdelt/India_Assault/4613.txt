Letter to BS: Govt should take action against those resorting to violence

The government needs to wake up from its slumber. After the unfortunate Pulwama attack where more than 40 CRPF personnel were killed, there were series of attacks on and intimidation of Kashmiri people.

More than 50 incidents of physical attacks, threats and intimidation have been reported across the nation, with the latest report saying two Kashmiris were assaulted in Lucknow by right-wing men.
