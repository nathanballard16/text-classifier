Army jawan wasn’t abducted: Defence Ministry

NEW DELHI: The defence ministry on Saturday said reports of the abduction of a soldier, Mohammad Yaseen Bhat, while he was on leave at his home in Budgam district of J&K, were “incorrect”, and that the “individual is safe” and back at home.There are conflicting reports about whether he managed to escape before he could be abducted by the unidentified gunmen or freed himself from their custody.“He returned home on Saturday,” said an official.
