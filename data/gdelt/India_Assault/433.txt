Man murders drunken father

Rajkot: Tired of his habit of creating a drunken ruckus every other day, a man stoned his father to death in Palasawa village of Junagadh taluka on Tuesday evening. “On the fateful evening, when Madha came home drunk and picked up a fight with him, Mahida picked up a stone and hit Madha on his head. Madha was rushed to Junagadh Civil Hospital where he was declared brought dead,” said an official from Gondal taluka police station.tnn During interrogation, Mahida said that he was tired of his father Madha’s (52) drinking habit. He would pick up a fight with family members and spoil the peace of his house.
