Mumbai: Vasai man calls girl for cartoon show, rapes her

A 42-year-old carpenter has been arrested for raping a five-year-old girl in Waliv near Vasai. A court in Vasai remanded the accused in police custody till September 16. She was playing outside with her friends when the accused, who is known to her, invited her to watch a cartoon show at his place. Once in his house, the police said, the accused bolted the door from inside the raped the girl. The man, who faces charges under POCSO, faces jail up to 10 years if convicted.
