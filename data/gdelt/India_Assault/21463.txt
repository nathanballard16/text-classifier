Ludhiana News - Times of India

Man held for assaulting cop

The Model Town police have arrested Baldev Singh of Daad village for assaulting a traffic cop, ASI Kamaljeet Singh. The latter said he was present near Dugri traffic lights with his team when the accused came on a bike along with two friends. When he was stopped at the naka, the accused misbehaved with him and also tore his uniform.

Security guard booked for theft

On the complaint of Brij Jhanna of Hotel Palm Court, the Sarabha Nagar police have arrested two security guards Sandeep Singh and Triptdeep Singh for stealing ration from the store room of a hotel.
