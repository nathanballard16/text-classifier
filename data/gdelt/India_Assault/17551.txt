Unnao Rape Accused Kuldeep Sengar Gets Parole For Brother's Cremation

Family sources said that the cremation will take place in the afternoon.

: Expelled Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP) MLA Kuldeep Sengar , the accused in Unnao rape case, and his brother Atul Sengar have been granted a 72-hour parole to attend the cremation of their younger sibling Manoj Sengar who died in Delhi. In Unnao, a 17-year-old was raped last year allegedly by BJP MLA Kuldeep Sengar. The incident came to light after the victim tried to set herself ablaze outside Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath's residence earlier this year. Kuldeep Sengar will be brought to Unnao from Delhi on Monday for the cremation. Atul Sengar, who is lodged in the Lucknow jail, will also be taken to Unnao.
