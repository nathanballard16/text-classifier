Thief assaults cop, tears his uniform

Ludhiana: High drama was witnessed in Model Gram on Friday evening when a “pistol-wielding” thief assaulted a cop.

On a tip-off, a police party, led by ASI Tarlochan Singh, was on the trail of car thief Gurbeer Singh of Mandi Ahmedgarh. “We started following him after he came out of the court complex. When cornered, he brandished a pistol, assaulted me and tore my uniform. My team came to my rescue and overpowered him. Later, it was found that he was threatening us with a toy pistol.”

A fresh FIR has been registered against Gurbeer.
