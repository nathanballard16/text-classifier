HC quashes Jeetendra sex assault case

SHIMLA: The Himachal Pradesh HC quashed on Monday a sexual assault case filed against actor Jeetendra at the women’s police station in Shimla in February last year on his cousin’s complaint.Shimla cops had booked the actor on charges of “outraging the modesty of a woman” after his cousin had accused him of sexually assaulting her 48 years ago when she had stayed with him at a Shimla hotel in January 1971. The complainant was 18 years old at the time. The complaint had been emailed to the Himachal Pradesh director-general of police in Shimla last year.Justice Ajay Mohan Goel ordered the quashing of the FIR registered against Jeetendra under Section 354 of the IPC.
