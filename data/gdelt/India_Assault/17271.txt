2 Indian soldiers killed in J&K ceasefire violation by Pakistan

Two Indian soldiers and a civilian were killed when Pakistani troops violated the ceasefire in Tanghar sector of Jammu and Kashmir's Kupwara district today, the area's police said according to our New Delhi correspondent.

Three civilians were injured in the firing by Pakistani troops, the correspondent reports quoting the officials.

Two houses were damaged in the incident in the latest ceasefire violation by Pakistan, they said.

More details are awaited.
