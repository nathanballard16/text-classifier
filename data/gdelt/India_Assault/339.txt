Man found dead near Vidhan Bhavan

Nagpur: An unidentified man was found dead on the footpath of Vidhan Bhavan’s compound in Sitabuldi Police Station area on Sunday.

Sitabuldi police said that the body was discovered by a citizen at 2.20 pm. Staff of Sitabuldi Police Station rushed to the spot and performed Panchanama.

The body was sent for post-mortem to Mayo Hospital. However, identity of the deceased was not established. Sitabuldi cops have registered a case of accidental death and started probe.
