Earlier this year in February, a CRPF convoy was attacked in Pulwama by a suicide bomber, in which 40 CRPF personnel were martyred, in the worst-ever terror attack in recent years. The attack was carried out by Pakistan based terror outfit Jaish-e-Mohammed. India carried out preemptive attack and destroyed a terror camp in Pakistan’s Balakot. In retaliation, Pakistan infringed India’s airspace and targeted Indian military instllations. In the aerial engagement that followed between the two countries India downed one of Pakistan’s F16 fighter jet.

An improvised explosive device (IED) explosion targeted an Army convoy in Pulwama district of South Kashmir on Monday evening, news agencies reported. Nine Army personnel were injured when militants triggered an IED fitted in a vehicle near an Army patrol in the militancy-hit Pulwama district, PTI quoted officials as saying. Terrorists targeted an Army convoy with an improvised explosive device (IED) blast in Pulwama district of Jammu and Kashmir, police said.
