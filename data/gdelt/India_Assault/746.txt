Landlord stabs 1 to death, 2 injured in Delhi’s Khayala

New Delhi, Jan 17 (ANI): In a shocking incident, a woman was stabbed to death while her husband and 18-year-old son were injured in the same attack. The fatal incident took place in Delhi’s Khayala on Wednesday. The accused was a landlord and the victims his tenants who were often involved in arguments. Police informed that, “We received a call around 7pm. 1 dead, while 2 injured are undergoing treatment after accused attacked 3 members of a family with knife. It happened following altercation between neighbours. The accused is absconding. Our team is investigating the case.”
