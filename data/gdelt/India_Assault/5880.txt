Indian Army destroys 7 Pakistan military posts, inflicts several casualties

Pakistan's Inter-Services Public Relations admitted three casualties among their troops at Rakhchakri in Rawalakot of the Pakistan-occupied-Kashmir.

JAMMU: Retaliating to the continued ceasefire violations by Pakistani troops, the Indian Army on Tuesday destroyed their seven military posts across the Line of Control. India's retaliatory firings also inflicted several casualties also on Pakistani troops. Seven Pakistani posts across the LoC in Rakhchakri and Rawalkot forward areas of Pakistan Occupied Kashmir (PoK) were destroyed, PTI quoted officials as saying. There have been several causalities also on the Pakistani side, they said. Three Indian villagers too were injured at Shahpur border area in Poonch district Tuesday in the shelling by the Pakistani army, the officials added.
