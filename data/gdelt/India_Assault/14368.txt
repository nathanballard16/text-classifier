Delhi Police arrests wanted robber

NEW DELHI: A notorious robber infamous for assault on police officers and wanted in many robbery cases has been arrested by crime branch of Delhi Police. Thereafter, he along with his associates started committing armed robberies in parts of Delhi and had been arrested several times. He was involved in several cases of robbery, assault on police officials, NDPS Act etc, in Delhi and Ghaziabad, he has been declared a proclaimed offender in two cases of Sarita Vihar, and Jyoti Nagar, Delhi. He was earlier residing at Sunder Nagari and Loni, Ghaziabad but frequently changed his address and was presently residing in Jhuggis Near Post office, Sultanpuri in Delhi. He has also been declared Proclaimed Offender in a case of Arms Act of Police Station Jyoti Nagar in Delhi.
