Man attempts to immolate self outside Delhi Police headquarters

New Delhi: A man allegedly set himself ablaze outside the Delhi Police headquarters on Sunday.

According to the Delhi Fire Department, the incident occurred at around 8 pm.

The man is currently undergoing treatment at a nearby hospital. His condition is critical.

An investigation is underway to ascertain the motive behind the incident.

Further details are awaited.
