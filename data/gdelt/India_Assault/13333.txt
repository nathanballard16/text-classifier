3-year-old Delhi boy abducted; three held

New Delhi, August 5A woman along with her son and daughter have been arrested for allegedly kidnapping a three-year-old boy for ransom in outer Delhi's Bawana, police said on Monday. The accused, residents of Daryapur Kalan, disclosed that their modus operandi was inspired by a crime show episode, they said. The boy was kidnapped for ransom and confined to a newly rented accommodation of the accused in Daryapur village. The accused offered the boy a fruit juice, while he was playing outside his house and took him to their newly-rented accommodation, the DCP said. — PTILured with juiceThe accused offered the boy fruit juice while he was playing outside his house and took him to their newly rented accommodation.
