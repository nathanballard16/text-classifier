Class V kid of Mohali sexually assaults 3-year-old cousin, booked

MOHALI: The police have booked 14-year-old boy for allegedly sexually assaulting his three-year-old cousin in Kharar.Police said the accused is a student of Class V. The girl and the boy’s father are brothers. All of them live in the same house. They hail from Nepal Officials of a child helpline said they received a call from the mother of the girl on Friday night about the boy assaulting her daughter. The girl was taken to Government Multi-specialty Hospital, Sector 16, Chandigarh. Police registered a case under Section 376 of the IPC and Pocso Act at the Kharar Sadar police station.In another incident of crime against minor, police arrested a 37-year-old man for allegedly kidnapping a seven-year-old girl. However, she was rescued by her parents. According to police, the accused has been identified as Sunny Mann, a resident of Baltana. Police said the victim was on her way back home after her school van dropped her, when the accused took her to his flat. When she did not return home, her parents started looking for her. They somehow reached the accused’s flat and rescued her.Police registered a case under Sections 363 (punishment for kidnapping), 365 (kidnapping or abducting with intent secretly and wrongfully to confine person), 323 (punishment for voluntarily causing hurt), 342 (punishment for wrongful confinement), 506 (punishment for criminal intimidation) of the IPC and 12 of POCSO Act at the Zirakpur police station.
