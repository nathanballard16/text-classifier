Punjab: Man arrested for raping sister-in-law in Ludhiana

(The victim's identity has not been revealed to protect her privacy as per Supreme Court directives on cases related to sexual assault)

LUDHIANA: Ludhiana police on Sunday afternoon arrested a person for allegedly raping his minor sister-in-law. The accused is 29-years-old. He is a native of Nepal and works in a factory in Ludhiana.The father of the victim, who has been living in state for last nineteen years, told cops that he has four sons and three daughters. He added that two years ago, they married his elder daughter to the accused and they lived in their neighbourhood.The complainant told police officials that on August 22, his daughter told her mother that her brother-in-law had raped her many times in June while she was going to cook meals at her sister’s home as she was unwell. He added that the accused even threatened her of dire consequences if she revealed about the matter to anyone.The complainant further said that after his wife told him about the matter, they took the victim to hospital where doctor told them that she is pregnant. He added that after probing the matter on their own they were shocked to know that their own son-in-law committed such a crime and later they the informed police.On being informed, cops from Sadar police station reached the complainant and started to probe the matter.Police later booked the accused under section 376 of Indian Penal Code (IPC) (rape) and sections 4 and 6 of protection of Children from Sexual offences Act. Investigating officer (IO) of the case, Sub-inspector Manju Choudhary said that after registering case into the matter, the accused was arrested on Sunday afternoon.
