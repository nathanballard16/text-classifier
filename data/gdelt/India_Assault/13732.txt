Ex BJP MLA Kuldeep Sengar charged with murder of Unnao rape survivor's father

The other charges against Sengar, others are for assaulting and framing him in the Arms Act case in 2018.

New Delhi: A Delhi court Tuesday framed charges against expelled BJP MLA Kuldeep Sengar and others for the alleged murder of Unnao rape survivor's father in judicial custody.

District Judge Dharmesh Sharma also framed charges against Sengar, others for allegedly assaulting him and framing him in the Arms Act case in 2018.

Charges have been framed against 10 accused.
