Karnataka: Three children buried alive under sand heap in Koppal

KOPPAL: Three children were buried alive under a heap of sand in Navali village of Kanakagiri taluk in Koppal district on Wednesday.The deceased have been identified as Sonu (07), Savita (04) and Kavita (02).Reports say that the deceased are children of labourers from Pune in Maharashtra and they work at brick kilns nearby.The Kanakagiri police have rushed to the spot.
