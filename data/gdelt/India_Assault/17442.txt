School set ablaze on eve of BDC polls

SRINAGAR: Terrorists set on fire a government higher secondary school at Nehama in Pulwama district of south Kashmir late on Wednesday evening.The school was designated as a polling station for the block development council (BDC) polls to be held on Thursday. Voters will decide the fate of 1,065 candidates across 310 polling stations.The setting ablaze of the Nehama school was second such incident in two days. Terrorists had on Tuesday set ablaze a school at Wattoo area in Kulgam district
