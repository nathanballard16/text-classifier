Robbers abduct cab driver then posed as driver to loot next passengers

NEW DELHI: In a thrilling incident, a father and son duo were kidnapped by the cab driver they booked on an online application. The twist in the tale, they were not cab drivers rather robbers who had earlier abducted the original driver of the cab, looted and dumped him. At around midnight, they accepted one booking from New Delhi Railway Station to IGI Airport. The criminals reached New Delhi railway station. This booking was made at midnight from New Delhi railway station by a Pune-based businessman.
