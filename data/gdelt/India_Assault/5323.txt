Raped by drunk teen, minor’s mutilated body found in bushes

The mutilated body of a six-year-old girl, who was raped and hacked to death after she was abducted by a drunk teen, was found by the police from the bushes in a village in Hyderabad.

The victim was the daughter of a daily wage earner of in Hyderabad’s Turkapally village, and was abducted on Thursday, reported The Hindu.

A case has been registered against the accused under relevant sections of the Indian Penal Code and Protection of Child Against Sexual Offences (POCSO) Act. The accused hails from West Champaran district in Bihar.

The accused had allegedly abducted the minor girl in an intoxicated condition and raped her before killing her late in the afternoon.

The neighbours and family of the girl started searching for her when she went missing while playing Holi, unable to find her, they lodged a missing complaint at Alwal Police Station.

A team immediately swung into action and later in the evening, the victim's body was found in the bushes, Cyberabad police said, as quoted by the Hindu.

The accused is at police custody at present, and a probe is on.
