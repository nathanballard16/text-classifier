Rajkot: Woman bootlegger attacks sarpanch

RAJKOT: A woman bootlegger along with her two women aides assaulted Bhingaran village sarpanch of Una taluka in Gir Somnath district on Saturday. The video of the incident had gone viral on social media platforms.Police booked Lasu Koli and her aides - Janu Makwana and Rebhu Solanki - for allegedly attacking sarpanch Jenti Solanki (28) with scythes and bats.“In his complaint, Jenti said Koli had been nursing a grudge against him ever since he lodged a complaint of rampant trade of country liquor in his village with Gir Somnath superintendent of police,” said an officer of Nava Bandar police station.
