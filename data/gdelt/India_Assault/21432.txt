22-year-old man arrested for sexually abusing minor girl

(The victim's identity has not been revealed to protect her privacy as per Supreme court directives on cases related to sexual assault)

COIMBATORE: The city police on Sunday arrested a 22-year-old man for sexually assaulting a minor girl. Police identified him as J Vinodh of Ammankulam in the city.Relatives of the 16-year-old victim had lodged a complaint with the all women police after she went missing on December 12. “Vinodh contacted the relative and told them that she was with him. He appeared before the police along with the minor and told them that he had married her and wanted to live with her,” a police officer said.Police booked him under sections 7 (sexual assault) read with 8 (punishment for sexual assault) of the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences (POCSO) Act, 2012 and arrested him. He was remanded in judicial custody and lodged in the Coimbatore Central Prison.
