Mumbai: 20-yr-old man held for ‘abducting’ minor boy

Police have arrested a 20-year-old man for allegedly abducting a six-year-old boy from Andheri. Police also said the minor told the constable that whenever he asked the accused to let him go, the man would assault him. A constable on patrol duty nabbed the accused after he received information that a man was dragging a child near KBM compound. Investigators said they will conduct a medical to check if the accused sexually assaulted the boy. The child was reunited with his family early on Monday, police added.
