Boy killed by three juveniles

New Delhi, September 7A 19-year-old man, Mandeep, was allegedly stabbed to death by his three juvenile friends over personal enmity in Shahadra’s Vivek Vihar area, police said on Saturday. The juveniles had been apprehended, the police said. The victim was an air conditioner mechanic and had left his home citing some work but did not return. His family lodged a complaint on Tuesday, a senior police official said. The body was found at an isolated spot on Friday.
