Youth booked for raping 12-year-old girl in Patiala

PATIALA: The Sadar Patiala police here have booked a man for allegedly raping a 12-year-old girl. The case has been registered on the complaint of rape survivor's sister who told the police that the accused had been raping the girl for last few months and had threatened of dire consequences if she revealed the whole incident to anyone.The police said that the accused, aged 24 years old, was arrested within 12 hours after the incident was reported to them. The accused used to work as an employee in a college in Ghanaur division of the district.The police said the complainant alleged in her statement that the rape survivor complained of pain in her abdomen on Tuesday following which she took her to the doctor. She said the rape survivor narrated her ordeal in the hospital that left the family in shock.The rape survivor, according to police, further revealed that the accused had raped her over a dozen of times in past four months. The police said that the accused used to live in the same locality where the family of rape survivor has been living in a village of Patiala.Meanwhile, the police got the medical examination of rape survivor conducted of which the reports are awaited. The police said since the girl was a minor, they had got her statements recorded in the presence of a child protection officer. The rape survivor has been kept under the observation of child protection officials as she was in trauma, they said.Station house officer, Sadar Patiala police station, Gurpreet Singh said, "We have nabbed the accused after registering a case against him under section 376 (rape), 506(criminal intimidation) and 34 (common intention) of Indian Penal Code . He was produced in the court on Tuesday following which we got one day police remand. The medical examination of the rape survivor has been conducted. She was living along with her sister and brother-in-law who worked as labourers."
