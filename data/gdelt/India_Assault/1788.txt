Teacher on the run after assaulting wife

A 38-year-old school teacher is on the run after he assaulted his wife when she refused to wear short dresses.The accused even bit her and later hit her with a wooden stick. He also reportedly twisted both her hands and the victim who managed to escape got admitted at the Nelamangala government hospital with the help of her mother. The woman has been identified as BS Kanaka, and the accused, BS Vasanth Kumar, of Vijaynagar in Nelamangala Town.The police have recorded her statements at the hospital after the staff informed them. Even the victim, who was earlier working as teacher, later quit her job and depended on her husband and from then she had allegedly been tortured. The two were in a courtship while in Mysuru before they got married in May 2004. The couple has two daughters.
