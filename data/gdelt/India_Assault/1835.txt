Rajasthan Gujjar agitation intensifies; Police vehicles torched, trains disrupted, highways blocked

The agitators also blocked highways connecting major cities on Sunday.

At least 20 trains were cancelled and seven diverted during the day as the protesters, led by the Gujjar Arakshan Sangharsh Samiti chief Kirori Singh Bainsla, continued their sit-in on railway tracks in Sawai Madhopur district for the third day on Sunday, officials said. The sit-in began on Friday evening and has affected the movement of over 250 trains through the region since.
