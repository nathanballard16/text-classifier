Civic volunteer in Nadia kills father for property

Kolkata: A civic volunteer in Nadia stabbed his father to death over a dispute related to property on Monday morning. Police have arrested his son Sukanta Saha. It is alleged that Sukanta had been forcing his father to give his property to him, but Rabindranath allegedly did not agree to give his property to his son. However, nothing deterred Sukanta from forcing his father to transfer his property to him. Sukanta threatening his father with dire consequences if he did not transfer his property to him.
