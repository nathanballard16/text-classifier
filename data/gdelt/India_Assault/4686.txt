S.S. Jain Subodh Law College, Jaipur announces the National Online Essay Writing Competition – 2019. It is an initiative taken by the institute with an aim of creating awareness regarding marital rape and the surrounding challenges. This theme has been decided to test the knowledge, research aptitude and writing skills of the participants. The competition will be held online, conducted and managed by S.S. Jain Subodh Law College Campus, Jaipur.
