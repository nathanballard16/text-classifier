‘Have Guts? Kill Modi’: This opposition leader gives call to assassinate PM

BENAGULRU: BJP unit of Karnataka on Tuesday retweeted a video Congress leader Belur Gopalkrishna wherein the leader is instigating people by giving a call to pick up arms and assassinate Prime Minister Modi.

According to India.Com reports, the Congress leader addressing an official party function reportedly said, “The people who are talking about Godse are anti-national and should be thrown out of the country… If anything or anyone tries to demolish this democracy, he should be killed… If you have guts, kill Modi.”

As per the reports suggested, the assassination video circulated by BJP Karanataka is a month-old.

This is not the first time that reports of assassination of Prime Minister emerged.
