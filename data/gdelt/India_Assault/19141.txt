One more minor held for raping minor girls in Kolkata

Kolkata, Nov 30 (IANS) One minor boy was arrested on Saturday in connection with the alleged abduction and sexual assault on two minor girls, who earn their living by begging in front of the famous Kali temple in Kalighat, the police said on Saturday.

This is the third arrest in the case. Two persons, including a minor, were arrested on Thursday, a day of the incident took place, the police said.
