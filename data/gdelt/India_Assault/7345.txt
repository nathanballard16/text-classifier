Crime Branch Deputy Superintendent of Police found shot dead inside his house

Crime Branch Deputy Superintendent of Police was found shot dead inside his house in Avadhpuri in Bhopal.

The deceased officer is identified as GL Ahiwar.

According to the sources, the accused is a hardened criminal of Bhopal.
