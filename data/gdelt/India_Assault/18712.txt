Rajkot: Surendranagar man stabbed to death in group clash

RAJKOT: A man was stabbed to death in a clash between two families in Dasada of Surendranagar district on Friday.Police said the accused, identified as Savji, had stabbed Raju Mulji to death.According to the police, the clash was the result of an alleged molestation of a woman from one family. Both the families decided to strike a compromise over this issue and hold a meeting. However, tension escalated in the meeting after heated arguments were exchanged and allegations traded.“The members of both the families attacked each other. Then in the heat of the moment, Savji flashed out a knife and stabbed Mulji to death,” said an official from Dasada police station.
