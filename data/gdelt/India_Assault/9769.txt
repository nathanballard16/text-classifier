Youth arrested for ‘killing’ a teenager four months ago

LAHAN, June 14: Siraha Police on Thursday arrested a person on the charge of abducting and killing a teenager about four months ago.

Gangaram Mandal Khatwe, 21, of Aurahi Rural Municipality-2, was booked from Naraha Rural Municipality-7. He has been accused of shooting and killing Subash Yadav, 17, of Dhangadhimai Municipality-6 of Siraha on February 20. The victim died while undergoing treatment at Patna Medical College and Hospital (PMCH), Patna, on February 21.
