Woman raped and murdered in South 24 Pgns

Kolkata: A woman was allegedly gang-raped and murdered at Basanti in South 24-Parganas on Saturday night by some miscreants in front of her own child. According to sources, the woman was about 40-year-old and used to reside with her child at a village in Basanti. On Saturday night, a few miscreants entered her house and allegedly raped her. When she fell down miscreants hit on her head again to ensure her death. Hearing her daughter's cry, local residents ran to the woman's house and found her lying on the ground bleeding profusely.
