Man Attempts To Enter Via VIP Entry Of Parliament With A Knife In Hand, Nabbed

The police have taken the accused for questioning and are attempting to find out whether the state of mind of the accused is fine or not; and also if it was a genuine conspiracy.

As per reports, the accused was on a bike and carried a knife in his hand. He tried to enter premises via Gate no. 1 which is meant for the VIPs like President and Prime Minister. As per sources, the accused, who is a resident of Laxmi Nagar, East Delhi; was chanting slogans for rape and murder accused self styled godman Gurmeet Ram Rahim.
