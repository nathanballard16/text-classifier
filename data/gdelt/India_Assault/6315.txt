Spanish tourist injured while taking selfie

A Spanish tourist was injured Thursday after he fell from a height of nearly 40 feet while clicking a selfie at Jaipur's Amber Fort, police said.

The tourist, 27-year old Marquez Gonzalez, was visiting the fort along with his mother. He fell nearly 40 feet down the wall he was standing near, they said.

He did not receive any grave injury and was rushed to a nearby hospital for primary treatment, police said.
