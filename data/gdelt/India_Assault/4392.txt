Pune: Man held for murdering domestic help

PUNE: A 40-year-old woman succumbed to injuries in the Sassoon General Hospital on Wednesday, a day after her employer severely assaulted her at Talegaon Dabhade on Tuesday night.The Pune rural police identified the deceased as Sharada Jadhav of Kamshet . Jadhav worked as a domestic help at his house.“Few days ago, Jadhav had a brawl with Bhagwane over her payment,” said an officer from the Talegaon Dabhade police.The officer said they had a brawl over the same issue again on Tuesday night. Her 51-year-old employer, Sudhir Tilak Bhagwane, was arrested for murder under Section 302 of the Indian Penal Code after his landlord, Ramdas Bhosale, lodged a complaint with the Talegaon Dabhade police.The police said Bhagwane stayed on rent in a room at Talegaon Dabhade. “Bhagwane attacked Jadhav with a sharp weapon. He slit her throat and assaulted her on her face, neck and ribs,” the officer said.Jadhav was first taken to a private hospital at Somatane Phata, and then shifted to the Sassoon General Hospital.“Our probe is on,” the officer said.
