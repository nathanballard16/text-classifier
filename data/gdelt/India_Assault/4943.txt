Juvenile girl abducted from police custody at Mayo Hospital

Nagpur: In an audacious act, a man abducted a juvenile girl from the custody of police at Mayo Hospital on Wednesday night. The Board granted remand of the juvenile girl and ordered to take her to Children’s Remand Home, Katol Road. Both the woman police and the juvenile girl were standing in front of Casualty Department around 7 pm. But before taking her to the Remand Home, the woman police sepoy took her to Mayo Hospital for medical check-up. The juvenile girl was brought to city from Kondhali by Woman Police Sepoy Joshna Bhajanrao Dhurve (26), attached to Kondhali Police Station on Wednesday for producing her in Juvenile Justice Board.
