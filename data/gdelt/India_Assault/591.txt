Businessman in Lucknow injures after being shot by bike-born assailants

Lucknow (Uttar Pradesh), Jan 13 (ANI): A businessman in Uttar Pradesh’s Lucknow was injured after being shot by three bike-born assailants. The man was injured in his leg and was taken to a nearby hospital. Police investigation is underway. Vikas Chandra Tripathi, Additional Superintendent of Police said, “In Raniganj area a man, Lalit Sadhwani, was shot in his leg while he was sitting in his shop. He was taken to trauma centre. Investigation in the case is going on.”
