Minor Girl Sexually Assaulted In Hyderabad

Hyderabad: A minor girl was allegedly sexually assaulted in the city limits on Thursday. The victim, a Class VIII student was sexually assaulted by a man, who has absconded after the incident which took place under Jubilee Hills police station limits.

The girl narrated the entire ordeal to her parents, who filed a complaint with the police. Based on the complaint, the police have registered a case and are investigating further. A manhunt has been launched to nab the accused.

Last month, a similar incident was reported when a three-year girl was sexually abused by the helpers at a daycare centre in Hyderabad.
