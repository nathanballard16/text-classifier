Rape survivor to seek abortion nod

RAJKOT: The 14-year-old rape survivor of Wankaner in Morbi district will approach court to abort her foetus.According to police, the mentally challenged girl was allegedly raped multiple times by accused Sangar Danagar, who is now on police remand. The parents of the pregnant girl, who wants her to abort the foetus, seeks court’s nod as it is a medico legal case.
