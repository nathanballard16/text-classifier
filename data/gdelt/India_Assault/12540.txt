Mamata urges PM to take action on Unnao incident

Kolkata: West Bengal Chief Minister Mamata Banerjee on Monday criticised the BJP government at the Centre for failing to ensure the safety of the Unnao rape victim. Banerjee urged Prime Minister Narendra Modi to form a high-level inquiry committee to probe the survivor's accident. The prime minister should take action. The prime minister should look after the country," she said. The mother of the rape survivor has termed the accident a conspiracy to wipe out her family.
