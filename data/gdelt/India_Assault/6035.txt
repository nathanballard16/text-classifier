Dalit man beaten up, his house vandalized

RAJKOT: A Dalit man was beaten up and his house in Ramnathpara of Navyugpara locality in Rajkot vandalized on Thursday night.Police said the accused, Jafar Makrani and his two unidentified associates, barged into the house of Bharat Chauhan, beat him up and vandalized his house and autorickshaw.“Makrani was sentenced to jail for allegedly murdering Chauhan’s mother Lakshmi . Out on parole he wanted to take revenge against Chauhan, and thus he attacked him,” said an official from Rajkot A division police station.Meanwhile, police have launched a manhunt for Makrani and his associates and deployed a bandobast in Navyugpara locality.
