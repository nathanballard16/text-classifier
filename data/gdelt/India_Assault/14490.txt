SoCal Woman, Upset that her Convicted Rapist is Out on Bail, Claims SF Indian Consulate Lax on Support for Women’s Justice

Jennipha-Lauren Nielsen, who was allegedly sexually assaulted in New Delhi in 2013 by Rajeev Panwar, nephew of former Delhi mayor Jayshree Panwar, has posted a viral video to Facebook excoriating the San Francisco Indian Consulate for not helping her in fighting against her attacker, who was convicted to a seven-year prison term but was granted bail on appeal.
