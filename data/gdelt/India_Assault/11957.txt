SSKM security staff assaulted by family members of patient

Kolkata: A security staff at the SSKM Hopsital was allegedly assaulted by family members of a patient on Thursday morning. According to sources, on Thursday morning a patient identified as Narayan Chandra Bagchi from Howrah was brought to SSKM Hospital in critical condition. After reaching the emergency ward, security staff were asked to provide a trolley. It is alleged that the security person took a lot of time to bring the trolley whereas security person claimed that he brought the trolley within 2-3 minutes. Later, they were produced at the Bankshall Court and the person who had allegedly slapped the security person has been remanded to judicial custody.
