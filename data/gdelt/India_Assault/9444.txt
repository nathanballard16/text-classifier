Body without a head found near Jahangirpuri Metro Station in New Delhi

On June 8, 2019, the Delhi Police found a woman’s dead body without a head near the Jahangirpuri Metro Station in New Delhi.

The Delhi Police found the dead body of the woman wrapped in a blanket, which was placed inside a metal trunk that was placed on a cycle.

The Delhi Police believe that the woman was killed 2-3 days ago as her body was starting to decompose.

The police added that the woman’s skin has also turned black.

The dead body has been sent to Babu Jagjivan Ram Memorial Hospital.

A case has been registered and a team has been formed to identify the woman and to find out who is responsible for her death.
