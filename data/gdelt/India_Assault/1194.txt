HIV positive man arrested for sexually assaulting nineyearold

Surat: A 35-year-old HIV positive man was arrested by cops from an area under the limits of Chowk Bazaar police station for sexually assaulting a nine-year-old girl here on Monday. The parents were not aware about the assault when the incident took place,” a police officer said. Police took the minor to hospital where she was given medication to prevent her from getting infected with HIV. Police arrested the accused, a father of two children, after the girl’s family lodged a police complaint. The accused, who is a tailor , called the girl to his house to take measurement for stitching her clothes, but sexually assaulted her on Sunday.
