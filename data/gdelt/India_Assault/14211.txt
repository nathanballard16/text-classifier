False marriage promise: Youth held for sexually exploiting girl

Hyderabad: A man promised to marry a girl and exploited her sexually.

According to the report of Neredmet Police, Srikanth (30), resident of Safeelguda promised a girl in 2016 that he would marry her and started assaulting her sexually. He used to go to her house in the absence of her parents to assault her.

For the past two months, the youth stopped talking to her. Irritated over it, the girl made a complaint to the police.

Police registered a case against the youth for sexually assaulting the girl under the pretext of marriage. Police arrested the accused and presented him in the court. The magistrate gave him in judicial custody.
