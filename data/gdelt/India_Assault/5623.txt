Three history-sheeters arrested for rape attempt

RAJKOT: Three history-sheeters were arrested on Wednesday for an alleged rape attempt on a 28-year-old married woman in Riddhi Siddhi Society of Gujarat Housing Board Quarters in Rajkot on the night of February 6.The accused, identified as Imtiyaz Rauma alias Lalo (28), Shahezad Julani (23) and Imran Kandia (28), had allegedly brutally battered the woman when she resisted their rape attempt.“The trio was nabbed from Juna Kataria village of Bhachau taluka in Kutch district ,” said an official from Rajkot crime branch.According to the survivor’s complaint lodged with Rajkot women’s police station, Imtiyaz barged into her house along with his accomplices, and saying, “I want to have sex with you”, tore open her clothes.“Imtiyaz’s accomplices tried to tie the survivor to her bed in order to gang rape her. However, when she resisted the attack, the accused thrashed her with the butt of a country-made pistol. So badly was she beaten up that the cot she was tied to broke under her and the accused’s weight,” added the official.The accused had chosen to target the survivor when she was alone at home. Her husband, an autorickshaw driver, was away and she was alone with her three children.After the attack, when the survivor raised a cry and the neighbours rushed to her rescue, the accused fled from the spot. The survivor called her husband, who admitted her to Rajkot Civil Hospital. She alleged that Imtiyaz had been stalking her for the past one month.
