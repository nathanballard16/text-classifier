Woman alleges rape, cop’s son booked

Amritsar, June 17The Sadar police have booked a youth for allegedly raping a married woman after kidnapping her. The complainant alleged that the accused kept her in illegal detention in a flat in Chandigarh and raped her several times. The accused, identified as Lovepreet Singh, is said to be son of an ASI-rank official. Though the police have registered a case in this connection, the accused is yet to be arrested. “Though her family members alleged that he had gone out of the country, we have information that he hasn’t left.
