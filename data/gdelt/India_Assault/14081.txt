7-day jail term to MLA for assaulting woman

New Delhi, August 19A Delhi court has awarded seven-day jail term to Aam Aadmi Party MLA Manoj Kumar for assaulting a woman in 2014. In this case, the accused Manoj Kumar has been convicted for commission of offence of under Section 352 (punishment for assault or criminal force otherwise than on grave provocation) of the IPC. According to the prosecution, the woman had gone to the lawmaker, seeking redressal of water logging related problems. The convict, however, asked her not to disturb him and pushed the woman inappropriately. — PTICase FileThe woman had gone to the lawmaker Manoj Kumar (in pic), seeking redressal of problems relating water logging.
