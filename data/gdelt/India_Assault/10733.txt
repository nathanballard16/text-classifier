Mumbai Police files an FIR of rape against actor-producer Aditya Pancholi

As per latest ANI report, a rape case has been filed against actor-producer Aditya Pancholi in Mumbai's Versova police station. Mumbai Police booked the actor under a rape case based on the complaint of an actress at the Versova Police Station.Reportedly, the actress had said that Aditya Pancholi abused her when she was 17 years old and also claimed that she had went to the police when she was abused, but Aditya was apparently let off with just a warning.Aditya Pancholi's last major role in a Bollywood film was in Sanjay Leela Bhansali 's 'Bajirao Mastani ' in 2015.
