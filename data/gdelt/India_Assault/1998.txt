Group of hooligans go on rampage, damage police van, heckle cops in Mankapur

Nagpur: A group of 10-13 miscreants on Tuesday night clashed with each other and pelted stones on shops. Later the hooligans pelted stones at nearby shops on both sides of the road. When cops reached the spot, the hooligans damaged police van by smashing window glasses and a wireless set. Meanwhile, a team of Mankapur cops reached the spot, the miscreants pelted stones on police van (MH-31/DZ 0383) and smashed its window glasses.
