Police hunt for two vendors accused of rape

(The victim's identity has not been revealed to protect her privacy as per Supreme Court directives on cases related to sexual assault)

SURAT: A fruit trader was booked by police on Monday for raping a 12-year-old girl for over 18 months in Limbayat area of the city. The girl was also raped by a street vendor once for which he was booked on September 7.Both the accused are on the run and the girl, who is pregnant, is in the hospital police said. The girl is a class VII student whose father drives an autorickshaw and mother works in jari factory. She told police that the fruit vendor lured her by promising sweets and good food. She also told cops that a few months ago, Ramesh Chaha, another street vendor, who lived near her house, had also raped her. Medical test showed that the street vendor was responsible for her pregnancy.“The case against street vendor is being investigated by DCB. Both the culprits are on the run and have been booked for rape and kidnap under IPC and POCSO Act,” cops said.
