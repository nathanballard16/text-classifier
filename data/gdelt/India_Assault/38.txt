Over 200 injured land in city hosps

Out of 179 persons brought to SMS Hospital for treatment of injuries suffered in road accident, there were those who suffered serious injuries. According to Sawai Man Singh (SMS) Hospital, there were 179 persons, who were rushed to hospital’s Institute of Traumatology and Orthopedics (ITO) for injuries. Jaipur: More than 200 New Year revellers landed in hospitals due to road mishaps and other related injuries. A senior SMS Hospital official said that the number of patients brought to the hospital increased suddenly on the Monday-Tuesday midnight.He said that among those injured, majority of them were found under the influence of liquor. Hotels, pubs and restaurants were jam-packed on New Year eve with revellers, who gathered to welcome 2019.
