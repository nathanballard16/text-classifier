Indore: 4-year-old girl kidnapped and raped, body thrown in well

According to police officials the girl was kidnapped on Sunday night from a slum cluster here when her parents were asleep. (File photo: Getty Images)

A four-year-girl kidnapped from a slum in Indore was found dead in a well on Monday evening, a police official said.

The girl was kidnapped on Sunday night from a slum cluster in Indore when her parents were asleep, the official said.

"She was raped and killed and her body disposed off in a deep well here. We believe it was done by someone known to the family. The slum cluster has 36 homes and we are questioning people from the locality," Senior Superintendent of Police RV Mishra said.
