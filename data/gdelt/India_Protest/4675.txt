TCLP directs members not to vote in MLC poll

In the whip, Leader of Opposition in the Assembly Bhatti Vikramarka Mallu stated that the Indian National Congress party has decided to boycott the Biennial Elections for Telangana Legislative Council. Hyderabad: Telangana Congress Legislature Party issued a Whip to party legislators to abstain from voting in the Biennial Elections for Telangana Legislative Council on March 12 between 9 am to 4 pm, in the Committee Hall No.1 of Assembly building.
