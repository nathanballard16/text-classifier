AAP protests against Punjab Hry govts over stubble burning

New Delhi: The Aam Aadmi Party (AAP) on Thursday protested in front of Haryana and Punjab Bhawan against the continuous increase in incidents of stubble burning in the neighbouring states. The AAP also raised the issue of the silence of both theneighbouring state governments and the centre on the issue of the stubble burning. The protest was attendedby party MLAs and councillors, and headed by AAP Delhi convenor Gopal Rai. But as soon as stubble burning started in the states of Haryana Punjab, pollution levels in Delhi started to spike." He also said that due to the stubble burning the air quality of Delhi suddenly became severe and this also proves that stubble burning is a major factor of increasing pollution.
