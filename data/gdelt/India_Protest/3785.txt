PM Narendra Modi to hold rally in Rajasthan’s Tonk today

rchopra@tribunemail.com

Our Correspondent

Jaipur, February 23

Prime Minister Narendra Modi will start the BJP Lok Sabha campaign in Rajasthan with a rally at Tonk on Saturday afternoon.

Tonk is the home constituency of Deputy CM Sachin Pilot.

Security arrangements have been stepped up by Rajasthan Police and the intelligence agencies ahead of the rally.

The PM is likely to land at the Jaipur airport and head to Tonk in a helicopter.

His next rally is in Churu district on February 26.
