Pulwama attack: Journalists stage protest in Visakhapatnam

Vizag (Andhra Pradesh) [India], Feb 15 (ANI): Andhra Pradesh Working Journalists Federation on Friday held a protest against the ghastly Pulwama attack that took place on Thursday.

The protest was staged at the Gandhi statue here. Journalists condemned the attack and raised slogans against the terrorists.

Desk journalists Association President Janardhan said ,

"India should battle against terrorism. Every citizen should condemn the attack and should support jawans who were killed yesterday."

Around 40 Central Reserve Police Force (CRPF) personnel were killed after a suicide bomber attacked a CRPF convoy in Jammu and Kashmir's Pulwama district with a vehicle laden with explosives.

The convoy comprised 78 buses in which around 2,500 personnel were travelling from Jammu to Srinagar. The deceased jawans were travelling in a bus which came under the terror attack.

JeM, a Pakistan-based terrorist group, has claimed the responsibility for the attack which left the bus extensively damaged. (ANI)
