Bihar: Robbers loot passengers aboard New Delhi-Bhagalpur Express

Robbers on Wednesday looted passengers aboard 12350 New Delhi-Bhagalpur Express.

The unidentified robbers reportedly looted cash worth Rs 25 Lakh, jewellery and mobile phones from passengers. The incident happened between Dhanauri- Kajra in Lakhisarai district.

Moreover, some passengers were thrashed for resisting the robbers.

The incident came into light after a passenger’s relative reached out to the Indian Railways for help through Twitter.

Subsequently, Railway Protection Force, Eastern Railway directed the concerned unit to take necessary action in this regard.

More details are awaited.
