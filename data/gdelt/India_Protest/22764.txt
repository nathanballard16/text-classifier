CAA protest: Thousands take to street in Morbi

Rajkot: Thousands of agitators took to the street protesting against the Citizen Amendment Act (CAA) in Wankaner of Morbi district on Thursday.

The rally was led by Wankaner MLA Mohemmed Peerzada. “We submitted a memorandum addressed to the president to mamlatdar in which we have urged the president to revoke CAA and not to implement National Register of Citizens (NRC) of India bill,” said Peerzada.

In Morbi, a group of 40 to 50 people submitted a memorandum to the district collector, demanding revocation of the CAA and NRC.
