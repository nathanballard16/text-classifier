Security guard beaten up by robbers

RAJKOT: Four unidentified robbers beat up a security guard at an automobile showroom in Hapa GIDC of Jamnagar on Saturday night.“The robbers had come to loot the showroom and when they made noise, it woke up Ratansinh Vaghela. The robbers attacked him with bats and fled when locals of the area rushed to the spot,” said an official from Panchkoshi B-Division police station.“We have registered the case and started searching for the culprits with the help of CCTV footage,” added the official.
