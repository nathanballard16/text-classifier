BSNL contractual workers stage protest, demand 7-month salary

Tribune News ServiceAmritsar, June 13Contractual workers of BSNL on Thursday staged a protest demanding payment of their salary pending for the past seven months. Jaskiran Singh Jaj, general secretary, BSNL Causal and Contract Workers’ Union, said the employees were informed on June 9 that their contract had expired. Now, the contract employees are demanding their pending salaries ,” he said.
