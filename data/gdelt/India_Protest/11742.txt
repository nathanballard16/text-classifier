Unrest at Kakatiya University, students seek re-evaluation

WARANGAL: Kakatiya University remained tense on Saturday after students damaged property protesting over alleged irregularities in the evaluation of scores in the varsity’s degree exams.Students along with members of Akhil Bharatiya Vidyarthi Parishad (ABVP) took out a rally from Naimnagar to the campus before staging a dharna at the administrative building. Police were at the scene..The students alleged that many had failed because of technical errors made by the examination branch and sought free revaluation. The university said the issues would be resolved at the earliest.
