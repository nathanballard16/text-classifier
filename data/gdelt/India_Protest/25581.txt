CAA Protest News: Congress to protest in all localities

HYDERABAD: Telangana Congress minorities cell chairman Shaik Abdullah Sohail on Sunday said the Congress would organise protests and flash protests against CAA and NRC in all localities across the state.Speaking to mediapersons after participating in a protest at Prabhat Nagar, Abdullah Sohail said the minorities cell of the Congress will organise protests in localities, lanes and bylanes as well to create awareness on the dangers of ‘anti-constitutional’ CAA and NRC. He said Modi is practicing the British policy of 'divide and rule' to divert people's attention from its failures.Sohail strongly condemned the TRS government’s silence on CAA, NRC and NPR.
