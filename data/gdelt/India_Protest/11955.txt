Congress leader Jyotiraditya Scindia says supports govt`s move on J&K

Though Congress leader have supported the bill but he raised question on the process that has been followed by govt.

Congress Leader Jyotriditya Scindia on Tuesday supported the government`s move of revoking Article 370 and bifurcation of Jammu & Kashmir into Union Territories.

Congress Party has widely protested against the center`s decision on Jammu and Kashmir in both the houses on Monday and Tuesday.

"I support the move on #JammuAndKashmir & #Ladakh and its full integration into union of India." Scindia wrote on twitter

Though Congress leader supported the bill but he raised question on the process that was followed by the govt.

"Would have been better if the constitutional process had been followed. ", he wrote

Congress leader`s tweet read

I support the move on #JammuAndKashmir & #Ladakh and its full integration into union of India.



Would have been better if constitutional process had been followed. No questions could have been raised then. Nevertheless, this is in our countrys interest and I support this. — Jyotiraditya M. Scindia (@JM_Scindia) August 6, 2019

Union Home Minister Amit Shah on Monday proposed the bill to revoke Article 370 from J&K, he also proposed the bifurcation Of J&K in two Union Territories in which Ladakh and Jammu & Kashmir will exist as two UTs

Earlier, Congress veteran Janardan Dwivedi, Deepender Hooda and Jaiveer Shergill has supported the government on Article 370
