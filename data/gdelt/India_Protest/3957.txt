Students hold voter awareness rally in Kakinada

Kakinada: A voter awareness meet was organised at Pragati Degree College here on Monday under the joint aegis of Systematic Voter Education& Electoral Participation (SVEEP), Malireddy Charitable Trust and 90.8 FM Radio in coordination with Election Commission of India (ECI).

Addressing the meet, Pragati College principal D Satyanarayana advocated for electing right and capable person without getting tempted for favours.

The college principal asked the voters particularly new voters and youngsters not to get tempted for favours in exercising their franchise.

Your choice of right and capable person is crucial for democracy, Satyanarayama observed.

Later, a rally was organised with college students.
