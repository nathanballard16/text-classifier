Union Home Minister Amit Shah to hold rallies in Haryana today

Union Home Minister Amit Shah on Wednesday will address rallies in Haryana.

He will address public meetings in Faridabad, Samalkha, Bahadurgarh and Gurugram.

PM Modi was in Haryana yesterday and held two rallies in Kurukshetra and Charkhi Dadri.

Political campaigns have been intensified and parties are trying hard to reach out to electorate in Haryana.

Haryana will vote on October 21 and the counting of votes will take place on October 24.
