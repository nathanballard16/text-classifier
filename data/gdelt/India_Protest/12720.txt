Indian minority groups stage protest outside London's Parliament Square to show solidarity with Kashmiris

Demonstrators condemned India's decision to change to Kashmir's status as an autonomous state.

LONDON (Dunya News) – Various religious minority groups from India stage protest outside London s Parliament Square on Sunday to show solidarity with Kashmiris, Dunya News reported.

The demonstration was held from the platform of the London-based South Asia Solidarity Group in front of a Gandhi s statue installed in front of Parliament Square.

The demonstrators strongly condemned Indian government s decision to change to Kashmir s status as an autonomous state. They also rejected Indian move to split Occupied Kashmir into two union territories.
