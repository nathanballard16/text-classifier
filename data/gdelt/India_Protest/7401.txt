PM on Monday conducted an aerial survey in Odisha to assess the loss and destruction caused by Cyclone Fani in Odisha.

PM Modi, is scheduled to hold three rallies later in West Bengal and Jharkhand.

Governor Ganeshi Lal, CM Naveen Patnaik and Union Minister Dharmendra Pradhan were also present with PM Modi during the aerial survey.
