FIR filed against Delhi firm in NASA trip case

The police claimed that a case had been registered after a probe. A case under Sections 420, 406 and 120-B of the IPC had been registered against all the accused at the Civil Lines police station. The action has been taken after the continuous pressure was built by the parents of the students’ parents, who had earlier also staged a protest outside the school main gate. Further, they claimed that the school had prompted the parents of students for the participation of their wards in this tour. The Deputy Commissioner had marked the probe to the SSP, Bathinda, into this matter and now after the probe, a case had been registered against company officials.
