Family protests as hospital delays autopsy

TIRUPUR: Relatives and neighbours of a 40-year-old man, who died of cardiac arrest on Friday, staged a protest at the Palladam Government Hospital after the hospital authorities allegedly delayed the postmortem process.The victim, K Saminathan of Pongalur, was working in a knitwear unit at Kaliappa Nagar in Palladam. He had collapsed at a tea shop on Friday morning. Though Saminathan was rushed to the GH, he was declared brought dead.His kin and neighbours staged a protest after doctors said that the time for postmortem process was over. A police team reached the spot and pacified the protesters. Subsequently, postmortem was completed before 6pm.
