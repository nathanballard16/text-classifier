DoT allots Airtel, Jio, Voda Idea 5G spectrum to showcase use cases

NEW DELHI: The telecom department on Wednesday assigned spectrum for Vodafone Idea,and Reliance Jio to demonstrate India-specific use cases of fifth generation mobile network technology, or 5G, together with their vendor partners at India Mobile Congress in New Delhi next week.Chinese Huawei and ZTE have also been invited."All telecom service providers and original equipment manufacturers and academic institutions willing to showcase their 5G technologies at IMC- 2019 are hereby directed to coordinate and share spectrum in the frequency bands 3.4GHz-3,6GHz as well as 26-28GHz during the event," DoT's Wireless Planning & Coordination Wing (WPC) in a notification order Wednesday said.Cellular Operators Association of India (COAI) which has been demanding the spectrum for demonstration, has also been directed to closely coordinate with participants willing to showcase relevant use cases.The wireless division has despatched notification copies to multiple recipients such as Bharti Airtel, Vodafone Idea, Reliance Jio, Ericsson, Huawei, Nokia, Samsung , and ZTE as well as academic institutions such as the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Chennai and IIT Delhi. The Delhi-based lobby group has asked the government to allocate 5G airwaves to telecom carriers and their vendor partners to showcase country-specific use cases based on the next-generation of technology during the mega three-day event starting October 14. However, the fullfledged 5G trials are yet to be kickstarted in India.
