Tens of thousands attend funeral prayers of Zakir Musa in occupied Kashmir

All shops, business establishments and schools are closed in the valley.

SRINAGAR (Web Desk): In occupied Kashmir, a sea of people participated in the funeral prayers of prominent martyred mujahid commander, Zakir Musa, in Tral area of Pulwama district, today.

Defying restrictions and braving rains, thousands of people from various areas of Pulwama district and other parts of the Kashmir valley have reached Noorpora area of Tral to participate in the last rites of Zakir Musa

The participants of the funeral are raising high-pitched pro-freedom and anti-India slogans.
