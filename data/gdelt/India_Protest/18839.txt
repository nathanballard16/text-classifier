Uttar Pradesh: SVDV students take out torch procession

VARANASI: Continuing their agitation against the appointment of Dr Firoz Khan in faculty of Sanskrit Vidya Dharma Vigyan, the students took out a torch procession from the BHU main gate on Wednesday evening.The procession, beginning from the main gate in Lanka area moved to Sant Ravidas gate and returned to the BHU gate before dispersing. “Our agitation would continue till our demands are met,” said a student Shashikant Mishra.Even after calling-off their 16-day dharna on November 22, the agitating students continued to hold programmes and boycott classes.
