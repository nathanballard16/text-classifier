Female housekeeping staff commits suicide inside school campus in Noida

Noida: A 36-year-old female housekeeping staff at Delhi Public School in sector 30 of Noida allegedly committed suicide inside school campus on Thursday evening, said police. However, deceased's kin suspected foul play and staged a protest outside school after placing the body at school's main gate on Friday. Rakesh Kumar, deceased's husband said that the woman had gone to school for Independence Day celebrations but didn't return home till evening. "She was supposed to reach home by 12 but when she didn't return home till evening, we began searching her. When we asked the security supervisor at school he searched the campus and found her hanging from the tree inside the school campus.
