Forces responding to terror groups in language they understand: Maurya

Varanasi: Deputy chief minister Keshav Prasad Maurya said that Indian forces were responding to the challenge being posed by terror groups like Jaish-e-Mohammad (JeM) in the language they understand.Talking to reporters at Circuit house here after leading Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP) Vijay Sankalp Yatra carried out as party’s nationwide programme on Saturday, Maurya hailed the action of the Indian forces and said that “each and every countryman is proud of the soldiers for teaching bitter lessons to terror groups”.He said , “Terror groups like JeM are now being taught lessons in the same language they understand.”Regarding reaction of opposition parties, he said, “Countrymen are losing faith in these parties due to their stand. Prime Minister Narendra Modi has made it clear that he cares for each and every countrymen and also soldiers.”Before the arrival of Maurya, hundreds of BJP leaders and workers had gathered in Shivpur area. On reaching there, Maurya joined them after which Vijay Sankalp Yatra procession was carried out. After passing through various localities, the procession culminated near the statue of Swami Vivekanand in Nadesar area.
