Lekhi protests against poor supply of water

Delhi Jal Board says BJP politicising issueTribune News ServiceNew Delhi, June 11Meenakshi Lekhi, MP from New Delhi parliamentary constituency, on Tuesday protested outside the Delhi Jal Board’s RK Puram office against poor water supply in her constituency. She claimed that even municipal areas were facing water shortage and many places in her constituency lacked piped water supply. “Lady Hardinge staff quarters, upper regions of Anand Parbat and Baljit Nagar lack piped water supply. In the areas of Naraina, Krishna Nagar and Arjun Nagar, which have piped water supply, there’s no water for the past 10-15 days,” Lekhi said. The entire country is reeling under a severe heat wave and facing water scarcity.
