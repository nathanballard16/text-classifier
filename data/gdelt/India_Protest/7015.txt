Bogus voting in Kadi village: FIR lodged

PALANPUR: Kadi police in Mehsana has registered a FIR for bogus voting, robbery and assault during polling on Tuesday , based on complaint lodged by two Congress office bearers. The accused in the case are yet to be identified, said police. Two Congress advocates Harshad Rawal and Rajkishan Barot of Mehsana were attacked and their gold chain and Rs 10,000 cash looted on April 23. The duo were rescued by the cops on poll duty in Tundal village of Kadi taluka. Police has registered complaint under section 395, 143, 323, 504, 427 and 506(2) of IPC.
