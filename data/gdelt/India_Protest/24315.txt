Pak woman granted Indian citizenship in Poonch

POONCH, Dec 23: Amid raging country-wide protests against Citizenship Amendment Act (CAA), a Pakistani woman was granted Indian citizenship on the basis of her marriage to an Indian citizen.

District Development Commissioner Poonch, Rahul Yadav Monday handed over a Certificate of Registration granting Indian citizenship to Khatija Praveen wife of Mohammad Taj, a resident of Poonch. Khatija Praveen was born in Pakistan.

She was granted Indian Citizenship on the basis of marriage to an Indian under section 5(1) (c) of the Citizenship Act, 1955.

Khatija Praveen along with her husband expressed happiness and gratitude on receiving the Certificate of Registration from Government of India, Ministry of Home Affairs, New Delhi, said a government spokesperson.
