Hyderabad: Protests erupt late night at Maulana Azad National Urdu University

Hyderabad: Protests erupted on Sunday night at the Maulana Azad National Urdu University in the city following the incidents at Jamia Milia university in Delhi.Students of the university at Gachibowli showed their anger by setting ablaze effigies of the government. They raised slogans against the government and the police for resorting to violence against students in Jamia Milia.Though the students had planned a protest against CAB for Monday, the turn of events in Delhi made them come out of the MANUU and resort to protests in front of the main entrance. The protestors were also joined by some students of the University of Hyderabad.Earlier during the day, thousands of people protested against CAB at Indira Park in the city.
