Intermediate Public Exams begin

Hyderabad: Intermediate Public Examinations started on a peaceful note across the State on Wednesday. The first year examinations commenced at 9 am and will conclude at 12 pm. A total 9,42,719 students, including 4,52,550 first year and 4,90,169 second year students, are expected to sit for exams which will be held till March 18.

The Board of Intermediate Education has made elaborate arrangements for smooth conduct of the exams. A total of 1,227 examination centers have been constituted with 24,508 invigilators. All the centers have been equipped with CCTV surveillance system.
