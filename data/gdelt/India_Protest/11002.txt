Irate Mahila Congress activists burn BJP effigy

A+ A-The Mahila Congress leaders of the State on Saturday burnt an effigy of UP Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath in front of Gandhi Bhavan city condemning the arrest of party leader Priyanka Gandhi. The Mahila Congress activists raised slogans against Prime Minister Narendra Modi and UP Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath as part of the protest program.
