Oppn unites, to protest ‘murder of democracy’

NEW DELHI: Opposition parties will protest against what they have said is a “murder of democracy” in J&K at Jantar Mantar on August 22. The street protest, the first since Parliament revoked the special status of J&K, will see all major political parties join forces to demand the immediate release of Kashmiri politicians in “illegal detention” for over two weeks.While the call for the protest at Jantar Mantar on August 22 was given by DMK chief MK Stalin, Lok Sabha MP TR Baalu said Congress, CPM, CPI, TMC and India Union Muslim League, among others, have confirmed their participation in the protest.
