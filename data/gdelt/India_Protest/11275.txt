CPI to protest over rising power tariff

Ludhiana, July 25The Communist Party of India is set to launch an agitation against the rising power tariff. During the meeting, CPI leaders blamed the government for failing to control the rising electricity prices. “The government is looting residents by increasing the power tariff. In addition, some unnecessary taxes, such as corporation tax and cow cess are putting burden on the people’s pocket,” they alleged. They said soon, the party would launch an agitation against the Punjab Government and would force the government to slash power tariff.
