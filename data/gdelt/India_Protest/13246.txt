School security guard molests KG student parents stage protest

Kolkata: Guardians staged protests outside an English medium private school in Hooghly's Sreerampur on Tuesday morning after a security guard of the school allegedly molested a KG I student during the school hours. The family members of the schoolchildren held the school authorities responsible for the incident. They also blamed the authorities for appointing a security guard who indulges in such a barbaric act. The accused security guard was taken to the local police station and detained for interrogation. Parents of the students have urged school authorities to take strict action against the accused.
