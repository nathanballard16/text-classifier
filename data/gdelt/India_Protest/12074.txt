Ex boxer heckled for stopping youths from drinking at public place

Kolkata: A former boxer from Shibpur in Howrah was heckled along with his son by some drunken youths after the victim protested against their drinking habits at a public place. The accused youths then proposed Samanta to have some liquor with them. When Samanta's son came out to rescue him, the accused youths threatened them with dire consequences. The local residents and the victim's family members staged a protest in the area questioning how the miscreants can drinking at a public place in and create nuisance in the area, which is so close to the state secretariat. The family members lodged a complaint with the police.
