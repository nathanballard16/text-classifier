Congress holds protest against screening of ‘The Accidental Prime Minister’ in Kolkata

Kolkata (West Bengal), Jan 11 (ANI): Congress workers in West Bengal’s Kolkata are protesting against Anupam Kher starrer ‘The Accidental Prime Minister’ outside cinema halls. The protestors demanded that the movie should not be shown as it shows former prime minister Manmohan Singh in bad light. The protestors also shouted slogans supporting Manmohan Singh. The movie has hit the theatres today.
