SAD councillors cancel protests against MC

Tribune News ServiceLudhiana, July 3After a meeting with Mayor Balkar Singh Sandhu, SAD councillors canceled their upcoming two more protests against the Ludhiana Municipal Corporation. The SAD councillors had staged protests at Zone A and B against the civic body for allegedly ignoring their respective wards last month. Now, we have decided to cancel our protests as the Mayor has accepted our demands.” SAD councillor from ward no. 2 Gurmail Singh Jajji said, “The Mayor has assured us that the development works will be started soon in our wards. I asked the councillors to tell me which official has refused to do work in their respective wards.
