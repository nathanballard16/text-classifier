Akhilesh to meet injured SP men on Feb 23

SP district spokesperson, Daan Bahadur, “He would be meeting leaders who were injured in the police lathi-charge during Tuesday’s protests.” Meanwhile, a delegation of Bahujan Samaj Party (BSP) leaders, on Thursday, also met the injured SP leaders including AUSU president Uday Prakash Yadav. Allahabad: Three days after he was stopped from boarding a flight to the city to attend an Allahabad University Students Union (AUSU) event which triggered violent protests, Samajwadi Party (SP) national president and ex-chief minister Akhilesh Yadav will be in the city on February 23.
