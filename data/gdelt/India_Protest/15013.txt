RBI hikes withdrawal cap to Rs 10,000 from Rs 1,000 for PMC Bank customers

The Reserve Bank of India (RBI) on Thursday increased the withdrawal limit for depositors in Punjab and Maharashtra Cooperative Bank (PMC) to Rs 10,000 from Rs 1,000 earlier. “The relaxation has been granted with a view to reducing the hardship of the depositors. The Reserve Bank is closely monitoring the position and shall continue to take further steps as are necessary to safeguard the interest of the depositors of the bank,” the RBI said in a statement.
