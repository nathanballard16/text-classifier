Air Force Day 2019: Spectacular Flying Display at Hindon Base

The Indian Air Force (IAF) is celebrating its 87th birthday today (October 08) with a spectacular air display that will include vintage and modern transport aircraft and frontline fighters. The air display commenced with flag bearing skydivers of the famous Akash Ganga team dropping out of an AN-32 aircraft in their colourful canopies. (Image: ANI)
