Anti CAA protesters gather at India Gate

1(68 Words) Download Download PrintPrint 12/20/2019 5:43:00 PMNew Delhi, Dec 20 (PTI) Scores of people gathered at the India Gate here to voice their dissent against the new citizenship law and a proposed countrywide National Register of Citizens (NRC). Raising slogans of 'Azaadi', 'No NRC, No CAA', the protesters, most of them college students, demanded that the new law be repealed. The national capital has witnessed a slew of protests against the amended citizenship law. (Image taken from The Indian Express)
