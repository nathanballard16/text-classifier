Protest in Srinagar against thrashing of Sikh driver in Delhi

In occupied Kashmir, members of Sikh community held a protest demonstration in Srinagar against the thrashing of a Sikh driver and disrespect of Sikh symbols in New Delhi.

The protestors appealed international human rights bodies to intervene into the matter. They also urged the Indian government to take strict action against the people who hurt religious sentiments.

Similar protest demonstration against the incident was held by Sikh and Muslim youth in Tral area of Pulwama, yesterday.
