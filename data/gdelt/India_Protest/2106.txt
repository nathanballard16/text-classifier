Congress to protest on Monday against Hindu Mahasabha members for firing at Mahatma Gandhi's effigy

The incident took place in a house in Naurangabad locality of the city where the Mahasabha had announced that it would observe Gandhi's death anniversary as Shauraya Diwas.

Other than Rajghat, Congress leaders are also expected to stage demonstrations near Mahatma Gandhi’s statue in the premises of Parliament.

The Congress party has announced a nationwide protest on Monday against the Hindu Mahasabha members who performed an objectionable act of shooting the effigy of Mahatma Gandhi on his death anniversary on January 30. The protest is slated to begin at 10 am on Monday at Rajghat, where the memorial dedicated to Mahatma is situated. As per sources, Congress president Rahul Gandhi is also expected to take part in the protest.
