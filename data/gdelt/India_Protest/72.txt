CPI to protest for AP special status at Jantar Mantar

New Delhi: CPI to hold protest against Modi government for not fulfilling the AP special category status promises. The protest will be held on 3rd and 4th January at Jantar Mantar in New Delhi.

PM Modi has done injustice to AP people by not announcing the decision to grant the special status, its the misfortune of the people of this country to have Modi as Prime Minister, said AP CPI Secretary K Rama Krishna.

To the comments made by Modi that only a few farmers are taking farm loans, to this RK reverted back that, in order to escape from farm loan waivers, Modi is making such statements.
