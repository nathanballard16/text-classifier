Gang of robbers busted in Badlapur, four held

Police claimed to have busted a gang of robbers that allegedly committed more than 15 robberies across Thane and Mumbai. The gang would steal jewellery and bring it to a “safe house” in Badlapur, added police. Police said the accused were using an empty house as a safe place for their loot for over two years. AdvertisingAccording to Badlapur police, the accused were identified as Mobil Salim Shaikh (28), Dipak Jadhaw (28), Chetan Waghere (25) and Hitesh Solanki (27). Police said Shaikh, the leader of the gang, smuggled jewellery to his wife in West Bengal, where she sold them and sent back the money.
