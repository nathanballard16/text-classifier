Mamata extends solidarity to Central govt employees in Bengal facing dire straits

"Members of bank unions, Air India officials and employees from other Central government organisations have come and met me. We will hold a meeting with the Central government employees on September 23 at this venue," Banerjee said, while addressing an organisational meeting of the state government employees at Netaji Indoor Stadium. "They (the bank union) have already called a strike nationwide to protest against the Centre's decision," the Chief Minister added. Iurge my senior party leaders to organise a convention involving the suffering Central government employees of the state," the Chief Minister added. The Chief Minister further expressed her concern over the Centre's decision of disinvestment of 43 PSUs across the country that include Burns Standard and Bengal Chemicals in the state.
