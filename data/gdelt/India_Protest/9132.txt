Maharashtra Doctors on strike today !

Doctors, resident doctors and students of all medical colleges in Maharashtra will be up for the strike on 14th June. All the major organizations MARD , ASMI , IMA will participate in this agitation.

The organizations has started protesting against the attack on Kolkata-based residents and two intern doctors on 11th of this month.

Doctors in the hospital will be shutting down OPD services but it will not affect the emergency services.
