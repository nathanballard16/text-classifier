10 Pictures Of Women Leading Protests Around The World In 2019

2019 has been a year of turmoil around the world. India, Hong Kong, Brazil, Lebanon, Chile, Sudan, are among the countries which have seen citizen-led movements against ruling governments. Be it the protests against the Citizenship Amendment Act in India, or indigenous people’s movement against deforestation of the Amazon, women have been leading from the front. As we step into 2020, here are 10 photos of women standing up to the world to inspire you to get up and fight that good fight.
