Delhi: Jamia Millia Islamia students protest outside campus against CAA

The protest at the main gate of the university was started by women students and later men also joined them. (Photo: ANI)

NEW DELHI: Students of Jamia Millia Islamia on Saturday staged a protest outside the university campus against the Citizenship (Amendment) Act.

The protest at the main gate of the university was started by women students and later men also joined them.

They raised slogans such as "Ladke lenge azadi" and "Inquilab Zindabad".
