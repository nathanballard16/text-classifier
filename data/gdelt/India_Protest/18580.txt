Make education affordable to all Hundreds protest against fee hike

New Delhi: Hundreds of people took to the streets in Delhi on Saturday in support of the students' protest against the hostel and mess fee hike in Jawaharlal Nehru University. The protesters, including members of civil society, JNU alumna and students, raised slogans, asking the varsity administration to roll back the fee hike and the government to "make education affordable to all". Kanhaiya Kumar, former Jawahalal Nehru University Students' Union (JNUSU) president, said it is not a question of right or left but a question of right and wrong. The women and backward people will be hit due to this fee hike," he said. "It's not just a fight for our education, but also for the education of the children of police personnel and media persons.
