1984 Anti-Sikh Riot Accused Jagdish Tytler Attends Congress’ ‘Symbolic Protest’ Led By Priyanka Vadra

This protest which began at 4 PM is being led by Congress general secretary Priyanka Vadra. It was expected to take place for the next 2 hours.

Besides Tytler, also attending the protest were Congress protests were senior party leaders K C Venugopal, AK Antony, P L Punia and Ahmed Patel.

While speaking at the event Vadra claimed that the government has attacked the youth which is the soul of the nation. She also added that every Congress member will fight against this tyranny and stand for the students.
