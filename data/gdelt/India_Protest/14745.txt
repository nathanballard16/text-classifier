Locals protest against mobile tower installations

Ludhiana: Residents in Jawaddi protested against installation of a mobile tower on Saturday early morning.

The residents rued that a shopkeeper was allowing the installation of a mobile tower at terrace of his shop without taking their consent. They first tried to stop the ongoing work, but later had to call the cops. They continued their protest even after the installation work had been put on hold.

The residents came to know about the installation process in the morning and they gathered in the locality for protesting. The police reached the spot and stopped the installation. The residents warned that in case the installation started again, they will resume their protest.
