Anti-CAA stir: Despite pleas, protests at Red Fort continue

New Delhi, 19 Dec (IANS) Despite pleas from the police, anti-CAA protesters gathered near the Red Fort area of the capital, refusing to vacate the place.

Earlier, the police had detained around 120 protesters and transported them away in buses.

Those detained included, socio-political activist Yogendra Yadav who was held at Alipur area.
