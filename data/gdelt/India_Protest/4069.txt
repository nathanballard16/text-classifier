Congress calls off CWC meet amid border tensions

NEW DELHI: With post-Balakot tensions between India and Pakistan on the rise, Congress has called off the meeting of its apex decision-making body, Congress Working Committee, and a political rally that was to be held in Ahmedabad on Thursday.AICC spokesman Randeep Surjewala said Congress president Rahul Gandhi took the decision "in view of the prevailing security condition". Rahul also called off a lunch for opposition parties which was to precede their meeting on Wednesday.The CWC would be rescheduled soon but is unlikely to be held in Gujarat In early February, Congress planned the gathering on the home turf of Prime Minister and arch rival Narendra Modi as a way of conveying its confidence for 2019 elections.
