RMPI postpones protest after CM’s invitation for talks

Tribune News ServicePatiala, September 13The Revolutionary Marxist Party of India (RMPI) on Friday postponed its ongoing indefinite protest against the Punjab Government after receiving an invitation for talks with the Chief Minister (CM), Capt Amarinder Singh. A massive protest rally was held by the RMPI in the city today. Holding placards, hundreds of RMPI members, including farmers, marched towards the CM’s residence. However, heavy police force deployed near Fountain Chowk halted their march. “CM’s representatives gave us a letter in which he has invited us for meeting regarding our demands,” he added.
