Kashmiri, Pakistani community stage protest in Spain against Indian atrocities in IOJ&K

Kashmiri and Pakistani community in Spain lodged a protest in Barcelona to condemn the Indian atrocities in Occupied Kashmir.

The protestors said that Kashmiris have never accepted Indian annexation and its constitution.

Holding playcards and banners against Modi and India, the participants of the rally raised slogans in favour of the people of occupied Kashmir and urged the world community and United Nations to play their role for Kashmiris rights.

On the occasion, speakers said Kashmiris cannot be suppressed by force. They termed Indian annexation an insult to the resolutions of UN on the issue.
