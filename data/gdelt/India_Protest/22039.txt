Student protests against CAA continues for the third day in Chennai

Policemen are posted at the gates of the university campus at Chepauk preventing entry of any students or reporters. Kaarthikeyan told Express, "We weren't physically attacked or harassed by the cops. So we will continue to voice out for the revoking of CAA" said Yuvasri, student of Political science department.
