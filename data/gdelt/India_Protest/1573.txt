DMK stages protest outside Raj Bhavan seeking resignation of CM Palaniswami

Chennai (TN), Jan 24 (ANI): As the standoff over the Kodanad estate break-in continues, Dravida Munnetra Kazhagam (DMK) staged a protest in Chennai on Thursday. The protestors raised the slogans and demanded the resignation of Chief Minister Edappadi K Palaniswami over his alleged involvement in the case. The sprawling property was frequented by the late chief minister J Jayalalithaa when she was alive. DMK has already urged Purohit to direct Palaniswami to resign from his post to ensure a free and fair probe.
