Congress to hold protest over nagging civic issues

Vadodara: The city unit of the Congress has planned a protest on Wednesday at Khanderao Market to raise the civic woes in the city. Prominent state leaders will be attending the protest.

The city unit of the Congress has been holding protests over civic issues periodically, but it will be for the first time that a battery of state-level leaders will attend such an event.

The programme will be attended by state Congress president Amit Chavda, former union minister Bharatsinh Solanki, All India Congress Committee in-charge of Gujarat affairs of the party Rajiv Satav and others.

The city unit of the party has started a social media campaign regarding civic issues in the city. The ‘Janta Memo’ campaign encouraged citizens to post regarding civic woes in their areas. tnn
