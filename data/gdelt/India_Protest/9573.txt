DMK members stage protest in Coimbatore seeking water, detained

COIMBATORE: Police on Wednesday detained 512 DMK members, including Singanallur legislator N Karthik , for protesting in front of the Coimbatore City Municipal Corporation (CCMS) office. Of them, 118 were women. They were demanding regular distribution of drinking water.Karthik announced the protest earlier this week. On Wednesday morning, police denied permission for it. However, the members staged a protest despite that. They were detained and were lodged in a marriage hall.Karthik said they had protested demanding water supply once in three to four days. “Also, we seek resignation of minister for municipal administration S P Velumani as he has not taken proactive measures to handle the water situation,” he said.They also protested the municipal body giving the water supply rights to a private firm.
