UPSC aspirants stage protest outside BJP president Amit Shah’s residence

New Delhi, Jan 04 (ANI): Union Public Service Commission (UPSC) aspirants who have been staging protest in Delhi for the past few days, demonstrated outside Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP) president Amit Shah’s residence on Friday. They have raised two demands, namely, government should announce compensatory attempt with immediate effect and the students sitting on hunger should be treated humanely. These protestors are in rage as police have treated those sitting on hunger strike in an inhumane way and five of them have been detained. They have been raising demands for those who suffered due to the Civil Services Aptitude Test (CSAT) exam pattern.
