Health staff continue pen-down stir

Ludhiana, July 24Thousands of members of clerical staff of subordinate health offices continued their pen-down strike for third consecutive day and held a rally at the Civil Surgeon office here today. The state president of Punjab Health Department Subordinate Offices Clerical Association, Sukhwinder Singh, announced that in the wake of indifferent attitude of the government towards the employees, the strike had been extended for three more days.
