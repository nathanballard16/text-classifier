Pak-India FMs may come face to face in Abu Dhabi

UAE Foreign Minister has invited Sushma to participate in the OIC session as 'Guest of Honour'.

ISLAMABAD (Dunya News) - Foreign Minister Shah Mahmood Qureshi may come face to face with Indian External Affairs Minister Sushma Swaraj during the 46th Session of the Council of Foreign Ministers of the Organisation of Islamic Cooperation (OIC) in Abu Dhabi.

The 46th session of the Organisation of Islamic Cooperation’s (OIC) Council of Foreign Ministers will be held in Abu Dhabi on March 1st and 2nd where India has been invited as the Guest of Honour .

Sushma Swaraj will also address the Inaugural Plenary of the 46th Session of the Council of Foreign Ministers of the Organisation of Islamic Cooperation.

Foreign Minister of the United Arab Emirates (UAE) Sheikh Abdullah bin Zayed Al Nahyan has invited Indian External Affairs Minister Sushma Swaraj to participate in the session as Guest of Honour .
