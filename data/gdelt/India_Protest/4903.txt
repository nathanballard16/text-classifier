NEW DELHI: The Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) held Class 12 Biology exam on Friday, i.e., March 15, 2019. The exam was conducted in various centers across India and abroad.Most of students found the paper easy and scoring. They also said they could finish the exam before the given time. It was the easiest paper of the all paper so far.As the questions were directly from NCERT, exam was really easy said some students from Joseph and Mary school. Some students said section C was the easiest whereas some said section D was the easiest. Some of the students opined that revising last year question paper helped them to manage their time well during exam. We had plenty of time to revise, said students while sharing their happiness.
