CAA protests: Magisterial probe ordered into deaths of Mangalore protesters in police firing

'Aapke paas koi order hai?' Rahul asks cops after asked to return

Rahul Gandhi asked police if they had an order after he and Priyanka Gandhi was asked to return while they were on their way to Meerut to meet the kin of victims killed in anti-CAA stir.

ADG Meerut Prashant Kumar said, "144 imposed in Meerut, they (Rahul, Priyanka) were asked to return from Partapur."
