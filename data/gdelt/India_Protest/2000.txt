Budget 2019: Rahul Gandhi criticises farmers’ scheme introduced by FM Goyal

New Delhi, Feb 01 (ANI): Congress president Rahul Gandhi joined the Andhra Pradesh Chief Minister Chandrababu Naidu, who is on his Delhi visit to protest against PM Modi and other opposition parties. Rahul Gandhi criticised PM Kisan Samman Nidhi scheme introduced today in the interim budget. “You can waive off Rs 3.5 Lakh Crore loan of 15 people but give only Rs 17 per day to farmers! What else is this, if not an insult? Election will be fought keeping in mind issue of farmers, unemployment and attacks on institutions, it’ll also be on the issue of Rafale”, said Rahul Gandhi. He also said that the opposition leaders will got to Election Commision over EVMs.
