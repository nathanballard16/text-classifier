RSOS 10th result 2019 declared at rsosapp.rajasthan.gov.in

Rajasthan RSOS 10th open result 2019: The Rajasthan State Open School, Jaipur has declared the result of class 10 board exams conducted in open school mode today – June 15 (Tuesday). Students can access their score from the official websites, rsosapp.rajasthan.gov.in, rajasthan.result91.com, and eductaion.rajasthan.gov.in. This is the result for the exams held in March-May. The Rajasthan State Open School (RSOS) exams are conducted twice a year.

About the Rajasthan Board

Board of Secondary Education, Rajasthan (abbreviated BSER) is a board of education for school level education in the Rajasthan. RBSE is a state agency of the government of Rajasthan and has its headquarters in Ajmer. The Rajasthan State Open School is a branch of BSER and is located at the capital of the state, Jaipur.
