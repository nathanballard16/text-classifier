CAA protest in Delhi: Arson, stone pelting, baton charge sum up violent Friday in Delhi

The capital witnessed another day of violence in central and northeastern areas over the Citizenship (Amendment) Act on Friday. Policemen had a tough day in controlling protesters mainly in Daryaganj, Jama Masjid, Seelampur and Jaffrabad areas where incidents of arson and stone pelting were reported.
