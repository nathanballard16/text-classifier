Truck driver found dead on Gondal highway

Rajkot: Body of a 45-year-old truck driver with head injuries was found on the service road opposite market yard on Gondal national highway bypass early Tuesday morning.

The deceased was identified as Suresh Surela a native of Liyad village of Chuda taluka in Surendranagar.

According to the complaint lodged by the truck owner Manoj Sojitara (40), Surela’s body was found besides the truck early Tuesday morning with head injuries. The police also found a blood stained stone near the body. However, police said that Surela’s wallet, mobile phone and cash were intact, hence it did not appear to be a murder with intent to loot. Surela was returning after delivering cotton consignment at a ginning mill in Gondal from Dhoraji. Police has registered a murder case against an unknown person and begun investigation. TNN
