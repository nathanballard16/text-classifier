Archana to represent India at Special Olympics

Archana Jaivittal has been selected to represent India at the Special Olympics World Summer Games to be held in the U.A.E from March 14 to 21. Her father, Jaivittal, Estate officer of MAHE, said that Archana, an international swimmer, will be in Delhi from March 1 to 7 for the final preparatory camp.

She won two bronze medals at the Special Olympics Asian Pacific Games at Australia in 2014.

She is a recipient of the Udupi district ‘Karnataka Rajyotsava Award-2012’ for sports.

In 2015, she received the national award in role model category by the Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Department of Empowerment of Persons with Disabilities.
