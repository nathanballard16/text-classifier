Over 350 protesters detained outside UP Bhawan

New Delhi: Police detained over 350 protesters outside Uttar Pradesh Bhawan on Friday, as Jamia Millia Islamia (JMI) gave a call for demonstration against alleged police brutalities, over people protesting over Citizen Amendment Act (CAA) and NRC, in the state. However, as Section 144 was imposed in the area since morning, and anyone coming near the vicinity was detained. When the protesters didn't follow the directions of Police, 357 protesters were detained," said Additional DCP Deepak Yadav. As soon as the protesters reached Assam Bhawan, which is near UP Bhawan, the protesters were picked up by the police. The students were protesting against Uttar Pradesh's alleged police brutality on citizens when they were protesting against CAA and NRC.
