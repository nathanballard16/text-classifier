Qatar: Indian Social Forum helps to repatriate mortal remains of Nelyady resident died of stroke

Doha/Qatar, 22 Mar: Indian Social Forum – Qatar helped to repatriate mortal remains of a expat, native of Nelyady, in newly-formed Kadaba taluk, Dakshina Kannada district of Karnataka who died owing to cardiac arrest whilst working in Qatar on March 13.

Adam is the deceased whose mortal remains were repatriated to India by Indian Social Forum and last rites were held at Rahmania masjid burial ground, Arasinamakki, Karnataka on Wednesday, March 20.

He is survived by his wife and children. The grieving family thanked the Forum for their noble deed.
