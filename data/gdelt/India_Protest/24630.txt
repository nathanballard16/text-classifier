Students continue protests against CAA across Delhi

New Delhi: Students across natinal Capital continued protesting at various institutions over Citizenship Amendment Act (CAA) and National Register of Citizen (NRC) on Wednesday. Launched as 'Young India National Coordination Committee', this group of students' organisations aims to boost the campaign against CAA, NRC and National Population Register (NPR). Speaking at the press conference, Kawalpreet Kaur, Delhi University student and president of the left-leaning All India Students' Association (AISA), said, "Young India Coordination Committee will bring together students group as a coordinated unit. The students' organisations to join this collective include the All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS) Students' Union, All Adivasi Assam Students' Union, Ashoka University Student Government, Bhim Army Students Federation, Jamia Millia Islamia Joint Coordination Committee, Film and Television Institute of India Students Union, Students' Collective of Indian Institute of Technology-Delhi and Jawaharlal Nehru University Students' Union.
