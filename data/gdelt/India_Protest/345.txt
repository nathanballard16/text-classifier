BJD members leave for Delhi in special train to hold farmers rally

Bhubaneswar (Odisha), Jan 06 (ANI): A special train ‘Krashak Adhikar Yatra’ carrying Biju Janata Dal (BJD) members (MPs, MLAs and party workers) have left for Delhi on Saturday. BJD will hold a farmers’ rally at Delhi’s Talkatora Stadium on January 8 in order to protest against the central government. This train will reach at Delhi’s Hazrat Nizamuddin railway station by January 8. To step up pressure on centre to implement the Swaminathan Commission and also demand of higher Minimum Support Price (MSP) for paddy crop farmers BJD will protest against the central government.
