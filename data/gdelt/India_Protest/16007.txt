Senior political leaders addressing rallies to woo voters in Maharashtra

In Maharashtra, senior leaders from all major political parties are addressing election rallies, publishing manifestos and addressing press conferences as the polling day is approaching soon.

MIM chief Asaduddin Owaisi is scheduled to hold a rally in Mumbai. While Shivsena Chief Uddhav Thackeray will address the rally at Dindori in Nashik district this afternoon, Union Minister Ramdas Athawale is also on a campaign trail in the same district.
