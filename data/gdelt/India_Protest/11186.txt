Student unions protest ABVP ‘interference’ in varsity syllabus

Tribune News ServiceNew Delhi, July 23Various Delhi University (DU) student unions, including the All India Students Association (AISA) and the Students’ Federation of India (SFI), today held a protest against the Akhil Bharatiya Vidyarthi Parishad (ABVP). They staged a demonstration at arts faculty in North Campus of the Delhi University against ABVP’s growing pressure tactics and intolerance aimed at intimidating DU teachers. They condemned the DU administration’s action of encouraging ABVP members to threaten professors of english and history departments and disrupting the earlier held Academic Council meeting. In the Academic Council meeting held on July 15 and 16, the members were intimidated by ABVP members regarding the change in DU syllabus. The members sought to be brought into submission when they proposed changes, incorporations and papers in DU undergraduate syllabus.
