Anti citizenship law stir No fresh violence reported in WB

Kolkata: No fresh incident of violence over amended citizenship law was reported in West Bengal on Wednesday morning, police said. West Bengal Chief Minister Mamata Banerjee is scheduled to take part in a protest march against citizenship law from Howrah Maidan to Dorina Crossing in Esplanade area of Kolkata on Wednesday. The state has witnessed pitched protests against the citizenship law since December 13. Police has stepped up vigil across the state and as many as 354 protesters were arrested for violence. Several trains passing through West Bengal were either cancelled or short-terminated due to the protests.
