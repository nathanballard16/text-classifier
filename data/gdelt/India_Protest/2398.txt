Golden Temple pic on mat sparks stir

Ludhiana: Youth Akali Dal (YAD) workers led by district president Meetpal Singh Dugri staged protest outside the warehouse of Flipkart in Ludhiana for pasting the photo of Golden Temple on Foot mat on Tuesday. The Youth Akali Dal demanded registration of case against CEO of company for hurting religious sentiments of Sikh Community. The workers closed the door of warehouse and warned the company to apologize and remove photos.

Advocate Meetpal Singh Dugri said that the foot mat with photo of Golden Temple is available on Flipkart which has deeply hurt the sentiments of Sikh community. The company has designed the photo of Golden Temple on foot mat will not be tolerated at any cost. He said that it was the symbolic protest against the company but they will sharpen the agitation if police will not register case against company.

He further said that earlier also they had protested against Amazon company and forced the company to remove photo of Golden Temple from various items and made the company CEO to apologize.

Youth Akali Dal district president Meetpal Singh Dugri said that the Congress led Punjab Government has closed its eyes over the important and sensitive issues.
