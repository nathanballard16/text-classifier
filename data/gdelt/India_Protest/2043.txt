Kisan Sangarsh Committee holds protest against Budget

Amritsar (Punjab), Feb 02 (ANI): A group of farmers staged protest in Amritsar against the interim budget presented on Friday. The protest was held under the banner of All India Kisan Sangharsh Coordination Committee. Farmers accused the Modi government of neglecting their demands and said the “paltry” amount of Rs 6000 per year would not help them.
