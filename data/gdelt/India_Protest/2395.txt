East Delhi councillors exchange blows during Budget meeting

A routine meeting to discuss budget of East Delhi Municipal Corporation (EDMC) resulted into exchange of blows between the councillors of East Delhi. AAP councillor and leader Rekha Tyagi also got injuries after the shattered glass struck on her hand. While Mayor, Bihari and other BJP leader staged a protest at DCP office complex against policemen's attitude. While the some councillors tried to bring peace between the parties, their efforts ended up with getting blows. However, this resulted into protest from both sides, as BJP councillor refuted the claims and demanded to go ahead with usual proceedings, while the AAP councillors demanded an apology from the accused councillor Romesh Gupta, who denied it.
