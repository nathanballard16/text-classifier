PTI to stage protest against prevailing peace situation in Occupied Kashmir on Friday

PTI has decided to stage a protest against prevailing peace situation in Occupied Kashmir and India's move to revoke Article 370 in Islamabad on Friday.

This was decided during a meeting of Party's leadership chaired by the Chief Organizer Saif Ullah Khan Niazi on Tuesday.

The leadership of the party condemned illegal act of India saying that freedom of Kashmiri people cannot be suppressed this way.
