Central Trade Union strike enters its 2nd day

Kolkata, Bardhaman, Siliguri (West Bengal), Mumbai, Jan 09 (ANI): The two-day nationwide strike called by central trade unions entered its second day on Wednesday. Normalcy resumed even as protestors continued their strike. The strike was called against the alleged repressive policies for workers adopted by Prime Minister Narendra Modi-led government. Protestors blocked Bardhaman-Howrah railway line.
