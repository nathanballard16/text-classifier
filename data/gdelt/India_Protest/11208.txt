Armed men hold gas agency staff, consumers hostage, loot Rs 1.5 lakh

amansharma@tribunemail.com

Tribune News Service

New Delhi, July 24

Eight motorbike-borne men looted Rs 1.5 lakh from a gas agency in broad daylight on Wednesday after holding its employees and consumers hostage at gunpoint in Mayur Vihar’s Chilla village.

The robbers also made off with mobile phones of those present in the agency office to avoid immediate police calls. When the employees and locals tried to resist, the fired in the air before fleeing the spot.

Police sources said after holding people hostage at gunpoint, the robbers asked the cashier to take out the cash.

A police team rushed the spot on receipt of information and started investigation. Special staff of the police have been roped in to scan the CCTV footage of the area to get any leads on the robbers.

A case has been registered against unidentified persons and investigations are under way.
