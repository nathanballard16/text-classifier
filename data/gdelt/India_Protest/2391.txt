Jat body to herd cattle at Shah, Yogi rally venue

AGRA: To protest against rising stray cattle menace in Uttar Pradesh , national president of Rashtriya Jaat Ekta Sangathan (RJES) Sunil Chaudhary on Tuesday announced that such animals would be herded to the venue in Aligarh where BJP national president Amit Shah and CM Yogi Adityanath are scheduled address party 's booth-level workers on Wednesday.Chaudhary said the CM's promise of resolving the crisis faced by farmers remained unfulfilled. "We just want to ask the CM what farmers should do in such a situation?" said Chaudhary.He added that around 200 people will gather at the venue with the stray cattle to make the CM realise the gravity of the situation.
