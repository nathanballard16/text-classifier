Prime Minister Narendra Modi slammed the opposition and said that these parties were coming together over fear of getting caught for corruption. Addressing a public rally in Madurai, he said that the people, who were making money by illegal means are facing the wrath. PM Modi urged the youth of Tamil Nadu not to get swayed by the negative sentiments created by vested interests. He also said that his government was taking all possible steps to eradicate corruption and any person, who has looted public money, will be brought to books whether they are within or outside India. He said that Tamil Nadu is one of the leading states in the Central government's "Make in India" initiative. Modi arrived at Tamil Nadu's temple town Madurai earlier in the day and laid the foundation stone for an All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS) and also inaugurated super specialty blocks at Rajaji, Thanjavur and Tirunelveli Medical Colleges.
