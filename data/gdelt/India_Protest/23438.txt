Protest in TN, Puducherry says no to CAA

Chennai, Dec 21 (IANS) Protests against the Citizenship (Amendment) Act were held by Muslims in several places in Tamil Nadu on Saturday.

In Puducherry, Chief Minister V. Narayanasamy said the amended law will not be implemented in the Union Territory.

In Madurai several hundred Muslims held protest and shouted slogans against the CAA.
