DMK & Its Allies Protest Against CAA in Chennai - See Pictures

The city police has removed a group of protesting students of the University of Madras from the campus and closed all the gates, said a student. The students were protesting against the Citizenship (Amendment) Act (CAA). The action, as per the student happened on Wednesday (December 18) late night. Students sit inside a Madras university compound during a protest against a new citizenship law and to show solidarity with the students of New Delhi's Jamia Millia Islamia university after police entered the university campus on Sunday, following a protest against the new law, in Chennai.
