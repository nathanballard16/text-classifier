Bharatiya Forward Bloc chief K A Murugan arrested for protesting inside flight

Madurai (Tamil Nadu) [India], Mar 30 (ANI): President of Bharatiya Forward Bloc (BFB) K A Murugan was arrested at Madurai airport on Saturday for holding a protest inside a domestic flight.

Murugan was inside an Indigo flight to change the name of Madurai to Muthuramalinga Devar.

The flight was heading towards Madurai from Chennai when Murugan and his party members started protesting inside and raised slogans in support of their demand.

Murugan had already requested the government to change the name. (ANI)
