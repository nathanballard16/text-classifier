Lok Sabha Elections 2019, April 9: As it Happened

Follow us on Lok Sabha Elections 2019 Live Updates

India is awaiting a new government and in the run up to the Lok Sabha polls 2019, campaigners and warriors belonging to Prime Minister Narendra Modi's Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP) and Rahul Gandhi's Congress have got battle-ready.

Keeping up with this, PM Narendra Modi on Tuesday will address election rallies across 3 states. Congress President Rahul Gandhi will also hold rallies Assam and Bihar. Modi will also share stage with Shiv Sena President Uddhav Thackeray for the first time in 28 months, at an election rally in Maharashtra's Ausa on Tuesday.

Congress General Secretary Priyanka Gandhi is also slated to hold a roadshow in UP's Saharanpur.
