NEW DELHI: A car was set on fire on Friday in Daryaganj area of Delhi during a protest against the new citizenship law, in first incident of arson in what had been a largely peaceful day of demonstration in the capital.Police used water cannons and lathi-charged a large group of protesters who were trying to march from the old Delhi to Jantar Mantar They were stopped at Delhi Gate by police.
