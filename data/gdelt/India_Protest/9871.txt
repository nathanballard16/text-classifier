Help Release Telugus Jailed In Kuwait: Mithun To Jaishankar

New Delhi: YSRCP MP Mithun Reddy called on External Affairs Minister S Jaishankar on Tuesday in New Delhi to request his intervention for the release of Andhra Pradesh citizens who were arrested in Kuwait.

Reddy impressed upon the Minister that the Kuwait law enforcement agency had arrested people of Andhra origin when they tried to stage a protest there against the rape of a nine-month-old baby girl in Telangana.
