Maharashtra SSC result to be announced on June 8, here's how to check results online

Maharashtra

Maharashtra board

Thousands of students who have appeared for theState Board of Secondary and Higher Secondary Education (MSBSHSE) examination this year can breathe a sigh of relief as the board will announce the results tomorrow.The board on Friday said that the result will be displayed on the official website on June 8, 2019, at 1 pm.The result will be declared for Mumbai, Pune, Konkan, Kolhapur, Amravati, Latur, Aurangabad, Nashik and Kolhapur divisions.Though the board will announce the overall division wise passing percentage during a press conference tomorrow morning, students will be able to view their result on the official sites from 1 pm.- www.maharesult.nic.in, www.sscresult.mkcl.org or www.maharashtraeducation.com.Students can also get their result on SMS through BSNL service provider.More than 17 lakh students attempted the Std 10 exam which was held in March 2019.Step 1: Visit the official websitesStep 2: Look for a blinking link which says Class 10 result or SSC result 2019Step 3: Open the link and fill in all the details including roll numbers, date of birth and other informationStep 4: Click on submit, the result will be displayed on the screen.Type: MHSSC (space) SeatNo and send to shortcode 57766
