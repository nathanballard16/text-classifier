Delhi: ‘Come to work or face action’, schools warn guest teachers

Schools have started sending showcause notices to guest teachers, who have not been reporting for duty due to their ongoing protest, threatening disciplinary action against them. AdvertisingAfter the contracts of 22,000 guest teachers ended on February 28, they have been protesting for the implementation of a policy providing them job security. AdvertisingLast week, the Delhi Cabinet had approved a policy which enables guest and contract teachers working with the DoE to continue working till the age of 60.
