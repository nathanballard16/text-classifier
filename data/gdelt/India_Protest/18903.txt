Protest continues at SVDV: Students now go ‘begging’

VARANASI: The agitating students of Sanskrit Vidya Dharm Vigan of Banaras Hindu University went for bhikshatan (begging) on Thursday to express their protest against the appointment of Dr Firoz Khan in the faculty.Raising slogans, the students took out a procession from BHU main gate and moved in Lanka area. They continue to boycott classes even after calling-off their 16-day dharna on November 22.Meanwhile, the Hindu Janjagriti Samiti came in support of the SVDV students and submitted a memorandum to the district administration urging the governor and CM Yogi Adityanath to intervene and annul Dr Khan’s appointment.
