Asaduddin Owaisi fumes at Congress protest banner

The banner of the Congress shows that KCR and Owaisi are looking at Draupadi (democracy in Telangana) being stripped. The banner shows voters as Draupadi, ECI as Dhritharasthra and ERO, DEOs and CEO as Dusshasana. Photo: ANIHyderabad: AIMIM Chief and Hyderabad MP Asaduddin Owaisi today slammed the Congress leadership for putting up a banner during a protest program against the Election Commission on Assembly polls. The Congress staged protest against the Election Commission citing irregularities during the recent Assembly polls. The MIM supremo also sought to know whether the Congress accepts a similar banner comprising AICC Chief Rahul Gandhi and newly appointed General Secretary Priyanka Gandhi.
