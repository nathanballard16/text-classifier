Opposition to stage protest over inter results in Hyderabad today

The leaders of all the opposition parties to stage a protest today at Indira Park against the intermediate exam results fiasco.

The protest will see all the leaders of opposition parties, student unions and various public organisations demanding action against the authorities for the goof-up in the intermediate results and justice to the families of students who ended their lives.

The parents of the students along with the student unions have been protesting against the intermediate exam results ever since the release of them.
