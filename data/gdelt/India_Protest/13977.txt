Nationalist Congress Party () leaderon Sunday joined in with the locals atwho are protesting against Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation's decision to slash 2,700 trees in the locality."I care about the Aarey Colony. Environmentalists are opposing the cutting down of trees in the area.has become a big challenge for us. In some places there is flood and in some there is drought," Sule said while speaking to reporters.She said that NCP holds that development should happen but not at the cost of cutting down trees. She further held that NCP has always voted against the cutting down of trees in the municipal corporation.Sule said that she will meet the Chief Minister and ask him to listen to the protesters."As a Mumbaikar, I would want to meet the Chief Minister of Maharashtraon September 12. There are many people who are opposing the cutting of trees and the Chief Minister should hear them out," she said.Residents of Aarey Colony have been protesting against the cutting down of 2,700 trees for making a car shed forMetro and the matter has even been moved to the court.
