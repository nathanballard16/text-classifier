Bigg Boss Telugu 3’s wild card contestant Tamanna Simhadri: All you need to know about the first transgender contestant in the show

Tamanna was passionate about acting and modeling right from childhood. She tried her luck in acting but soon shifted to Mumbai. With the help of her transgender friend, Tamanna started working with an NGO in Mumbai for the welfare of women and the transgender community. In 2010, Tamanna won her first beauty pageant, Miss Transgender Mumbai. She recently extended her support for Sri Reddy in protest against casting couch in the industry.

Tamanna later shifted to Vijayawada to pursue her political ambitions and even contested in the recent 2019 Legislative Assembly elections in Andhra Pradesh from Mangalagiri constituency.
