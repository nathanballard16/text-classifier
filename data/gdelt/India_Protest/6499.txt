ABVP activists protest over delay on Inter results

Hyderabad: The activists of ABVP on Monday staged a dharna in front of State Intermediate Board office demanding a detailed inquiry into the allegations of missing of Intermediate answer sheets in the State. The activists raised slogans against the TSBIE officials and demanded immediate suspension of Board secretary Ashok.

Addressing the gathering, ABVP leaders Swami and Praveen Reddy alleged that the State government was delaying the declaration of the Intermediate results despite the fact that they were held during the same dates as neighbouring AP State. They alleged that answer papers were missing ever since they were transported by the officials in a vehicle.
