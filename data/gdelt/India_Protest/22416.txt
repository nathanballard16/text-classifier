Youth Congress to protest outside Rajghat

New Delhi, Dec 19 (IANS) The Congress's youth wing will hold a protest outside the Rajghat here on Thursday at 4 p.m. against the December 16 police action in Jamia Millia Islamia.

A statement issued by the Youth Congress said: "Indian Youth Congress to organise a peaceful sit-in demonstration at the Gandhi Samadhi at Rajghat on December 19, 2019 (Today) at 4.00 p.m. in condemnation of the government sponsored brutality on university students."
