Amit Shah, Yogi Adityanath held more rallies than PM Modi, Rahul Gandhi for phase 6

Number of rallies by main national leaders (restricted to phase 6)

NEW DELHI: BJP President Amit Shah and Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath held more rallies than Prime Minister Narendra Modi and Congress President Rahul Gandhi during the campaigning for the sixth phase of Lok Sabha elections, polling for which will be held on Sunday. While Shah held 27 rallies including a roadshow in six states, his party colleague Adityanath conducted 25 public meetings, including three with Modi, in four states. Modi, who once again led the BJP's campaign stepping up his attack on Opposition parties, held 20 rallies across seven states. Gandhi was part of 17 rallies including four roadshows in six states while his sister and Congress General Secretary Priyanka Gandhi canvassed in 14 rallies including four roadshows in three states for her party.
