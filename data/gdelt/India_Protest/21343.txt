Students, civil society stand against police brutality at Jamia

A+ A-Daneesh MajidNew Delhi: The Delhi Police’s brutality against the Jamia Millia Islamia students who were protesting peacefully against the Citizenship (Amendment) Act has sent shockwaves across India. It has also galvanized activists from various civil societies into action against the perpetrators of violence against JMI’s population. Despite such footage being removed by Facebook, concerned citizens continue to share it. Besides the video and of image female Jamia students resisting the physical attacks of officers with lathis, NDTV’s powerful interview of a tearful law student has also gone viral. Be it IIT Mumbai, Jammu University, Hyderabad’s Maulana Azad National Urdu University, Pondicherry University etc.
