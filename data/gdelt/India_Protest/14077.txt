Telugu Desam Party (TDP) chief and former Chief Minister of Andhra Pradesh Chandrababu Naidu and his son Nara Lokesh have been put under preventive detention at their house. Police has locked the main gate of Naidu's residence who was leaving for Atmakur for party's 'Çhalo Atmakur' rally despite being put under preventive custody by the police, news agency ANI reported. As per sources, the move has been made to prevent them from participating in a massive protest against the YS Jagan Mohan Reddy government in Andhra Pradesh.
