Biker dies in NH accident

Ballari: The death of a biker on National Highway 63 at Kudtini on Sunday sparked protest by villagers at the accident spot with police accused of negligence in shifting the victim to the hospital. The accident occurred when Hotel Veeresh, a resident of Rajapura in Sandur taluk, was headed for his village after completing a work at Kudutini. He lost control and fell off the bike.People, who gathered at the spot, said that police were informed of the accident, but delayed shifting Veeresh to a hospital in Kudutini.
