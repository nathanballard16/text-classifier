Gujarat: Temple priest beaten up, looted

RAJKOT: A temple priest was beaten up and looted by three unidentified robbers on the outskirts of Halvad on Tuesday night.Police said the robbers attacked Dayalgiri Bapu, the priest of Makari Hanumanji temple, with bats and robbed him of Rs 30,000 cash, a gas cylinder and two gunny bags of rice from the temple premises. “We suspect it to be the handiwork of migrant labourers working near the ashram. On the basis of this suspicion, we are interrogating labourers near the ashram ,” said an official from Halvad police station.
