PCS aspirants polish shoes in protest, arrested

Allahabad: A group of eight civil services aspirants were arrested for protesting against the 2018 LT grade exam paper leak case near Subhash Crossing on Sunday.During the agitation, some of the protesters even polished shoes of passersby, while other shouted slogans against the commission.After being alerted, police rushed to the spot and detained eight protesters on the charges of disturbing law and order. The detained protesters were taken to Police Lines and were later arrested.SP (city) Brajesh Kumar Srivastava said, “These protesters assembled at Subhash Crossing despite the fact that section 144 of Code of Criminal Procedure had been already enforced in the city. We lodged a case against protesters.”The protesters also demanded that the state government should take strict action against those found guilty in the paper leak case.
