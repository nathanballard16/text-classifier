9 held for violence in Seelampur Section 144 imposed in N E Delhi

New Delhi: The Delhi Police on Wednesday arrested nine people in connection to the violence that ensued in Seelampur following protests over the Citizenship Amendment Act. Earlier, three FIRs were filed in Seelampur, Jafrabad and Dayalpur police stations for rioting and damaging public property during the incident. Meanwhile, section 144 has been invoked in the North East district. "Section 144 has been invoked in the North East district. People are appealed to maintain peace and harmony and not to lend credence to rumours," tweeted DCP North East Delhi.
