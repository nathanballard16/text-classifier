7 Combatants, 2 Civilians Killed in Kashmir Fighting

Kashmiri villagers shout slogans during the funeral procession of a militant commander of the Jaish-e-Mohammed, Naseer Pandith, at Pulwama, south of Srinagar on May 16. Three Kashmiri fighters, an Indian soldier and a local civilian were killed on May 14 morning during a gunbattle between militants and Indian government forces, reports said.
