Gandapur urges India to stop targeting civilian population

Minister for Kashmir Affairs and Gilgit-Baltistan, Ali Amin Gandapur has said that continuous ceasefire violations on the Line of Control by the Indian forces pose serious threat to regional peace.

Addressing a function in Islamabad, he said the international community should take notice of gross human rights violations at the hands of Indian forces in Occupied Kashmir.

Ali Amin Gandapur urged India to stop targeting civilian population along the Line of Control, Working Boundary and in Occupied Kashmir.
