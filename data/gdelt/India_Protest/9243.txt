Noida: Protest at ESIC as doctor ‘slaps’ official

NOIDA: Health services were disrupted at the ESIC hospital (Sector 24) for three hours on Friday after a doctor allegedly slapped an office staffer.Protests broke out after that as employees raised slogans against hospital management. The office superintendent of the hospital was allegedly slapped on Thursday by a doctor who complained about medical officers being included in committees on administrative issues.Office superintendent Samir Nayak gave a written complaint against the doctor to the director of the institute. But as no action was taken till Thursday, the employees decided to go on a strike . Around 25 employees refrained from work between 9 pm and noon. Deputy medical superintendent Dr Balraj Bhandari told TOI the management has decided to constitute a committee to look into the case and removed the doctor from administrative posts.
