Chandrababu faults EC, to move SC, plan protests in Delhi

The TD chief said he was not afraid of anyone and work for State’s development. He said the YCP has ruined the entire poll process using rowdies and the EC could not take steps for fair polls. He said he would involve other 22 parties in the protests he planned to hold against the EC and the Centre. The YCP used its workers and rowdies against the TD during the polls, he charged. The Chief Minister also said he will hold protest dharnas at national level against the EC and the Centre.
