Mukherjee Nagar clash: MLA Manjinder Singh Sirsa manhandled during protest by Sikh community

Sikh community took out a massive street protest in Mukherjee Nagar on Monday. | Photo Credit: ANI

New Delhi: The protest by Sikh community against the thrashing of a tempo driver in Mukherjee Nagar by personnel of Delhi Police took a violent turn on Monday evening. The protesters reportedly manhandled Shiromani Akali Dal-BJP MLA, Manjinder Singh Sirsa, during the agitation.

According to a report in ANI, the community took out a massive street protest in Mukherjee Nagar on Monday against the beating of tempo driver Sarabjeet Singh and his son by police.

Meanwhile, the agitated protesters thrashed a Delhi Police ACP in Mukherjee Nagar after he went to the spot to control protesters.

KG Tyagi, who is posted as ACP in Shalimar Bagh Police Station, had to face the ire of the protesters following a police-tempo driver clash in Mukherjee Nagar.

Earlier on Monday, Delhi Police filed a cross FIR in the case and suspended three police officers, including two Assistant sub-Inspector and a constable.

Deputy Commissioner of police, Madhur Verma said the FIRs have been filed from both sides including a policeman, as well as from the man who was thrashed by the police.
