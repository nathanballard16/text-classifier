MANUU students protest against police action at Jamia Millia Varsity

Follow us on Image Source : PTI MANUU students protestagainst police action at Jamia Millia Varsity (representational Image)

Students of the city-based Maulana Azad National Urdu University (MANUU) on Monday boycotted their exams and held protests against the police action on their counterparts at Jamia University in Delhi. Protests erupted at MANUU campus on Sunday night which continued beyond midnight with several students raising slogans against the central government.

The MANUU student unions gave a call to boycott various exams scheduled from Monday, a MANUU official said.

Students of Jamia Millia Islamia University, where the police entered after a protest against the amended Citizenship Act nearby spiralled out of control on Sunday evening.

Forty people, including students, policemen and firemen were injured as violence erupted during a protest by Jamia students against the contentious Act.
