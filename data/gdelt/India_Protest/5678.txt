Around the World in 8 Minutes: Episode 14

In this episode, we are joined by Phakamile Hlubi-Majola from South African Socialist Revolutionary Workers Party to talk about the issue of police repression on community members from Boipatong, Vanderbijlpark. Kavita Krishnan from the Communist Party of India (ML) Liberation also speaks to Peoples Dispatch regarding the arrest of activist Jean Drèze and two other food rights activists. We also take a look at the war crimes committed by Israeli occupation forces as Palestinian volunteer medic, Sajed Muzher, was killed by the IDF soldiers.
