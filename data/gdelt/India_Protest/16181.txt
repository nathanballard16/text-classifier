Another AU stir over dissolved students union

Allahabad: The demand that a students union replace the existing students council triggered another protest near Union Hall on Allahabad University ( AU ) campus, under the limits of Colonelganj police station, on Monday.Police had to use mild force to disperse the agitating students who had brought along a donkey to the protest. Inspector (Colonelganj) Arun Kumar Tyagi said, “The students had planned on taking out a march on campus along with a donkey, but they were stopped by the security.”“When they didn’t relent, the police had to use mild force,” he said. The protest was planned in the wake of the forthcoming student council elections. AU authorities said they had already written to police seeking more security ahead of the student council polls.
