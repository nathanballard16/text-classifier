Pro-CAA rally at IIT-Bombay, protest at Chembur

Around 130 students from IIT-Bombay staged a demonstration in support of the Citizenship Amendment Act (CAA) on campus on Tuesday. Students staged a march from the management school to Hostel 12. A representative of the students said, “We wish to welcome the persecuted religious minorities from Pakistan, Afghanistan and Bangladesh. Through our protest , we also wish to condemn the violent protests, politicisation of educational campuses and spread of misinformation on CAA .”Meanwhile, an anti-CAA protest was held in Chembur . The protest was organized by Bharatiya Ekta Manch. Around two thousand locals, college students and Dalit women marched. Over 200 volunteers were on duty to maintain peace.
