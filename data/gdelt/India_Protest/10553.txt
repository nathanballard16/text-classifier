RSS' role in 'nation-building' part of Nagpur varsity syllabus

: The history of the Rashtriya Swayamsevak Sangh and its role in nation-building has been included in the syllabus of a university-based in Nagpur, where the Sangh has headquarters.

The National Students Union of India has threatened to launch an agitation demanding the removal of the part from the syllabus.
