Protest over baby s death at hospital

Kolkata: Tension ran high at Canning Sub-divisional hospital on Monday morning following the death of a baby whose family members staged a demonstration levelling negligence charge against some doctors. The family members of the victim alleged that no treatment was meted out on the patient till 8 am which eventually led to her death. The family members of the victim staged protest demonstration inside the hospital. After being informed the police reached the hospital and dispersed the mob to bring the situation under control. The agitation was also lifted after the police assured the victim's family members that the matter would be looked into.
