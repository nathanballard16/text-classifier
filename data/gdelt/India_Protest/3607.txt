terrorism: Candle light march against terrorism

After the dreadful attack on CRPF jawans in Pulwama at J&K, where 40 soldiers lost their lives, the entire country came out to protest against the terrorism. Indoreans also showed their rage, in a candle light march held at Regal Square, which saw many citizens demanding justice through posters. Citizens thronged in hundreds to show their solidarity for the families of the slain jawans and requested the government to take necessary action against terrorism in the country. Besides Indore , many other cities also saw similar candle light marches, vigils and protest against the dastard attack in J&K. The entire nation has been voicing their rage on social media in a similar way.
