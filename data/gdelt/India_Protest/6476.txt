Demand to release Varavara Rao, activists of Civil Societies register protest

Hyderabad: Activists of Civil Society and Democratic Teachers’ Federation registered their protest at Telangana NGO’s Bhavan, Nampally demanding the release of revolutionary writer, Mr. Varavara Rao from prison.

Police arrested the agitating teachers including Dr. Haragopal, former Professor of Political Science, University of Hyderabad.
