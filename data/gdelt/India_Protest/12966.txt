Congress registers protest against school demolition

Hyderabad: Congress Party registered a strong protest against the demolition of Humayun Nagar Urdu Medium High School by GHMC officials.

Senior Congress Leader, Mr. Feroz Khan presented a memorandum to District Collector and DEO separately. He was accompanied by Mr.Osman Khan Vice President of City Congress Committee.

Joint Collector of Hyderabad District assured that alternative accommodation would be provided soon.

Mr.Feroz Khan alleged that this action has been taken in connivance with the local MLA. And corporate of GHMC.
