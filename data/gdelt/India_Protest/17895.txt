Amazon to protest Microsoft's Pentagon cloud award - WSJ

Nov 14 (Reuters) - Amazon.com Inc will protest a recent cloud computing contract award given to Microsoft Corp by the Pentagon, The Wall Street Journal reported on Thursday.

Amazon did not immediately respond to a Reuters request for comment. (on.wsj.com/2CLjsdK) (Reporting by Shariq Khan in Bengaluru; Editing by Shounak Dasgupta)
