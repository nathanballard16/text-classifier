Pulwama attack: Protesters burnt Pakistan Flag and effigy of Masood Azhar in Sarupathar

Sarupathar: Several public of Sarupathar under Dhansiri Subdivision on Monday congregated in Sarupathar town and burnt Pakistan's national flag and also burnt the effigy of Masood Azhar in protest against the terror attack on a CRPF convoy in restive Jammu and Kashmir on Thursday.

The public of Sarupathar paid deep homage and one-minute silence prayer to get their soal peace and rest.

The public raised anti-Pakistan slogans, the gathering, comprising mostly youth, asked the neighbouring country to stop supporting terror activities and giving terrorists a safe haven to launch attacks against India.

At least 44 CRPF personnel were killed and five injured on Thursday in one of the deadliest terror attacks in Jammu and Kashmir when a suicide bomber rammed a vehicle carrying over 350 kg of explosives into their bus in Pulwama district.
