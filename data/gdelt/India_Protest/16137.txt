Employees of Hindustan Aeronautics Ltd in Sunabeda stage indefinite protest over wage revision

Sunabeda:l Hundreds of employees of Hindustan Aeronautics Ltd in Sunabeda of Koraput district stage indefinite protest over wage revision and other demands. Over 20,000 employees of all the 9 units of HAL across the country have joined the strike call by All India HAL Trade Union.
