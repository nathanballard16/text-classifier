ROB construction delay: 500 held for protesting

COIMBATORE: The city police on Friday arrested over 500 people, who staged a protest demanding the state government to speed up the construction of the SIHS Rail Overbridge DMK and alliance parties, who organised the protest, accused the ruling party of not completing the project even after eight years.“During the DMK regime in 2010, Rs 23crore was sanctioned to construct the rail overbridge at SIHS Colony. In 2011, AIADMK came to power and started the construction work. But the AIADMK government failed to acquire land to lay service roads. The ruling government did not do any work in the last seven years and the people of SIHS Colony are suffering. We will intensify the protest if the government fails to take action,” DMK MLA N Karthik said.The overbridge will give easy access to Trichy Road and Avinashi Road.Coimbatore MP PR Natarajan requested the government to acquire land required to lay service roads by providing good compensations to the owners.Members of DMK and alliance political parties including Congress, CPM, CPI, Kongunadu Makkal Desiya Katchi (KMDK), Viduthalai Chiruthaigal Katchi (VCK), Marumalarchi Dravida Munnetra Kazhagam (MDMK) and Thanthai Periyar Dravidar Kazhagam participated in the protest and raised slogans against the government for delaying the work.City police arrested more than 500 protesters and took them to a marriage hall. They were let off in the evening.
