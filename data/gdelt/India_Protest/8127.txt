Gujarat Board Announced 10th class Results

Gandhinagar: Gujarat Secondary and Higher Secondary Board (GSHSEB or GSEB) has announced the results for Class 10 examination held in March on the official website www.gseb.org. The Gujarat Board 10th results have been announced for more than 7 lakh students. Last year GSEB SSC result was announced in the last week of May, on May 28. The GSEB results for Class 10 or Secondary School Certificate (SSC) exams have been released on gseb.org.
