Karnam Sekar takes over as IOB MD and CEO from R Subramaniakumar

Former Dena Bank MD Karnam Sekar has taken over as the new Managing Director and CEO of Indian Overseas Bank from today. He replaces R Subramaniakumar, who was instrumental in taking measures to turn around the bank, which has been reporting loses due to high NPA. Sekar was on Special Duty and has been a whole-time director of the Bank since April 2019.
