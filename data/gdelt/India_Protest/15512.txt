National Medical Commission members to be chosen by draw of lots

Part-time members of NMC will be chosen from the MAC. (Representational Image) Part-time members of NMC will be chosen from the MAC. (Representational Image)

Members of National Medical Commission (NMC) will be selected by a transparent, public draw of lots on October 14, Health Secretary Preeti Sudan told the media on Friday. The NMC will replace Medical Commission of India as the apex regulator of medical education.
