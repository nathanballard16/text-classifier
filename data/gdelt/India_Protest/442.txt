TDP MP Naramalli Sivaprasad dresses up as MG Ramachandran to demand special status for AP

New Delhi, Jan 07 (ANI): Telugu Desam Party (TDP) MP Naramalli Sivaprasad dressed up as former Tamil Nadu Chief Minister MG Ramachandran during TDP protest in Parliament demanding ‘Special Category Status’ for Andhra Pradesh. He has earlier dressed up as a magician, a woman, a washerman and a school student among others.
