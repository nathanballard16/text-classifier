Pakistan: Hindu Girl Found Murdered In Hostel Room; Protests Held In Karachi

Voicing their anger against the Pakistani government, the protestors demanded justice for the deceased girl who was a final year student of Bachelor of Dental Surgery (BDS).

People staged protest on the streets of Karachi against the alleged murder of a Sindhi Hindu girl, Namrita Chandani which took place in Larkana on Tuesday. Namrita was found with a rope tied to her neck in her hostel room in Larkana, Sindh. This comes a day after people vandalized properties including a temple in Ghotki after a school principal from the minority Hindu community was booked on charges of alleged blasphemy.
