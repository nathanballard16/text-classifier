BHU Sanskrit Dept students continue agitation against appointment of Muslim teacher

Varanasi: Students of the faculty of Sanskrit Vidya Dharm Vigyan at Banaras Hindu University ( BHU ) continued their protest against the appointment of a Muslim teacher in the department, on the second consecutive day on Friday.Students are demanding cancellation of the appointment of Dr Firoz Khan as an assistant professor in ‘Sahitya’ (literature) department of the faculty of Sanskrit Vidya Dharm Vigyan claiming that the appointment of a non-Hindu in the department is against the soul and spirit of the institution.However, university administration said the appointment was made as per University Grants Commission (UGC) rules and the BHU Act in a transparent manner on the basis of the eligibility of the candidate.
