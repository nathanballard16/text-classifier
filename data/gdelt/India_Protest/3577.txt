Indore couple looted at knife-point

Mhow: An Indore couple was looted at knife-point on Rau-Khalghat highway under Kishanganj police station limits late on Monday.Sanjay Mahajan and his wife Jyoti, residents of Hare Krishna colony in Aerodrome area, were on their way to Indore from Pithampur in car when the incident took place, police said.When they were passing through Bhaislay village, two persons intercepted them and asked the couple to handover all the valuables by keeping Sanjay at knife-point. They made away with cash and gold ring worth Rs 30,000.
