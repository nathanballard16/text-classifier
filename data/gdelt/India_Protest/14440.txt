Rally in support to Kashmir and Khalistan held in Houston

F.P. Report

HOUSTON: A Truck rally to stage protest against siege of Kashmir and on Referendum 2020 was organised by Friends of Kashmir and Sikhs for Justice in Houston.

The Rally with around 30-40 trucks and vans plied on different roads of Houston. Slogans regarding restoration of Article 370, referendum in 2020 and Save Kashmir Save Humanity were displayed on the vehicles. Similarly slogans like Modi – Face of Indian democracy were also displayed.

Similarly, a protest rally was organised by Pakistani Americans Association of Connecticut from in which over 100 people participated.

The protesters carried placards inscribed with slogans in support of Kashmir. They demanded of the UN to play its role and asking India to stop genocide in Indian Held Kashmir.
