Vandals target playground equipment

Article content

Vandals armed with spray paint have twice targeted playground equipment at the Delcrest Park in Delhi.

Profanities were spray-painted on the playground equipment sometime on Monday. Members of the Norfolk County Parks and Recreation Services cleaned off the graffiti only to have the culprits return and spray paint a second time.

County employees contacted Norfolk OPP on Tuesday.
