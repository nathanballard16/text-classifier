Lok Sabha Polls LIVE: In his address, PM says Kashi has given him strength to answer terrorists

New Delhi: Prime Minister Narendra Modi will hold a grand rally in his Lok Sabha Constituency Varanasi on Thursday in the afternoon, a day before he is scheduled to file his nomination.



He will be holding a seven kilometer long road show in the afternoon today, which will be followed by the traditional Ganga Aarti at Dashashwamedh Ghat.



The road show is slated to start from the Pandit Madan Mohan Malviya's statue and end at Dashashwamedh Ghat, according to a party press release issued on Wednesday.



As per the itinerary, Modi is likely to begin his roadshow at 4: 15 pm from Pandit Madan Mohan Malviya’s statute. The roadshow will then cover the areas of Lanka, Assi, Bhadaini, Sonarpura, Madanpura, Godowlia and will end at Dashashwamedha Ghat



"After the roadshow, the Prime Minister will participate in the Ganga Arti at Dashashwamedh Ghat. After that, at 8 pm, he will have a conversation with eminent personalities of Varanasi at Hotel De Paris," the BJP said.
