Protestors in Kolkata detained amid 48-hour nationwide strike

Kolkata, Jan 08 (ANI): As the 48-hour nationwide strike called by the All India Central Council of Trade Unions (AICCTU) is underway, Kolkata Police detained protestors, who were disturbing public order. Trade Unions are protesting all over the country. Their demands include higher minimum wages, social security among others. Earlier in the day, protestors in Howrah disrupted train movements.
