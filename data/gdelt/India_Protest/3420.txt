Pulwama terror attack CRPF personnel and residents hold candlelight march in Delhi

Pulwama terror attack: CRPF personnel and residents hold candlelight march in Delhi17 Feb 2019, 08:32PM ISTSource: TNNMassive protests are being held in different parts of the country against Pulwama terror attack. The terror attack on Thursday in Pulwama claimed the lives of 40 CRPF personnel when an explosive-laden car hit the CRPF convoy's bus carrying jawans. In Delhi CRPF along with residents organised a candle light march at Mayur Vihar Phase 3.
