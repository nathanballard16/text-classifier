Top companies where promoters have trimmed their stake in the past one year

Emami promoters on Monday sold 10 per cent of their stake in the company for around Rs 1,230 crore to pare debt. This was the second stake sale undertaken by the FMCG (fast moving consumer goods) firms' promoters in less than six months.

Earlier, in February too, they had sold a 10 per cent stake for Rs 1,600 crore. Promoters paring stake in their company is nothing new.
