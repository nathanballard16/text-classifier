Bank employees stage protest against Centre's decision to merge public sector banks in Chennai

By PTICHENNAI: Members of the All India Bank Employees' Association on Saturday staged a protest here against the Centre's decision to merge 10 public sector banks into four entities. Employees of all public and private sector banks wore black badges to work as a mark of protest to the government's decision. He alleged the merger of public sector banks would mean closure of six banks. Finance Minister Nirmala Sitharaman Friday said 10 public sector banks -- Punjab National Bank, Canara bank, Union Bank of India, Indian Bank, United Bank of India, Allahabad Bank, Syndicate Bank, Corporation Bank, Oriental Bank of Commerce and Andhra Bank would be merged. He recalled that when the financial recession was experienced world over in 2008, the domestic banking system was safe because of public sector banks.
