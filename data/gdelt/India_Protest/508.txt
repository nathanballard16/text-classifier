Bharat Bandh Highlights: Trade Unions Go On 2-Day Strike

The mining activities in the Chhattisgarh were partially affected, production was completely stalled in the Manikpur coal mine of state-owned South Eastern Coalfields Limited (SECL) in Korba district while it was partially affected in Kusmunda, Gevra and Dipka coal mines of the district.





Transport service were unaffected and schools and colleges also remained open across the state. Agitators held protests at several place in the state capital Raipur.
