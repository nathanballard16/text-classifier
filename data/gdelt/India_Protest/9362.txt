LIVE Doctors' Strike LIVE UPDATES! West Bengal Chief Minister Mamata Banerjee has accepted the proposal of doctors to set up Grievance Redressal Cell in Government Hospitals.

Doctors' strike LIVE updates: Agitating doctors in West Bengal softened their stand on Sunday, leaving it to Chief Minister Mamata Banerjee to decide the meeting venue with them, but insisted that it should be held in the presence of media and recorded. The chief minister is learnt to have agreed to a meeting on Monday at an auditorium adjacent to the state secretariat.
