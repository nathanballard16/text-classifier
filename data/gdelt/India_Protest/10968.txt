Nursing students stage protest

Nursing students in the city staged a protest on Friday demanding action against persons who reportedly assaulted five students on the premises of the Government Kasturba Gandhi Hospital (KGH) for Women and Children.

Relatives of a patient who died after suffering a cardiac arrest on Wednesday had reportedly manhandled the students of Diploma of General Nursing, School of Nursing, KGH.

Following the incident, nearly 150 nursing students staged a protest at KGH, while students of the Madras Medical College, Government Stanley Medical College and Government Kilpauk Medical College staged separate demonstrations. A. Edwin Joe, Director of Medical Education, visited KGH and met the protesting students.
