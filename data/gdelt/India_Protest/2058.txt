Upendra Kushwaha gets head injuries in baton charge

PATNA: Former Union minister and Rashtriya Lok Samata Party chief Upendra Kushwaha on Saturday received injuries on his head and right palm in police lathi-charge to stop his party members from marching towards Raj Bhavan to press for educational reforms. A dozen others were also injured, with one later slipping into coma.Kushwaha was rushed to the PMCH where doctors conducted CT scan of his head. Among those injured was former MP Bhudeo Choudhary, a party spokesperson said. RLSP spokesman Fazal Imam Malik said grand alliance allies would organise a Bihar bandh on Monday to protest against the “brutal” lathicharge.
