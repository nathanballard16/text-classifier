Robbers Loot Rs 30 Lakh From Hyderabad Jeweller

HYDERABAD: Unidentified miscreants looted Rs 30 lakh from a man spraying pepper into his eyes at Mahankali temple area of Secunderabad on Tuesday night.

According to police, Roopa Ram, working with Rohit jewellers, went to another Navkar jewellers. He collected the cash and en route he was attacked.

Unidentified men sprayed pepper on him and took the cash and ran away.

Police have registered a case.
