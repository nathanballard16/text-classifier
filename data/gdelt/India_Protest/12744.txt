Human Rights totally violated in Occupied Kashmir: CM West Bengal

In India, West Bengal Chief Minister Mamata Banerjee has asserted that human rights have been totally violated in occupied Kashmir.

On the occasion of World Humanitarian Day today, Chief Minister said she had once taken to the streets to protest against human rights violations.

She also urged people to pray for peace in the Valley.
