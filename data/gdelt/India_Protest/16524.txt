Members of Kashmiri community hold ‘Kashmir Freedom March’ in Southampton

In Southampton, members of the Kashmiri community held a ‘Kashmir Freedom March’ to raise awareness of the violations of human rights in occupied Kashmir.

The march began at the 02 Guildhall, West Marlands Road and concluded at the Bargate, High Street.

The protesters chanted slogans “Time for the UN to act for Kashmir” and “India stop torture in occupied Kashmir”.
