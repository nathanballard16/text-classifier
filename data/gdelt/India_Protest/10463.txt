LNJP doctors call off strike

New Delhi, July 8Doctors of Lok Nayak Jai Prakash Narayan (LNJP) hospital ended their protest today. Earlier in the morning, the doctors went on strike over an alleged attack on a junior doctor last night by a patient’s attendants. The Resident Doctors’ Association president said a patient was brought to the emergency last night and died due to some complications. An attendant later assaulted a postgraduate student of Maulana Azad Medical College around 11 pm at LNJP Hospital. The doctors’ association of LNJP Hospital demanded security in emergency, including deployment of marshals and installation of CCTV cameras on the hospital premises.
