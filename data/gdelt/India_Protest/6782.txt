Chairman JKLF condition worsens as hunger strike enters 12th day

Health of ailing Chairman of Jammu and Kashmir Liberation Front, Muhammad Yasin Malik, has worsened on twelvth day of his hunger strike in a hospital in New Dehli. He was shifted from an illegal custody of India’s infamous National Investigation Agency to a hospital in New Delhi after his condition deteriorated.

Muhammad Yasin Malik is on hunger strike against the illegal and obnoxious attitude of the National Investigation Agency.
