UBC students hold rally to express solidarity with oppressed Kashmiris

A large number of students of University of British Columbia (UBC) in Canada held a rally to express solidarity with the oppressed people of Occupied Kashmir.

Students protested against the restrictions and communications blackout imposed by India in occupied Kashmir.

They chanted pro freedom of Kashmir and anti-India slogans.

The organizers and Kashmiri students in their address, voiced about massive human rights violations in Occupied Kashmir.
