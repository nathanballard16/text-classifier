Lok Sabha Election 2019 HIGHLIGHTS: Cong tried to grab power even as Parrikar was ailing, says PM

Lok Sabha Election 2019 LIVE updates: Rahul Gandhi files his nomination from Amethi. Lok Sabha Election 2019 LIVE updates: Rahul Gandhi files his nomination from Amethi.

Election 2019 India LIVE News: Hours after the Congress party targeted BJP over SC order on Rafale, the BJP on Wednesday hit out at Rahul Gandhi, accusing him of gross contempt of court. Defence Minister Nirmala Sitharaman lashed out at the Congress chief, saying that he has attributed to the apex court what it never said in its order on the fighter jet deal with France. Earlier in the day, Rahul filed his nomination from Amethi, with his mother and UPA chairperson Sonia Gandhi, sister Priyanka Gandhi and brother-in-law Robert Vadra by his side. He held a roadshow from Munshiganj-Darpipur to Gauriganj before filing the nomination. Rahul also held two rallies in Bihar and West Bengal.
