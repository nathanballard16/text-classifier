CM Naidu, TDP MLAs wear black shirt to protest over special status of AP

Amaravati (Andhra Pradesh), Feb 01 (ANI): MLAs of Telugu Desam Party (TDP) were seen protesting over the special status to Andhra Pradesh on Friday. The MLAs wore black shirt to attend Assembly session. Chief Minister N Chandrababu Naidu also wore black shirt. Meanwhile, TDP MPs were seen protesting in front of Mahatma Gandhi statue in the Parliament premise in Delhi over same issue.
