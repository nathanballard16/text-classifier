Goons Loot Rs Nine Lakh From Petrol Pump In Jharkhand

Ranchi: Unidentified criminals looted Rs nine lakh from a petrol pump situated in the Premnagar area on the outskirts of the city, police said on Wednesday, October 9.

According to the police, seven bike-borne miscreants held the petrol pump employees at gunpoint and fled with the cash on Tuesday night. A manhunt has been launched to nab the culprits, the police added. (IANS)
