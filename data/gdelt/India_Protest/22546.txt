Anti-CAA protests: 19 Delhi Metro stations closed, IndiGo cancels 19 flights



The Delhi Metro Rail Corporation (DMRC) has closed gates of 19 metro stations in view of protests against the Citizenship Amendment Act. Entry and exit gates of Rajiv Chowk and Janpath are closed. Trains will not be halting at Barakhamba metro station, however, interchange facility is available at Rajiv Chowk metro station. Entry and exit gates of Patel Chowk, Lok Kalyan Marg, Udyog Bhawan, ITO, Pragati Maidan, Central Secretariat, Khan Market, Vasant Vihar and Mandi House are closed. DMRC informed about the closure of these metro stations in a tweet.



Anti-CAA protests in Delhi has led to the cancellation of 19 IndiGo flights. Sixteen flights from Delhi have been delayed as staffers were stuck in the traffic jam on NH-8. Additionally, mobile internet services have been restored in Delhi. Meanwhile, protests against Citizenship Amendment Act in Lucknow turned violent after a mob pelted stones and torched vehicles parked outside a police post.
