Mumbai Protest News: Students, activists hold anti-CAA protest in Mumbai

MUMBAI: Students and social activists on Friday held a protest against the Citizenship Amendment Act and the National Register of Citizens ( NRC ).The protesters gathered at the Azad Maidan in south Mumbai and raised slogans against the government. They carried banners decrying the Modi government over the move."This is a authoritarian regime. It thinks they can do anything. It is this government's responsibility to protect and not oppose the Constitution," Nikita Pathak, a student, said.Another protester said this was a "faceless government", adding the Act is aimed "not against just one community but against the whole country."
