Boy electrocuted inside under construction building

Kolkata: Tension ran high in Howrah's Shibpur on Wednesday afternoon after a boy was electrocuted to death inside an under construction building. Local residents accused the developers of the under-construction building for the incident. The local residents rushed to the spot and admitted the victim to a nearby hospital where he was declared brought dead. Another boy was injured in the incident but his condition is stated to be stable. The local residents staged demonstration at the spot and demanded the arrest of the developers of the building.
