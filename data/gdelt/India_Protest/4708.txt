Kashmiris stage protest demo outside Human Rights Commission headquarters in Geneva

Kashmiri people staged a protest demonstration outside the Human Rights Commission headquarters in Geneva.

The participants of the demonstration said that durable peace in South Asia is linked to the resolution of the Kashmir dispute.

They said India cannot suppress the freedom struggle of Kashmiri people by use of brutal force.

They also condemned the ceasefire violation at the Line of Control by India.

The protesters chanted pro-freedom and anti-India slogans.
