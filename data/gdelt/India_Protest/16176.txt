Snatchers kill gang leader after fight over sharing stolen money

New Delhi: The dispute in the sharing of looted amount resulted in the murder of gang leader by his associates near Mundka Railway Station. The whole gang was involved in more than 20 cases of snatching, theft and related crime incidents. Police said that after consuming drugs, the gang leader Gaurav with his four associates identified as Ankit, Gautam besides two juveniles with weapons roamed around railway tracks or sometimes they entered trains passing through Sarai Rohilla and Sabji Mandi railway stations route. "They used to commit theft, snatching with railway passengers," an official said. In retaliation, all the four overpowered Gaurav and stabbed knife on his vital body parts.
