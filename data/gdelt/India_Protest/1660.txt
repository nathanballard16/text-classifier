Protest in Old city against the illegal detention of youthsHYDERABAD: Tension prevailed at Purani Haveli when a group of Hussainialam residents staged a protest near the police commissioner’s office. After which, the residents of Hussainialam protested against the illegal detention of youth by Task Force police. According to the report of Times Of India, the Commissioner’s Task Force had reportedly picked up several youths from Hussainialam area. Other residents along with the family members of a detainee staged a protest and demanded his immediate release. B Anjaiah, ACP Chairman said, “We are analysing the CCTV footage of the clashes and the arrests will be evidence-based.”
