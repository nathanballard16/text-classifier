70th anniversary of Constitution’s adoption celebrated in Vadodara

VADODARA: In a first, Vadodara District Legal Services Authority held a Harmony March on Tuesday to celebrate 70th anniversary of adoption of Indian Constitution.Participants took a pledge before the start of the march near the statue of Dr Babasaheb Ambedkar . The march was organized to create awareness about fundamental duties of citizens. Around 5,000 participants, including judicial officers, senior officials of city and rural police, district collector, civic body officials, lawyers, school students and volunteers of NGOs walked all the way from Dr Ambedkar’s statue at Race Course Road to district court building.
