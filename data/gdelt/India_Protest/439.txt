Kerala has become political murder place, says Prakash Javadekar

New Delhi, Jan 07 (ANI): Senior BJP leaders protested infront of Gandhi statue outside Parliament today. They staged protest against Kerala’s Communist government in reference to BJP legislator’s house was attacked.They alleged that this is the true character of Kerala’s government. Union HRD Minister, Prakash Javadekar said that most of the violent incidents take place in CM Pinarayi Vijayan’s Kannur. “It has become a political murder place”, said Javadekar. Meanwhile, other leaders condemned the violence and demanded justice.Violence erupted in the state of Kerala after cadres of Communist Party and BJP fought over Sabarimala row.
