Flights cancelled, passengers stranded at Bengaluru Airport as trade union strike continues

Bengaluru, Jan 08 (ANI): Due to nationwide strike called by the AICCTU (All India Central Council of Trade Unions), flights got cancelled at the Bengaluru Airport. Passengers were seen stranded, waiting for their flights, while buses were parked at the parking bay. While the strike is still underway, protestors and police clashed at various locations all over India.
