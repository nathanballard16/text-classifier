Leopard that killed two kids caged

RAJKOT: A male leopard aged 8-9 years, that had killed two children and attacked one adult in Bhavnagar district was nabbed by forest officials on Sunday night. The leopard will remain caged for rest of its life, said forest officials.According to forest officials, the leopard had killed an 11-year-old girl Shobhna Parmar in Itia village of Jesar on June 15, 2018. It had also targeted deputy sarpanch of the village and a woman from the same village. The leopard had also attacked another girl in Kobadia village of Jesar, who later died.
