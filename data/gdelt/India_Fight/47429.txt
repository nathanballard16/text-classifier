MP: Lokayukta Raids Properties Of State Excise Department’s Assistant Commissioner

In the documents recovered so far, most of the properties are on the name of Khare’s wife or children.

The Lokayukta Police team raided the properties of Alok Khare, assistant commissioner in Madhya Pradesh excise department, in a case of disproportionate assets case, on Tuesday morning. Searches are underway at Khare’s properties in Bhopal, Indore, Raisen , Chhatarpur and other locations. As per the Lokayukta team, Tuesday’s raids can be the biggest action in the state.
