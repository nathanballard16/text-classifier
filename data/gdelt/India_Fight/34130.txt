Fire breaks out at Delhi hospital; 6 rescued

New Delhi [India], July 12 (ANI): At least six people have been rescued after a fire broke out at the Employees' State Insurance Model Hospital in the Basai Darapur locality in Delhi on Friday.

A team of 6 fire tenders have been deployed on the spot to extinguish the fire.

No casualties have been reported so far.

Further details awaited. (ANI)
