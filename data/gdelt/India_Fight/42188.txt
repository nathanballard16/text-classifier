Student martyred during anti-India demonstrations in Srinagar

In Occupied Kashmir, a student was martyred during anti-India demonstrations in Srinagar on Wednesday.

The student was injured in the pellet firing by Indian troops on demonstrators in Soura area of Srinagar. He was shifted to hospital where he succumbed to his injuries.
