Fatehpur, U.P: 6-year-old raped and murdered after being lured with biscuit; Accused arrested

Speaking on the incident, the deceased’s mother said, “My daughter was playing outside the house when Farooq lured her with food and took her away and did wrong to her. After this daughter’s body was found in a sack”. She also said that she went to Farooq’s house while looking for her daughter, but he said that she was not there.

While he was laying the dead body in a sack, a villager saw him do so. He was grabbed and thrashed and finally handed over to the police. The accused has currently been admitted in the hospital, as he suffered several injuries after getting beaten up. Police are also patrolling the village to ensure the atmosphere of the village is calm.
