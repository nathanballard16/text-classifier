Hyderabad: Businessman murdered in Balapur

Hyderabad: In Balapur area of Pahadi Shareef, a real-estate businessman was murdered.

After getting the news, incharge DCP of LB Nagar and other police officials reached the spot.

The deceased is identified as Idrees (43), resident of Jahanuma. The report of his missing was registered at Balapur Police Station yesterday. This was informed by DCP of Crime, Mr. Yadagiri.

He also said that the dead body was found in Pahadi Shareef area.

According to the report of DCP, Idrees was last seen in a property dealing office yesterday. Police is in search of his business partner, Mohsin. Police suspects that Mohsin might have murdered Idrees.
