Pune: Wife of cop suspended for ‘rape’ alleges harassment by officer, attempts suicide at police station

The wife of a police officer, who was suspended after being accused of rape, attempted to set herself on fire at a police station in the city on Thursday morning. A press release issued by the police in the evening said an inquiry had found that there was no substance in the woman’s complaint of sexual harassment. AdvertisingThe woman had filed an application at the police station alleging that a policeman had harassed her sexually. An offence was lodged against her for attempting to commit suicide under the Indian Penal Code. Her husband, meanwhile, is facing an inquiry after he was booked in a case of rape.
