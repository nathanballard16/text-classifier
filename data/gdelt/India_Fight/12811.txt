Salman Khan and Katrina Kaif wrap up shooting for 'Bharat'; Katrina shares an adorable pic with Salman

The nation is eagerly waiting to see the super hit pair of Salman Khan and Katrina Kaif hit the screens again. Both the actors will hit the screens once again with Ali Abbas Zafar 's ' Bharat ', slated for Eid 2019 release. The makers of the film had earlier revealed a teaser introducing the character of Salman Khan. Salman reportedly plays five different roles in the film. The latest news is the both Salman and Katrina has wrapped up shooting for the film.Salman and Katrina have dedicated a good amount of time for this film. Both were shooting for the film since August last year. The makers have shot extensively for the film in different locations abroad and at home. The last schedule of the shoot was reportedly taking place in Mumbai.Today, Katrina took to her Instagram handle to announce that they have wrapped up shooting for the film. The actors have shot for the film in Malta, Chandigarh, and Mumbai. Katrina shared an adorable picture with Salman Khan and called him the best boy.Sharing the image, Katrina captioned, "And that’s a Picture wrap on Bharat its been the most incredible & exciting character for me, the whole process of making this film has been so inspiring .....thank u @aliabbaszafar and @beingsalmankhan @atulreellife ..the bestest boys and Alvira khan (the bestest girl)."Katrina Kaif and Salman Khan last featured together in 'Tiger Zinda Hai'. 'Bharat' will be the second film, which will see the collaboration of Katrina, Salman and Ali Abbas. The film is slated to hit the screens on Eid 2019.
