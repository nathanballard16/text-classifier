Several explosions hit UAE port of Fujairah

ABU DHABI: Several powerful explosions on Sunday hit the port of Fujairah in UAE following a huge fire that engulfed at least seven oil tankers, state media reported.The blasts were heard between 4 am and 7 am local time (00:00 - 03:00 GMT), the Al-Mayadeen broadcaster reported, adding that from seven to 10 oil tankers were in flames.However, according to local sources, the causes of the incident remains unknown, Sputnik reported.However, within hours of the report of blast, the UAE emirate of Fujairah denied the "unidentified" media reports about the blast at the port, reported Reuters quoting Fujairah government media office.
