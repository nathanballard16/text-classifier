Donning sultry outfits, Janhvi Kapoor is here to woo you with her killer expressions - see photos

Bollywood actress Janhvi Kapoor | Photo Credit: Instagram

Last year with Shashank Khaitan's Dhadak, Boney Kapoor and late Sridevi's elder daughter, Janvhi Kapoor made her big Bollywood debut. The diva was not only praised a lot by the audience for her spectacular performance but she also garnered a massive fan following after her first film. Apart from this, with her simple and chic sense of style, Janhvi has also become a fashion influencer and a style icon for many millennials. Recently, she featured on the cover of a fashion magazine and we are smitten by her killer looks.

The 22-year-old actress took to her Instagram and shared her cover of the Peacock magazine. On the cover, Janhvi is seen donning a red crop-top with a plunging neckline which she teamed up with a shimmery red skirt and red boots. She has accessorised her look with chunky earrings and a bangle.

Another photo of the actress was also shared on the official Insta handle of the magazine. In the snap, Janvhi looked super hot in a black sequin mini dress with thigh-high boots. Take a look.

On the movie front, Janvhi has bagged a vital role in Karan Johar's upcoming directorial, Takht. The period drama is set in the Mughal era and also stars Ranveer Singh, Kareena Kapoor Khan, Vicky Kaushal, Bhumi Pednekar and Anil Kapoor in lead roles.

Apart from this, Janhvi will also be playing the lead role in the upcoming Gunjan Saxena biopic. Gunjan Saxena, the first woman Indian Air Force combat pilot, played an important role during the Kargil War in 1999. For her daredevilry, she was conferred the Shaurya Chakra by the Government of India.
