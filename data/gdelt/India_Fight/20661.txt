Sye Raa Narasimha Reddy: Chiranjeevi, Amitabh Bachchan starrer film to be shot in dense Kerela forests

Sye Raa Narasimha Reddy to be shot in Kerala forests

When two megastars of the film industry come together for the film, there is no doubt that it will be the grand one. Telugu superstar Chiranjeevi and Bollywood's iconic actor Amitabh Bachchan have joined hands for the upcoming film Sye Raa Narasimha Reddy. Made in the whopping budget of Rs 200 crore the film is produced by Ram Charan's Konidela Production Company will release this year, however, the release date is not finalised yet.

The film is not only making a lot of noise for its stellar star cast but the mammoth budget is the making a lot of headlines too. According to many reports on the internet, the makers are spending nearly Rs 54 crore on the war sequence itself. It will reportedly appear in the climax and runs for eight minutes.

There were reports of Sye Raa Narasimha Reddy team taking nearly 150 people from Hyderabad to Georgia for the shooting with hundreds of costumes. Apart from these 150 people, they have hired 600 local artists to be part of the war episode. Well, that sounds huge, doesn't it?

No other reports have cropped up on the social media which state how the several scenes of the film will be shot in the alluring forests of Kerela. The 10-day schedule will have not only the battle sequences but even some of the crucial sequences will be shot in the dense green forests.
