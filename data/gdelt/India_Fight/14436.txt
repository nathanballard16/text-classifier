Youth shot dead in Shahdol

BHOPAL: A youth was shot dead by an unidentified accused in Beohari town of Shahdol district on Monday. The incident took place at Bordiha village of the district. Sources said that the deceased has been identified as Ram Narayan Gautam, alias Monu, 30.Ram was was walking on a road near his village at around 10am when he was shot at, said sources.The accused fled the spot immediately after the incident, sources added.Panic gripped the area after the incident and locals immediately informed the police.Senior police officials, including SP of Shahdol, rushed to the spot and started investigation.A case has been registered against an unidentified youth, said police.
