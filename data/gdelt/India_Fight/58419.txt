MP: Six dead as elevator at farm house crashes

Six people were killed on Tuesday when an elevator crashed at a farm house near Mhow, 25 km from Indore, the police said.

Sub Divisional Officer of Police Vinod Sharma told PTI that one of the deceased was identified as Puneet Agrawal.

The incident took place around 6 in the evening and further probe was on, he said.
