New Delhi: Police arrests 3 for murder

The police have arrested Priti Jain, wife of deceased Subodh who allegedly conspired the murder along with her lover Rahul Jain. According to police, all three accused- Priti Jain, Rahul Jain, and Vijay Hooda have confessed to their involvement in committing the crime. A key Subodh’s main gate has also been recovered from accused Rahul Jain,” the police further said. The police have registered an FIR under section 302 (murder) of the Indian Penal Code on May 2. New Delhi: Delhi Police arrested three accused in a murder case which took place in New Usmanpur area of the national capital, the police said on Friday.
