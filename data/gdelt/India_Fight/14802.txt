Hyderabad: Police busted gambling racket, six held

HYDERABAD: Special Operation Team of Shamsabad Zone arrested six persons who were involved in gambling. They were playing flashcards in the Port Grand area.

On receiving a tip, police raided the area and arrested six persons. Police also seized Rs 8,330 and a set of flashcards from their possession.

They were handed over to Shamsabad police for taking further action.
