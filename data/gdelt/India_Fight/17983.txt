NSG takes over PM meet venue

Rajamahendravaram: Arrangements are being made for the election meeting of of Prime Minister Narendra Modi to the on Monday. National Security Guard (NSG) personnel took over the control of the Government College grounds where Modi will participate in a public meeting at 1 pm.

Officials who came from New Delhi inspected government headquarters hospital and took a ward under their control to meet any emergency situation. They also ran a trial convoy from airport to the venue of the meeting.

District collector Karthikeya Misra also accompanied the officials from the Delhi. BJP national leaders, state president Kanna Lakshmi Narayana, state leaders and MP candidates will take part in the meeting.
