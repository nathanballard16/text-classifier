Boy killed after falling on railway track

Compiled by MARTIN CARVALHO, OH ING YEEN and R. ARAVINTHAN

A BOY in Tamil Nadu, India, was killed after he fell onto a railway track and was struck by an oncoming train, Malaysia Nanban reported.Identified only as Aditya, the nine-year-old was apparently playing on the platform while waiting with his parents at a train station in Chennai.
