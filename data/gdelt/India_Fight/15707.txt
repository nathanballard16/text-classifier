Fire broke out at factory in Mundka in west Delhi

Fire officials said a massive fire broke out at a factory situated at Swaran Park in west Delhi’s Mundka.

The fire department was called at around 11.35 pm and dispatched 20 fire tenders to the spot.

No casualties were reported and efforts were on to douse the blaze, said the officials.

In another incident, a minor fire broke out at a shop in Govindpuri. Three fire tenders were rushed to the spot and the fire was doused successfully.
