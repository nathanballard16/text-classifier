Orchid Isle Estates investigation classified as murder

Hawaii Island police have identified the male victim whose body was found dead on Wednesday in the Orchid Isle Estates subdivision in Puna. He is identified as 75-year-old Shingo Honda.

Hawaii Island police have identified the male victim whose body was found dead on Wednesday in the Orchid Isle Estates subdivision in Puna. He is identified as 75-year-old Shingo Honda.

Approximately 7:18 p.m. Wednesday, Puna patrol officers responded to the subdivision for a report of a missing elderly male. A short time later, officers located the male’s deceased body several hundred yards from the residence.

ADVERTISING

On Friday morning an autopsy was performed and Honda was subsequently identified through fingerprints.

Detectives with the Area I Criminal Investigation Section are continuing this investigation. It has been classified as a murder.

ADVERTISING

Anyone who may have information about this incident is asked to call the Police Department’s non-emergency line at 935-3311 or Detective Todd Pataray at 961-2382 or todd.pataray@hawaiicounty.gov.

Tipsters who prefer to remain anonymous may call the island-wide Crime Stoppers number at 961-8300 and may be eligible for a reward of up to $1,000. Crime Stoppers is a volunteer program run by ordinary citizens who want to keep their community safe. Crime Stoppers does not record calls or subscribe to any Caller ID service. All Crime Stoppers information is kept confidential.
