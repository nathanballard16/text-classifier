Haren Pandya’s wife gets 3-year extension as child rights body chief

The Gujarat government has granted an extension to Jagruti Pandya, wife of former Gujarat Home minister and senior BJP leader Haren Pandya, who was shot dead in Ahmedabad in 2003, as chairperson of State Commission for Protection of Child Rights (SCPCR). After this, Jagruti was appointed as the chairperson of SCPCR in January 2016.
