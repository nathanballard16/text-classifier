Sivakarthikeyan's next titled Doctor; shooting to begin this week

Sivakarthikeyan announces his next project | Photo Credit: Twitter

Sivakarthikeyan, who was last seen on the big screen in Pandiraj's Namma Veetu Pillai, announced the title of his next film on Monday. This film is titled Doctor and it will be jointly produced by Sivakarthikeyan’s production house and KJR Studios. The film is to be directed by Nelson Dilip Kumar, who had debuted last year with Nayanthara's Kolamaavu Kokila. Anirudh Ravichander will be the composer for the movie and cinematography will be done by Vijay Kartik Kannan.

Very happy to share that my next film will be with my dearmost friends @Nelson_director & @anirudhofficial titled as #DOCTOR Once again happy to be associated with @kjr_studios Shoot starts soon @SKProdOffl pic.twitter.com/W82ltJrbHK — Sivakarthikeyan (@Siva_Kartikeyan) December 2, 2019

Debutant Priyanka Arul Mohan will play the female lead. Yogi Babu and Vinay also play important roles. Doctor will go on floors in Chennai from December 6.

Meanwhile, Sivakarthikeyan is waiting for the release of his upcoming film Hero, which is directed by Mithran. The film also stars Abhay Deol, Arjun, Kalyani Priyadarshan and Ivana in important roles. The music of Hero has been composed by Yuvan Shankar Raja. The film is produced by Kotapadi J Rajesh under his banner of KJR Studios. Hero is set to hit the screens worldwide on December 20.

Earlier, the teaser of Hero received a good response from the audience, especially on social media. The teaser of the film already has over 6.3 million views on YouTube.
