Kerch Strait mishap: Bodies of sailors, rescued men unlikely to reach India soon

AdvertisingOfficials said that with four days having passed since the mishap, shipping authorities have also expressed reservations regarding the retrieval of bodies of the missing sailors. The missing Indian seamen have been identified as Siddharth Meher, Neeraj Singh, Sebastian Britto Breezlin Sahayaraj, Rushikesh, Akshay Baban Jadhav and Anandasekar Avinash. Indian authorities have said six sailors have been killed, four have been rescued and six are still missing. The official added that the survivors are expected to be questioned by local investigators to understand the cause of the mishap. “We need concurrence of from the next of kin to conduct autopsies before the bodies can be released,” said the official.
