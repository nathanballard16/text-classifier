Two Jasdan diamond association members die in accident

RAJKOT: Three including two members of Jasdan Diamond Association were killed when the car they were travelling in was rammed by an unidentified vehicle near Visnagar town of Mehsana district on Monday.The deceased were identified as Jasdan Diamond Association members — Nikeshkumar Shah and Alpesh Shah — and the car driver, Jigar Goswami.“Nikeshkumar and Alpesh were returning to Jasdan from Visnagar where they had gone to purchase diamonds when they met with the accident. Such massive was the impact of the collision that their car got badly mangled,” said an official from Visnagar police station.
