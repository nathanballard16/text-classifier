Body of missing 18-year-old found in Golden Gate canal

A death investigation is underway after a body was found in Golden Gate on Monday.

Deputies said the body belonged to 18-year-old Anthony Green who was reported missing by his family on Sunday.

The body was found at Santa Barbara and 16th Place Southwest.

Family members said he got into a fight with some friends and that he tried to run away through a canal. He didn’t make it across.
