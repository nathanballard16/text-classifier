Fire breaks out at shoe factory in Delhi's Narela, 12 fire tenders rushed

NEW DELHI: A massive fire broke out at a shoe factory in Delhi's Narela Industrial Area in the early hours of Tuesday.

According to ANI, at least 12 fire tenders have been rushed to the spot to douse the fire.

Delhi: Fire broke out at a shoe factory in Narela Industrial area in the early morning hours, 12 fire tenders present at the spot. pic.twitter.com/MvNGDiJ9VW — ANI (@ANI) 19 February 2019

It is not known yet as to what caused the fire.

Delhi: Fire broke out at a shoe factory in Narela Industrial area in the early morning hours, 12 fire tenders present at the spot, fire fighting operation underway pic.twitter.com/gH7mt1wrQ2 — ANI (@ANI) 19 February 2019

''A fire broke out at a shoe factory on Tuesday,'' a Fire Brigade official said, adding that there were no reports of any casualty.

The blaze, that broke out at 3.15 AM, spread across three other factories located in the Narela industrial area,

"12 fire tenders were rushed to the spot and cooling operation is underway," the fire department official said.
