Ludhiana: Hit-and-run claims 55-year-old man’s life

LUDHIANA: A 55-year-old man was killed in a hit-and-run at Karabara Chowk, Jodhewal, late on Tuesday night.Dharminder Kumar, a resident of Karabara, worked as a mason. After winding up his work, he was walking back home when an unidentified vehicle hit him, killing him on the spot.Jodhewal police station SHO sub-inspector Arshdeep Kaur Grewal said a case has been registered against the unidentified vehicle driver.
