Delhi: Shootout near Rohini’s Swarn Jayanti Park, 5 people of Neeraj Bawana gang taken into custody

Five criminals of Neeraj Bawana gang were taken into custody after a brief shootout in Delhi’s Rohini area during early morning hours on Monday. The Neeraj Bawana gang members were wanted in two murder cases. The shootout happened near Swarn Jayanti Park in Rohini. The police said that five types of weapons have been seized from their possession. According to the news agency ANI, a team of Special Cell of Delhi Police had undertaken the operation after a specific tip-off about the criminals.
