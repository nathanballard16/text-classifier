Pilgrimage turns fatal for family from Telangana

Five members of a family from Karimnagar in Telangana were killed, and a four-year-old boy was seriously injured when the car in which they were travelling rammed a truck on the Chennai-Kolkata highway at Mocharla village, near Gudlur, in Prakasam district on Friday.

Thirty-year-old V. Vamsi, a software professional, reportedly lost control of his car and rammed the truck, resulting in the instantaneous death of himself, his wife V. Archana, father-in-law K. Madhava Rao (62), mother-in-law K. Leela and his son, V. Krishanth (3). Vamsi’s another son, V. Advaith, was rushed to a private hospital in Nellore for treatment, Kandukur Deputy Superintendent of Police B. Ravichandra said. The family on a pilgramage was proceeding to Srikalahasti in Chittoor district.
