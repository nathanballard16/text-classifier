Four killed in India gun battle

Kolkata: A gun battle between supporters of India’s ruling right-wing party and a regional rival has killed four people in West Bengal, police said, as violence raged in the tinderbox state.

At least 18 others were injured in the clashes that broke out on Saturday in the eastern state that has been on edge since Prime Minister Narendra Modi’s Bhara­tiya Janata Party (BJP) launched an aggressive campaign to win parliamentary seats last year.
