2 murdered over suspected monetary dispute 1 held

New Delhi: Two persons were shot dead in a suspected monetary dispute case in Shahbad Dairy area on Monday. Police identified the deceased as Amit (30)and his servant Nikhil Gulati (40). The report was lodged by Amit's brother Jaideep the next day at police station Shahbad Dairy. "An altercation took place between them which resulted in the murder of both Amit and Nikhil Gulati," said DCP. Later on, the bodies of both Amit and Nikhil Gulati were found in the canal in the area of K. N. Katju Marg police station on October 17.
