Vadodara: Missing medicine wholesaler found dead in river after 2 days

VADODARA: The medicine wholesaler from city who was swept away with his car from a causeway in Dev river was found dead on Thursday – two days after the incident. Chetan Thakkar (36) was on his way to supply medicines to a doctor in Vyara village of Waghodiya taluka on Tuesday when the district was lashed with heavy rains. While crossing the causeway over the river which was already in spate, Thakkar could not judge the strong currents of water and decided to wade his car through water.Thakkar, a resident of Waghodiya Road, was moving ahead slowly when a strong wave of water dragged his car away and within no time he disappeared along with his car in the river. The National Disaster Response Force (NDRF) searched for the man on Tuesday and Wednesday but could not find anything.As the water level in the river decreased on Thursday morning, NDRF personnel found his body at a check dam which is located three kilometers downstream of the causeway.“The man’s family members have identified him, but his car has not been traced yet,” said sub-inspector B H Rathod of Waghodiya police station. NDRF team is making efforts to find the car as well.
