Kartik Aaryan heads out to gorge on street food in Lucknow amidst Pati Patni Aur Woh shoot; See Pics

While shooting in different parts of the country, Bollywood actors get a chance to enjoy the food specialities of that place. Speaking of this, Lucknow is famous for its street food and often when stars shoot there, they head out to relish some snacks. Speaking of this, Pati Patni Aur Woh remake starring Kartik Aaryan, Ananya Panday and Bhumi Pednekar is being shot in Lucknow. Kartik, who is a foodie, seems to have decided to gorge on special delicacies of the city.

A couple of photos of the Luka Chuppi actor have surfaced on social media in which Kartik can be seen heading out with a crew member from the shoot to relish some of the delicious street food snacks. In the photos, we can see Kartik standing on a kachori place with the crew member. While the street vendor makes the kachori for Kartik, he can be seen inspecting how they are made. Indeed, fans in Lucknow are having a gala time seeing Kartik heading to local places in their city.
