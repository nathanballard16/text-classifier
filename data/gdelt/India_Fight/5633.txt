Truck mows down watchman

Surat: A 55-year-old watchman of a textile market was found dead on a road near Jay Mahavir Textile Market here on Tuesday. Three persons on the truck walked up to him and gave him some money for medical treatment and went away. Police sent the body for postmortem.Police identified the deceased as Rajujagar Tiwari, a watchman of Adarsh Textile Market-2. There were no visible injury marks on his body, but his nose and mouth oozed blood. A case will be registered against the truck driver for rash and negligent driving or culpable homicide not amounting to murder.
