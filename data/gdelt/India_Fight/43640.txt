Three of Indian family killed in road accident in Oman

Dubai: An Indian couple and their eight-month-old baby were killed while another child was injured when their car crashed into another vehicle in Oman, officials said Sunday.

All the three suffered severe head injuries in the road accident that took place while they were on way back here from Salalah, the Indian Consulate here said.

Ghousulla Azmathulla Khan, 30, his 29-year-old wife, Ayesha Siddiqua, and their eight-month-old son Hamza Khan died on the spot in the accident while their three-year-old baby Haniya Siddiqua is fighting for her life in the hospital.

The bodies of the deceased Indians were sent to their hometown in Hyderabad from Muscat this morning, the Indian Consulate said.

“We have been in touch with the company where Ghousulla was employed as well as the relatives of the family and our mission, the Indian Embassy in Muscat,” Consul General of India in Dubai Vipul was quoted as saying by ‘Gulf News’.
