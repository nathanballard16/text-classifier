Jammu&Kashmir: One Terrorist Neutralised In Budgam Encounter; Operation Continues In Chadoora Area

One terrorist was decimated in an encounter going on in Jammu and Kashmir’s Budgam. The encounter broke out on Sunday between terrorists and security forces in Budgam district of Jammu and Kashmir, police said. Security forces launched a cordon and search operation in Bugam area of Chadoora in Budgam district in central Kashmir on Sunday morning following specific inputs about the presence of militants there, a police official said.
