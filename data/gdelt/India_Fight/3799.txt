Fire Breaks Out At Building In Mumbai's Girgaon

Fire tenders rushed to the spot and a rescue operation is underway, police said.

A fire broke out at a building in Mumbai's Girgaon on Wednesday, police said.

The fire started at Mochi building in Girgaon. The cause of the fire is not yet known. No casualties have been reported.

Fire tenders rushed to the spot and a rescue operation is underway, police said.
