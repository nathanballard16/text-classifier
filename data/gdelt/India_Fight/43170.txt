40 Terrorists Entered Into Kashmir

Srinagar: Nearly 40 terrorists have crossed the border from Pakistan to India near Line of Control (Loc), said security forces.

According to reports, terrorists may plan for terror attacks in any of the sensitive areas of the country. We have made attempts to stop several terrorists from entry into the country, but there is still a possibility that some of the terrorists might have entered into the country, says Dilbag Singh, J&K Police director-general.
