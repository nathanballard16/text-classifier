Gym trainer arrested for killing his father in Dwarka

New Delhi: A 27-year-old gym trainer was arrested for killing his father in Dwarka district. Police said that during interrogation accused claimed that his father was very abusive. On interrogation, Kumar stated that his father had lost his right hand a few years back in an accident. On August 27 deceased confronted the accused on some domestic issue but accused avoided and did not give much attention. Accused, took out the pistol and shot his father when he was playing cards with his friends in the neighbourhood.
