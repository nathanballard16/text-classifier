2 killed in car accident near Bridgman, police say

LAKE TOWNSHIP, Mich – According to the Baroda-Lake Township Police Department, a 59-year-old man and 70-year-old woman were killed in a car accident near the intersection of Jericho and Linco Roads Saturday afternoon near Bridgman, Michigan.

The man and woman were in the same car driving southbound when there was a collision between them and the truck of a 21-year-old male driving westbound on Linco Road.

The man driving the truck did not sustain any injuries.

The man and woman who died are both from Rochester, and the 21-year-old is from Stevensville.

The accident is under investigation.
