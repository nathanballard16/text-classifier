Jagan hails IAF air strike on Pak terrorist camps

Taking to Twitter, the YSRCP chief conveyed his best wishes to the Indian Air Force team for their brave action against the terrorist camps in Balakot today morning. Hyderabad: Leader of the Opposition in Andhra Pradesh Assembly and YSR Congress Party chief YS Jaganmohan Reddy today hailed brave action by the Indian Air Force on Pakistani terrorist camps in retaliation for Pulwama attack. “Congratulations to @IAF_MCC and its fearless pilots for their commendable action against JM terrorists. We are proud of you for #Surgicalstrike2.”Source: NSS
