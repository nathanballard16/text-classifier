Over 100 cars gutted in fire at parking lot in Chennai's Porur

New Delhi:

More than 100 cars have been gutted in a major fire at a parking lot in a ground opposite to Ramachandra Medical College in Chennai’s Porur on Sunday. Fire engines had to battle for more than two hours to douse the fire. The cause of the fire is yet to be determined. The incident came a day after 300 cars were gutted in a fire at a parking lot at the Aero India show in Bengaluru.
