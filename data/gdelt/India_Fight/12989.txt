Gujarat: CWC meet on March 12, Congress to firm up strategy for Lok Sabha battle

The Congress Working Committee is set to meet on March 12 in Ahmedabad to firm up its election strategy for the Lok Sabha polls, the party announced on Tuesday. Congress president Rahul Gandhi, former president Sonia Gandhi, former prime minister Manmohan Singh, newly inducted general secretary Priyanka Gandhi, and other top leaders of Congress party will take part in the CWC meet and the public rally. "The CWC meeting will take place on March 12. More than 60 party leaders will be attending the CWC meet, which is taking place in Gujarat for the first time since 1961. The party's top leadership would take part in the meeting where the strategy for the upcoming Lok Sabha elections would be discussed.
