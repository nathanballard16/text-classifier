Three-year-old attacked by leopard dies

RAJKOT: The three-year-old girl who was attacked by a leopard in Hadmatiya village of Gadhada taluka in Gir Somnath district on July 4 succumbed to her injuries at Rajkot Civil Hospital on Monday.Police said Neha (3), daughter of Vipul Mer, was sleeping with her grandmother Rami in the courtyard of her house.The leopard seized her by her throat and started dragging her. When Rami raised a cry and the neighbours joined in, the leopard dropped Neha and fled into the forest.“However, the leopard left deep wounds in the throat of the girl which suppurated, leading to her death,” said a police offici al.
