Group Editor of 'India Unbound' found dead in Thane

Thane (Maharashtra) [India], Mar 17 (ANI): Group Editor of direct-mailed magazine 'India Unbound', Nityanand Pandey's was found dead here on Saturday, police said.

Pandey's body was found underneath a bridge in Bhiwandi Taluka area on Saturday.

Additional Superintendent of Police, Thane (Rural) said the journalist was missing since March 15.

Investigation is currently underway. (ANI)
