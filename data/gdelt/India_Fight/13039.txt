Supreme Court reserves order on mediation in Ayodhya case

The Supreme Court on Wednesday reserved its order on whether the politically-sensitive Ram Janmabhoomi-Babri Masjid land dispute can be settled through mediation.

A five-judge Constitution Bench headed by Chief Justice Ranjan Gogoi heard the contesting parties.

11:30 am

Umesh Jadhav shares stage with BJP leaders

Umesh Jadhav (first from Left), who recently quit Congress, shares the dais with BJP leaders including B.S. Yeddyurappa at a rally in Kalaburagi on Wednesday.

Putting an end to all speculations, Umesh Jadhav, the Congress MLA from Chincholi who submitted his resignation to Assembly Speaker Ramesh Kumar on Monday, was seen on the stage in Kalaburagi, where Prime Minister Narendra Modi is going to address public rally in a couple of hours.

BJP State unit president B.S. Yeddyurappa, former chief minister Jagadish Shettar, Bidar Lok Sabha member Bhagwant Khuba, and state BJP leaders Govind Karajol, Malikayya Guttedar, and Baburao Chinchanasur were also present.

Mr. Jadhav's supporters from the crowd cheered and raised slogans hailing him as he emerged on the stage. - Kumar Buradikatti

Fire breaks out at Delhi’s CGO complex

Fire department personnel trying to douse the flames on the fifth floor of Pandit Deendayal Antyodaya Bhawan of the CGO complex in New Delhi on Wednesday. | Photo Credit: R.V. Moorthy

A major fire was reported at Pandit Deendayal Antyodaya Bhawan at CGO Complex on March 6 morning. No casualties have been reported. At least 24 fire tenders have reached the spot and are carrying out the firefighting operations.
