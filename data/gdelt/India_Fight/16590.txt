Fire at SBI's Kurnool main branch

A massive fire broke out at the State Bank of India's (SBI) main branch here on Saturday.

Important files, computers as well as cash were burnt in the fire. Several bank employees were trapped in the fire, sources said.

The firemen rushed to the spot to extinguish the fire. The reason behind the fire is yet to be known.
