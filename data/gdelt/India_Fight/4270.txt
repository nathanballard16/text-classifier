Pune man tries to kill wife on suspicion of having illicit relationship with her father-in-law, arrested

Later, in the evening he attacked her with a knife. | Photo Credit: Thinkstock

Pune: In an appalling case reported from Pune, Maharashtra, a man was arrested by the Pimpri-Chinchwad police for attempting to kill his wife on Friday. The accused had a suspicion on her wife of her having an affair with his father.

According to a report in the Hindustan Times (HT), the man was identified as 28-year-old, Siddhesh Sanjay Satav, a resident of Sumangal residency in Susgaon, Mulshi.

Reportedly, he was arrested by police on Friday at around 8:40 pm. Meanwhile, his 23-year-old wife claimed that he hit her on multiple occasions as he suspected her to have relationships with other men including his own father.

The woman filed a complaint with the local police and mentioned that on Friday morning, the man first allegedly beat her up with a plastic bat as he believed that she was speaking with his father over the phone. Later, in the evening he attacked her with a knife.

In her complaint, she further mentioned that he also tried to strangle her with his hands. The Sub-inspector, Yogesh Ramekar of Hinjewadi police station told HT, that the accused is a gym instructor and she is a housewife. They have been in an arranged married since 2016 and have a one-and-half-old daughter.

However, the police have filed a case under various sections of the Indian Penal Code.
