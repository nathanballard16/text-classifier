Ranbir Kapoor makes a handsome appearance at an event: View Pictures

Ranbir Kapoor is one of the most handsome and popular Bollywood actors. The actor enjoys a huge fan following. His popularity is such that he is also the face or the ambassador of several brands in the country. He is often spotted shooting for brands or attending events for the same.

Today, in the evening, the Rockstar actor was snapped at an event in Mumbai. It was after a long time Ranbir was papped in the city. For the event, Ranbir dressed up in a semi-casual look and looked handsome as ever. He was all smiles for the shutterbugs and even posed for them.

Last year, Ranbir Kapoor was seen in the film Sanju. The film was a biopic on actor Sanjay Dutt's life and Kapoor impressed everyone with his performance. The actor has been very busy shooting his films lately. He wrapped the schedule of Ayan Mukerji's Brahmastra a few months ago in which he stars alongside Alia Bhatt and Amitabh Bachchan.
