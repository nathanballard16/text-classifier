Kareena Kapoor Khan gets a special souvenir for Taimur Ali Khan from Australia and we totally love it; See pic

Taimur Ali Khan and Kareena Kapoor Khan are back to the bay after their Australian sojourn and yesterday, this mother-son duo were papped at the Mumbai airport. While Kareena stunned in a formal look as she wore a blazer with jeans, Taimur as always, looked cute as a button as he wore a blue tee paired with blue jeans but what caught our attention was Taimru’s tee as it read Australia and looks like, mommy decided to buy a souvenir for his son which he decided to wear on his way back to the bay.



For all those who don’t know, Kareena Kapoor Khan was in Melbourne to unveil the trophy for the T 20 matches and during an event, she was asked as to what will she do if she was stuck in a lift with Ranbir Kapoor’s girlfriend Alia Bhatt and his ex girlfriends- Katrina Kaif and Deepika Padukone and Bebo had said that she will make sure that Ranbir, too is in the lift.

On the work front, Kareena Kapoor Khan recently wrapped up the shooting of Homi Adajania’s Angrezi Medium starring Irrfan wherein Bebo will play the role of a cop named Naina, and next, she will start shooting for Karan Johar’s period drama Takht co-starring Ranveer Singh, Alia Bhatt, Janhvi Kapoor, Vicky Kaushal, Anil Kapoor and Bhumi Pednekar.
