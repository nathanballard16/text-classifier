Kejriwal's car attacked in Narela, no casualties: Official

Delhi Chief Minister Arvind Kejriwal's car was on Friday allegedly attacked by a mob.

New Delhi: Delhi Chief Minister Arvind Kejriwal's car was on Friday allegedly attacked by a mob armed with sticks in Narela, an official in the Chief Minister's Office said.

Nobody was hurt, he said. A group of about 100 men tried to stop Kejriwal's car and attacked it with sticks.

The incident occurred when Kejriwal had gone to the outer Delhi locality to inaugurate development works in 25 unauthorised colonies, he said.
