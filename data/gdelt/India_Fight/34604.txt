Andhra: 3 killed, human sacrifice suspected

TIRUPATI: Three people, including the priest of a local temple, were killed in a suspected human sacrifice horror in Anantapur district of Andhra Pradesh early on Monday.Priest Sivarami Reddy (70), his sister K Kamalamma (75) and a devotee Satya Lakshmamma (70), who came from Bengaluru to fulfill her vow by spending a night at the local Shiva temple in Korthikota village , were found brutally murdered with their throats slit and their blood poured over the Shivalinga and ant-hills adjacent to the temple.Devotees who arrived at the temple in the morning were shocked to see the bodies and alerted the police.
