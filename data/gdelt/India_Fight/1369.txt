DSC guard commits suicide

Mumbai (Maharashtra) [India], Jan 10 (ANI): A guard of the Defence Security Corps (DSC), deployed at Naval Armament Depot, committed suicide by shooting himself while on duty on Thursday.

Kesar Singh (56) shot himself with a 7.62 mm SLR this morning.

A case of accidental death has been registered at Trombay Police Station.

The body has been shifted to JJ Hospital for post-mortem.

More details of the incident are awaited. (ANI)
