Trekker dies of heart failure in Ladakh

A trekker from Mumbai was found dead on Thursday by the Indian Air Force rescue team in Ladakh region of Jammu and Kashmir, officials said.

"Daval Suresh Shah suffered cardiac arrest near Tibb Cave on Chadar Trek in Leh district," officials here said.

The body was airlifted to Leh air force station and after completing the legal formalities it will be sent to Mumbai.
