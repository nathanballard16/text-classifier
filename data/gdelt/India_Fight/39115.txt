Three held for killing two in robbery bid

Ghaziabad: Ghaziabad Police have arrested three criminals for allegedly killing two persons amid a robbery attempt in Tronica city area. Cops said that the arrested persons are members of dreaded Bawariya gang. On August 5, two persons were killed while another sustained bullet injuries when nearly half a dozen robbers barged into their house to commit robbery. The incident occurred around 3 am in Pooja colony area when a grocery shop owner, Dharamveer was sleeping outside his house. However, when the family members tried to resist the robbery attempt, the criminals opened fire on them in which Dharamveer was killed after three bullets hit him.
