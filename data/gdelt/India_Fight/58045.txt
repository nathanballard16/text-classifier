Congress Leader Rahul Yadav Shot Dead By Unidentified Assailants In Bihar's Vaishali

New Delhi:

Congress leader Rahul Yadav was shot dead in Bihar’s Vaishali by unidentified assailants on Saturday, reported ANI. The police have begun investigating the matter.

Bihar: Congress leader Rahul Yadav shot dead in Vaishali by unidentified assailants.Police begin investigation — ANI (@ANI) December 28, 2019

This comes at least two weeks after a constable, 46, posted at the residence of BJP MP Brij Bhushan Sharan Singh has shot himself dead. According to the reports, the official was identified as Unish Kumar of Security Unit of Delhi Police.

The constable, Narela resident, killed himself with his service pistol. No suicide note was recovered from the spot. PCR reached the spot and found the body of the PSO lying in pool of blood on the floor with entry and exit wounds in his head.

Earlier, in October this year Hindu Mahasabha leader, Kamlesh Tiwari was shot dead at his office by unidentified assailants in a broad daylight in the Naka area of Lucknow in Uttar Pradesh. Tiwari was shot once and stabbed 15 times, the autopsy report revealed.
