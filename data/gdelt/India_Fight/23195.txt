Massive fire breaks out at Delhi’s Shastri Bhawan

A huge fire broke out at Shastri Bhawan in the national capital on Tuesday afternoon. At least seven fire tenders have been rushed to the spot to douse the blaze.

Shastri Bhawan is a government building in central Delhi which is also from where the Ministry of Welfare – among other ministries – operates.

A fire had broken out here in July of 2017 as well. A short circuit was the cause fire at the time and it was quickly brought under control. Before this, a minor fire had broken out here in 2014 as well.
