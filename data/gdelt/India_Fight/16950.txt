5-year-old girl killed in leopard attack at field

PIMPRI CHINCHWAD: A five-year-old girl was killed in leopard attack at Sakori village, around 75km from Pune, on Sunday evening.This is the second death of a child in a leopard attack in Pune district this year so far. The grandmother, Kusum Kaduskar, had gone to harvest maize for fodder in the field around 5pm on Sunday and Shrutika accompanied her. “After the attack, the girl cried for help. It abandoned the child and fled,” he said.The village is located along Ghod river and has many sugar cane fields here. This is when the leopard, which was hiding nearby, attacked her.
