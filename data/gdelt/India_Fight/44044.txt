Oman: Indian suspected of murdering Omani family arrested

Muscat: An Indian national suspected of the murder of an entire Omani family has been arrested, according to online reports.

The Gujarat Anti-Terrorism Squad (ATS) detained the suspect in Ahmedabad after a notice for arrest was posted on international police channels, including Interpol.

The man is one of several people wanted in Oman for allegedly murdering five members of a family, including three children aged 12, nine and six.

Omani authorities had provided Indian police with details that led to his capture, according to a report in the Times of India.
