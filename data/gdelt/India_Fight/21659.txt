Clash between LDF-UDF workers in Kollam: Kerala

The last day of campaigning in Kerala that is scheduled to undergo polling tomorrow, witnessed a clash between the workers of Left Democratic Front (LDF) and United Democratic Front (UDF).

The other parts of state as well witnessed the violence between cadres of political rivals, forcing police to resort to lathi-charge and firing tear gas to disperse warring crowds.

The Communist Party of India (Marxist) – led LDF, Congress-led UDF and BJP are the three prominent parties to contest for the seats in the state.

The counting of the votes will be done on May 23.
