School principal found dead in Mahi River

Vadodara: A 35-year-old principal of a private school in Bajwa was found dead in Mahisagar River in Lanchanpura, around 40 km from the city, on Friday. Bhargavi Suthar had gone missing city on Wednesday. Her family members had registered a missing persons complaint at Sama police station on Thursday.

According to police, Bhargavi had left the city on her two-wheeler. Sama police inspector PD Parmar said that they found the two-wheeler near the spot where Suthar’s body was found. Cops suspect it to be a case of suicide.

Savli police, probing the case, have also taken statements of the family members.
