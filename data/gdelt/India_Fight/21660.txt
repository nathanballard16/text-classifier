Lucknow: In a case reported, a 37-year-old man was arrested on Sunday for allegedly killing a woman. The 73-year-old woman was identified as Savitri Devi, who was found dead with her throat and wrist slit on Wednesday morning, reported news agency ANI. The incident happened in Modinagar district of Uttar Pradesh.

The police officials mentioned that the body was found by her neighbours in a room near the courtyard in Devi's home. The deceased's son Tejpal had filed an FIR under section 302 of the Indian Penal Code. The Superintendent of police, rural area Neeraj Kumar Jadaun said that several injury marks were found on the deceased's body.

Meanwhile, police added that Devi's body was lying in a pool of blood on her coat and her hands and legs were tied with a sari. The deceased's ornaments were not missing and her mobile phone was also recovered near the body.

The police have arrested Rajbir Singh, a native of Sikandrabad town in Bulandshahar district, in connection with the case. The accused presently living near the Sikri Railway Phatak of the district. The ANI report mentioned that Singh had confessed to the crime and claimed that he barged into the courtyard where the woman was sleeping in a bid to sexually assault her.

While he initiated the crime the woman began screaming, officials said. The ANI report mentioned that Singh has been sent to jail and he was in an inebriated at the time of the alleged killing.
