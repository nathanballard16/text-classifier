Delhi Police inspector beaten to death while filming illegal liquor trade in Vivek Vihar

However, the police investigation in the case is underway. | Photo Credit: Getty Images

New Delhi: In a horrifying case reported from the national capital, a Delhi police Sub-inspector was killed by criminals in East Delhi. The deceased inspector was identified as Sub-inspector Raj Kumar.

According to sources, Kumar was filming illegal trade of liquor in the area when he was brutally assaulted by the bootleggers. Meanwhile, the family members claimed that Raj Kumar were threatened by criminals earlier as well and yesterday during checking at Kasturba Nagar area at Vivek Vihar he was beaten to death by the criminals.

However, the police investigation in the case is underway.
