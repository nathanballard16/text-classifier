Pakistan violates ceasefire on LoC in Poonch, India responds

UNI

Jammu/UNI: Pakistan troops yet again on Saturday violated the ceasefire agreement by firing unprovoked along the Line of Control in Poonch district of Jammu and Kashmir.

Defence Spokesperson said here that at about 1130 hours Pakistan initiated unprovoked ceasefire violation by firing of Small arms and shelling with Mortars in Sunderbani Sector in District Rajouri.

"Indian Army is retaliating befittingly," he further said and added that no injury or major damage was reported.

Earlier on Nov 20 also Pakistan violated the truce in Poonch.
