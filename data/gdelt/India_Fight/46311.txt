Two trainee pilots killed as trainer aircraft crashes in south India - Xinhua

Source: Xinhua| 2019-10-06 21:38:23|Editor: Xiaoxia

Video Player Close

NEW DELHI, Oct. 6 (Xinhua) -- Two trainee pilots were killed after a trainer aircraft crashed on Sunday in the southern Indian state of Telangana, state-run broadcaster "All India Radio" reported.

The incident occurred in Bantawaram Mandal of Vikarabad district of Telangana.

It is reported that the aircraft, belonging to a private aviation training institute in the city, crashed due to bad weather.
