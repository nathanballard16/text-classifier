Is This the Beginning of the End for Trinamool?

Three Trinamool Congress MLAs including Subhrangshu Roy, Tushar Kanti Bhattacharya and CPI-M MLA Kanti Bhattacharya along with 52 councilors join BJP in the presence of BJP leaders Kailash Vijayvargiya and Mukul Roy at the party's headquarters in New Delhi on May 28. (IANS photo)
