Jawan shoots self to death in Jamnagar

Rajkot: An Amry jawan shot himself to death using his Insas rifle at the quarter guard of the 31 Infantry Brigade Bravo Company in Jamnagar on Tuesday night. After hearing the shots, other jawans posted there rushed to the place. One of the Army jawans posted there, Arun Vanniyar, informed us,” said an official from Jamnagar A-Division police station.“Kumar’s wife lives in the village. The deceased was identified as Vishakh Kumar P (25), who hailed from Pangode village in Thiruvananthapuram district of Kerala.“Kumar shot the bullet into his temple. We are yet to investigate the reasons for his suicide,” the official added.
