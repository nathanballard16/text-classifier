Fire breaks out at Aero India 2019 show

A fire broke out at the Aero India show 2019 in the general parking area at the front of Yelahanka Air Base in Bengaluru. The smoke was witnessed at the end of the morning aerobatics display on Saturday causing panic.

Defence officials tried to keep the situation calm at the Air Base. “The fire and smoke you see that from normal domestic fire of dried leaves. There is nothing to worry,” said announcements during the show.

According to the reports ten fire tenders have been appointed.

Fire department officials have claimed of 20 vehicles gutted in the fire that broke out at the Gate 5 of the general parking area. The actual cause of the fire is unknown, but is believed to be a ground fire from the dried grass in the parking area.
