New Delhi: The person, who opened fire outside the office of BJP MP Hans Raj Hans in Delhi's Rohini on Monday, was arrested.

The man, identified as 51-year-old Rameshwar Pehalwan has been booked under section 336 and 427 of the Indian Penal Code (IPC) and his vehicle and weapon have been recovered.

Pehalwan fired shots outside BJP MP's office earlier today and fled the scene immediately after the incident. No one was injured in the firing.

Shortly after the incident, the police reached the site to inquire about the matter.

Earlier today, Deputy Commissioner of Police, Rohini SD Mishra, said that an investigation was initiated into the matter.

"Bullets were fired in air outside BJP MP Hans Raj Hans' Rohini office. The person has been identified. Further investigation underway," the senior police official was quoted as saying by news agency ANI.

The singer-turned-politician is the BJP MP from North West Delhi. Hans, who joined the BJP in 2016 had won North West Delhi seat in this year's Lok Sabha elections, defeating Aam Aadmi Party leader Gugan Singh by over six lakh votes.
