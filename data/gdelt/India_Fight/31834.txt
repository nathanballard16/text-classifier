Sara Ali Khan and Kartik Aaryan enjoy shooting for Love Aaj Kal 2 in Himachal & their latest PIC is a proof

Sara Ali Khan and Kartik Aaryan are currently busy shooting for the upcoming Imtiaz Ali movie Love Aaj Kal 2. The movie has caught the attention of the gossipmongers ever since its inception. Moreover, the fans are equally excited to watch Karik and Sara paired up together in the silver screen soon. The two actors have been shooting in Himachal since the past few days and their pictures from the sets of the movie have been doing rounds on the internet. Love Aaj Kal 2 also happens to be one of the most awaited movies of the year.

Recently, another picture of Kartik and Sara has went viral on social media from the sets of the movie Love Aaj Kal 2 itself. In the picture, Sara can be seen in a tradional avatar while she covers her head with her red dupatta. On the other hand, Kartik Aaryan wore a white coloured shirt teamed up with a black full sleeve tee. The two of them were all smiles as they posed together for the picture.

The upcoming movie Love Aaj Kal 2 is going to feature Kartik Aaryan and Sara Ali Khan in the lead roles while Randeep Hooda will also be seen in a pivotal role. Other than Himachal, some of the scenes of the movie have also been shot in New Delhi and Mumbai. Post Love Aaj Kal 2, Sara Ali Khan will be next seen in the upcoming movie Coolie No. 1 in which she will be paired opposite Varun Dhawan. On the other hand, Kartik Aaryan will be next seen along with Ananya Panday and Bhumi Pednekar in the movie Pati, Patni Aur Woh.
