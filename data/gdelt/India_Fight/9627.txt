Pakistani drone shot down in Gujarat

The Indian Army shot down a Pakistani drone last night in Kutch area of Gujarat, bordering Pakistan, sources said.

Ahmedabad: The Indian Army shot down a Pakistani drone last night in Kutch area of Gujarat, bordering Pakistan, sources said.

The drone was shot down in Ambasa village, the sources added.

Local police and Army officials were at the spot to examine it, they said.

Early this morning, the Indian Air Force carried out air strikes at terror camps at multiple places in Pakistan and Pakistan-occupied Kashmir, destroying camps and eliminating a large number of terrorists.(ANI)
