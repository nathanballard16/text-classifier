Fire breaks out at factory in Delhi's Samaypur Badli

A fire broke out at a factory manufacturing plastic bags in Samaypur Badli on Tuesday, the Delhi Fire Service said.

A call about the incident was received at 11.57 am and 22 fire tenders were rushed to the spot, a senior fire officer said.

The fire-fighting operations are underway, he said, adding that no casualty has been reported so far.

The cause of fire is yet to be ascertained.
