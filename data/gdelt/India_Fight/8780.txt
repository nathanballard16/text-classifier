Newborn found dead in Bapod

Vadodara: A newborn baby girl was found dead in an open ground in Bapod on Thursday evening.

A local noticed the body and informed the police. The girl was rushed to the hospital but she had already died.

Police has started investigations to track down the baby’s parents. “The baby must have been delivered in some hospital nearby. We are inquiring in every hospital to find out women who delivered babies over last two days. We hope to find her parents soon,” said T J Bamaniya, Bapod police inspector.

Police suspects that the act may be of a single mother or a couple that didn’t want baby girl.tnn
