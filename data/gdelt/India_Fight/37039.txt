Terrorist killed in Shopian encounter

SRINAGAR: A terrorist was killed in an anti-insurgency operation at Baderhama Zawoora area of south Kashmir’s Shopian district on Wednesday. Two or more terrorists were believed to be trapped at the site, even as the encounter continued till the filing of this report.Based on intelligence inputs, a joint team of Army’s 23 Paras, Special Operations Group of J&K Police and CRPF had launched a cordon-and-search operation in the area. Holed-up terrorists opened fire at the search party, triggering a gunfight in which one terrorist was killed, said official sources.
