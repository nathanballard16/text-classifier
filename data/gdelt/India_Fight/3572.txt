Khurda villagers bid tearful farewell to Republic Day martyr

BHUBANESWAR: A pall of gloom descended on the native village of Army jawan Hemant Panda, who was reportedly killed by terrorists in the Baramulla sector of Jammu and Kashmir on Republic Day The mortal remains of Panda were consigned to flames at his village Atharang under Begunia block in Khurda district on Monday in the presence of local MLA Prashant Jagdev, senior government officials, his family members and friends.Arun Behera, additional district magistrate, Bhubaneswar, visited the jawan's home and offered his condolences to his family members. His body was flown to Bhubaneswar from New Delhi and was later taken to his village on Sunday night, Behera said.
