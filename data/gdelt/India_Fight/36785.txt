Five women electrocuted, Yogi Adityanath grants monetary aid

GORAKHPUR: Five women including two sisters were electrocuted around 6.30 pm on Monday while working in paddy fields in Sidhwari Tola village of Pharenda tehsil in Maharajganj district Angry over the incident, the villages blocked the road demanding compensation for the victims’ families.They blamed officials for negligence. SDM Pharenda, R B Singh, along with police reached the spot and pacified the villagers.CM Yogi Adityanath announced a compensation of Rs 13 lakh each to the kin of the victims. A committee has been set up to look into the incident.“The CM has ordered a committee comprising Basti (division) chief engineer of power department and deputy director of electrical safety, Gorakhpur to investigate the matter. Strict action will be taken against those found lax in discharge of duty. Rs 13 lakh will be given to the kin of each victim. Of this, Rs 3 lakh will under Mandi Yojna, Rs 5 lakh will be given by the power department and Rs 5 lakh will be from Kisan Durghatna Bima Yojna,” said DM Maharajganj Amar Nath Upadhyay.The victims in the incident have been identified as Subhawati, 45, Laxmi, 17, besides Radhika, Soni and Vandani, all 18 years old.
