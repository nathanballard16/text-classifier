Clash between security forces and protesters in J and K's Budgam

Srinagar (Jammu and Kashmir) (India), Jan 21 (ANI): A clash broke out between security forces and protesters in Budgam’s Chadoora area here on Monday in the wake of an encounter in which two terrorists were killed.

According to police, an encounter between terrorists and security forces broke out at Zinpanchal, Chrar-e-Sharief in Budgam district, earlier on Monday, during which two terrorists were neutralised.

Monday's encounter comes a week after two terrorists were killed in an encounter with the police and security forces in Kathpora area of Yaripora. More details waited. (ANI)
