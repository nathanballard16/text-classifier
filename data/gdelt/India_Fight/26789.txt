Mother-son duo killed in accident

RAJKOT: A mother-son duo was killed and three injured in a car acident that took place on Rajkot-Bhavnagar highway near Dhasa village of Gadhada taluka on Wednesday morning.The incident happened when the car they were travelling in overturned after colliding with a road divider. The deceased were identified as Atul Parmar (48) and his mother Dhanlakshmi Parmar (70).Atul's wife Ila Parmar and their daughter Krupa (20) and son Vansh (16) were also injured.
