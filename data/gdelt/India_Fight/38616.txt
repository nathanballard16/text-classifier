Gambling turns ugly, man dies in fight

Ludhiana: A 37-year-old man was killed during a fight over Rs 300 in Daresi area on Thursday night.

The man was gambling with some of his friends, which triggered the scuffle, and the latter escaped from the spot.

Later, the victim’s kin rushed him to the hospital where he was declared dead.

The deceased has been identified as Ravi Shankar of Mohalla Fatehgarh of the Daresi area, who owned a fish vend. Daresi police have identified the accused as Sarvan (26) and Sunil (28) who live in the same locality.

SHO Vijay Kumar said the trio were gambling in their locality and broke into a fight over Rs 300, which Ravi had earlier lost to Sarvan. However, Ravi did not pay the money, triggering an argument between the two.

The SHO added that all the three were drunk during the fight. As per investigation, Sarvan slapped Ravi, who then fell down and died. The accused hot scared and fled. Police have booked both the accused under Section of 304 (culpable homicide not amounting to the murder) of the IPC.
