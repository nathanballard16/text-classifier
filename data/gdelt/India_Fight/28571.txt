Odisha: Crocodile kills farmer near Bhitarkanika

KENDRAPADA: The body of a 45-year-old farmer, half-eaten by a suspected saltwater crocodile, has been found in the Baitarani in Mandapada village near Bhitarkanika National Park on Monday. The deceased has been identified as Sanjay Pradhan (52) of the same village.Pradhan is the latest victim of increasing crocodile attacks in the riverside villages adjoining the national park. Locals, forest guards and fire brigade personnel launched a search for him on Sunday after he was dragged away by a crocodile while bathing in the river.This is the fifth fatal crocodile attack near Bhitarkanika in a year.
