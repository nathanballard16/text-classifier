Female leopard found dead

Coimbatore: A four-year-old female leopard was found dead inside the Stanmore tea estate at Valparai on Monday. The cause of death remains unknown.

Estate workers noticed the dead leopard and informed the forest department. N Naveen Kumar, range officer of Valparai range, and his team reached the spot and retrieved the carcass. It was taken to the forest department’s animal disaster management and rescue centre at Valparai, where the postmortem was conducted and vital organs were harvested for further forensics study. Following the postmortem, the carcass was burnt inside the reserve forest.

Leopards are often spotted in tea estates at Valparai. Sometime leopards come to residential areas and attack cattle.
