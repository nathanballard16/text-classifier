Priyanka Gandhi should not be allowed in Kashi Vishwanath temple: Lawyers

VARANASI: A group of lawyers on Monday handed a letter to the district magistrate, claiming that Congress general secretary Priyanka Gandhi Vadra should not be allowed to enter the Kashi Vishwanath temple as she is a Christian The letter claimed that Priyanka was a Christian and she should be stopped from entering the temple, keeping in mind the ancient values of Sanatan Dharma The letter, addressed to chief minister Yogi Adityanath , stated that her place of worship was a church
