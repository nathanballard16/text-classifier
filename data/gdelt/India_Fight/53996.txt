Post the release of ' Pati Patni Aur Woh ', Kartik Aaryan will be seen next in Imtiaz Ali's 'Aaj Kal'. The film will feature Sara Ali Khan and it marks their first-ever collaboration on-screen. It is touted to be the sequel to Saif Ali Khan and Deepika Padukone starrer 2009 film ' Love Aaj Kal '.The actor who is on a promotional spree for Bhumi Pednekar and Ananya Panday starrer recently opened up about Imtiaz Ali's film. He said, "I am excited about Imtiaz Ali's next because I'm in love with Imtiaz sir. He has changed my thought process a lot. I think I'm a changed man now. There has been a huge change in me since I've shot for his film''.He further added and said that "Both, my acting process and personal mindset has changed after working with him. I've been a fan of his since the beginning, I couldn't believe I was doing his film.''The actor also revealed, "In fact I cried while shooting the last scene with him. That last sequence is extremely emotional. I was really emotional at that time because that journey has been amazing. I'm really looking forward to the film."Talking about the film, it was shot extensively in Delhi and Punjab. It is slated to release next year.
