Rs 18 lakh duplicate Ray-Bans seized in raid, four held

Representative image

MUMBAI: The property cell of the crime branch on Monday raided four wholesale shops at Musafirkhana that sold duplicate Ray-Ban sunglasses and seized items worth Rs 18 lakh.

Acting on the complaints from the company Ray-Ban, the police raided the four shops and arrested Mohammed Memon, Vasant Nimbalkar, Moin Shaikh and Ayyaz Multani. They have been booked under the relevant sections of the Indian Penal Code.
