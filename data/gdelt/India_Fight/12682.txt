Chennai, Mar 5: At least two people were killed and around 21 injured after three vehicles collided with eacg other in Tamil Nadu's Ramanathapuram district on Tuesday morning. The devastating road accident took place in Ramanathapuram district's Uchipuli area.

The injured have been rushed to nearby hospital for treatment. It is yet ascertained as to what caused the accident.

Condition of all the injured is not know, but some reports say that a few are seriously injured. The local poice is probing the matter, said reports.
