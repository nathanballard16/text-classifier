Chhota Udepur farmer mauled to death by leopard

Vadodara: A leopard killed a farmer early on Friday morning in the Pavi Jetpur taluka of Chhota Udepur district neighbouring Vadodara. It may have been hiding in a checkdam near the village before attacking the farmer. The leopard was subsequently caged and shifted to a rescue centre at Pavagadh. The leopard escaped into the wild after villagers and Rathwa’s wife raised an alarm.It is believed that the leopard may have come to Vavdi after descending from a hill near the village. Rathwa’s wife also sustained minor injuries while trying to save her husband.Villagers rushed to the aid of Rathwa, but he had died by the time they could get help.
