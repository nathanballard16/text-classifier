Nine Die In Rain-Related Incidents In Flood-Hit Bihar, Assam; Toll Rises To 207

Of the five persons killed, two were from Baksa district while one death each was reported from Barpeta, Cachar and Biswanath, the Assam State Disaster Management said in its report.

The national capital received 0.5 mm of rainfall, according to the Safdarjung Observatory, and the maximum temperature settled at 32.5 degrees Celsius and the minimum at 26.2 degrees Celsius. The death toll in Assam increased to 80, with five more persons dying in floods, even as water level in affected districts maintained a receding trend, an official report said.
