Natty replaces Gautham Menon in ‘Walter'

Dec 28, 2019 KRR

Director Gautham Menon, who was supposed to join the cast of Sibiraj starrer ‘Walter’, has been replaced by actor Natty, who has already started shooting for the film. His portions are being shot in Kumbakonam, Tanjore and Chennai.

Gautham Menon had to opt out owing to personal commitments, according to the film’s director Anbu. Sibiraj plays a police officer in this thriller.
