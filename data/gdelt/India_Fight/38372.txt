Care about the climate? Go vegan

Solving climate change doesn’t have to be hard. With products like the Impossible Burger, it isn’t even a sacrifice or inconvenience anymore. If we reframe our perspective to consider not what we have to lose by taking action (truly, nothing!) but what we stand to gain, perhaps we’ll find it’s easier to make progress. The scientists’ alarming reports will keep on coming, but we must not see them as doom and gloom. Instead, let’s see them as an opportunity to create a world that is better for us all.
