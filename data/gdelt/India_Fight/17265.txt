Crime branch raid at firangi restro and hookah Parlour in city

Nagpur: In another pro-active action, Unit 5 of Nagpur Crime Branch under the supervision of DCP (Crime) Nilesh Bharne raided Firangi Cafe, Restaurant at Kamal Chowk were hookah parlour was running illegally. More than 30 people including two girls were found at the time of raid among which 10 are said to be minor. Further investigation is on at the time of filling this report. More details awaited.
