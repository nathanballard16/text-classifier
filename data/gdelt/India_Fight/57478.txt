A fire broke out in two factories in Narela here following a cylinder blast on Tuesday morning, a Delhi Fire Service official said. One of the factories manufactures shoes. A call was received about the first blaze at 4.52 am and 32 fire tenders were rushed to the spot. Soon after reports were received about the second blaze.
