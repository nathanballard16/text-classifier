Man mowed down by JD(U) MP's convoy in Bihar's Lakhisarai; locals block road

Representational Image | Photo Credit: PTI

Patna: A man was mowed down by the convoy of JD(U) MP from Munger Rajiv Ranjan alias Lallan Singh in Bihar’s Lakhisarai district on Monday.

The incident took place on NH 80 at Mano village in Bihar’s district.

Even after the accident, the MP's convoy did not stop and rather sped away after which angered people blocked the road.

The local police reached the site and asked the people to remove the blockade.

SDM Murli Prasad Singh gave Rs 50,000 to the family of the deceased.
