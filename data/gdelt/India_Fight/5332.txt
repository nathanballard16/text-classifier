IMFL seized from truck

Rajkot: A total of 259 bottles of Indian-made foreign liquor (IMFL), worth Rs 77 ,500, were seized from a truck in Vallabhipur of Bhavnagar district on Saturday night.The accused, identified as Harpalsinh Gohel, who is the owner of the truck, fled from the spot.“Gohel had made secret slots behind the driver’s seat and above the truck cabin where he had stored the IMFL bottles. We carried out the raid after receiving tip-off about the IMFL cache stored in the truck,” said an official from Vallabhipur police station.
