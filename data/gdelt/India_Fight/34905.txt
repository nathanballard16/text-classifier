UP Police killed 76 criminals in encounters in two years of Adityanath rule

At least 76 criminals have been gunned down by Uttar Pradesh Police in encounters under the current Yogi Adityanath government, which came to power in March 2017. Besides, 5 UP cops also laid down their lives while 642 policemen were wounded in these armed conflicts across the state till May 27, 2019.
