2 die in building collapse in India

(MENAFN) Two people have died and 28 others rescued and hospitalized after an under-construction building collapsed Tuesday in southwestern Indian state of Karnataka.



After the collapse, authorities rushed medical teams, disaster response force personnel and police to the rescue.



A police official in Dharwad said: "One person was killed in the incident and around 50 persons are feared trapped under the debris."



Chief Minister of Karnataka said: "The rescue operation is on and five people have been extricated so far from the debris. I have instructed the concerned officials to be on location."
