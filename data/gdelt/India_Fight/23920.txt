102 kg ganja seized, 3 held

The City Task Force (CTF) police on Saturday arrested three persons, including two from Delhi and Tamil Nadu, from whom they have recovered 102 kg ganja, at Kakani Nagar near NAD Junction under Airport Police Station limits.

The arrested have been identified as A Mani (32) of Kakani Nagar, of Vizag, Ashok Vijay Kumar (26) of Delhi, Punagartha Pandian (34) of Chennimalai, Tamil Nadu.

According to officials, the CTF along with team of Airport police conducted raid at a house in Kakani Nagar, where they seized the ganja stuffed in 51 packets.

The police also seized a car and three mobile phones from the accused.
