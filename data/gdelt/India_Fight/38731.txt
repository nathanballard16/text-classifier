Husband kills wife for refusing him money

Kolkata: A 42-year-old woman from Hooghly's Pandua was allegedly murdered by her husband after she refused him money for gambling. The incident had infuriated the accused who allegedly slashed the victim with a sharp weapon. The accused, Nirmal Mistri, fled the house following the incident. On Friday night, after returning home, Nirmal asked the victim for money which she readily refused. As the situation turned worse, the accused indiscriminately slashed his wife and left the house.
