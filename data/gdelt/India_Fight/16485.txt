Constable, two others killed in separate road accidents

Nagpur: A police constable was among three people who were killed in separate accidents between Wednesday and Thursday night at different locations in the city. The truck driver fled the spot leaving behind the vehicle. Jaripatka police have arrested the truck driver Gendlal Warkhede.In another incident, two friends were killed after their bike was hit by a speeding truck from behind on Wednesday late night at Dongargaon Shivar. An injured Thakur was rushed to a nearby hospital where he died during treatment. The deceased, Ramji Chanap and Pramod Chulpar, were headed to Jamtha when the accident took place.
