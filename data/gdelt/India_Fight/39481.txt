Independence Day 2019: Kareena Kapoor Khan sends in her warm wishes to everyone; View Post

The whole country is in a celebratory mood today as India celebrates its 73rd Independence Day. Our favourite B – Town celebs have also taken to social media and have wished their fans on the special occasion. Right from Amitabh Bachchan to Kartik Aaryan, Varun Dhawan , Taapsee Pannu, Ajay Devgn and others, a host of Bollywood celebs have wished their legion of fans on the special occasion.

Kareena Kapoor Khan has also sent in her wishes to all her fans on the special occasion through an Instagram post. The Veere Di Wedding actress, who has been quite busy with her upcoming projects these days, writes, “Here's to freedom...Happy Independence Day!” For the unversed, Bebo has been juggling between London and Mumbai since the past few days because of her hectic shooting schedules.
