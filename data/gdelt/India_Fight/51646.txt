NZ woman due to marry boyfriend dies in India

A New Zealand woman has been found dead at a hotel in New Delhi.



The victim, a 49-year-old, was found unconscious by her boyfriend on Saturday (local time), media reports say. She was believed to have suffered a cardiac arrest.

"The deceased woman belongs to New Zealand and the man with her is from Australia," the hotel owner told Times Now News.

Hotel staff had called an ambulance when informed she was unconscious by her boyfriend, who she was due to marry in India. She was rushed to hospital but later died.

"Later, we informed the police," the owner told Times Now News.

Inquiries into her death are ongoing and the New Zealand High Commission has been notified.
