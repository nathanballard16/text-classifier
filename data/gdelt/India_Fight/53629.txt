Mahila Cong holds candlelight march against vet’s rape, murder

Tribune News ServiceChandigarh, December 2The Chandigarh Pradesh Mahila Congress today held a candlelight march in protest against the rape and murder of a 27-year-old veterinarian in Hyderabad. Chandigarh Pradesh Congress president Pradeep Chhabra was also present on the occasion. Chhabra said the killers should be arrested and the case should be heard in fast-track court. Chandigarh Women’s Congress president Deepa Dubey said the condition of women was getting worse during the present government. Dubey said women were feeling helpless in their own country.
