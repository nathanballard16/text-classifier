Kia Seltos vs Hyundai Creta: Who is the winner in terms of October 2019 sales?

Hyundai Creta was the leader of the SUV segment in India for a very long time. However, all of it changed with the launch of Kia Seltos. Ever since its introduction here in August 2019, Seltos has garnered all the limelight and pushed Creta in a corner. Now the sales figures of both the SUVs for October 2019 are out and Seltos has once again trumped Creta.

Kia Motors India sold 12,850 units of Seltos in October. In comparison, Hyundai Motor India reportedly disposed of 7,269 units of Creta during the month. Seltos had outsold Creta in August and September as well. While 6,236 units of Seltos were sold in August as against 6,001 units of Creta, the September sales numbered at 7,754 units for Seltos and 6,641 units for Creta.

Kia Seltos sales in October have pushed Kia Motors India into the league top five largest carmakers of the country, behind Maruti Suzuki India, Hyundai Motor India, Mahindra & Mahindra and Tata Motors. The popularity of the new SUV can be gauged from the fact that Kia Motors India has garnered over 60,000 bookings for Seltos till now.

Kia Seltos gets three BS6-compliant engine options. There is a 1.5-litre petrol motor making 115 PS and 144 Nm of torque, a 1.5-litre VGT diesel powertrain delivering 115 PS and 250 Nm of torque, and a 1.4-litre Turbo GDI petrol mill churning out 140 PS and 242 Nm of torque. There is a standard 6-speed manual gearbox available with all the three engines. Besides, the 1.5-litre petrol mill gets an IVT, the 1.5-litre VGT diesel engine comes with a 6-speed AT and 1.4-litre Turbo GDI petrol is offered with a 7-speed DCT.

In terms of features, Kia Seltos gets a tiger-nose front grille, LED headlamps with LED DRLs, ventilated and power seats, eco coating, smart pure air, 8-inch HUD (Heads Up Display), three-spoke multifunction steering wheel and 10.25-inch touchscreen AVN system with an 8-speaker Bose sound system. There is also the UVO connectivity technology with 37 features.

Apart from Hyundai Creta, Kia Seltos rivals MG Hector, Mahindra XUV500, Tata Harrier and Jeep Compass. It is priced between Rs 9.69 lakh (ex-showroom, Delhi) and Rs 16.99 lakh (ex-showroom, Delhi).
