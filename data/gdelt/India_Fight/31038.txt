Alia Bhatt and Ranbir Kapoor take a mini break from Brahmastra shoot to spend time with Rishi Kapoor & family

Ranbir Kapoor and Alia Bhatt have been constantly shooting for their upcoming film, Brahmastra. Ayan Mukerji’s film has been keeping the duo busy. However, recently Ranbir and Alia had returned to Mumbai after a stomach bug caught Alia and she needed rest. Now, after recovering, Ranbir and Alia were snapped at the airport a day back again. The Brahmastra duo was heading to New York for a family reunion to spend some time with Rishi Kapoor, Neetu Kapoor and Ranbir’s sister, Riddhima.

As per a report in Mumbai Mirror, Alia needed a break from work since she has been shooting for her father’s film, Sadak 2 as well as Brahmastra. Ranbir needed to meet his parents. Thus. they both took a mini break from shoot to head to the big apple for a vacation. Ranbir and Alia will be spending time with Rishi Kapoor and Neetu Kapoor over the next couple of days as they did at New Year’s time back in December.

As per Mumbai Mirror, “They were with Rishi and Neetu for New Year celebrations as well. This time, Ranbir’s sister Riddhima is also there so it’s going to be a family reunion. RK and Alia will be returning to Mumbai in a few days to resume work on their film.” Post the vacation, Ranbir and Alia will be returning from New York to resume Brahmastra shoot.

On the work front, Alia has a number of projects in her kitty including Brahmastra, Sadak 2, RRR and Inshallah. Ranbir, on the other hand, is working on Shamshera and Brahamstra. In the Brahmastra, Alia and Ranbir will be seen as Shiva and Isha in the film. The film also stars Amitabh Bachchan, Dimple Kapadia, Nagarjuna Akkineni and Mouni Roy. Brahmastra is being shot in Varanasi and will release in the summer of 2020.
