56 old ambulances damaged by fire in Hyderabad

: As many as 56 old and condemned ambulances parked in an open space near here were gutted in a fire Monday, police and fire service officials said.

No one was injured in the incident, a police official told PTI.

The cause of the fire, which broke out at 2 pm, was not known. Investigations were on, the police said.

Two fire-tenders brought the flames under control, they said.
