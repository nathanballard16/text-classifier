Electrician killed at Brahampur village

Our CorrespondentRaikot, November 13A youth of Brahampur village in Ludhiana district was allegedly murdered near his house by farmer and his accomplice with sharp weapons on Tuesday night. He worked as an electrician at Brahampur village. The suspects, identified as Balkar Singh of Brahampur village and his nephew Gurjant Singh Jantta of Sultanpur village in Sangrur district, are at large. Preliminary investigation revealed that Balkar and his accomplice allegedly attacked Dalwar after calling him at cremation ground road on the village outskirts at 9 pm on Tuesday. The main suspect, Balkar Singh, had retired from the Army recently.
