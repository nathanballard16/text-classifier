Over 20 truckloads of sand seized

Madikeri: Somwarpet police on Monday seized over 20 truckloads of sand illegally extracted and stored on the banks of Kumaradhara river in Nadnalli village.Police raided the spot after a tipoff, but could not trace the accused. Reshma, an officer attached to the department of mines and geology, visited the spot and seized the sand.Meanwhile, residents of Nadnalli complained that the recent murder of a person in the village was connected to the illegal sand mafia
