Mini bus collides with truck, kills 7 in India

(MENAFN) Police declared that a truck has crashed into a mini-bus, with Hindu travelers onboard, leaving over seven people dead and one wounded.



The crash occurred close to Palani in Dindigul district, 428 km south of Chennai, the capital city of Tamil Nadu.



A police official declared: "Today a mini-bus carrying pilgrims collided head-on with a goods truck here killing seven people."



He added, "Four people died on spot and three others succumbed inside hospital. One person is still undergoing treatment."
