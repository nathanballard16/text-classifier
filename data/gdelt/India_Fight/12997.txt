Constable shot at by 2 men during night patrol in Naka

The injured cop, Ajit Yadav, was posted at Naka police station and was on night patrol when the incident occurred at around 3am. UP DGP O P Singh and ADG Zone Rajiv Krishna met the injured constable and announced a reward of Rs 10,000 for his bravery. LUCKNOW: A constable was shot at by two miscreants when he intercepted them for a routine checking under Naka police station area early on Tuesday. Other policemen rushed to the spot and nabbed the other accused.SSP Lucknow Kalanidhi Naithani said Yadav was rushed to the KGMU Trauma Centre. The bullet hit him in the lower back.The wounded constable caught hold of one of the miscreants.
