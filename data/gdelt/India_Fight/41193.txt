Fire breaks out at DCW office

New Delhi, August 27A major fire broke out on the second floor of the Delhi Commission for Womens office at Vikas Bhawan in central Delhi on Tuesday, fire officials said. However, DCW sources said several documents and office furniture were gutted in the fire and they estimated a loss of around Rs 5 lakh. The Fire Department received a call about the blaze at around 6.00 pm, following which eight fire tenders were rushed to the spot. According to the sources, there were 22-25 people present inside the office when the fire broke out in the conference hall. They said they will analyse the level of damage on Wednesday as the cupboards in the conference hall had some case files too and they will check which files were gutted in the blaze.
