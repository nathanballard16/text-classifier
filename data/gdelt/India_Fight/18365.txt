Student shot at on BHU campus

VARANASI: A student of Banaras Hindu University was shot at by some unknown miscreants on the campus on Tuesday evening. He was rushed to the Trauma Centre, where his condition is stated to be serious. Police were investigating the matter.According to reports, when Gaurav Singh , an MCA student of the university, was passing near Birla Hotel, three miscreants on a motorcycle opened fire on him and fled from the scene.He sustained bullet injury on his stomach and was immediately rushed to the Trauma Centre by students.Panic prevailed on the campus after the shooting. The police swung into action after getting information. BHU spokesperson Rajesh Singh said that the police were informed and legal formalities were being initiated.
