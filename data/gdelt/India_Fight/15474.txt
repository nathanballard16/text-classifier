Clashes erupt between Indian, Pakistani soldiers

(MENAFN) Indian military declared that soldiers of India and Pakistan have clashed and hit targeted each other's positions on Line of Control (LoC) separating Kashmir.



A defense ministry spokesperson confirmed that the two rivals battled in frontier Poonch district, about 180 km southwest of Srinagar, the summer capital of Indian-controlled Kashmir.



The clashes kept going for a while, however, no deaths were reported yet in the fight on either side.
