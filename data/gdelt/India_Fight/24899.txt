Rains create havoc in Nagarkurnool

Nagarkurnool: In what can be termed as nature's fiery, heavy rains combined with hail storms created havoc in Amrabad and Achampet mandal of Nagarkurnool district during evening today.

Hails storms in Mannanoor village damaged mango plantations and other crops and high speed winds combined with hail storms uprooted trees across Hyderabad_Srisailam Highway.

In an another incident, a bull belonging to a farmer Jala Balaiah tied under a tree was killed due to lighting at Macharam village of Achampet mandal.

Overall, the sudden torrential rains in this part of Nallamalla region brought a great loss to the poor farmers.
