One held for shooting man in Usmanpur

New Delhi, October 19A 21-year-old man was arrested for allegedly killing a person last month in Northeast Delhi’s Usmanpur, police said on Saturday. The arrested assailant was identified as Rizwan Ali, a resident of Bulandsahar district in UP. On September 26, one Mohhamad Hassan was shot dead by Ali and his accomplice Anwar in Usmanpur market, a senior police officer said. Interrogation revealed that Ali, Anwar and Hassan were residents of Bulandshar. Hassan and Anwar were living in Usmanpur area and had rivalry over monetary disputes, the DCP said.
