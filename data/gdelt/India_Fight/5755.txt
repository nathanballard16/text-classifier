Fire at furniture godown in Lucknow

Lucknow (Uttar Pradesh) [India], Feb 13(ANI): A fire broke out on Wednesday in a furniture godown in Lalbagh, Lucknow on Wednesday.

Fire tenders rushed to the spot to douse the flame. The cause of the fire is yet to be ascertained.

No casualty or injury has been reported till now. Further details awaited. (ANI)
