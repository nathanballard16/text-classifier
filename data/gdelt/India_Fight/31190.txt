Amitabh Bachchan’s quirky new look

Amitabh Bachchan never fails to impress his fans. The veteran actor who is known to experiment with different looks for his characters will now be seen yet again in an interesting avatar in Shoojit Sircar’s next Gulabo Sitabo.

An image of the actor with a long beard and huge glasses has surfaced on the internet. The 76-year-old actor, who is shooting for the film in Lucknow plays the role of a landlord in the film.

Taran Adarsh tweeted, “Unveiling Amitabh Bachchan’s quirky character look from #GulaboSitabo... Costars Ayushmann Khurrana... Directed by Shoojit Sircar... 24 April 2020 release (sic).”

The team is currently in Lucknow for the first schedule and a source close to the team reveals how the entire look has been designed. “In all their collaborations, Amitji and Shoojit have worked on extremely unique and quirky characters. When Shoojit showed Amitji the look sketch, he was quite overwhelmed and excited. The result is here for all to see.”
