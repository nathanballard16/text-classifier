Farmer found murdered in Tankara village

RAJKOT: A farmer’s body was found from a cabin in his farm in Savdi village of Tankara taluka in Morbi district on Wednesday. Police booked an offence of murder against unidentified assailants whose number could not be ascertained for the crime.According to the police, the deceased, identified as Ghela Chauhan (62) had left his farm on Tuesday night and his body was found on Wednesday morning. “After Chauhan’s family members alleged that he was murdered, we sent the body for forensic postmortem to Rajkot Civil Hospiital. The postmortem report revealed that Chauhan had been strangled, following which we booked unidentified assailants for the crime,” said L B Bagada, sub-inspector with Tankara police station.
