Kansas City Police Department Files Lawsuit Against Gun Shop That Sold Weapon to Indian Engineering Student’s Killer

University of Missouri Kansas City engineering student Sharath Koppu was killed in July 2018, as a gunman attempting to rob the restaurant where he worked shot him in the back. The Kansas City Police Department is suing a local gun store at which Koppu’s killer subsequently bought a weapon to shoot at police officers. (Facebook Sharath Koppu Remembrance page photo)
