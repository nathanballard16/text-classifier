Delhi Police arrest another suspect in Dwarka Mor shooting case

New Delhi: In a link with a shootout earlier this week, Delhi Police on Friday arrested a suspected gangster in Kanjhawala area of the capital.

According to the report, the accused was identified as 20-year-old, Ankit Dabas, a resident of Sampla village in Haryana.

The two suspected criminals on Sunday were killed in the shootout near the Dwarka Mor Metro station in West Delhi. The shootout was an outcome of a property dispute, between rival gangs. The police officials mentioned that Dabas was also involved in the shootout.
