Man gets 10 years imprisonment for killing calf

RAJKOT: Dhoraji additional district sessions court on Saturday sentenced a man to 10 years imprisonment for slaughtering a calf earlier this year. According to public prosecutor Kartikey Parekh, accused Saleem Chadar had stolen a female calf from Saptah Majothi and slaughtered it for meat at his daughter’s wedding function on January 29 this year.“Chadar was arrested on the complaint of Majothi, and the experts of forensic science laboratory confirmed the meat served at the function was beef,” said Parekh.Apart from the imprisonment, the court also slapped Chadar with fine of Rs 1,02,000.
