Which movies are coming soon to Sky Cinema? The best new arrivals from Bohemian Rhapsody to Star Wars

S ky Cinema viewers have a great choice of films to pick from this September with a host of first-rate movies available to watch.

Autumnal viewing on the box has never been better with the Oscar-winning Bohemian Rhapsody among the features coming to the channel this month.

Holmes & Watson, The Front Runner and 2016's The Predator are all available to watch in full now, while thriller Hotel Mumbai will drop at the end of September.

If you're ready for a month of top quality cinema, here's a list of films available on the channel in September.
