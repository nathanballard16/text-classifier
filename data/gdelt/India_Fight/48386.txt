Infant killed, five children injured due to fire in Hyderabad hospital

An infant was killed and five other children were injured on Monday due to a fire in a Hyderabad hospital in Telangana.

A nine-month-old baby girl died in Shine Children Hospital in LB Nagar area of Hyderabad. All the children were shifted to nearby hospitals for further treatment.

The cause of the fire is a short circuit inside the hospital.
