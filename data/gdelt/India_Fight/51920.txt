Teenager killed, 8 injured in clash in UP

PRATAPGARH: A teenager was killed and eight injured after two groups clashed over a land-related dispute here on Tuesday, police said.The two factions used sticks and rods in the fight in which Manjit Patel, 16, was killed, assistant superintendent of police Dinesh Dwivedi said.The injured have been admitted to a hospital where their condition was said to be stable.No arrests have been made so far and police is investigating the case, the ASP said.
