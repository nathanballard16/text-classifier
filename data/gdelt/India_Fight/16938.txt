Lok Sabha Election campaign: As it happened on March 26

BJP has fielded Jaya Prada from Rampur. Issuing a fresh list of candidates for Lok Sabha elections, BJP announced that newly inducted Bollywood actress against SP's Azam Khan. Meneka Gandhi will contest polls from Sultanpur, currently held by her son Varun Gandhi.

Varun Gandhi will contest from Pilibhit. The seats have been swapped between mother-son duo. Rita Bahuguna Joshi has been fielded from Allahabad, an erstwhile bastion of her father. The party also issued a list of 10 candidates from West Bengal. The list of Delhi candidates is also expected today.
