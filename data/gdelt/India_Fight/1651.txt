Rajkot: The state GST department booked a city-based trader of healthcare products, handicraft and gift articles in connection with a bogus billing scam committed during the regime of VAT. The accused submitted bogus bills for the purchaser to claim input tax credit. According to the FIR, during the period between October 2015 and July 2017, the trader issued bills of Rs 99.57 crore without making any sales.The trader had secured the VAT number only to issue bills to various firms across the state. When his business premise was raided, neither any stock was found, nor did the records show transactions.
