Alia Bhatt’s Breathtaking Underpool Shoot For Vogue Magazine

Mumbai: Bollywood beauty Alia Bhatt recently caught the attention of her fans with a breath taking and hot underwater photo shoot for Vogue Magazine. The shoot was for the cover of a leading fashion and lifestyle magazine's November issue.



The Kalank star is seen casually holding her breath under the pool, as she is seen posing in a sequinned swimsuit, to a bright neon gown and a blue embellished swim suit in unbelievable poses. The fans went crazy over her drop-dead gorgeous pictures from the shoot and naming her as 'new mermaid in the town'.



Vogue India also shared the hottest underwater photo shoot of Ali Bhatt on Instagram. Every picture from the shoot will definitely make you think how as to how did she actually manage for such perfect poses under the pool with such ease and grace.
