Sansani: Satta bazar bets Rs 15000 crores on Lok Sabha Election results

As per latest reports the Satta Bazar has bet at least Rs 15000 crore on Lok Sabha Elections 2019. The results will be out on May 23 but the results not only matter to the people of India but the Satta bazar as well

Shatrughan Sinha, Ravi Kishan, Hema Malini, Sunny Deol are the celebrity politicians on whom maximum bets have been placed
