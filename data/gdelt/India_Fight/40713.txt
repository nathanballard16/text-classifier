Leopard attacks teenager in Visavadar village

RAJKOT: A 13-year-old boy was injured after a leopard attacked him in Jambala village of Visavadar taluka in Junagadh district on Friday morning.According to forest officials, Mukesh Solanki was attacked by the leopard when he was walking near a farm. Mukesh suffered minor injury on his head and right ear. The boy is native of Maharashtra and his parents are farm labourers in Junagadh district. He was taken to nearby government hospital where he was discharged after treatment
