Atmosphere of fear prevails in VNSGU, says senator

Surat: A senate member of Veer Narmad South Gujarat University (VNSGU) shot off a letter on Tuesday to the university registrar levelling allegations against those in power. He also claimed that casteism was being promoted in the university.“There was an atmosphere of fear and culture of leg-pulling in the university now while its academic standards were deteriorating rapidly. Prime Minister Narendra Modi’s vision for education is being neglected,” Dhameliya wrote.He has said, “I feel sad for the university as I am a senator. He alleged in the letter that from heads of the departments to professors and clerks, everyone in the university was working under threat. They were pressurized to convince Dhameliya to withdraw his candidature.
