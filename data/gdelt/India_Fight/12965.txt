Private financier shot dead in crowded Navsari Bazaar

Surat: A 52-year-old private financier was shot dead by unidentified assailants in Navsari Bazaar area of the city on Tuesday afternoon. Shah used to give money against jewellery and valuables. The deceased was in his shop when the assailants came and shot him multiple times in chest and head resulting in his instantaneous death. An offence of murder was registered at Athwa police station.Police recovered a bullet and one empty cover of a bullet. Neighbours rushed to the shop only to find Shah lying on floor in a pool of blood.
