Arms supplier arested from Rajasthan

NEW DELHI: Delhi Police special Cell has arrested an interstate rewarded ammunition supplier identified as Jitender Singh from Dholpur in Rajasthan. Two semi-automatic pistols and 105 live cartridges of .315 bore and .32 bore have been recovered from him. He is involved in over 18 cases of extortion, dacoity and robbery, attempt to murder, encounters with police in Uttar Pradesh, Rajasthan, Madhya Pradesh, Delhi and Gujarat. "On October 5, specific information was received in Special Cell/NDR that above named Jitender Singh is seen in Dholpur. He remained in jail for over 1 year which included his arrest in many cases of robbery, threat, shootout extortion of MP, Rajasthan and Gujarat.
