Sale of cat meat in the name of Mutton?

Visakhapatnam: Police arrested a six-member gang which was engaged in selling cat meat in the name of mutton in hotels and fast food centers.

It is alleged that they were killing cats and selling their meat.

On receiving a tip, police raided in Jugulapalem area and arrested a six-member gang which includes three women.

Police are making an attempt to ascertain whether this meat was being supplied to fast food centers.
