Slain BJP leader’s car recovered from Dooru in Anantnag

SRINAGAR: A day after BJP’s district vice-president Gul Mohammad Mir was gunned down by terrorists at his Nowgam residence, security forces on Sunday recovered his private car — which the assailants had taken away after the attack — from Dooru area in south Kashmir’s Anantnag district “Security forces recovered Mir’s personal car, a Wagon R, from Gujerbasti area of Dooru’s Watnad region on Sunday morning, during a search operation in Nowgam, Verinag , Dooru and adjoining areas,” said a police officer.Cops have registered a case and a probe is underway.Mir’s death came just two days ahead of the last leg of parliamentary elections in south Kashmir.
