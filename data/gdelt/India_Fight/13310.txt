J&K: 1 terrorist killed in encounter with security forces in Handwara; search operation underway

: Security forces gunned down one terrorist in ongoing operation in Handwara in Jammu and Kashmir. The operation is underway. The identity of the terrorist has not yet been ascertained. Patrolling parties in Handwara were attacked by terrorists on Wednesday night. The security forces were on their way to cordon off the Kralgund village, when they were attacked. The terrorists were surrounded after a search operation that was initiated following the attack.
