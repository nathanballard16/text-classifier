Haryana Congress spokesperson Vikash Chaudhary shot dead

Haryana Congress spokesperson Vikash Chaudhary was killed by unidentified assailants in Faridabad on Thursday. He was shot at broad daylight when he was travelling by car after getting out the gym. He was shot multiple times by the criminals. He was rushed to the Sarvodaya hospital but Chaudhary succumbed to his injury.

Police is looking at CCTV footage from the area to find clue about possible attackers. The murder took place at Sector 9 in Faridabad. The car has been battered by bullet shots which went through the windscreen.

Ashok Tanwar, Haryana Congress President slammed the Manohar Lal Khattar government in the state for lack of law and order. He was quoted by ANI as saying, "It is a 'jungle raj', there is no fear of law. The same kind of incident happened yesterday, where a woman who opposed molestation was stabbed. There should be an investigation".
