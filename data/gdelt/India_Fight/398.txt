IT focuses on Kannada actors : Raids on Puneet Rajkumar and Rockline Venkatesh's homes

Bangalore : The IT department has launched a mission this morning and has undertaken raids since 6 AM mainly focussing on Kannada Film Industry actors. The raids have been in full swing in various parts of Karnataka since this morning.

Puneet Rajkumar whose family is considered to be the first family of Kannada Film Industry has been raided. All his business transactions are under scrutiny. Rockline Venkatesh who is a prominent film producer has been raided as well.

The raids have been conducted in 60 locations across the state and prominent film personalities have come under the radar. More raids are set to happen in the future focussing on prominent Kannada actors.
