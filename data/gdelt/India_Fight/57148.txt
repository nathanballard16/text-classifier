57-year-old man stabbed to death

Rajkot: A 57-year-old man was killed in Bhavnagar city on Saturday. Victim Bharat Meghani was resident of Nirmal Nagar. Police said four persons chased the victim and stabbed him in chest. The victim was engaged in construction work. Police believe he was killed due to animosity between the victim’s son and the accused. The accused are yet to be identified.
