Ahan Shetty joins maiden film's set

Aug 6, 2019 KRR

Ahan Shetty, son of actor Suniel Shetty, is beginning his film career on Tuesday as his maiden film kicks off at a single screen theatre in South Mumbai with a brief schedule.

Directed by Milan Luthria, the major chunk of the tentatively titled film, ‘Tadap’, will be shot in Mussoorie. Tara Sutaria is the female lead in the film.

Ahan’s character runs a movie theatre in the film, which is an official adaptation of the 2018 Telugu hit, ‘RX 100’. It is produced by Sajid Nadiadwala.
