Flesh trade racket busted in Junagadh, 3 held

RAJKOT: Three persons were arrested for their involvement in alleged flesh trade racket operating from an apartment building on Junagadh Bypass Road in Junagadh late night on Monday. The women police team that raided the Darshan Apartment also rescued one women, who was allegedly involved in flesh trade.The accused were identified as Firoz Sidda of Junagadh, Nilesh Shrimali of Sutrapada and Sehjad Mohammad Lakha of Veraval. All three were customers, said police.According to police, the manager of the racket Kiranben Sheta was not present at the apartment. Police has launched a hunt for her.
