Man arrested for murdering father

COIMBATORE: A week after he was attacked by his son , a 65-year-old Saravanampatti resident passed away at the Coimbatore Medical College and Hospital (CMCH) on Monday.Police identified the victim as Krishnan of Murugan Nagar at Viswasapuram near Saravanampatti. “His son K Kumaravel, 31, had attacked him with a wooden log after he refused to give him money to buy liquor on September 3. Krishnan suffered severe head injuries and was in CMCH’s intensive care unit,” a police officer said.The Saravanampatti police had registered a case against Kumaravel under sections 75 (i) (c) of the Madras City Police Act, 1888 and 323 (punishment for voluntarily causing hurt) and 506 (i) (criminal intimidation) of the Indian Penal Code , and arrested him. He was remanded in judicial custody and lodged in the Coimbatore Central Prison. Police have altered the case to 302 (murder) of the IPC.
