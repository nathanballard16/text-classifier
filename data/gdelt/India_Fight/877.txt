Viswasam Ticket Booking Started at Karur Cinemas Online and Tickets Selling Like Hot Cakes

The reservations for Thala Ajith - Siruthai Siva combo's festival entertainer Viswasam is starting one by one in various places. The Capital city Chennai bookings have already started and going on full swing followed by overseas bookings. Now the ticket booking has been started in the Karur theatres in their online reservation platform.

Viswasam is releasing on January 10 and the team is curious about delivering a mass festive entertainer for the Pongal 2019. Fans are expecting an out and out Thala Ajith show all the way after seeing the power-packed trailer. The movie is censored with U- certificate and is said to be having a run time of 151 mins without any cuts.
