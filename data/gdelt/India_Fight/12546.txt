Petrol attack victim Ravali dead

Hyderabad: Ravali of Warangal, who was set ablaze by her jilted lover one Anvesh, suffered 90 % burns last week, succumbed to injuries in a hospital here on Monday, plunging her family members into grief.

It may be mentioned here that culprit Anvesh, who bore grudge against the girl for keeping him away, trailed her while going to college, poured petrol and set her ablaze.
