Gangster group, in FB post, threatens to kill all gang rape accused

Ludhiana: In the case of horrifying gang rape reported in Issewal village of Ludhiana Rural area, the group of infamous gangster Davinder Bambiha, through Facebook, has warned the police to arrest all the accused quickly. The gang has also stated that if they managed to get the accused before the police, they would kill them all. It may be noted that the gangster was killed in 2016, but his gang members have been running his Facebook page.

The post was updated on the profile named ‘Davinder Bambiha Group’ on Wednesday morning. The post states: “In the case of Ludhiana gang rape reported on Saturday, if our group manages to nab the accused before the police, they all will be killed, even if the accused turn out to be their friends. Police should also do their work honestly in this case. If any MLA, sarpanch, or police officer comes to help the accused, they will also not be spared. Police are requested to arrest all the accused quickly, or face the action.”

By late evening, the post got 300+ likes, 66 comments, and 33 shares. The people commenting on the post were lending their support, and agreed with it. The picture of suspects, whose sketches were made by police, were also uploaded with the post.

DIG-Ludhiana Range Ranbir Khatra said: “I’m not aware of any such post. The police are doing their best to arrest all the accused. Moreover, we are working 24 hours on this case. We have arrested three accused, and names and addresses of other accused are with the police. They are on our radar, and will soon be in the police net.”
