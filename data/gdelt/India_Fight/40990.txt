Samantha and Naga Chaitanya to play the lead pair in Bangarraju

As reported earlier Tollywood actor Akkineni Nagarjuna is reportedly in talks with the director Kalyan Krishna Kurasala for the sequel to 2016 hit film Soggade Chinni Nayana.If the reports are anything to go by, the movie will start rolling soon, and real couple Samantha and Naga Chaitanya will be seen essaying the lead pair in the upcoming film titled Bangarraju . Nagarjuna will produce the movie under his home banner Annapurna Studios . Director Kalyan Krishna who has previously helmed Soggade Chinni Nayana will direct the film. If they are on board, the film will mark the fourth time collaboration of Sam and Chay after Ye Maya Chesave, Manam, Autonagar Surya and Majili. However, an official announcement regarding the same yet to be made by the makers.Soggade Chinni Nayana was notable for its fascinating story of a village youngster Bangarraju who has been murdered by thugs for a treasure, comes down with the help of Yama to settle his son’s married life and meanwhile, finds out that the reason behind his death. Sources say this sequel is going to be produced on a vast scale.
