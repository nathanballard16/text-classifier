India: 3 terrorists die in clashes with security forces

(MENAFN) Local police declared that Indian security forces have shot dead at least three terrorists loyal to the proscribed terror group "Hizbul Mujahidden" on Saturday in India-controlled Jammu and Kashmir.



The clash between the security forces and the terrorists broke out in Awantipora area in the Pulwama district.



The dead terrorists were named Showkat Dar, Irfan War and Muzafar Sheikh.



The police confirmed that fatalities were believed to have been taking role in terror crimes in the past, adding that arms and ammunition were confiscated from their possession.
