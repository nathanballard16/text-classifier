2 workers killed as soil heap caves in

Our CorrespondentKhanna, November 20Two workers were killed while one was rescued when earth caved in during the laying of sewer lines at Jagat Colony here today. The three labourers were working near a heap of soil. Suddenly, a big portion of the heap caved in and the three workers were buried alive. After two-hour struggle, one worker was saved while the two others were choked to death. He said the worker rescued from the pit was out of danger.
