Death toll rises to 120 as torrential rains lash India

New Delhi: The death toll due to torrential rains in northern India rose to 120, with over 50 deaths reported in the past 24 hours alone, officials said.

Monsoon rains, accompanied by storms and lightning, hit large parts of Uttar Pradesh and Bihar states since Thursday. The incessant downpour felled trees and electricity poles, and brought down roofs and temporary structures. Officials at Uttar Pradesh’s Disaster Management Department said that 49 more people were killed in rain-related accidents, in addition to the 64 deaths reported since Thursday. (DPA)
