J&K: Grenade Attack On Security Forces On Diwali Eve In Srinagar; 6 Personnel Injured

The attack took place at around 200 metres from Karan Nagar police station.

On the eve of Diwali, a grenade attack was launched on the security forces by terrorists in Kakasarai area of Karan Nagar in Sringar, Jammu and Kashmir, injuring six CRPF jawans . One of the injured jawans is in a critical situation, while five are said to be out of danger. The Central Reserve Police Force (CRPF) team was manning a checkpoint when the grenade attack took place. Terrorists lobbed a grenade at the troops near Karan Nagar police station and then opened fire.
