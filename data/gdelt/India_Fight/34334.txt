Fire breaks out at Nandaram market in Kolkata

A major fire broke out on Saturday afternoon at Nandaram market in Kolkata in West Bengal. At least six fire tenders are present at the spot and the attempts to douse the fire are underway.

The fire was reported from the 9th floor of Nandaram market.

The cause of the fire has not been ascertained yet. An investigation in the case has been initiated.

The same market was gutted in a major fire nearly a decade ago. It was repaired and the business establishments had resumed work then.
