Shraddha Kapoor wraps up shooting for Prabhas starrer 'Saaho' in Austria

It's a wrap! @ShraddhaKapoor in Austria for #Saaho https://t.co/ZUqH3R7NBf — ETimes (@etimes) 1561717840000

One of the most-anticipated films of the year, ' Saaho ' has been in the news ever since its inception. The film which marks the return of 'Baahubali' fame Prabhas on the big screens, will see some high-octane out and out action sequences. The teaser of the film was recently revealed and as expected it promises a visual marvel that the piqued the anticipation of the audience.Bollywood actress Shraddha Kapoor plays the role opposite Prabhas in the film was seen performing some intense stunt sequence in the teaser. The actress has outclassed everyone with her acting prowess and is set to create another milestone with the film. Recently, Shraddha and the team jetted off to Austria to shoot for the film. The actress has been teasing her fans and followers with updates on her social media handles.Today, Shraddha's stylist shared a couple of pictures with the actress from the snow-capped mountains of Europe as she wraps up shooting for the film. Donning a white jacket, the actress is seen strikingly beautiful in these sunkissed selfies.'Saaho' sees Prabhas romancing the leading lady Shraddha Kapoor. Neil Nitin Mukesh plays the main antagonist. 'Saaho' also stars Arun Vijay, Chunky Pandey, Jackie Shroff, Mahesh Manjrekar, Mandira Bedi, Evylin Sharma, and Vennela Kishore.Written and directed by Sujeeth , the trilingual being shot in Hindi, Tamil and Telegu is also touted to be the second-most expensive film in India. The film is slated to hit theatres on 15 August.
