Minor fire breaks out at hospital in Delhi's Basai Darapur

NEW DELHI: A minor fire broke out at ESI hospital in Delhi's Basai Darapur village near Moti Nagar on Friday morning. Six fire tenders were pressed into service to douse the flames.According to fire officials, no casualties have been reported so far in the incident. Six patients who were on the third floor at the time of the incident were rescued by the fire team since the smoke had spread through the AC vents.Chief fire officer Atul Garg said that they had received a call around 8.30am about a fire at the third floor of the building. It was found that the fire broke out from a suspected short circuit at the operation theatre and had damaged the false ceiling. It was doused within a few minutes.An inquiry has been ordered into the cause of the blaze.
