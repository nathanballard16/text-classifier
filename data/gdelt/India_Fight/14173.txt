Bhopal cops crack Shahjehanabad murder mystery, one arrested

Rajesh Malviya has been nabbed, while Ram Gopal is on the run. When he got inebriated, the accused Rajesh Malviya with the help of his uncle murdered Pappu using a sharp-edged weapon and later disposed the body on a vacant plot. However, he has been nabbed and has accepted his crime, police said, adding that matter remains under investigation. Police said that the sharp-edged weapon used in the crime has also been seized. Regarding the motive of crime, police said the accused and deceased were residents of the same locality and the deceased worked as a labourer while the accused has a laundry shop.
