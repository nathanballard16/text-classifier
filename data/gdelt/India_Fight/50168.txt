Priyanka Chopra Jonas lands in Delhi to shoot for The White Tiger, asks everyone to be safe in the critical weather conditions : Bollywood News

After mesmerising her fans with her performance in The Sky Is Pink, Priyanka Chopra Jonas promoted the film in full swing in India and America. Now, after a break, Priyanka Chopra Jonas is back in Delhi to shoot for The White Tiger with Rajkummar Rao for Netflix India. While everyone is aware of the critical weather conditions in Delhi, Priyanka Chopra Jonas posted a selfie with a mask on, asking people to be safe.

She posted the picture with the caption, “Shoot days for #thewhitetiger. It’s so hard to shoot here right now that I can’t even imagine what it must be like to live here under these conditions. We r blessed with air purifiers and masks. Pray for the homeless. Be safe everyone. #airpollution #delhipollution???? #weneedsolutions #righttobreathe”
