Pak Air Force's F-16 jet shot down by Indian Forces

Srinagar: Pakistan Air Force's F-16 jet that violated Indian air space has been shot down in Indian retaliatory fire 3KM within Pakistan territory in Lam valley, Nowshera sector, reported ANI.

A Parachute was seen as Pakistan Air Force's F-16 was going down, condition of the pilot is unknown, it further said.

Earlier, three Pakistan Air Force (PAF) F-16 fighter jets violated Indian air space in Nowshera sector of Jammu and Kashmir's Rajouri district but were pushed back by Indian aircraft, a top official had said.
