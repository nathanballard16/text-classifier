New Delhi: Delhi Police has finally arrested the husband of a woman who was found dead with a gunshot wound in Vishwas Nagar of Farsh Bazar in Shahdara district of Delhi on Wednesday. Inspection of the crime scene led police to believe that it was 'murder' and not 'suicide' as claimed. Moreover, the initial post mortem report also indicates that it could not be suicide, rather its a murder.
