Ghaziabad police foils ATM robbery arrests four

Ghaziabad: Following a gunbattle, the Ghaziabad police have nabbed four criminals who were trying to rob an ATM in Muradnagar area of Ghaziabad on the intervening night of Saturday and Sunday. Cops said that the accused persons had almost broke open the ATM machine and were trying to take out cash when police intercepted them. According to police, the arrested accused persons have been identified as Govinda, Rohit, Ajay Riyal and Vikas, all residents of Netaji Subash Chandra Palace in New Delhi. "The accused persons opened fire on police party and tried to run away. Police have recovered three .315 bore country made pistols along with a few live cases of ammunition and tools that were being used to break the ATM machine.
