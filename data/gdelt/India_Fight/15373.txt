Boy found dead in locked house under mysterious circumstances

Ludhiana: A 14-year-old boy was found dead under mysterious circumstances in Guru Ajrun Dev Nagar on Friday night. Since Gurdeep had to prepare for final exams of class VIII, they locked Gurdeep inside the house so that he could study. Devinder claimed that when they retuned after about half-an-hour, Gurdeep was found lying dead on the bed. When parents returned, they found the boy lying motionless on the bed, police quoting family members said. We are yet to receive the autopsy report.”The ACP said though doctors might have used the word strangulation in the autopsy, the exact of death would be ascertained after getting the viscera report.
