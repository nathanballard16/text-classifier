Relentless Rain Savages Maharashtra; 6 Killed, 400 Stranded

Mumbai was lashed by heavy rains, with monsoon showers leading to waterlogging and flooding in parts of the city that caused massive traffic congestion. Commuters were in for trouble as waterlogging slowed down traffic and submerged railway tracks.
