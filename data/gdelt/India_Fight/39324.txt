Massive fire breaks out at MIDC chemical factory in Pune district

Fire breaks out at a company based in Kurkumbh MIDC area near Pune-Solapur highway. | Photo Credit: ANI

Pune: A major fire broke out at a chemical factory in Kurkumbh MIDC in Pune district's Daund area near the Pune-Solapur highway on Wednesday night. Fire tenders have arrived at the scene and efforts are being made to douse the fire. No reports of casualty so far.
