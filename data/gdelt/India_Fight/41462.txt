That’s how Akshay Kumar travels for his 'Laxmmi Bomb' shoot. Jetty style!

Akshay Kumar's upcoming film 'Laxmmi Bomb' has been the talk of the town since the announcement. The actor is currently busy shooting for the film which is being helmed by Raghava Lawrence . It's a Hindi remake of Tamil film 'Muni 2: Kanchana'.Akshay is shooting for the film at Madh, Versova in Mumbai, for which the actor has been travelling to and fro through ferry rides. And today, he was snapped at Versova jetty post the shoot of his film. He was wearing a grey coloured printed hoodie and matching joggers paired with white shoes.Check out the pictures here:Meanwhile, 'Laxmmi Bomb' stars Kiara Advani as the female lead, megastar Amitabh Bachchan and Tusshar Kapoor in lead roles along with Akshay. The horror-comedy is backed by Tusshar and Shabina Khan . It has been slated to hit the theatres in June 2020.Akshay will also feature in Rohit Shetty 's 'Sooryavanshi' co-starring Katrina Kaif and Raj Mehta's 'Good News'.
