India: 11 die in road crash

(MENAFN) Police declared that over 11 people have died as four more were wounded after their vehicle drifted off a mountainous road and plunged into gorge in Indian-controlled Kashmir.



The crash occurred in Rajgarh area of Ramban district, 177 km south of Srinagar city, the summer capital of Indian-controlled Kashmir.



Senior superintendent of police Ramban, Anita Sharma confirmed: "Eleven people including five children were killed and four others injured after a vehicle carrying them fell into gorge at power house Rajgarh road here today."



Police officials have reported a case and called for probes to determine the reason behind the fatal collision.
