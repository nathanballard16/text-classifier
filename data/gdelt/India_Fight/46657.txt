50 live bombs seized in West Bengal, nine held

HOWRAH: The police have seized fifty live bombs from Amta area in West Bengal 's Howrah district and arrested nine persons in this connection, a senior officer said.Acting on a tip-off, the police raided two villages -Chandrapur and Serpur- and recovered the bombs on Monday, he said.A bomb disposal squad has defused the explosives, said the officer of Amta police station.An investigation has been initiated in this reagrd, he added.
