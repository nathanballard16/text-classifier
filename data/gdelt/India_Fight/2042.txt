Airtel brings 4G connectivity to remote Indian communities

By Chris Kelly, Total Telecom

Wednesday 16 January 19

Airtel is the first operator to bring 4G connectivity to the remote island chain

Indian telco Bharti Airtel has launched 4G mobile services in the remote Andaman and Nicobar Islands, bringing twenty first century connectivity to one of India's most remote destinations. The move was welcomed by India's Telecoms Secretary, Aruna Sundararajan…
