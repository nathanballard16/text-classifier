Chased by lover’s kin, 22-year-old falls into Buddha Nullah, dies

LUDHIANA: A 22-year-old man died after he fell into Buddha Nullah in Shivpuri while trying to escape a group, who was chasing him, on Wednesday.Police, quoting locals, said Akashdeep Singh, 22, of Preet Vihar Colony, Civil Lines, had gone to a woman’s house, who had got married recently. “Akashdeep had an affair with the woman. He was upset as she had tied the knot last month when he was out of town. When he reached her house on Wednesday morning, her in-laws, brother and husband had a spat with him. As tempers frayed, the woman’s kin started beating Akashdeep with sticks. To save himself, Akashdeep ran away from there,” said assistant commissioner of police (ACP-Central) Waryam Singh.As the accused chased him, Akashdeep got into an auto. “When the woman’s kin intercepted his auto, Akashdeep jumped out. But he fell into Buddha Nullah. The accused left on seeing him stuck in the drain,” he added.Passersby helped bring Akashdeep out. He was taken to a nearby hospital, where doctors declared him dead.Police said a CCTV camera installed in the area has captured the woman’s kin chasing Akashdeep.Police have registered a case under section 304 (culpable homicide not amounting to murder) of the IPC against the woman’s brother, husband, brother-in-law and father-in-law, who are yet to be arrested.
