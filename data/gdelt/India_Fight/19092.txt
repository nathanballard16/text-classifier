Delhi: Massive fire at plastic factory destroys goods worth lakhs; no casualties reported

Follow us on Image Source : INDIA TV Massive fire at plastic factory in Delhi

A massive fire broke out at a plastic factory in Delhi's Narela Industrial area late on Saturday. The incident was reported from G-block of the area. Nearly 22 fire tenders were present at the spot and efforts to douse the flames continue. Goods worth lakh of rupees were destroyed in the fire.

No labours were present inside the factory at the time of the incident, no injuries or casualties were reported.

According to information received by the fire officials, heavy amount of plastic contents inside the factory led to the rapid spread of the fire.

The reason of the fire, however, was yet not ascertained.
