"Had multiple meetings with Indian & foreign companies to understand & work together on various issues regarding e-commerce & data flow": Piyush Goyal, Minister of Railways and Commerce, Government of India. The minister has been busy meeting representatives of the domestic and international e-commerce firms amid intense lobbying to shape the industry's future; courtesy of Piyush Goyal via Twitter

Synopsis

The commerce ministry’s draft e-commerce policy has stirred up a hornets' nest. American e-commerce players are resisting its key provisions, while the lobby of domestic retailers and traders broadly appears to be on the same page with the government.
