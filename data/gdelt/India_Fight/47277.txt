Rajkot: Couple killed in accident

RAJKOT: A couple were killed after their motorcycle was rammed by an unidentified car in Jodia of Jamnagar district on Saturday night.The victims were identified as Uga Jadav (52) and his wife Somi Jadav (48).“The accident occurred when the Jadav couple were returning to their home in Jodia on the bike from Kunal village. Near Badanpar village on the bridge over Kunal River, a four-wheeler rammed the bike on which the couple were riding. The couple were killed on the spot,” said an official from Jodia police station.The vehicle that had caused the accident also rammed a cyclist, who was taken to GG Hospital in Jamnagar in a seriously injured condition.
