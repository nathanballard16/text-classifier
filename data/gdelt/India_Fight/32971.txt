Kiara back in Delhi for shoot, shares memories

After shooting for the recently released filmat various colleges of the Delhi University, actress Kiara Advani is back in the national capital to shoot for her next film.

Without revealing many details about the movie, Kiara took to Instagram Stories and wrote: "Memories... Miranda House college. Had shot here for Kabir Singh and now back here for the next one."

Along with it, she shared a photograph of the college's entrance.
