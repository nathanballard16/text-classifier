Terrorists planning Pulwama-like attack on Burhan Wani's death anniversary in J&K: Reports

As per sources, Pakistani terrorists are likely to target the security forces at the national highway with the use of snipers. The secret agencies tell that the same is being planned out by a team of 6-8 terrorists, who have been hiding in Kashmir with changed identities.

The reports suggest that there are chances that IED and snipers could be used for the same. It is important to know that IED explosive was used in large amount to blow away the CRPF convoy in Pulwama, which claimed the lives of 40 Indian soldiers. A high speeding car with around 100 kgs of IED was used to blow the CRPF convoy in Jammu and Kashmir’s Pulwama on February 14, 2019.
