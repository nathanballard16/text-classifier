Two Nigerians held for duping women

Two Nigerians held for duping women06 Mar 2019, 11:42AM ISTSource: TNNThe cyber crime unit of Delhi Police arrested four men, including two Nigerian nationals, for duping women on matrimonial websites. The Nigerians came India to float their garment business but when they failed to get success, they started duping women through matrimonial websites to make money. The gang came on police radar when a Pune-based girl lodged a complaint of cheating with the police. A team of Delhi Police raided the premises and nabbed the accused. The police also recovered Mobile phones, laptops and fake passports.
