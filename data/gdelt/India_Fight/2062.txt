PAC jawan arrested after wife found dead in Mathura home

AGRA: The 26-year-old wife of a Provincial Armed Constabulary ( PAC ) jawan was found dead inside her house in Chanakyapuri Colony in Mathura early on Wednesday morning. The deceased was identified as Saraswati, wife of Sunil. A case has been registered against Sunil and his father, Mahendra Singh, who have been arrested, on a complaint filed by the deceased’s father Ramveer. Sunil is presently posted at 41 battalion PAC in Ghaziabad and had come to Mathura on Tuesday. Talking to media, one of the deceased’s relatives, Jitendra, alleged that Saraswati was killed by Sunil.
