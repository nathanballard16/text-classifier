India retaliates for Pulwama : IAF drops 1,000 kg bombs on Jaish camps

New Delhi : In a massive retaliation for the Pulwama terror attack, the Indian Air Force has carried out a major attack on the terrorist camps across the LoC, 13 days after the deadly attack, which left 40 CRPF jawans dead.

According to the reports, 12 Mirage 2000 jets took part in the operation that dropped 1000 kg bombs on terror camps, completely destroying it.

The Indian Army sources said that the jet struck training camps in Balakot, Muzafarrabad at around 3.48 am and Chakoti at around 3.58 am. NSA Ajit Doval has briefed Prime Minister Narendrab Modi regarding the situation.
