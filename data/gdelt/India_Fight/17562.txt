Hyderabad: Hookah Center raided, three arrested

Hyderabad: Task Force Team of Hyderabad City Police (South Zone), Asif Nagar raided hookah center which was running at Asif Nagar road.

Police arrested the manager of hookah parlour, D. Sai Goutham and an employee, N. Ganesh.

It is reported that the owner of hookah center, Razzak is absconding. The articles seized and the persons arrested were handed over to Asif Nagar Police for taking further action.
