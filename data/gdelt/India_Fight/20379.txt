Terrorist behind killing of RSS leader in Jammu identified; associated with Hizbul’s killer team

On April 9, RSS leader Chandrakant Sharma and his PSO were killed in the militant attack launched by Zahid Hussain in a health center in Kishtwar.

As per sources, the terrorist named Zahid Hussain is a member of Hizbul’s killer team , which was set up for target killing in Kishtwar.

He had joined Hizbul this year on April 6 and was included in the killer team of the outfit, sources said.

Zahid had opened fire at a health centre in Jammu and Kashmir\'s Kishtwar town, in which local RSS leader, identified as Chandrakant Sharma, was killed.
