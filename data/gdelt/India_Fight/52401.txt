Sale of 'purple ecstasy' busted by ANC, one person arrested

MDMA

Police arrested a 25-year-old man outside a mall in Malad on Saturday with Rs 4.85 lakh worth of ‘purple ecstasy’ tablets, or methylenedioxy-methamphetamine (), apsychoactive drug.These tablets can pass off as purple confectioneries to the untrained eye and help peddlers sell it to youth without arousing much suspicion, an official said.The Anti-Narcotics Cell’s Kandivali unit arrested Utkarsh Patange with 97 purple ecstasy tablets.“We are working on unraveling the peddler network in this case,” the official said.
