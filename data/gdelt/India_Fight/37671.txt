Servant hangs self from fan at master’s house

Ludhiana, August 4A 21-year-old servant of a hosiery factory owner was found dead in his house at Vivek Nagar today. Kamlesh Kumar, a native of MP, had been working at Girish’s house for 16 years. The SHO of PS 8, Gurmeet Singh, said the incident came to light this morning. The owner repeatedly rang his servant up but he didn’t answer. The deceased got married about two months ago at his native place and since then, he had reportedly been in tension.
