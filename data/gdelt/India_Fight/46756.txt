2 robbers held after scuffle with police in Dwarka

NEW DELHI: Delhi Police has arrested two robbers who fired at the constables at Baba Haridas Nagar after they confronted the robbers in Dwarka. Both the constables suffered injuries in the scuffle though they dodged the bullets fired by the robbers. Constable Sanjeet along with Constable Hansraj reached the spot as per the information where they found that the room is closed from inside. Both the accused could be overpowered after a short scuffle and both the police personnel also get injured during this grappling. While, 02 live cartridges were recovered from the possession of co-accused Sikandar, a resident of Muzaffarpur, Bihar.
