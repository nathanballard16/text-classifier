J K killings a pre planned murder says Mamata

Kolkata: Chief Minister Mamata Banerjee on Thursday said the incident, in which five migrant labourers in Kashmir were killed was a 'pre-planned murder', and demanded a high-level inquiry. She said the Narendra Modi-led government failed to check terror attacks despite heavy deployment of Central forces in Kashmir. She questioned why Bengal labourers were abducted and killed by terrorists when the law order situation in the area remains in Centre's hands. "It was a pre-planned murder. The Chief Minister said the murders had taken place on the day when a delegation of European MPs visited Kashmir.
