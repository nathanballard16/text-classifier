Sharpshooter involved in four murder cases arrested

Apart from this murder, police said that the accused was found involved three more murder cases. Police identified the accused as Ible Hassan (38 years)-sharpshooter of Abdul Naseer gang- entered the criminal world in the 90s to earn quick money. Additional Deputy Commissioner of Police (southeast) Kumar Gyanesh said in 2014, due to enmity with Naseer Gang, Hassan along with his associates murdered one Akil Mama in Jafarabad.
