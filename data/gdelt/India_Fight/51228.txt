Diamond Harbour station master found dead in drain

Kolkata: The station master of Diamond Harbour railway station in South 24 Parganas district was found dead under mysterious circumstances in a drain near his rented house on Sunday. According to police sources, the local people identified him as station master Nirmal Kumar on Monday. As per sources, Kumar, who hailed from neighbouring Bihar, went to the local market on Saturday, but did not return till late. Sources said he used to stay at the rented house alone, but recently his wife and brother-in-law had shifted there. "Local sources told us that he had left his house in anger after having a fight with his family.
