Two BTech students on scooter crash into bus after it slams brakes, killed

The girl, Vaishnavi, did not have any protective gear. Utkarsh, who was riding the scooter, had a helmet on, but it broke into two under the impact of the collision. Both had been pursuing a BTech course in electronic communication from IIMT College in Knowledge Park area.The accident occurred around 12.15pm. Both were declared dead on arrival at a private hospital.Police said Utkarsh was from Allahabad while Vaishnavi was a Varanasi resident. The bus, which was travelling at a speed of 60-70kmph on the expressway, stopped suddenly to pick up passengers at a stop.
