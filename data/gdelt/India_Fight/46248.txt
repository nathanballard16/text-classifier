Kartik Aaryan and Kiara Advani's 'Bhool Bhulaiyaa 2' to go on floors this Dussehra

Kartik Aaryan has been working on several projects since the start of the year. As soon as he wrapped up Imtiaz Ali's Love Aaj Kal sequel with rumoured girlfriend Sara Ali Khan, he started shooting for the remake of Pati Patni Aur Woh. The film is set to star Kartik alongside Ananya Panday and Bhumi Pednekar. And now if reports are to be believed the actor is all set to start shooting for his next big project.

Back in August, Kartik announced his new project as Bhool Bhulaiyaa 2 and surprised his fans with the film's first poster. The actor is already prepping to start shooting for the film, Bhool Bhulaiyaa 2 is set to go on floors in a few days. According to Mumbai Mirror, the film will hit the floors on Dussehra on October 8. A source was quoted by the portal, “It’s an auspicious day. It will be a brief two to three-day schedule in Mumbai with Kartik. The second schedule will kick-off early next year with Kiara also jumping into the maze.”

The report also stated while the first schedule will feature Kartik alone, Kiara Advani will join the cast in the second schedule next year. Bhool Bhulaiyaa 2 is an awaited sequel of the 2007 release of the same name starring Akshay Kumar. The horror-comedy is expected to wrap by March next year as the makers are eyeing the film's release for July 2020.

Meanwhile, Bhool Bhulaiyaa 2 director Anees Bazmee is will be busy with Pagalpanti's post-production. Starring John Abraham, Anil Kapoor, Arshad Warsi and Ileana D’Cruz in lead roles, the film is scheduled for a November 22, 2019 release.
