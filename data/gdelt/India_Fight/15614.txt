One Killed, Several More Wounded in Shooting in Kashmir - Reports

"A soldier was martyred and three others were injured in a ceasefire violation by Pakistan in the Keri Battal area of Akhnoor this morning", an army source told the Indian newspaper.

In the meantime, a local official has told Sputnik that the firing reportedly started at 5:15 AM local time and continued until 7:15 AM.
