School Bus Involved in a Crash in Delhi

One woman is suffering minor injuries after her car collided with a school bus this morning in Delhi.

DELHI - A 23 year old woman suffered minor injuries after colliding with a school bus this morning in Delhi.

Luckily no children were on board the bus when the crash happened. Police say the two vehicles collided on at James St. and Crosier Street this morning.

OPP are reminding everyone to please drive safe, pay attention and make sure you reach your destinations safely.
