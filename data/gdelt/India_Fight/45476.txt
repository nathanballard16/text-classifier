Nepalese man held for rape, murder of 6-yr-old

New Delhi, September 29A Nepalese man absconding since 2015 after raping and killing a six-year-old girl was arrested here, the police said on Sunday. Vijay (49) is a resident of Bharatpur, in Nepal and used to work in a garment export house in Okhla. He often provided financial help to the victims family, they said. As the victim’s father had abused him and to take revenge, he raped and killed the girl on August 10, 2015, the police said.
