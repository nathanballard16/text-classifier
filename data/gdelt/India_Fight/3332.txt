Exchange of firing going on at more than eleven positions on LoC in Poonch

Srinagar: The armies of India and Pakistan Saturday trade heavy fire and shelling along the Line of Control (LoC) in Poonch district of Jammu and Kashmir.

Reports said firing and shelling started at about 11:15 AM at forward area of Mankote in Krishna Ghati sector.

Adding that the firing exchange is going on at more than eleven positions on the LoC.

Meanwhile there was no immediate report of any loss of life or injuries on either side.
