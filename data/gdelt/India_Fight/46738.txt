Man shot by criminals on Dashami night

Kolkata: A 38-year-old man from Kanchrapara in North 24-Parganas was shot dead by some unidentified miscreants on Tuesday night. The incident took place at Netaji Nagar in Kanchrapara on Dashami. Tension prevailed in the area on Wednesday morning as local residents demanded a crackdown on nearby criminal dens. A contractual labourer by profession, Kurmi was returning home from a nearby area around 3 am when the miscreants stopped his way and shot him from close quarters. The miscreants managed to flee from the spot before local residents reached the area.
