Pulwama: Should India go for war against Pakistan? Watch analysis by Dhruv Rathee

Dhruv Rathee, a political, social and environmental activist, a popular YouTuber in his latest video talks about the recent Pulwama attack in which 49 CRPF personnel were killed on Thursday when their convoy was targeted in Pulwama district on the Srinagar-Jammu National Highway.

As there are voices within the country to go for war against Pakistan over the recent terror attack in Pulwama, Dhruv Rathee explains why war is not an ideal choice considering the consequences of war.
