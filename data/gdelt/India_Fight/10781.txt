State-run airline Air India is waiving off all applicable charges for date change to Armed Forces personnel for travel to their Base Stations & will be accepted on Priority as Go-Show passengers.

Amid the escalating tussle, IndiGo is extending zero cancellation fee to all the Indian soldiers who are on leave and have been asked to resume their duties by the Indian Armed Forces. It may be noted that the airline temporarily suspended flights to and from Srinagar, Jammu, Chandigarh, Amritsar and Dehradun, yesterday due to airspace closure. Passengers who have cancelled their reservations Srinagar, Jammu, Chandigarh, Amritsar or Dehradun can mail their PNR to customer.relations@goindigo.in with the subject line - (Waiver request for airspace closure) to claim a refund. Applicable for travel till 31st March).
