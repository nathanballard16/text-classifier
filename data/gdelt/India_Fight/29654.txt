First Honda BS6 Two-wheeler India unveil highlights: Prices, Features, Images and Specifications

First Honda BS6 two-wheeler India unveil highlights: Honda has unveiled its first BS6 compliant two-wheeler today in India. It is the new Activa 125 cc which comes with a whole array of new technology along with some minor cosmetic and feature updates.

Honda Motorcycles and Scooters India (HMSI) has unveiled its first BS6 complaint two-wheeler today in India. As expected, it is the new Activa 125 scooter. It gets the same engine however it has been upgraded to meet to upcoming BS6 emission regulations. Honda has also deployed a new system which replaces the conventional starter motor resulting in a silent starting procedure. Furthermore, the engine comes with an idle stop-start system. The new BS6 Honda Activa comes with a number of minor cosmetic updates, along with some feature alterations in well. For instance, it comes with a new instrument cluster, redesigned under-seat storage along with a new cubby hole at the front. Honda is yet to officially announce the prices of the new BS6 Activa 125, but the company says that it is going to cost 10 to 15 per cent more than the current model. Furthermore, the new Activa will go on sale sometime during the second half of 2019.
