Families, Friends Mourn Loved Ones Lost in Tragic Delhi Fire

At least 43 people were killed and over a dozen others injured early on Sunday (December 08) when a fire broke out in a factory in a crowded market in West Delhi's Rani Jhansi Road area, the national capital's Fire Service Department said. Pictures show families and friends grieving as they wait to collect the bodies of their loved ones who lost their lives in tragic Delhi Fire.Mukhtar Alam, who lost his relative in a fire, cries as he waits for the body outside a mortuary in New Delhi.
