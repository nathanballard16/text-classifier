Sasikumar's next to have Bharathiraja and Sarathkumar!

Filmmaker turned actor Sasikumar is all set to begin shooting for his next heroic venture with director Nirmal Kumar offame. The recent update is that the film will have veteran director Bharathiraja and Sarathkumar in pivotal characters. Produced by Ram Mohan of Kalpatru Pictures, Radhan offame is composing music for the film and Ganesh Chandra will be cranking the camera.

The film went on floors recently in Chennai city with a formal pooja. After Nirmal Kumar confirms the rest of the cast and crew of the film, he will be beginning the principal shoot of the film.
