ATS nabs serial killer wanted for killing 3 in Gujarat

Ahmedabad : The Gujarat ATS has nabbed a serial killer wanted in 3 murders in Gandhinagar.

Police said that Monish alias Madan Mali, native of Rajasthan settled in Ahmedabad since 1995, had murdered 3 people in similar manner in October and December last year and January this year.

He has admitted to have stolen a pistol for the crimes. He had also looted many other people apart from those whom he killed.

He was also earlier jailed once in the past.
