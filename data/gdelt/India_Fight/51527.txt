Lawyers call off strike 2 weeks after clashes at Tis Hazari

NEW DELHI: Lawyers in Delhi called off their strike on Friday almost two weeks after clashes with police at the Tis Hazari court here. The two were suspended after the incident of Tis Hazari violence. "We respect the order of the High Court so the abstinence from work is suspended, work to be resumed from Saturday. Delhi Police personnel and lawyers were involved in an altercation on the premises of Tis Hazari Court on November 2 after a clash over a parking dispute turned violent. The lawyers' strike was called off a day after the Delhi Police resumed duty at the Tis Hazari court premises after a gap of about two weeks.
