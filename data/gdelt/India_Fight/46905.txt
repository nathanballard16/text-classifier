Jiaganj triple murder Cops recover hand written note

Kolkata: In the triple murder case of Jiaganj in Murshidabad, the police have found several clues including a handwritten note. According to sources, on Tuesday, bodies of Bandhu Prakash Pal (40), his wife Beauty Pal (30) and their son Angan Pal (5) were found inside their home. Local milk vendor Rajib Das and his friend were the first to spot the bodies at Prakash's Lebubagan residence in Jiaganj. After getting inside, Das found Prakash lying in a pool of blood.
