CRPF personnel shoots himself dead in Bawana

New Delhi: A CRPF personnel allegedly shot himself dead in Bawana area of Outer North district. Police said no suicide note was found and his family members have not suspected any foul play. Deputy Commissioner of Police (outer north) Gaurav Sharma said that on Sunday at 7.14am they received information from CRPF Bawana control room that ASI Ram Gilas Meena has committed suicide. In another case, a 32-year-old labourer died after suffering an electric shock while working at a factory in northwest Delhi's Wazirpur Industrial Area, police said on Sunday. The deceased has been identified as Dayaram.
