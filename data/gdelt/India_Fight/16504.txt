Two killed as bike slips

RAJKOT: Two persons were killed when the motorcycle they were riding on slipped on Morbi-Navlakhi Road near Morbi on Wednesday night. The deceased have been identified as Harsh Parmar and Meet Merja, both in their twenties and residents of Vaghpara locality of Morbi.According to an official from Morbi B division police station, Parmar and Merja, accompanied by five friends went for a motorcycle ride on Holi. While returning the bike which Parmar was driving skidded, and both he and Merja, who was riding pillion, fell. They received grievous injuries in the accident. They were rushed to Morbi Civil Hospital but doctors there declared them brought dead.
