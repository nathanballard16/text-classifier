Man kills girlfriend chops body into pieces for spurning marriage proposal

NEW DELHI: Special Cell of Delhi Police arrested a man for murdering his girlfriend in cold blood. Ayub then chopped her body into five pieces so that the body could not be identified. On August 30, the Special Cell received a specific input about the whereabouts of Ayub, who was trying to flee Delhi. According to the input, Ayub was coming near Turkman Gate area, with a plan to flee Delhi and settle in someother place. The Special Cell team then rushed to the spot and apprehended Ayub.
