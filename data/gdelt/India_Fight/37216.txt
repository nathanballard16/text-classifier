One leopard electrocuted, another mauls woman in Amreli

RAJKOT: A leopard attacked a 75-year-old woman in Sarkhadia village of Khambha taluka in Amreli district at 2am after midnight on August 1.According to the forest department, Samjuben Khunt had gone to relieve herself outside the house, when the leopard, hiding behind the bushes, pounced on her. She received injuries on her head and hand and had to be given stitches at the Khambha government hospital.In another incident, a female leopard around 10 years of age got electrocuted in Jamvala range of Gir Somnath district after it came into contact of a live electric wire in Bhima Makvana’s farm.
