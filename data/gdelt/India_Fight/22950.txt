Here is why Suriya is a doting dad!

Suriya is a doting dad that he has always been spending leisure time with his kids Dev and Diya.

The actor was recently in Indonesia for the last song shoot of his upcoming magnum opus Kaappaan which is being directed by KV Anand. After the shoot, the actor took a flight to Delhi to watch his son Dev’s performance in the Karate competition and together they returned to Chennai.





The audio and trailer launch of Suriya’s political drama ‘NGK’ is all set to be held today at a star hotel today in Chennai. Produced by Dream Warrior Pictures and directed by Selvaraghavan, Yuvan Shankar Raja is composing music for NGK.
