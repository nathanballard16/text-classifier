Pipes covered with 10kg heroin seized at Delhi airport

NEW DELHI: Customs on Tuesday seized over 10kg heroin worth Rs 45 crore from a Mozambique passenger at IGI Airport . The customs official recovered plastic pipe rolls from the passenger's baggage, which were concealed in a black plastic sheet to avoid detection. The pipes were filled and covered with layers of a powdery substance, the official said.The woman had arrived from Addis Ababa at T3 with a huge bag on flight ET-686. Customs commissioner Manish Kumar said she was arrested under provisions of the Narcotic Drugs and Psychotropic Substances Act for carrying 10.7kg heroin worth approximately Rs 45 crore concealed in the pipes. "A total of 10.69 kg of the substance was seized and initial testing confirmed it to be heroin," Kumar said.
