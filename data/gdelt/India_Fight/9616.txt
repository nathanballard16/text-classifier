Congress party women leader was murdered

Former Corporator of Gajuwaka area, Congress party women leader Bhogasamudram Vijaya Reddy was brutally murdered here on Tuesday at her Padma Bhaskar apartment (5th floor)near NGGO's colony in Visakhapatnam.

According to the family members,some of the unidentified people called her up for buying the flat in the apartment,later she was found dead. The women leader body was found in a pool of blood by her family members.Immediately they informed the police.

Police registered the case and moved the body to KGH for postmortem in the city. The reasons for the murder was yet to be ascertained.
