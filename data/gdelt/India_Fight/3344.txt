Himachal Pradesh: Four shed of labourers gutted in fire at Bahang area of Kullu district

#HimachalPradesh https://t.co/37WaL3GniT — ANI (@ANI) January 26, 2019The year 2019 continues to prove disastrous for Mumbai when it comes to combating fire incidents. "Four shed of labourers gutted in fire at Bahang in Kullu district this evening. At least four sheds of labourers were gutted in fire and several others partially damaged at Bahang in Himachal Pradesh's Kullu district on Saturday. 3 more sheds gutted in the fire at Bahang in Kullu district; the fire has been doused now. No injuries reported," the news agency ANI reported.
