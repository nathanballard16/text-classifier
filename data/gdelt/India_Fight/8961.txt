More than 100 separatists held in Kashmir raids in pre-election crackdown

More than 100 separatists in Kashmir were detained in overnight raids, police officials said on Saturday, as part of a crackdown on groups that might cause trouble ahead of the elections set to be held by May. The move comes days after a suicide car bombing killed at least 40 security personnel on February 14.
