Uttarakhand loses second son in two days: Major VS Dhoundiyal martyred in J&K as nation bid adieu to Maj Chitresh Bisht

Uttarakhand braveheart Major Vibhuti Dhoundiyal attained martyrdom in an encounter with terrorists in Pulwama on Monday.

Even as the last rites of Major Chitresh Bisht were still underway in Haridwar on Monday, Uttarakhand received the news of another brave soldier - Major Vibhuti Dhoundiyal - attaining martyrdom in an encounter with terrorists in Pulwama in Jammu Kashmir.

Major Dhoundiyal, a resident of Dehradun, was among four Army men killed in an encounter with JeM terrorists in Pulwama district just a short distance away from the spot where 40 CRPF personnel were killed recently in a suicide attack by the outfit. Sepoy Hari Singh, Sepoy Ajay Kumar and Havaldar Sheo Ram were also killed in the 12-hour encounter.

Major Chitresh Bisht was killed while defusing a mine on the LoC in Nowshera sector of Rajouri district on Saturday. His mortal remains were consigned to flames in Haridwar on Monday afternoon.
