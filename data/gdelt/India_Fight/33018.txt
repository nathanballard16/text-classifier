5 Pakistani Army soldiers martyred, 1 injured near Jammu sector : ISPR

Five Pakistani army soldiers have been martyred and one injured in an explosion that took place a few metres from the Line of Control in Chamb sector of Azad Jammu and Kashmir (AJK), the Inter-Services Public Relations (ISPR) said in a statement on Wednesday.

The nature of the blast is being ascertained.

The military’s media wing said the incident “is evident of state-sponsored terrorism by India violating bilateral ceasefire agreement and the international rules”.
