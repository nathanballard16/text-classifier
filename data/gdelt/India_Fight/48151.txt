Elderly British tourist found dead at Jodhpur Park lodge

Kolkata: An elderly British citizen on a two-months tour to India was found dead in a guest house at Jodhpur Park in South Kolkata on Saturday afternoon. The woman, identified as Helen Maria, had put up at the lodge along with five other persons, all hailing from Spain. The Lake police station received information about the woman lying unconscious at the first floor of the Jodhpur Park guest house. On Friday night, she had dinner and also had alcohol along with her co-travellers inside the guest house. The sleuths are also questioning the owner of the guest house for further leads in the case.
