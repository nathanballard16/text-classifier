Gold seized at MIA

Mangaluru: Customs on Tuesday seized 333.4 grams of gold valued at about Rs 10.84 lakh, from a passenger who arrived at Mangaluru International Airport from Dubai on board SpiceJet flight SG60.The gold was concealed in the arch of the feet of a passenger, which was underneath socks. A week ago, officials seized 91.05 grams of gold from a passenger who arrived at Mangaluru International Airport from Dubai on board Air India flight IX814. The gold valued at Rs 2.96 lakh was concealed in paste form inside four branded soaps and was of 24-carat purity.
