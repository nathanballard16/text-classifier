Mysuru: Elderly couple found murdered in farm

Representative image

MYSURU: An elderly couple, Erathanna (80) and Shivamma (75), was found murdered in a farm in Mysuru Taluk's Nagavala village.

The incident happened late Monday night but came to light only on Tuesday.

The couple was brutally attacked with iron rods by unknown assailants, killing them on the spot.

Yelawala police are probing the incident.
