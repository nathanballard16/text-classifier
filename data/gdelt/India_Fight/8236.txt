Telangana man shot dead in Florida departmental store

A man from Telangana, who was working as a manager at a departmental store in Florida, US was shot dead by unidentified persons on Tuesday evening.

K Govardhan Reddy, who hailed from Telangana’s Yadadri Bhongir district was allegedly attacked by a group of men inside the store in Florida’s Pensacola city.

According to the reports, another employee of the store also got injured in the attack,

Reddy is survived by wife and two daughters. His family has appealed to the Government of India to help them in bringing back the body home.

The accused however, reportedly did not take away any cash from the store after opening fire, sources said.
