TRAGIC! 6 school students killed in bus-truck collision in Maharashtra’s Gadchiroli district - SEE PICS

Truck-Bus collision in Gadchiroli, 6 children die | Photo Credit: BCCL

Nagpur: At least six children were killed and nine others left injured when a Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation (MSRTC) bus collided with a truck in the state’s Gadchiroli district on Wednesday morning.

According to Deputy Superintendent of Police Shashikant Bhosle, the accident occurred at around 8.30 am when the truck and the bus collided near Gurupalli village in Gadchiroli, located nearly 170 km from Nagpur.

The collision was so strong that the cabins of both the vehicles were crushed. Six school students died in the mishap. As per Bhosle, the victims were rushed to a nearby hospital for treatment.

(Photo courtesy: BCCL)

As per eyewitnesses, a number of school students were travelling on the bus when the accident took place.

(Photo Courtesy: Twitter)

After the accident, locals rushed to the spot and helped in the rescue operation.

(Photo Courtesy: BCCL)

Left agitated due to the accident, villagers set the truck ablaze.
