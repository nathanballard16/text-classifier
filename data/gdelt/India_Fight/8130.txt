Brothers die as SUV rams bike

Rajkot: Two brothers were killed and two others injured when an SUV rammed into two motorcycles near Babra town of Amreli district on Wednesday. The duo belong to Madhupur village near Babra. However, the SUV driver managed to flee from the spot.According to police, two brothers — Narsinh Jadhav (70) and Daya Jadhav (60) — who were returning after visiting their bank in Babra died in the accident. They were on their way to distribute invitations for marriage in their family. Two other people who were on the other bike received injuries and have been hospitalized.The injured were identified as Mahesh Nagji Aagal and Suresh Babu Dabhi, residents of Nanima village in Sayla taluka of Surendranagar district.
