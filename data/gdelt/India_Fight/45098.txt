RRR: SS Rajamouli's film starring Ram Charan and Jr NTR gets delayed? Find Out

Baahubali director SS Rajamouli's upcoming project titled RRR has reportedly got delayed. The film, RRR starring superstars Ram Charan and Jr NTR will now be releasing on the eve of Sankranthi 2021 instead of July 30, 2020. As per the latest reports, the film, RRR has got postponed till Sankranthi 2021 and will not be hitting the big screen on July 30, 2020. There is a strong buzz going around the film, RRR that British actor Daisy Edgar Jones leaving the film for personal reasons has left the makers of the film RRR in a fix.

In addition to British actor Daisy Edgar Jones walking out of the film, the lead actors of the film RRR also suffered injuries. As per the latest reports, Ram Charan and Jr NTR's injuries took weeks to recover and this resulted in the Pune schedule of the film RRR helmed by SS Rajamouli getting cancelled. The reports also suggest that the Baahubali director SS Rajamouli has successfully finished shooting the key action scene with megastar Jr NTR in Bulgaria, but still the cast and crew is unfortunately running behind schedule. The film titled RRR is expected to be a period drama, which is directed by the well known director SS Rajamouli.

The film, RRR's story line is set in the era of 1920s pre-independent India. As per the latest reports, the film called RRR is based on the life story of Komaram Bheem and Alluri Seetharama Raju who were the brave freedom fighters from the states of Telangana and Andhra Pradesh.
