J&K Police identify Anantnag grenade attack perpetrators

SRINAGAR: Police have identified two Hizbul Mujahideen terrorists who perpetrated the grenade attack during a back-to-village programme in Anantnag district last Tuesday, killing an agriculture department employee, Sheikh Zahoor Ahmad, and sarpanch Syed Rafiq.DGP Dilbagh Singh said the terrorists, Junaid and Zubair, will be brought to book and strictly dealt with for the attack at Hakura area. He also directed police officers to strengthen the ‘nakas’ (checkpoints) across the Valley and augment security grids to ensure citizens’ safety.The DGP was chairing a review meeting at PCR Kashmir where he took stock of the security situation in Kashmir Zone. He was briefed by officers representing different agencies.
