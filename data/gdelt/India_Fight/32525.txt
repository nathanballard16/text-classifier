80-year-old woman murdered

New Delhi: An 80-year-old woman, Santosh Devi, was murdered by a burglar who barged into her house in wee hours on Saturday. The police said the burglar attacked the woman with iron rods. He later slit her throat. Members of the family sleeping in the other room woke up on hearing the noise and caught hold of the burglar. Seeing policemen coming, accused Gidesh inflicted cuts on his hand.
