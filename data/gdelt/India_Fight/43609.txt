Gujarat ATS arrest 'serial killer' for murdering three people in three months

AHMEDABAD: The Gujarat ATS arrested a man for allegedly murdering three people within a span of three months in Gandhinagar.ATS sources said one Monish Mali, who hails from Rajasthan, used to kill people for robbery.Noteworthy, Mali shot dead three people in October, December and January.ATS sources said the accused had been earlier arrested in a theft case. He used to roam in the city with a handcart to carry out recce of the place for killing people.
