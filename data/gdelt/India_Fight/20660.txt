Sex racket at Pachpaoli flat busted, 2 Bangladeshi women, one from Raipur rescued

Two of the rescued women were found to be nationals of Bangladesh and the third hailed from Raipur, Chhattisgarh. Nagpur: A squad of Social Security Wing of Crime Branch on Monday raided a flat in Rai Society in Pachpaoli and busted a sex racket being operated from there. The three rescued women have been sent to Women’s Remand Home till further action. The raiding cops found three women aged between 20-32 years being pushed into flesh trade by the accused Mohd Sarfaraz Memon. A man running the sex racket has been arrested.
