Delhi man shot at

Amritsar, August 16A Delhi resident was shot at by two unidentified robbers on the intervening night of Wednesday and Thursday. The victim, Raman Chhabra, was waiting for a bus at the Beas bus stand when the incident took place. Chhabra stated to the police that he had come to pay obeisance at a religious dera at Beas and after performing seva. He was waiting for a bus for Delhi at the Beas bus stand when two motorcycle-borne youths turned up at around 1.40 am. The accused chased him and snatched his purse containing Rs 200 in cash besides other belongings.
