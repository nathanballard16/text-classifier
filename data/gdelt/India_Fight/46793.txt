Cop among 2 killed in road mishaps

Amritsar, October 9Two persons were killed in separate road mishaps in rural areas here on Monday. When they reached near Bhangwa village, a rashly driven tractor hit a car which went out of control and hit their bike. Harpreet was killed on the spot while his son was seriously injured who was rushed to a government hospital. Meanwhile, in another mishap, a police officer was killed after an unidentified vehicle hit him when he was going to his office. According to Kuljinder Singh, son of the victim, said when Jaswant Singh reached near Chogawan, an unknown vehicle hit him leaving him seriously injured.
