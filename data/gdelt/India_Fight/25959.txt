Kevin murder case: Another witness alters statement

Kochi: Another witness in the Kevin murder case has altered his statement. With this, six witnesses have altered their statements in support of the accused.

Imthyas, witness number 102, altered his statement on Friday. Imthyas was the witness to recovering the mobile phone that belonged to the 11th accused. On Friday, Imthyas gave his statement that the mobile phone was not recovered in his presence.

Meanwhile, Peroorkada State bank of India (SBI) manager Krishna Chandran confirmed that 13 accused including Shanu Chacko filled fuel in their vehicle using an ATM card.

Eight witnesses including Reji Johnson, who alerted the police that Kevin’s dead body was found, gave statements in support of the prosecution.
