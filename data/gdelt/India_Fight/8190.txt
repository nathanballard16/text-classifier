Woman intruder from Pakistan shot at, admitted to Amritsar hospital

AdvertisingBelieved to be around 35 years of age, the injured woman was identified as Gulshan, wife of Wahid from border village in Pakistan’s Tajpura. She admitted to the Guru Nanak Civil hospital in Amritsar after being administered first aid at a health facility at Dera Baba Nanak. No arms or drugs were recovered from injured woman. Our men noted a woman had entered Indian territory and she was moving towards the iron fencing. A pair of men’s shoes and men’s underwear and other such things have been recovered from her bag.
