Pulwama: CRPF personnel injured in grenade attack by terrorists

Pulwama (Jammu and Kashmir) [India], Mar 30 (ANI): A CRPF personnel sustained injuries in a grenade attack carried out by terrorists here on Saturday.

The CRPF personnel, belonging to 182 Battalion, received splinter injuries on his thigh after terrorists lobbed a grenade at a CRPF bunker near a State Bank of India (SBI) branch here.

The injured officer was immediately shifted to a hospital for treatment.

Further details are awaited. (ANI)
