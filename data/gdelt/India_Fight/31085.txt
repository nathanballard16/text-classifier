Ranbir Kapoor, Alia Bhatt to spend time with Rishi Kapoor and family in New York

Alia and Ranbir's Brahmastra also stars Amitabh Bachchan, Dimple Kapadia, Nagarjuna Akkineni and Mouni Roy.

Mumbai: Love birds Ranbir Kapoor and Alia Bhatt have been constantly shooting for Ayan Mukerji's Brahmastra. The duo is extremely busy for many days, hence, they have taken a mini break from the hectic schedule.

The Brahmastra stars have recently spotted at Mumbai airport as they headed to New York to spend time with Rishi Kapoor, Neetu Kapoor and Ranbir's sister Riddhima. As per Mumbai Mirror report, Alia needed a break from her work as she has been simultaneously shooting for her father Mahesh Bhatt's Sadak 2 and Brahmastra. Apart from Alia, Ranbir also wanted to meet his parents in New York.

A source told Mirror, "They were with Rishi and Neetu for New Year celebrations as well. This time, Ranbir’s sister Riddhima is also there so it’s going to be a family reunion. RK and Alia will be returning to Mumbai in a few days to resume work on their film.” Post mini vacation, Ranbir and Alia will be returning from New York to resume Brahmastra shoot.

On the work front, Alia and Ranbir's Brahmastra also stars Amitabh Bachchan, Dimple Kapadia, Nagarjuna Akkineni and Mouni Roy.
