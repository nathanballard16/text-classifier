Two cousins die as SUV rams their bike

RAJKOT: Two cousin brothers were killed when the bike they were riding was hit by an SUV in Juwanpar village of Kalyanpur taluka in Devbhoomi Dwarka district on Sunday evening.The deceased were identified as Dahya Makwana (55) and his cousin Hamir Makwana (60).“The victims were returning home in Nandana village of Kalyanpur taluka from Juwanpar when the SUV rammed their bike from the rear. They were rushed to Kalyanpur Civil Hospital where they succumbed to their injuries shortly after admission,” said an official from Kalyanpur police station.
