Governments do not have permanency: Congress on CBI raids BS Hooda

New Delhi, Jan 25 (ANI): Soon after the Central Bureau of Investigation (CBI) raided former Haryana chief minister B.S. Hooda’ residence in a land scam case, Congress party on Friday said Prime Minister Narendra Modi was “staring at an imminent defeat” and that’s why “these actions” were taking place just ahead of the general elections. “Officials must hear this. Governments do not have any permanency. There are few weeks left for the general elections to begin. It is certain this government is rattled, Prime Minister is staring at an imminent defeat, that’s why these actions,” Congress leader Anand Sharma said.
