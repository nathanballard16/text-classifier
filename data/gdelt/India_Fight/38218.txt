mining mafia: Punjab: Officials attacked by mining mafia in front of cops

LUDHIANA: A team of mining department was attacked by the mining mafia in the wee hours of Wednesday at village Kharak of Ladhowal area in Jalandhar district of Punjab It is alleged that the team escaped from the spot to save their lives while the attackers vandalised their vehicles to stop them. The incident occurred in the presence of Ladhowal police who also escaped from the spot.In this case, three tractor-trolleys have been seized along with an earth-mover. On the complaint of SDO Balwinder Singh, the Ladhowal police have registered a case against one Sham Singh Dodariya and his eight unidentified accomplices.In his complaint mining department SDO Balwinder Singh said that he got a tip-off about illegal mining in the area at around 12:30. After an hour he reached there with his team. Before reaching there, he also informed Ladhowal police to send force on the spot.He added that the Ladhowal police sent four cops on spot. On the spot they found six tractor trolleys and started noting their details. When they moved ahead to check mining further, a group of miscreants opened attack on them with stick in their hands following which they rushed in their car and tried to escape from there.The complainant added that the accused attacked their car also and broke its window pans. They also attacked the driver who has sustained injuries on his left eye, right arm and back. However, he managed to drive away from there.The team then reached Ladhowal police station to lodge a complaint and then reached civil hospital for medical examination and treatment.SHO at Ladhowal police station inspector Manmohan Singh said that "our cops also reached on spot. However, the accused sprinkled sand in their eyes. Besides, the police have lodged an FIR under different sections against Sham Singh Dodariya and his unidentified accomplices. The accused will soon be arrested".
