Gzb bizman shot dead outside house

Ghaziabad: Unidentified bike borne assailants shot dead a 42-years-old Ghaziabad Based business man outside his house in kavi nagar area of Ghaziabad on Wednesday afternoon. Cops said that the incident occurred when victim was parking his going to his under-construction site in fortuner car. According to police, the incident occurred around 2:30 pm in Sector 6 of Chiranjeevi Nagar area in Kavi Nagar. The deceased has been identified as Amit Seth who ran property and finance business and was also in clothes trading. Phool Singh, security guard deployed at the house of victim said that three masked men opened fire on Seth when he was sitting in his car.
