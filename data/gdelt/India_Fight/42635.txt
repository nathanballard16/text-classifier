One killed in oil tanker, SUV collision

RAJKOT: A person was killed and one injured when a oil tanker collided with a SUV near Aadesar village in Rapar taluka of Kutch on Saturday.According to police, the tanker which was coming from Gandhidham collided head-on with an SUV coming from the opposite direction. In the accident , the tanker helper, Majid Malkan (25), died on the spot.In the accident, both vehicles overturned and injuring a motorbike rider, who is under treatment at Radhanpur hospital.
