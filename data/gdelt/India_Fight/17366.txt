Indian troops martyr three more Kashmiris in IoK

Indian forces conducted raids on homes in Shopian and Hindwara districts. Photo: File

SRINAGAR: Indian troops martyred three Kashmiris in occupied Kashmir's Shopian and Hindwara districts on Thursday.

Indian forces conducted raids on homes in Shopian and Hindwara districts and opened fired killing three.

As the news of the killings spread, several took to the streets to protest against Indian state terrorism.

Anti-India sentiment runs deep in occupied Kashmir, which in recent years has seen repeated public protests against Indian rule.

Nearly 70,000 people, mostly civilians, have been killed in Indian military crackdowns in the occupied valley.
