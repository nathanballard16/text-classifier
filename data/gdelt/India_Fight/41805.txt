Friend murders another friend

Hyderabad: In Kukatpally Housing Board police station limits, a serious case of murder took place.

According to the report of the police, the accused Hanumanth was the friend of M. Satish Babu (35), resident of Moosapet.

Hanumath killed Satish Babu using a lethal weapon. The reason of the murder is reported to be money transaction. The accused and the victim, both are software engineers and classmates.

Police registered a case and started investigation.
