Woman’s body found tied to a pole in Vadodara

VADODARA: A 22-year-old woman was found dead near Darbar Chowkdi in Manjalpur in the city on Sunday morning. The body was found tied to an electric pole with a dupatta The woman has been identified as Varsha Mohaniya . “There are no injury marks on the body, but a post-mortem has been conducted to know the exact cause of death,” said a cop, adding that a mobile phone found near the body had around 30 calls made to a same number. Police will obtain call detail records.Deceased lived in a hut near the spot where her body was found. She had moved to the city two months ago from Dahod in search of work. She recently got engaged to a boy from her village. Cops registered a case of accidental death.
