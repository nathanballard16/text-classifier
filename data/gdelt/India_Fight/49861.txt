Murshidabad youth hacked to death in Jharkhand

Kolkata: As the state was crawling back to normalcy after five Bengal labourers were killed in a gruesome terror attack in Jammu and Kashmir, yet another case of a Murshidabad youth came to light who was allegedly hacked to death in Jharkhand. According to sources, the deceased has been identified as Israil Sheikh (32) of Bilpara at Raghunathganj in Murshidabad who went to the neighbouring state along with few others for work. MLA of Raghunathganj Md Akhrujjaman said he had got in touch with the concerned police station in Jharkhand about the incident. The body will soon reach Murshidabad by road. Though three suspects have been arrested, it is still unknown why Sheikh was killed.
