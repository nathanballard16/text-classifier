‘Kabir Singh’ girl Kiara Advani back in Delhi University for new shoot

New Delhi: After shooting for the recently released film ‘Kabir Singh’ at various colleges of the Delhi University, actress Kiara Advani is back in the national capital to shoot for her next film.

Without revealing much details about the movie, Kiara Sunday took to Instagram Stories and wrote: “Memories… Miranda House college. Had shot here for ‘Kabir Singh’ and now back here for the next one.”

Along with it, she shared a photograph of the college’s entrance.
