Jaunpur SP neta shot by six masked men

VARANASI: A Samajwadi Party leader, Lalji Yadav (45) was shot dead by six unidentified men in Sarai Khwaza area of Jaunpur on Friday.SP president Akhilesh Yadav has condemned the murder. The party has decided to conduct its own probe into the incident.Lalji was going to Jaunpur in his SUV. His vehicle slowed down at a speed-breaker when six masked men on three motorcycles intercepted him and opened fire. According to eyewitnesses, the killers left the place only after they were sure that Lalji had died. Lalji, who worked as a contractor in the district, had several criminal cases registered against him, police added.SHO Ramesh Kumar Yadav said the killing of Lalji, a SP leader, was being probed from different angles. An FIR has been lodged against unidentified persons.
