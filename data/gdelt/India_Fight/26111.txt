Leopard attacks labourer in Amreli village

RAJKOT: A leopard attacked a 20-year-old labourer in Dudhala village that falls under the Dalkhania range of Gir forest in Amreli district Friday late night.According to forest officials, Mahavir Gadhiya, a native of Bhilwada in Rajasthan was attacked by leopard when he was sleeping in an open hut The leopard ran away after Gadhiya began screaming for help . He sustained several injuries on the body and was shifted to Dhari hospital where his condition is said to be stable.
