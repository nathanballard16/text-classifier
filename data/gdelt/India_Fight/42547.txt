Desi ghee sample seized

Ludhiana: A team of officials from the health department raided premises of a departmental store in Jagraon area of Friday and seized 144 kg of desi ghee. The samples were later sent to a laboratory for testing. The premises were raided on suspicion of adulterated dairy products being sold.

District health officer, Andesh Kang, told that they were receiving complaints of adulterated milk item being sold in several areas of Jagraon. “We have sealed several cartoons of desi hee weighing approximately 144kg and worth around Rs 60,000,” said the official. He said if the reports from the laboratory suggested that the dairy products, especially ghee was adulterated then the owners would have to pay a penalty of Rs 13 lakh and their license too might be cancelled.
