Punjab: Fire breaks out at Ludhiana’s Trimurti Hosiery Mills

A massive fire broke out on Wednesday at Trimurti Hosiery Mills in Ludhiana’s Sunder Nagar.

Fire tenders have been rushed to the spot to douse the flames.

According to the sources, the rescue operation is currently underway.

Meanwhile, no casualties have been reported in the incident.

Further details are awaited.
