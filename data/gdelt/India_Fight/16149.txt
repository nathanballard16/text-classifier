After tiff, CRPF man in J&K kills 3 colleagues

JAMMU: A CRPF jawan shot dead three of his colleagues late on Wednesday evening inside a camp in Udhampur district of Jammu & Kashmir.“Three CRPF men were killed when their colleague opened fire in a fit of rage ,” said police. Constable Ajit Singh shot the three men dead in the CRPF camp at Battal area of Udhampur, SHO Vijay Singh Choudhary said.“The incident took place after the jawan got into a verbal duel with his colleagues over some issue,” police added. “The assailant shot himself as well but survived,” said MK Sinha, IGP Jammu range. Constable Singh was taken to Military Hospital in Udhampur in a critical condition.
