Enraged over love marriage man shot sister s father in law

New Delhi: Enraged over their love marriage, a 21-year-old man shot his sister's father in law in North West Delhi on Wednesday. The injured victim along with his younger son chased the accused on their bike and later caught him and his friend. "The investigating officer (IO) reached the hospital and collected the MLCs of both the injured persons identified as Inderjeet and his son Ved Prakash. Ved Prakash was having abrasions only and was discharged. Meanwhile, police staff of NPL also came there and caught hold of both the accused persons.
