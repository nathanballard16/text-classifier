Woman found murdered in Gondal

RAJKOT: A 30-year-old woman was found murdered under the bridge over a stream on Vhora-Kotada Road in Gondal of Rajkot district on Wednesday evening.The deceased was identified as Gita Chudasama , a resident of Rajnagar locality of Gondal.“The body was found half-naked. It bore several stabbing wounds. Her head appeared to have been smashed with a stone. Chudasama had left her home on Tuesday evening. Her husband Bharatsinh lodged missing complaint as she had not returned home till Wednesday morning,” said H M Jadeja , Gondal deputy superintendent of police.Gondal police have booked unidentified assailants, whose numbers are also unknown, for the crime and sought the help of forensic science laboratory.
