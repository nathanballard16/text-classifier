Fire breaks out in paediatric ward of SSG Hospital at Vadodara; all children safely evacuated

Gujarat hospital fire | Photo Credit: ANI

Vadodara: A fire engulfed the paediatric ward of a hospital in Gujarat’s Vadodara on Tuesday. As per reports, the fire broke out in the Shree Sir Sayaji General (SSG) Hospital here. As per details available, all the children were safely evacuated from the shifted paediatric ward.

Fire-fighting operations were underway when the reports last came in.
