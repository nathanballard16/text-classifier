2 contractors booked for attack on engr

Allahabad: Two contractors were booked for assaulting an assistant engineer of irrigation department inside his office in Civil Lines area of Pratapgarh on Friday afternoon.Inspector, Kotwali, Surendra Kumar said the incident took place around 2:30pm when contractors Narendra Singh and Shani Singh of Paltan Bazar reached the office of assistant engineer Munna Lal and pressured him to clear their dues for removal of silt in several areas without submitting documents and bills.The assistant engineer said when he asked the contractors to submit documents and bills for payment, they manhandled him and damaged computers and other articles in his office.They created ruckus for over half an hour and even threatened Munna Lal of dire consequences. They escaped when other employees of the department started assembling there.Acting on Lal’s complaint, police lodged an FIR against the two contractors under sections 323 (hurting), 353 (assault or criminal force to deter a public servant from discharging his duty), 504 (insult with intent to provoke breach of peace), 506 (criminal intimidation) and 427 (causing damage) of the Indian Penal Code.The inspector said police teams were carrying out raids to arrest the accused contractors.
