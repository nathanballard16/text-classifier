New Delhi:In the wake of coward terror arrack in Pulwama, a ceramic factory in Morbi district of Gujarat has found a unique way to protest against neighbouring country Pakistan. The company started manufacturing tiles with the inscription 'Pakistan Murdabad'

Company manufactures the tiles which feature Pakistan's national flag in the background and are inscribed with ‘Pakistan Murdabad'. According to reports tiles will be used for the construction of public toilets in protest against Pakistan supporting terrorist groups.

Going viral : Morbi ceramic industry are producing tiles with print “Pakistan Murdabad” which will be distributed for free of cost for use in construction of public toilets.
