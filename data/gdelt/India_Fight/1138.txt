Elephant chased out of town after killing two men

This the dramatic moment officials chased a huge elephant out of town after the herd trampled two men to death.

The footage above shows forest workers confronting the huge animal in the street, with sirens blaring, and running it out of town along empty roads.

Locals say a herd of wild elephants had been spotted around the town of Surajpur in Chhattisgarh, India, and two men were killed in separate incidents on Friday.
