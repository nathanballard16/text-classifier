Allahabad: Woman killed, two hurt in mysterious blast

ALLAHABAD: A 24-year-old woman was killed and two others sustained injuries in a mysterious blast that took place inside a house at Koraon block under the limits of Koraon police station on Sunday.The blast took place inside the house of Illiyas. His father Haneef is a licensed cracker manufacturer and he might have had hide explosives underground, SSP Allahabad A S Pankaj said.Forensic experts and personnel of bomb-defusing team were probing the case.Due to the explosion, two neighbours were also injured and some portion of their houses got damaged.
