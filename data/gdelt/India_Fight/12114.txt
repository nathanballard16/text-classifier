Civilians die in India, Pakistan clashes

(MENAFN) Indian police declared hat 3 civilians have died as many were wounded in cross-border clashes between Indian and Pakistani soldiers on Line of Control (LoC) dividing the disputed Kashmir region.



A senior police official confirmed that The battle erupted Friday evening and kept going for many hours in frontier Poonch district, about 180 km southwest of Srinagar city, the summer capital of Indian-controlled Kashmir.



Locals revealed that the intense clashes sparked fear in nearby villages.



Meanwhile, the Pakistani army announced that two civilians have died as two more were wounded along the LoC in the past 24 hours.
