Agra: Neighbour rapes minor, bludgeons her to death; arrested after four-day chase

The accused was booked under relevant sections of the Indian Penal Code (Representative Image). | Photo Credit: Getty Images

Agra: After a four-day-long struggle, the police nabbed a man accused of raping and murdering a minor. The alleged incident took place in the Firozabad district of Uttar Pradesh. The accused was identified as a daily wage worker and he hails from the Madhai Fatehpur village of Shikohabad.

The accused was identified as Virendra Baghel. The accused was allegedly living on rent in Sant Nagar. The area falls under the jurisdiction of the Linepar police station.

The accused was allegedly living with a married woman who had left her husband's house. It was alleged that the accused attacked the victim with a brick and hit her till she passed away. Following the accused's confession, the police located the brick used by him.

While talking to the Times of India, the station house officer of Linepar, Sanjay Singh stated that the deceased was the neighbour of the accused. The accused allegedly tempted the victim using a chocolate and took her to a dilapidated building in the Sofipur village. It was there that the accused raped the minor and bludgeoned her.

The body of the victim was found a day after the incident. The body was found by the Basai Mohammadpur police. The parents of the victim identified the face of the victim. The parents alleged that they had last seen their daughter with the accused.

On being caught, the accused allegedly confessed to his crime. The accused was booked under relevant sections of the Indian Penal Code (IPC) and the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences (POCSO) Act.
