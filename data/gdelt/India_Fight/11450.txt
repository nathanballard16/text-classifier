UP: Property dealer shot dead at wedding function

Lucknow

pti-PTI

Etah (UP), Mar 1: A property dealer was allegedly shot dead at a wedding function by two unidentified persons in a village here, police said Friday.

Atul Tiwari, 26, was attacked on Thursday night at Gumanpur village in Rizor area, they said.

The body was sent for post-mortem and a probe on in the matter. No arrests have been made so far, the police said.
