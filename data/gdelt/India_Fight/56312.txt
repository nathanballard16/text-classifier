20 Thai women found working in spa illegally

SURAT: A team of prevention of crime branch (PCB) raided a dozen spas in Rahul Raj Mall on Gaurav Path on Tuesday and found 20 Thai women working there without work permit. The raid that started in the evening continued till late night.“We came to know that foreign nationals working in these spas. All of them had come on visitor visa and are not permitted to work,” said Sanjay Bhatia, police inspector, PCB. He said that owners and managers of the spas will be arrested for employing foreign nationals who do not have work permit. The women found employed in the spas will be made witness in the case, police added.The details of the women will be sent to foreign regional officer under the ministry of external affairs which will decide further actions against the women. The women are likely to be deported to Thailand
