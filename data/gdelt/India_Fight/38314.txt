Mining Department officials attacked

Tribune News ServiceLudhiana, August 7A team of the Mining Department was attacked in the wee hours of Wednesday when they were going to lodge a complaint at the Ladhowal police station after stopping illegal mining in fields of Khark village. Following a tip-off about illegal mining in the fields of Khark village, located on the Ladhowal-Hambran road, the Mining Department SDO, Balwinder Singh, JE Manpreet Singh and driver Tek Chand had gone to Khark village after midnight. An official of the Mining Department said the fields, where illegal mining was going on, belonged to the relative of a police officer. After the attack mining officials reached the Ludhiana Civil Hospital for treatment. The mining officer, Ludhiana, Harjot Singh, said the team had gone to check illegal mining in the fields.
