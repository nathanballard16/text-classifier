Karnataka: I-T conducts raid on BJP leader Dandin's properties in Gadag

HUBBALLI: Income Tax department officials have conducted raids on BJP leader and secretary of Kanakadas Education Society Ravi Dandin 's house and properties on Wednesday night in Gadag The officials conducted raid on hotels, bar and restaurants run by Dandin.Sources said that IT offiicials from Goa and Bengaluru conducted simultaneous raids on his properties. It is said that they have seized important documents related illegal properties he owns and evasion of taxes.Ravi Dandin is an influential leader in Gadag and he is running Kanakadas and Kalidas Education Society in Gadag city.He was one of the aspirant to contest the up coming West Graduate constituency election for Legislative council.
