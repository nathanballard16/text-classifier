Dabholkar-Pansare murder case: HC puts CM in the dock over tardy probe

Mumbai/Nagpur: The Bombay High Court on Wednesday expressed its displeasure over tardy investigation into murder case of rationalists Dr Narendra Dabholkar and Govind Pansare. The court also put the Chief Minister Devendra Fadnavis into the dock by posing him pointed questions. Are you Chief Minister of the State or a leader of a party,” the High Court questioned Fadnavis. The High Court rapped the Chief Minister and the State Government during the hearing held on Wednesday in the murder case of Dr Dabholkar and Pansare. Petitions filed by the family members of Dabholkar and Pansare sought court supervision in the probes conducted by the central and state agencies.
