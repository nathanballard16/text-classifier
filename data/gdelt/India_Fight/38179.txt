Dabangg 3: Salman Khan Issues A Strict RULE For All The Members On The Sets!

Bollywood Superstar Salman Khan who was last seen on big screen opposite Katrina Kaif, in ‘Bharat’ is all busy these days shooting for his next, ‘Dabangg 3‘. The film which went on floors recently has been in talks all over from a very long time.

Advertisement

The film basically will be a prequel of ‘Dabangg’ and ‘Dabangg 2‘, and will have Salman playing younger and older versions of his character, Chulbul Pandey.

The latest news related to the film is that the superstar has strictly asked the entire cast and crew of the films to keep away mobile phones from the sets of ‘Dabangg 3‘, reports TOI.

The no mobile phone on the sets of the film rule applied by Salman Khan is valid on every member of the film.

This step was taken by Salman to prevent the characters and looks of the actors from getting leaked.

Advertisement

Talking further about the film, apart from Salman, the film also has Sonakshi Sinha along with veteran actor Mahesh Manjrekar’s daughter Saiee Manjrekar in lead. Saiee will be making her grand debut with this big-budget film opposite Salman.

Salman’s brother Arbaaz Khan & Kannada superstar Sudeep will also be seen in major roles in the film.

‘Dabangg 3‘, is being helmed by Prabhudheva and is slated for its release on 20th December 2019.
