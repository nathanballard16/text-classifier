Police to arrest Kulwant in Choti Sardarni?

MUMBAI: Loyal viewers of Colors' popular show Choti Sardarni will witness interesting drama in the upcoming episode.

It was earlier seen that Kulwant kills Manav and throws him in the river.

Meher runs to stop Kulwant but fails to reach in time.

Meher and Manav get separated forever by Kulwant, who wants Meher to get married to Sarabjit.

Kulwant forces Meher to marry Sarabjit, and she just to save her unborn child.

In a turn of events, the police interrogate Kulwant about Manav's murder.

It will be interesting to see whether the police learn the truth and arrest her.
