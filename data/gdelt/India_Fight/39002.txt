Fire breaks out at Gandhi Nagar Market in Delhi; 10 fire tenders rushed

A major fire broke out at the Gandhi Nagar Market area in the national capital on Tuesday morning. At least 10 fire tenders were rushed to the spot, reported news agency ANI.

The market near Seelampur and Shahdara areas of eastern part of Delhi is home to several clothes and fabric wholesalers. There were no casualties till last report came in.
