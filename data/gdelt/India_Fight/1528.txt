Criminal gunned down in encounter procured SIM cards on false names

Bareilly: Investigations have revealed that two criminals who were gunned down earlier this week had procured SIM cards from Rampur and Delhi on false IDs. Another SIM was purchased from Rampur. Within 10 hours of the incident, police had gunned down two accused and recovered the stolen money. Refusing to divulge details, he added, “It will not be appropriate to give details about absconding accused right now. Both SIM cards were issued on different IDs other than of the deceased criminals,” SSP G Muniraj said.The police were confident that they would nab the three criminals who had escaped from the encounter site.
