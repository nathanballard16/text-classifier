Gutka worth Rs. 2,10,000 seized in Hyderabad

Hyderabad: A 35-year-old man was arrested for possessing 50,835 packets of gutka or processed tobacco on Monday.

A Special Operations Team raided a house in H.B Colony, Moula Ali, and found the contraband.

According to police sources, the SOT have seized gutka pouches of various brands worth Rs 2.10 lakh.

The sources said, “The accused, Santosh Kumar, 35, a resident of H.B Colony was trading in tobacco products from his house.”
