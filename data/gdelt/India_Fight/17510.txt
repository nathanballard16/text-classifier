Delhi: Gangster wanted in multiple cases arrested Delhi: Gangster wanted in multiple cases arrested

An alleged member of the Neeraj Bawana gang, carrying a reward of Rs 1 lakh, was arrested from Rohini. Police said the accused, Dushyant alias Monu Bajitpur (35), was wanted in several cases, including the recent killings in alleged gang wars in Outer Delhi.
