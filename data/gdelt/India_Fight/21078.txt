Watch: Sadhvi Pragya Thakur breaks down in tears alleging torture by jail officials

#WATCH: Alleging torture by jail officials, Sadhvi Pragya Singh Thakur, BJP Lok Sabha candidate from Bhopal, breaks down while addressing the party workers pic.twitter.com/UVUomvmJZ2 — ANI (@ANI) April 18, 2019

BHOPAL: BJP candidate from Madhya Pradesh's Bhopal parliamentary constituency, Sadhvi Pragya Singh Thakur on Thursday claimed she was tortured "day and night" by jail officials during police custody. She alleged she was kept in "illegal" custody for 13 days during an interrogation, where she was "beaten up brutally" and "pressurised" to confess to false charges against her. Thakur, who was addressing party workers here, broke down in tears and claimed she was verbally abused by the cops.Thakur, an accused in the 2008 Malegaon blasts case, was on Wednesday fielded by the BJP from Bhopal Lok Sabha seat against Congress heavyweight and former Madhya Pradesh chief minister Digvijay Singh.
