Delhi: Two criminals injured in shootout with police in Rohini

Two criminals were injured in a shootout between them and the Delhi Police on Sunday night. The encounter broke out in Rohini area of Delhi.

The criminals have been identified as Akash and Karan Kapoor. Akash (22) is an active snatcher and has 17 cases filed against him. Twenty-nine-year-old Karan Kapoor is also an active snatcher and has 25 cases against him.

As per the reliable information, the two criminals had recently committed snatching incidents.

At around 9:10 pm, the duo was spotted on a road between Pansali and Sector 32, Rohini. The police team tried to stop them but they tried to run away. The duo even fired upon the police party.

In retaliation, the Delhi Police also fired upon them. In the shootout, Karan sustained a bullet injury on his left leg whereas Akash sustained an injury on his right leg.
