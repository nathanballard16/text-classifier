India: 2 Naxals die in clashes with police

(MENAFN) Police declared that two Naxals have died in clashes with policemen in central Indian state of Chattisgarh.



The clashes erupted inside the forests of Pamed area in Bijapur district, about 440 km south of Raipur, the capital city of Chhattisgarh.



A police official posted in Bijapur confirmed: "Today a gunfight broke out between Naxals and joint contingents of district police and Greyhound Force here inside Pamed forests of Kavargatta area."



"Two Naxals were killed in the anti-Naxal operation and two country made weapons have been recovered from the spot."
