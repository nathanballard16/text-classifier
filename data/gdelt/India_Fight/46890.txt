Man arrested for plotting mom s killing

New Delhi: The Delhi Police on Thursday said that they have arrested a person who had hired killers to commit the robbery as well as murder of his mother in Outer Delhi's Paschim Vihar area. According to DCP (outer) A Koan on October 6, two unknown persons entered the house of complainant forcefully and tried to rob the complainant. The statement of the victim was recorded on which a case under sections 393/452/34 of IPC was registered. DCP further added that on the disclosure of the JCL the following accused were arrested. They further disclosed that one Ansh Dhingra hired the killers to commit the robbery as well as murder of his mother.
