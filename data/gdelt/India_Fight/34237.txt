Diamond worker murdered in Sihor

RAJKOT: A diamond worker was murdered by unidentified assailants whose numbers are yet to be ascertained in Songadh village of Sihor taluka in Bhavnagar district on Thursday.Police said the deceased, identified as Ramji Kantariya (28) was going on his bike when the assailants waylaid him near Mota Suraka village and fled after killing him with sharp edged weapons.“Kantariya bore severe injury marks on his head that appeared to have been caused by sharp-edged weapons. We are examining the details of his call records to find out with whom he had spoken last with to zero in on the suspects. That apart, we are also probing whether he had any enmity with anyone,” said an official from Sihor police station.
