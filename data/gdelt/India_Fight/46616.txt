2nd husband of 'killer' may be made approver

KOCHI: The crime branch is undecided if Shaju Skaria , the second husband of prime accused Jolly, should be made an accused or approver in the Kerala family murder case. He was summoned to Vadakara district police office on Monday and questioned for hours.Shaju has been asked to appear before the investigating team whenever necessary.Police may also seek the assistance of forensic laboratories abroad to conduct 'trace analysis' of potassium cyanide on bodies exhumed in the case related to alleged serial murders at Koodathayi in Kozhikode State police chief Loknath Behera told reporters that the investigation team would seek the court's permission in this regard. He also said more officers and experts would be included in the investigation team as the case demanded more circumstantial evidence.
