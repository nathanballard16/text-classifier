Delhi: Minor kills 17-year-old for bullying, beating; arrested from Bhalswa area

The accused is also a 17-year-old boy from whom the police have recovered a country-made pistol and one empty cartridge. | Photo Credit: Getty Images

New Delhi: In an appalling case reported from the national capital, a minor has been arrested for killing a 17-year-old boy in outer Delhi's Bhalswa Dairy area.

The police officials told news agency ANI that the boy was killed in Shardhanand Colony area by the accused and one more person on Thursday. The police had registered a case under section 302 (punishment for murder) of the India Penal Code. Reportedly, a five-member police team led by inspector Vishesh Kumar was curated to nab the accused and probe the case.

Meanwhile, the police scanned the CCTV footages of the area and interrogated the locals after which they apprehended the juvenile in the case. The accused is also a 17-year-old boy from whom the police have recovered a country-made pistol and one empty cartridge.

The ANI report mentioned that during the preliminary interrogation, the accused confessed that he killed the minor with the help of one more person. The accused minor revealed to the police that the deceased boy used to beat and bully him. He even beat him up two days ago.

However, the police are trying to nab the other accused in the case.
