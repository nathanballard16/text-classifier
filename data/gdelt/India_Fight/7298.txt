Rostec CEO says bureaucracy delaying delivery of helicopters to India

By Stanley CarvalhoABU DHABI (Reuters) - Russia is battling bureaucracy and waiting for some formalities to be completed before it delivers 60 military helicopters to India, the head of state-owned giant Rostec said on Monday. India and Russia signed an agreement in 2016 to jointly manufacture 200 KA-226T helicopters for the Indian Armed Forces.
