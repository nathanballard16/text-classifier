Bihar: Journalist’s son shot dead in Nalanda, investigation underway

Follow us on Image Source : ANI Image

Son of a journalist was shot dead by unidentified miscreants in Bihar's Nalanda district, on Sunday.

“There’s bleeding from his eyes. No injury visible on the body except near eyes. Cause of death will be known after post-mortem,” ANI quoted Nilesh Kumar, SP Nalanda, as saying.

An investigation was underway.
