Vishal injured on the sets on Sundar C film!

Actor Vishal who recently started shooting for Sundar C’s film in Cappadocia, Turkey was severely injured while riding All-terrain vehicle (ATV).

The actor himself rode the ATV bike and got injured while performing an action scene choreographed by stunt master Anbariv. Thanks to the local crew, the actor was immediately rushed to the hospital and given proper treatment.





Produced by Trident Arts, Tamannaah and Aishwarya Lekshmi are the two heroines in the film for which Yuvan Shankar Raja has been roped in to compose music.





Actor Vishal will return back to Chennai to cast his vote and he will also look after the release of his commercial action entertainer ‘Ayogya’ which is scheduled to hit the screens on May 10.
