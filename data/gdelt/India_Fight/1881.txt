West Bengal: Criminal shot dead in Sodepur, say police

A 47-year-old man was shot dead by unidentified assailants in his flat at Sodepur in North 24 Parganas on Sunday night, police said. Then they went to Ramua’s flat and allegedly shot him dead. As per sources, Ramua was originally from Howrah but Sodepur was his current hideout. However, we have come to know that the killers first knocked on the door of Ramua’s wife’s flat. The deceased identified as Rammurti Diyar alias Ramua, had a criminal background, sources said.
