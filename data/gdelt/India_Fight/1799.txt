India,Pakistan exchange fire along LoC in Poonch

Srinagar: The armies of India and Pakistan Monday exchanged gunfire along the line of control (LoC) in Poonch district of Jammu and Kashmir.

As per reports the exchange of fire took place in Khari Karmara area of Poonch district along LOC.

Adding that there is no report of causality in the exchange of fire.
