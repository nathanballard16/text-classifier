Man killed in accident with truck

LUDHIANA: A 50-year-old man was killed after he was crushed by a construction truck of the National Highways Authority of India (NHAI) outside Gate 2 of Punjab Agricultural University (PAU) on Tuesday evening.The truck was engaged in the construction of a flyover on Ferozepur Road.Darshan Singh, 50, of Lalton was riding a motorcycle when he was hit by the truck and then crushed under its wheels.Bystanders raised the alarm and stopped the truck. Inspector Paramdeep Singh, SHO of PAU police station, said the kin of deceased had been called to record statement. “The FIR is being registered and accused has also been apprehended,” he said.
