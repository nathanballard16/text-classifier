Deepika Padukone remembers her character Veronica as Saif Ali Khan & Diana starrer Cocktail completes 7 years

Back in 2012, Deepika Padukone, Saif Ali Khan and Diana Penty starrer Cocktail had released and it surely was liked by all. The film was based on three characters, Veronica played by Deepika, Gautam which was portrayed by Saif and Meera essayed by Diana. The film traced the story of three friends who eventually fall for each other and Deepika’s role of Veronica was bold, fun-loving and full of life. The film was helmed by Homi Adajania and was produced by Saif Ali Khan and Dinesh Vijan.

Today, the film completed 7 years and Deepika took to her Instagram story and shared the poster of the movie along with a video of her holding the clap board during the filming of a scene. Deepika also remembered her character, Veronica from the film and penned a heartfelt note for the same. The 33-year-old actor also mentioned that she missed the character and that it was hard for her to believe that it had already been 7 years.
