Delhi: DTC employee shot dead in front of his 5-year-old son

Follow us on Image Source : ANI Image

A Delhi Transport Corporation (DTC) employee was shot dead in front of his 5-year-old son in New Usmanpur area of the national capital on Sunday night.

Akibuddin (40), shot dead from a point-blank range. The incident happened around 11:45 pm.

The reason for the crime was not known. A police investigation was underway.
