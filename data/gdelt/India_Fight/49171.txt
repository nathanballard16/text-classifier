Fire Breaks Out at a Market Near Patna Junction

Patna: A massive fire broke out in a market near Patna Junction on late Saturday night destroying many shops and other properties to the tune of several crore rupees.

Because of the timing of the fire, no one was said to be hurt in the incident.

As reported, the fire broke out at a market near GPO Roundabout under Kotwali police station. The market is adjacent to a number of huts made of thatched roof that were also gutted in the fire.

Soon a number of fire tenders arrived at the spot and after battling the blaze for nearly two hours, brought the situation under control.

As always, electrical short circuit is being blamed for the fire.
