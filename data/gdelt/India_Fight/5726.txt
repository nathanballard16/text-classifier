WHAT? Salman Khan and Katrina Kaif will be celebrating Valentine’s Day doing THIS together

It’s that time of the year again when everything around you will be filled with love. Yes, it’s Valentine’s week and tomorrow is Valentine’s Day. Many fans would love to know what their favourite superstars will be doing on Valentine’s Day. In Bollywood, many of the stars who are committed or married might be celebrating their day with their loved one. However, some of the superstars who are single might be working. Speaking of this, stars of the upcoming film Bharat, Salman Khan and Katrina Kaif will be busy on Valentine’s Day as well.



As per a report, both Salman and Katrina will be having a working Valentine’s Day. They both will be shooting a sequence for their upcoming film Bharat. As per a report published in Mumbai Mirror, “A day long shoot on February 14, Valentine’s Day, will see Salman and Katrina reuniting with Jackie (Shroff), Tabu and Sunil (Grover) for a dramatic sequence. Salman and Katrina recently shot an elaborate wedding song which was preceded by another big song featuring Salman and Disha.”



Well, looks like the Bhaijaan of Bollywood is in the mood to finish of Bharat soon. Talking about the film, the movie is based on a South-Korean film, An Ode To My Father. The movie stars Disha Patani, Tabu, Jackie Shroff, Sunil Grover and Nora Fatehi in important roles along with Salman Khan and Katrina Kaif in the lead.

Bharat is helmed by Ali Abbas Zafar and has been shot in parts of Abu Dhabi, Punjab and Delhi. Now, the final schedule is being shot in film city Mumbai where a big set costing Rs 10 crores has been erected and will be destroyed as a part of the climax. Well, this year Eid plans for all Salman Khan fans are completely sorted as Bharat will release on June 5, 2019.
