Happy Birthday Nagarjuna: The superstar to celebrate his 60th birthday with Naga Chaitanya and Samantha Akkineni in Spain : Bollywood News

Telugu superstar Nagarjuna is celebrating his 60th birthday today, August 29 and it seems like he has big plans to celebrate his day. The actor is currently in Ibiza, Spain, along with his family and close friends. While his short vacation will end soon, it plans to celebrate it with his sons Akhil Akkineni, Naga Chaitanya and daughter-in-law Samantha Prabhu Akkineni.

Nagarjuna will be having an intimate celebration with his family and close friends around. With that being said, the actor will be returning to India to shoot for Bigg Boss Telugu. Samantha Prabhu has been updating her social media with her vacation photos and it seems like the family is enjoying their trip to Ibiza.

On the work front, Nagarjuna was last seen in Manmadhudu 2 alongside Rakul Preet Singh. He will also be returning to Hindi cinema with Brahmastra alongside Alia Bhatt, Ranbir Kapoor, Dimple Kapadia and Mouni Roy. The film will be releasing in summer 2020.
