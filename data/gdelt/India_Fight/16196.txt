Jammu and Kashmir: Army soldier martyred as Pakistan violates ceasefire along LoC in Rajouri

Follow us on Jammu and Kashmir: Army soldier killed as Pakistan violates ceasefire along LoC in Rajouri

An Army soldier was killed when Pakistan violated the ceasefire by heavily shelling forward areas and posts along the Line of Control with mortar bombs in Jammu and Kashmir's Rajouri district Thursday, officials said.

Pakistani troops violated the ceasefire in Keri belt of Sunderbani sector this morning, they said.

One soldier was was killed in Pakistani firing, they added.

Pakistani troops have violated the ceasefire over 110 times along the LoC since January.
