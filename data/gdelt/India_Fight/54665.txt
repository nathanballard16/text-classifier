One dead 3 hurt in 3 fire incidents

Kolkata: A woman died and a few people suffered minor burn injuries in the city on Sunday. She was rushed to SSKM hospital where she was declared brought dead. At 3.30 am, a fire broke out in a road side toy shop at R.B.Avenue under Gariahat Police Station area. While no report of human injury was reported, the toy shop was damaged. According to sources, no one was reported during the fire accident.
