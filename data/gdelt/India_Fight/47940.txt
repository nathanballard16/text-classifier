IS terrorists had plans to kill Hindu Samaj Party leader Kamlesh Tiwari

IS terrorists, in their 2017 interrogation, said they were asked to kill Kamlesh Tiwari | File photo from AajTak

Hindu Samaj Party and Hindu Mahasabha leader Kamlesh Tiwari was attacked by some miscreants in broad daylight on Friday in Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh. Kamlesh Tiwari was immediately rushed to a trauma centre where he succumbed to his injuries.

Kamlesh Tiwari was also on target of Islamic State (IS) terrorists.

In 2017, Gujarat Anti-Terrorism Squad (ATS) arrested Ubaid Mirza and Qasim of the IS. Apart from Gujarat ATS, central agency also interrogated the terrorists.

Both the IS terrorists had mentioned of Kamlesh Tiwari. Ubaid and Qasim, in their interrogation, said that they were asked to kill Kamlesh Tiwari.

Gujarat ATS had filed a chargesheet in which the conspiracy to kill Kamlesh Tiwari was revealed.

Kamlesh Tiwari hit the national headline in December 2015, when he made objectionable remarks about Prophet Mohammad.

STABBED 15 TIMES WITH KNIFE

It is learnt that saffron-clad attackers came into Kamlesh Tiwari's office in Khurshid Bagh area carrying knife and country-made pistol in a box of sweets.

The attackers took out from the box of sweets the knife and slit Kamlesh Tiwari's throat with it. The entire incident was captured by a CCTV camera.

The pistol recovered also had a bullet stuck in in the pistol.

According to the CCTV camera footage, the attackers stabbed Kamlesh Tiwari more than 15 times and fled from the spot.
