Kolkata: Protest over patient refusal





KOLKATA: Locals on Monday night blocked Gobinda Auddy Road in Chetla alleging two government hospitals of denying medical treatment to Bappi Das (43), a resident of Chetla. Kolkata Municipal Corporation mayor Firhad Hakim, who is the councillor from the ward, intervened and Das was taken to a private hospital off EM Bypass.According to relatives, Das, a driver by professsion, has been suffering from cardiac problems. "For two weeks, we have been shuttling between two hospitals. We resorted to the road blockade out of desperation. We are thankful that Hakim stepped in and helped us," said a relative.
