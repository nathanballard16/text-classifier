Caught on cam: Miscreants open fire near Delhi’s Karol Bagh

New Delhi, Jan 19 (ANI): A group of miscreants opened fire near Karol Bagh area in New Delhi. Miscreants covered their face during aerial firing. The incident was captured on CCTV. Police are analyzing CCTV footage to nab the miscreants.
