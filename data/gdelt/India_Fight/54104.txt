Medical and sales representatives at a rally in Bengaluru to demand implementation of a sales promotion employees' law

Synopsis

Strangled by steep targets and manipulative doctors, the medical representative’s profession is at its lowest ebb. In the absence of a code of ethical marketing practices, they are getting sucked into a vicious cycle of corruption involving drug makers and doctors. High time the government stepped in to ease the pressure on these professionals and restored respect to their trade.
