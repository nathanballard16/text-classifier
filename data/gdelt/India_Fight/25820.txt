Allahabad University student’s murder: Fourth accused held

ALLAHABAD: More than a month after the former Allahabad University student leader was shot dead, Special Task Force ( STF ) arrested another accused in the Rohit Shukla murder case on Thursday. The arrested youth Abhishek Yadav alias Navneet, is among the six accused named in the FIR lodged in connection with the murder case.Police have already arrested two of the accused, while the key accused in the case Adarsh Tripathi surrendered in the court a few days back.STF officials said they received a tip off on Thursday morning about Yadav’s whereabouts and he was later arrested from a bus stand while trying to flee the city.
