Woman cop injured during Priyanka Gandhi’s roadshow

Follow us on Image Source : PTI/FILE Priyanka Gandhi

During a roadshow of Congress general secretary Priyanka Gandhi, on Wednesday, a woman constable was injured while discharging her duty in the neighbouring Mahoba district of Uttar Pradesh.

City Circle Officer Jatashankar Rai said Constable Khushi was injured when the front wheel of a vehicle in the Congress leader's roadshow ran over her feet, reported PTI.

The woman constable was admitted to the district hospital.

Khushi is posted with Local Intelligence Unit of the UP police.
