Alia Bhatt enjoys a lazy day off with her pet & shares an adorable photo; writes ‘like mother like cat’

Bollywood actors are proud pet parents. Some of them have cats while others are fonds of dogs and are often seen chilling with them at home. Speaking of this, Alia Bhatt is a cat person and is a proud pet parent to a white cat. Often Alia shares photos of her cat while she is chilling at her home in Mumbai. Recently, Alia returned from her Los Angeles trip and has been enjoying some days off before shooting at home. Yesterday, Alia was seen chilling with her sister Shaheen at home.

Today, Alia took to Instagram to share an adorable picture with her cat. While Alia is usually a workaholic, whenever she gets time off she spends time with her cat. In the photo, the Brahmastra star can be seen lying down on her bed next to her cat. With her sister’s book on her hand and her adorable cat by her side, Alia looked at peace. Clad in a white and blue floral outfit, the Raazi star posed for a cute picture.

Alia captioned the photo as, “like mother like cat.” The Raazi actress often likes to spend time at home with her pet and had shared it one of her Vlogs as well. Meanwhile, on the work front, Alia has quite a number of projects in her kitty. She was shooting for Mahesh Bhatt’s Sadak 2 before heading to LA. Soon, she will resume shooting for Brahmastra with Ranbir Kapoor. It also stars Amitabh Bachchan, Dimple Kapadia, Nagarjuna Akkineni and Mouni Roy. It is slated to release next year. Apart from this, Alia also has Sanjay Leela Bhansali’s Gangubai Kathiawadi and Karan Johar’s Takht.
