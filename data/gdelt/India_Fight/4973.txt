Photos: Arjun Kapoor, Kriti Sanon jet off to Jaipur for the next schedule of 'Panipat'

Arjun Kapoor and Kriti Sanon are teaming up for the first time for the film titled 'Panipat'. This film is one of the most anticipated movies of the year. Panipat is a period drama and fans are really excited to see this fresh pairing for the first time on big screen. Recently, Kriti and Arjun were snapped at the Mumbai airport as they have headed to Jaipur for the film’s shoot.Kriti Sanon and Arjun Kapoor were snapped at the airport as they were leaving the city. Arjun looked dashing in his airport look and donned a mustache and also wore a cap. He paired a casual printed t-shirt and tracks., on the other hand, Kriti wore a brown coloured pantsuit. The duo while entering their airport was all smiles for the paparazzi.Apart from Arjun and Kriti, the film also stars Sanjay Dutt in pivotal roles. Directed by Ashutosh Gowariker, the film will hit the silver screens on December 6, 2019.
