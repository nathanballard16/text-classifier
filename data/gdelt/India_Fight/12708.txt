Salman Khan & Katrina Kaif wrap the shoot of Bharat; Kat calls the superstar bestest boy in this picture

Salman Khan and Katrina Kaif make for an amazing onscreen jodi. The duo has worked together in several films like Maine Pyaar Kyun Kiya, Partner, Yuvvraj, Ek Tha Tiger and Tiger Zinda Hai. The audiences love to see them together and after an action-packed and blockbuster 2017 film Tiger Zinda Hai, their fans want more of them. Salman and Kat will be next seen in Ali Abbas Zafar's Bharat.

Since August last year, Salman Khan and Katrina Kaif have been shooting for Ali's film. Today, Katrina Kaif took to her Instagram page to share the news that they have wrapped the shoot of Bharat. The actors have shot for the film in Malta, Chandigarh and Mumbai. Kat shared a happy picture with Salman and called him the best boy.

She captioned the pic, "And that’s a Picture wrap on Bharatits been the most incredible & exciting character for me, the whole process of making this film has been so inspiring .....thank u @aliabbaszafar and @beingsalmankhan @atulreellife ..the bestest boys and Alvira khan (the bestest girl)."

Bharat will be Salman-Kat-Ali's second film together. It also stars Sunil Grover, Disha Patani and Nora Fatehi in pivotal roles.

Last month, the first teaser of Bharat was released and the audiences got to see 5 different avatars of Salman in it. Now, everyone is really looking forward to the trailer.

Bharat will hit the screens on Eid this year.
