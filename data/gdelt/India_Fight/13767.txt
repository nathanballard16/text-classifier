Cops arrest murder accused, wife

Ludhiana: Meharban police on Friday morning arrested the accused Jagdev Singh, 33, who shot his elderly neighbour in Noorwala village. The accused was arrested from near Hawaas village.Police have also arrested Jagdev's wife Kamaljeet Kaur who allegedly provoked Jagdev to fire at neighbour. The woman was arrested form near Bajara village on Thursday evening.On Thursday morning Jagdev Singh shot his 67-year-old neighbour Balbir Singh. It is alleged that Jagdev Singh would often open fire in air with his licensee weapon in inebriated condition. Village Sarpanch Rupinder Singh who is also nephew of the deceased Balbir lodged a complaint against Jagdev with Meharban police.
