'The Sky Is Pink': Priyanka Chopra, Farhan Akhtar and Zaira Wasim to shoot the final schedule in Andaman and Nicobar Islands

Director Shonali Bose 's upcoming film ' The Sky Is Pink ' starring Priyanka Chopra, Farhan Akhtar and Ziara Wasim in the lead roles had shot extensively for the film in London, Delhi, and Mumbai. The cast and crew of the film then went for a break from December owing to the grand wedding of Priyanka Chopra and Nick Jonas. The actress was working hard for the film till the eve of her nuptials. Now after two months, the team is back once again to complete the final schedule of shooting.As per reports, the final schedule of 'The Sky Is Pink' will kick off from the mid of February in Andaman and Nicobar Islands. Also, the team will also shoot some patchwork in Mumbai soon after that. Before Priyanka's wedding, the team was also spotted in Delhi shooting for a romantic song featuring Farhan and Priyanka. The movie went on floors last year in the month of August.'The Sky Is Pink' will see Zaira Wasim playing the role of the motivational speaker Aisha Chaudhary who was diagnosed with pulmonary fibrosis. Priyanka and Farhan will be playing parents to Zaira. The film is slated to release this year.
