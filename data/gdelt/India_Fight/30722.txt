Man murdered in Limbayat

SURAT: A 30-year-old man was murdered by two men in Limbayat area of the city on Tuesday. Imran Sha Rafik Sha, a resident of Mithi Khadi , was allegedly attacked by Babu Batkando alias Batko and Vinod More near Markandeshwar Mahadev Temple in Limbayat at around 7pm on Tuesday. Sha, who was unemployed, was thrashed with a blunt wooden object.He suffered serious injuries on head and admitted to SMIMER hospital by police where he died during treatment. Police have registered a murder case and begun investigation. Police suspect the victim was attacked due to personal animosity.
