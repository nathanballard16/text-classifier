Mobile battery explodes, kills 12-year-old boy

INDORE: A 12-year-old boy was killed when a mobile phone battery exploded after he plugged it into a universal charger in a Dhar village on Wednesday night.Lakhan Singar was alone at home in Lilikhedi village of Badnawar when the phone exploded. His uncle, Mukesh, who lives nearby, heard the blast and came running.Through the smoke, Mukesh could see Lakhan lying on the floor. “His fingers were missing and face was disfigured. He was bleeding. I called his father and we took him to the hospital where he was declared dead,” he said.
