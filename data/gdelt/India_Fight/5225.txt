Truck mows down newspaper hawker in Jaipur

A speeding truck hit him and ploughed him. JAIPUR: A 50-year-old newspaper distributor was killed after a speeding truck mowed him down near Shastri Nagar area of the city on Saturday early morning.The deceased was identified as one Kedar, a resident of a nearby locality in Shastri Nagar. He later succumbed to his injuries and died a few hours later,” said an official.A case has been registered by the city accident police station. The CCTV footage of the area are being scanned by the police and further investigation into the matter in underway. Kedar was killed when he was distributing newspapers on his cycle.
