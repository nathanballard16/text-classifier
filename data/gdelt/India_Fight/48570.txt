UP Police Releases Photographs Of 2 Suspects In Kamlesh Tiwari Murder Case

Earlier on Monday, the UP Police brought three other accused in the case to Lucknow from Gujarat after a court in Ahmedabad granted 72-hour transit remand to the police on Sunday.

As the suspects of Hindu Mahasabha leader Kamlesh Tiwari’s murder, remain absconding even after five days of the crime, Uttar Pradesh police has released the photographs of the two suspects Ashfaq and Moinuddin on social media. The police also announced a reward of Rs 2.5 lakh each for the arrest of two suspects on Monday. The UP Police also produced an accused Sayyed Asim Ali in Kamlesh Tiwari murder case in a court in Nagpur and obtained his transit remand, said Maharashtra ATS.
