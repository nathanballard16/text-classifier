CBI conducts raids at 110 locations in 19 states

NEW DELHI: In yet another mammoth operation, the CBI conducted raids at 110 locations in 19 states and Union Territories as part of its investigations into 33 cases which the agency registered recently. The cases concern offences of corruption, arms smuggling and criminal misconduct , officials said.This was the second large operation in a week. Last Tuesday, the CBI had carried out countrywide raids against alleged loan defaulters after several banks registered FIRs with the agency.Searches were conducted in Lucknow and some places in the NCR in connection with the sugar mills disinvestment scam which allegedly took place during Mayawati's tenure as Uttar Pradesh chief minister.The agency searched the premises of former IAS officers Netram and Vinay Priya Dhune and sons of former MLC Iqbal Singh — Wajid Ali and Mohammed Javed — in this case. Netram was Mayawati’s secretary at the time. The CBI had registered an FIR and six preliminary enquiries in this case in April.Another FIR was lodged against former Haryana CM Bhupinder Singh Hooda and searches were conducted at the office of Krrish Builders in Jasola in south-east Delhi in this case.In Kanpur, the CBI searched the premises of K A Duggal, the then CMD of BICL, and company secretary K V Vajpayee in a case of alleged irregularities in the tendering process during the sale of BICL properties.Searches were also conducted in the case of a missing youth from Rajasthan and disbursal of 13 housing loans worth Rs 3 crore by an SBI branch manager in Haridwar.Another raid was in connection with a cheating case where a Mumbai-based businessman, Ramesh Chaurasaia, was sold 980 acres of land for Rs 11.5 crore in Chhindwara district of Madhya Pradesh on the basis of forged government seals and stamp papers.The CBI took over the investigation on the order of the Madhya Pradesh high court . Searches were conducted at six places in Jabalpur, Baitul and Indore. The CBI recovered an original MoU signed between the parties apart from some receipts and property-related documents and bank passbooks.In J&K, searches were conducted at private gun houses in Udhampur, Srinagar and 11 places in Jammu in connection with two cases regarding irregularities in issuing of gun licences. Raids and searches at premises owned directly and indirectly by corporate lobbyist Deepak Talwar, an accused in the aviation scam, were also taken up.The raids were carried out across Delhi, Mumbai, Chandigarh, Jammu, Srinagar, Pune, Jaipur, Goa, Raipur, Hyderabad, Madurai, Kolkata, Rourkela, Ranchi, Bokaro, Lucknow, Kanpur and other locations in UP, Uttarakhand, Odisha, Himachal Pradesh, Punjab, Haryana, Andhra Pradesh, Karnataka and Bihar.
