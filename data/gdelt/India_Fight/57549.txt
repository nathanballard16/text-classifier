Karnataka CM BS Yediyurappa's convoy attacked in Kerala

Karnataka Chief Minister BS Yediyurappa's convoy was attacked allegedly by a few activists belonging to the Democratic Youth Federation of India(DYFI) and Youth Congress in Kerala on Tuesday, December 24.

The incident took place in Kannur district of the state. Yediyurappa reached the state capital Thiruvananthapuram on Monday (December 23) to offer prayers at Sree Padmanabha Swamy temple. Activists belonging to Kerala Students Union (KSU) tried to block his way while displaying black flags.

On Tuesday, as Yediyurappa was returning from Kannur, the activists tried to block his car.

A video of the incident has gone viral on social media, where a few people can be seen showing black flags along with their party flags to Yediyurappa while shouting "Go back".
