Dreaded goon brutality murdered in Panchpaoli, 2 arrested

Nagpur: A dreaded goon was brutally murdered in Panchpaoli area late Friday night. The goon has been identified as Gaurav alias Pandya Pillewan. Old rivalry was said to be reason behind the murder.

Police has arrested two person Yogi Dhanvijay and Chintu Vishwakarma .

More details are awaited.
