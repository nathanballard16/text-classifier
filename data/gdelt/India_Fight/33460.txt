UP: BJP Yuva Morcha Leader Booked With 6 Others In Connection With Wife's Murder

As per earlier information, Singh was driving a car accompanied by his wife 25-year-old wife Sneha Singh.

Bharatiya Janata Party’s Yuva Morcha leader Rahul Singh, was booked on Sunday in connection with the murder of his wife Sneha Singh in Uttar Pradesh’s Barabanki earlier in the day. A case has been filed against the BJP leader and 6 others, by the father of the deceased woman. While Singh said that unknown miscreants shot her dead after robbing them of their belongings when they were travelling, the father of the woman has alleged it to be a case of dowry-death.
