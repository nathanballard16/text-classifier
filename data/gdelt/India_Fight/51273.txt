RRR update: Ram Charan & Jr NTR to shake a leg on a folk song; First poster to be out on THIS date?

Megastar Ram Charan is back on the sets of his upcoming film, RRR. After a long gap and recovery from an ankle injury, Ram Charan has joined the RRR team at Ramoji Film City, Hyderabad. The actor is currently shooting courtroom scenes and fans can't wait to know what's in the store next. Also, Jr NTR and Ram Charan will come together for a folk song in RRR, which will be shot in coming days. Also, according to reports, the first title poster of RRR will be out on January 1, 2020. However, the makers of the film are yet to make an official announcement about the same.

RRR, being directed by SS Rajamouli is one of the much-awaited films of 2020. The film has been in the news since its inception. From star cast to the storyline, RRR has managed to set curiosity among the moviegoers. The period drama will see Ram Charan and Jr NTR essaying the roles of freedom fighters. Bollywood stars Ajay Devgn and Alia Bhatt will be seen in supporting roles. RRR will have music by Keeravani. Well, the audience can't wait to know what's in the store next.
