Police did not hit Arjun did a great job controlling violence on Sunday Gyanwant Singh

Kolkata: BJP MP Arjun Singh has been held responsible on Monday by Additional Director General (ADG), Law and Order (L&O) Gyanwant Singh, for the violence on Sunday. Since early morning, BJP workers put up a blockade on the railway track at Kankinara railway station at around 6:05 am. Some shop owners were allegedly beaten up by BJP workers as well, for keeping their shops open. Police soon rushed to the spot and tried to control the situation but were again attacked by the BJP workers. It may be mentioned that Governor Jagdeep Dhankhar went to visit the injured BJP MP on Monday.
