Budget leaked, claims Congress

New Delhi : Senior Congress leader Manish Tewari on Friday alleged that a few points relating to the Union Budget leaked.

The Former I&B minister posted a number of screen shots and suggested that the pointers mentioned in the images were the part of the budget and it can be amounted to a budget leak.

These pointers are being Circulated to Media people by Govt Sources . The Finance Ministry on Thursday, a day before the budget made it clear that the upcoming budget would be called as the interim budget 2019-202 and not a regular budget.
