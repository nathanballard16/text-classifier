Tourist raped, murdered obnoxiously in India

(MENAFN) A tourist, who travelled to India in order to overcome her depression, has been knocked unconscious, raped and murdered before her body was found hanging upside down in a forest.



Police declared that Liga Skromane was lured by two men, who sexually abused her and beheaded her.



The victim, aged 33, who was born in Latvia but lived in Dublin, Ireland, with her lover, has travelled to Kerala with her sister.
