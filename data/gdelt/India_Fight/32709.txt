Former Bigg Boss contestant Akshatha Pandavapura opens acting studio

Former Bigg Boss contestant Akshatha Pandavapura has started an acting studio called as I am an actor and Studio Kitchen in the last weekend in Bengaluru. The studio was inaugurated on the occasion of Akshatha’s birthday Akshatha who is also a theatre artist had acted in movies like Pallata before she entered into Bigg Boss house. In newly opened acting studio, nuances of acting will be taught for new comers, and, at the same time they can also take up cookery classes. Akshatha participated in the last season of Bigg Boss. She is the ninth contestant to get evicted from the house.
