Indo-Pak armies exchange fire along LoC in Rajouri

Srinagar

Armies of both India and Pakistan on Sunday again exchanged gunfire along the Line of Control in Nowshera area of Rajouri district of Jammu division.

Reports said that the firing in Nowshera sector of Rajouri district started today afternoon at about 1:15 pm and was going on last reports came in.
