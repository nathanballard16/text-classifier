Kulgam encounter: Last rites of DSP Aman Thakur to be held in his hometown Doda today

When the terrorists fired upon the police party, Thakur got seriously injured in the neck. The officer succumbed to his injuries when he was being flown to the army hospital.

Thakur, who was serving as Deputy Superintendent of Police, was leading the police team from the front in the gunfight with terrorists. The final rites will be conducted with full State Honours in his hometown Doda district of Jammu.
