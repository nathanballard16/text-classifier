: Two terrorists have been decimated by the security forces in Jammu and Kashmir’s Anantnag district. As per initial reports, the security forces have surrounded the area and a search operation in underway to track more terrorists. A brief encounter took place in the Bijbehara area of the Anantnag in which the two terrorists were gunned down. The forces had launched a search operation in Banger Mohalla in Banger Mohalla after receiving credible inputs and during searches a brief exchange of fire took place.
