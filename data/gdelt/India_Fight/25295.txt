Massive Fire Breaks Out At Resort Near Kolkata

Vedic village spa resort fire: Firefighters struggle to douse the flame

A massive fire broke out at a resort near Kolkata last night after a lightening strike there.

The fire at Vedic Village Spa Resort, which is about 20 minutes away from the Kolkata international airport, has now been brought under control and no injuries have been reported so far. No details were immediately available about the loss of property.

A video showed firefighters struggling to douse the massive blaze.

Several flights were cancelled in Kolkata last evening due to bad weather.
