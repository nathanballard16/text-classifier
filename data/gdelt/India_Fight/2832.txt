Good news Mahesh Babu fans, Maharishi finally has a release date - details inside

Good news for all the Mahesh Babu fans

Words don't do justice to how phenomenal a performer Mahesh Babu is. Apart from every aspiring actor's dream to work with the superstar, Mahesh has carved a niche for himself with films Murari, Arjun, and Srimanthudu. His last release, Bharat Ane Nenu wasn't just a phenomenal hit but also went on to become Telugu cinema's third highest grossing films of all times. Post the success of Bharat Ane Nenu, fans have eagerly been waiting for an update on their Prince's next release, Maharishi.

And we finally have some news! Maharishi starring Mahesh and Pooja Hegde's film gets an April release this year. While interacting with fans in Tirupathi, Raju revealed that the film will release on April 25. After a long schedule in the United States of America last year, the team is currently shooting in Pollachi in Tamil Nadu. As per reports in Indian Express, the team will also be shooting in Abu Dhabi and Hyderabad.

The film also stars Mohenjo Daro fame heroine, Pooja Hegde. The cast and crew of the film have just wrapped up the first schedule in Dehradun. Directed by Vamshi Paidipally, this film marks Mahesh's 25 film in the industry that has left the fans all the more curious.
