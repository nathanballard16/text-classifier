Three people suffer knife injuries at UK's Manchester Victoria station

Dec 31 (Reuters) - UK Police said late on Monday that three people suffered knife injuries in an incident at the Manchester Victoria station on New Year's Eve.

Police said http://bit.ly/2BPPwMF they have detained a man in the incident which left a woman, a man and a police officer injured. (Reporting by Kanishka Singh in Bengaluru; editing by Chris Reese)
