Fire breaks out at Vadodara's SSG Hospital

Follow us on BREAKING: Fire breaks out at Vadodara's SSG Hospital

A fire broke out at the Shree Sir Sayaji General Hospital in Gujarat's Vadodara on Tuesday.

The blaze erupted at the paediatric ward of the SSG Hospital. All the children have been safely shifted from the ward. Meanwhile, fire fighting operations are underway.
