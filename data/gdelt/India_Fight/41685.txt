Maharashtra: 12 killed many injured in explosion in chemical factory

AGENCIES / MUMBAI

At least 12 workers were killed and 66 injured in an explosion of gas cylinders at a chemical factory in Dhule district of Maharashtra on Saturday morning, according to reports.

At least 100 workers were present in the factory in Waghadi village in Shirpur taluka when the explosion occurred around 9:45 am, a senior police officer told news agency Press Trust of India.

“Ten workers were killed and 40 injured in the explosion,” Dhule Superintendent of Police Vishwas Pandhare said.

Several teams of police, disaster management, fire brigade were conducting search and rescue operations at the site.
