Bihar: Man shot dead by unknown assailants in Patna

Patna (Bihar) [India], May 27 (ANI): A man on Sunday morning was allegedly shot dead by unknown assailants near Kidwaipuri Park here.

The deceased has been identified as Ravi Rai.

Rai went out for a morning walk and as soon as he reached Jaikund Apartment, unidentified assailants started firing at him. He suffered bullet injuries on his head and stomach.

"The matter is being investigated and, prima facie, the deceased was shot dead. The dead body has been sent for post mortem," Police Inspector Dilip Mishra said.

Further details are awaited. (ANI)
