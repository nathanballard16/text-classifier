BJP supporters clash with police in Bhatpara Arjun Singh injured

Kolkata: BJP Lok Sabha MP Arjun Singh was injured after a huge clash took place in the Bhatpara-Kankinara area on Sunday. Following the clash, BJP workers led by Arjun put up a road blockade and allegedly pelted stones at the police. While doing so, BJP workers attacked the Trinamool workers and beat them up. Later, BJP workers led by Arjun put up a blockade in the area. Police had to fire several tear gas shells to disperse the violent BJP workers.
