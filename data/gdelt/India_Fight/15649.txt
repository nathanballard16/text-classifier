Arun Vijay wraps up shoot for Prabhas and Shraddha Kapoor's 'Saaho'

Sujeeth’s upcoming film ‘ Saaho ’ starring Prabhas and Shraddha Kapoor in lead roles is one of the most-awaited films of this year.The film which features an extensive star cast with actors from various Indian film industries has seen Tamil actor Arun Vijay wrap up shoot for it. The actor posted pictures from behind-the-scenes of his last day on-set and wrote, “Its a wrap for me!! #Saaho. Happy to have been a part of this incredibly hard working team. Thanks to #Prabhas @sujeethsign @UV_Creations & the rest of da cast n crew for makin this journey a memorable one!” (sic)Also confirming that the film is, in fact, slated for release on August 15, he added, “Wait for an exhilarating experience in theatres from Aug 15! C u all..” (sic) ‘Saaho’, which began filming in June 2017 also stars Neil Nitin Mukesh Evelyn Sharma , Mandira Bedi, Chunky Pandey, Jackie Shroff and more in key roles. It was shot in Hyderabad, Mumbai, Abu Dhabi , Dubai, Romania and other parts of Europe.
