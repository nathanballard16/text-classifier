Radikaa Sarathkumar to host KBC Franchise in Tamil!

Radikaa Sarathkumar is all set to host the KBC franchise 'Kodeswari' in Tamil.

The successful game show 'Who Wants To Be a Millionaire' was adapted in India long back as 'Kaun Banega Crorepati' with 'Big B' Amitabh Bachchan as its host. Suriya also hosted a season of 'Nengalum Vellalam Oru Kodi' , the Tamil version of the show in Vijay TV.





The unique feature of Radikaa's Kodeswari is that the participants of the show will be women and men are not eligible to be the contestant. To be aired Colors TV Tamil, Radikaa has done the test shoot recently for the reality show.
