Engineer dies while getting off running train

An engineer was killed while trying to get down from a moving train in K.R. Puram railway station on Thursday evening.

The deceased, Kiran Kumar Kanakata, 38, is from Nellore in Andhra Pradesh. He was working in a leading IT firm in Bengaluru and was residing at Ramamurthy Nagar.

The police said Kiran Kumar and his wife had a child three months ago.

On Thursday evening, he was travelling from Chennai to Bengaluru on the Chennai Mail. The train does not have a stop at K.R. Puram railway station. During an attempt to get down from the moving train, Kiran Kumar got stuck between the train and the platform. He was killed on the spot, said Cantonment Railway Police.
