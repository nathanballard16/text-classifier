Abhinandan will inspire generations, says CM Yogi

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath addressing assembly on supplementary budget in Winter Session of Uttar Pradesh Assembly in Lucknow on Thursday. TOI Lucknow Photo - By Manoj Chhabra

Gorakhpur: The valour and courage of Wing Commander Abhinandan Varthaman will inspire coming generations. Everyone has seen how government took decisions and how soldiers returned safely after destroying terrorist camps. Nothing is impossible for Modi, said chief minister Yogi Adityanath at the inauguration of two-day Kisan Mela and agriculture on Saturday.

CM Yogi flagged off BJP Vijay Sankalp motorcycle rally from Chowk Mafi in Campirganj assembly area.

CM also inaugurated the administrative building of Mahayogi Krishi Vigyan Kendra at Pipiganj, launched the website of Krishi Vigyan Kendra and distributed soil test card and soil health card among few farmers. Union Minister of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare, Radhamohan Singh was also present at the occasion.
