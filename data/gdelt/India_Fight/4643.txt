Ahmedabad: Rs 1 cr cash sized in raid on Kushal Tradelink

Nine lockers, Rs 1 crore in cash, Rs3 crore worth of unaccounted fixed deposits and several cash vouchers were also found. Its turnover was Rs 338 crore in the year 2015-16 and at that time, the share price was Rs 550 while in 2016-17 the turnover was Rs 383 crore and the share price shot up to Rs 3000 crore. The department suspected the company was artificially managing its share price. The Income Tax officials carried out survey operations at various locations including residential premises and offices of Ahmedabad-based Kushal Tradelink. The company was put on the radar of the I-T officials after its share price which was around Rs 2 per share during demonetisation shot up to Rs 3000 per share later.
