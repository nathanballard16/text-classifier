Jammu and Kashmir: 2 to 3 terrorists believed to be trapped in Tral, encounter underway

On Saturday, two terrorists were killed in a brief gunbattle with security forces in Jammu and Kashmir's Shopian district, police said. The terrorists opened fire on a patrolling party of the Army from an orchard in Shopian, following which the soldiers retaliated, a police official said. He said two ultras were killed in the exchange of fire. The identity and the affiliation of the slain terrorists were being ascertained, the official added.

A fresh encounter broke out between terrorists and joint security forces on Sunday morning in Jammu and Kashmir's tral; hours after two terrorists were decimated in the valley in Shopian. The encounter started at Midoora Tral area in South Kashmir. Two to three terrorists are believed to be trapped in the area.
