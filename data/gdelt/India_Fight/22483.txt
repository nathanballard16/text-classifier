Mumbai: Autorickshaw driver ‘stabs wife to death, attacks daughter’

Police booked a 52-year-old autorickshaw driver for allegedly murdering his wife and stabbing his 24-year-old daughter late on Thursday. “He had a fight with his wife Manisha over his name missing from the invitation card. Police said he stabbed his wife with a chopping knife after he found out that his name was missing from the invitation card of his daughter’s wedding. He stabbed her multiple times and even attacked his daughter, who tried stopping him,” the officer said. When his wife protested, he rushed to the kitchen and brought out a chopper.
