Sandeep Agarwal murder case: Indore court extends remand of Rohit Sethi for one day

INDORE: Rohit Sethi , the prime accused in Sandeep Agarwal murder case, was again produced before local court on Thursday and his remand was extended for a day.“The court has sent Sethi on police remand till Friday. Constant pressure by Agarwal to return the money prompted Sethi to hatch a plan to contract his killing through Maratha. They were asked about the place where they hatched a plan of murder. Sethi had also offered to sell him a land owned by him, but, the deal did not materialize. We have to produce him in Dehradun court on March 9.
