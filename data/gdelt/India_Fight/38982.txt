Temple demolition: Stage set for Punjab bandh today

Deputy Commissioner Varinder K Sharma has ordered the closure of all government, private and aided schools and colleges of the district so that the students are not inconvenienced. The ministers, while expressing pain and anguish over the demolition of the temple in New Delhi, said the Punjab Government, led by Chief Minister Capt Amarinder Singh, would support them. Both the ministers said if the community agreed, the state government was ready to bear the entire expenses for the legal battle to rebuild the temple. The ministers said the state government was ready to contribute or fully bear the expenditure for the reconstruction of the temple at the same place. The resolution pledged to wage a peaceful battle for regaining the land for the temple, thereby ensuring its reconstruction.
