Youth shot at by uncle over family dispute

Kolkata: A youth was shot at by his uncle on Thursday afternoon at Bhowanipore over some family dispute. The injured youth identified as Kunal Shaw alias Bittu suffered minor injury as the bullet went pass after touching his body. The accused persons identified as Manoj Shaw has been arrested. On Thursday around 1 pm, all of a sudden Manoj entered Kunal's house and fired a round at him. Hearing the sound of gunfire and screaming, local residents ran to Kunal's house and detained Manoj.
