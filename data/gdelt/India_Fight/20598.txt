CRPF jawan, 3 Maoists killed in Jharkhand gunfight

A CRPF jawan and three Maoists were killed in an encounter in Giridih district ’s Bhutakulha, near the Jharkhand-Bihar border, on Monday morning.A joint team of police and CRPF was undertaking a combing operation in the area when around six Maoists on two bikes opened fire on them. CRPF jawan Biswajit Chouhan killed two Maoists before being hit.
