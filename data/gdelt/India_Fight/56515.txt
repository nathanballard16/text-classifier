Mumbai: Shiv Sena leader Shekhar Jadhav shot at by unkown assailant

Follow us on Firing on Shiv Sena functionary in Mumbai; assailant nabbed

An Shiv Sena functionary was injured after an unidentified person opened fire on him in suburban Vikhroli on Thursday morning, a police official said. The incident took place around 8 am near Sai Mandir in Tagor Nagar area of Vikhroli, he said.

The Sena leader, identified as Shekhar Jadhav, sustained injuries on his hand and was admitted to Godrej Hospital in Vikhroli, the official said.

The assailant was caught by passers-by and handed over to police, he added.
