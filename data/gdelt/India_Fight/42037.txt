Passer-by killed as criminals fire on police in Delhi

"Our constable Ajay, who is posted in the Nand Nagri police station, was passing through the Tanga Stand around 11.39 p.m, when he noticed some suspicious persons sitting in a Swift Desire car," said Atul Thakur, Deputy Commissioner of Police (North East).

On inquiry, the car sped away, but the constable tried to chase it.
