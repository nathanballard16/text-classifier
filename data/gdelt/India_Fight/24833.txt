City eves bag top slots in PU exams

Ludhiana: As Panjab University, Chandigarh declared the result of BEd first semester examination held in December 2018, some students from Malwa Central College of Education for Women, Civil Lines, Ludhiana have excelled in the exam and secured university positions.

Parul Preet Kaur and Shilpa Jain secured the 5th position in Panjab University by getting 393/450 marks while Kriti Goel secured the 8th position by getting 389/450 marks. They credited their success to the faculty for supporting them and motivating them do excel.

College principal Dr Naginder Kaur appreciated the students for their achievement and wish them success in the future.
