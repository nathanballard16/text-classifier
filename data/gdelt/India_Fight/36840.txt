Srinagar (Jammu and Kashmir): Pakistani army violated the ceasefire for the third consecutive day today by resorting to firing and shelling along the Line of Control (LoC) in Jammu and Kashmir's Rajouri district. Pakistani Rangers in Rajouri, Tangdhar and Kupwara opened fire at around 1:30 pm this afternoon. An Indian army jawan was killed while two civilians were injured in cross-border firing in Sunderbani sector of Rajouri. Indian soldiers retaliated strongly to the firing and have in return killed two Pakistani soldiers. "The Pakistan army resorted to unprovoked ceasefire violation along the LoC in Sunderbani sector of Rajouri district today," Public Relations Officer (PRO) of the Army Lt Col Devender Anand said.
