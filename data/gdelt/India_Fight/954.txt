Two found dead in outer Delhi’s Ranhola

New Delhi, Jan 07 (ANI): In a shocking incident, two men including a cab driver were allegedly shot dead and their bodies were dumped beside the Nangloi-Najafgarh road in Baprola area of outer Delhi’s Ranhola. The deceased have been identified as Deepak, a driver of cab aggregator Uber, and Neeraj. The police rushed to the spot to investigate the matter. Further investigations are underway.
