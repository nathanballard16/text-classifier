3 women injured after bus rams garbage truck

Tirupur: Three passengers of a state-run bus sustained serious injuries after the vehicle crashed into a city corporation’s truck carrying garbage in the city on Friday.

The Tamil Nadu State Transport Corporation bus plying between Erode and Tirupur was passing through Uthukuli Road at Palayakkadu when the accident happened. Three women, who were sitting in front rows, sustained serious injuries, said a police officer. The windshield of the bus was destroyed on impact.

The injured were identified as Amutha, 40, of Pichampalayam, Meena, 44, of Rakkiyapalayam and Kanaga, 40. They were rushed to the Tirupur Government Hospital.

The Tirupur north police have registered a case.TNN
