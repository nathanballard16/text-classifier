Sand worth Rs 70K seized

Mangaluru: The Mangaluru City police on Wednesday seized sand worth Rs 70,000 that was being illegally stored at a site in Kemmanjooru Based on a tip-off, police from the Mangaluru Rural police station raided the site at Kemmanjooru between Adyarkatte and Adyarpadav, belonging to Uday, and seized 15 tipper-loads of sand. Police said Uday and Jayasheela Adyanthaya, who stored the sand, did not have the necessary documents to store the sand. The seized sand has been handed over to the department of mines and geology.
