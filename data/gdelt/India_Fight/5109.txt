Dad-in-law foils sexual assault of woman, dies

Representative image

PATIALA: The police here have booked two persons for allegedly killing a 53-year-old man and outraging the modesty of a woman.

The deceased, identified as Baljit Singh of Rajgarh village, had tried to prevent the accused from molesting his daughter-in-law, but was murdered instead. Police said the accused were related to the deceased and live in the same village.

Based on the complaint, the police registered a case under Sections 302 (murder), 354 (outrage the modesty of a woman) and 34 (common intention) of the Indian Penal Code at the Pasiana police station.

The police said the accused, identified as Gurpreet Singh and Varinder Singh, on Wednesday attacked Baljit Singh with iron rods after he allegedly attempted to save his daughter-in-law. The accused were dragging the woman towards their house following which she raised an alarm and immediately her father-in-law came to save her.

“As soon as my father came to rescue my wife, my accused cousins attacked him with iron rods following which he faced severe head injuries. He was then rushed to a local hospital. He had to undergo various stitches. Later, he was shifted to a government hospital in Chandigarh where he succumbed to the injuries,” the complainant told the police.

Pasiana station house officer Harwinder Singh said, “We are conducting raids to nab the accused who both have managed to escape after the incident. The deceased succumbed to the injuries on Thursday evening following which a case of murder was registered against the accused duo. Our investigation of the case is on.”
