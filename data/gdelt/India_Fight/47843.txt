Encounter breaks out between police, criminal near LSR college in Delhi

The criminal identified as Iqbal has been shot during the encounter.

A criminal carrying Rs 1 lakh reward has been arrested following an encounter in Delhi. The encounter took place near Lady Shri Ram on Friday morning after the criminal reportedly opened fire at police.

The police arrested the criminal identified as Iqbal after he was injured during the encounter.

Iqbal had 25 criminal cases registered against him.
