Grenade Lobbed On SBI Pulwama

(MENAFN- Kashmir Observer) SRINAGAR — Suspected militants on Tuesday evening lobbed a grenade towards a state bank in Pulwama district of South Kashmir which exploded without any damage.

The grenade as per sources was hurled at State Bank of India (SBI) in main town Pulwama followed by the firing. Sources said that alert cops retaliated the attack with firing. However, no loss of life or injury was reported in this attack.

Meanwhile, latest reports said that firing has stopped as of now.
