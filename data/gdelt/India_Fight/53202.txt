Major fire at hosiery unit in Ludhiana

monicakchauhan@gmail.com

Ludhiana, November 30

A major fire broke out at a hosiery unit located in Wait Gunj -- one of the most congested markets of the city -- on Saturday, fire brigade officials said.

As many as 13 fire-fighting engines of the Ludhiana Municipal Corporation struggled hard to douse the flames, they said.

The cause of the fire in the three-storey building was yet to be known, police said, adding no causality was reported. PTI
