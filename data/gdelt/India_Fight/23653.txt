Fire breaks out at Telugu superstar Chiranjeevi's farmhouse; details inside

Chiranjeevi's upcoming 'Sye Raa Narasimha Reddy' is a historical war film based on the life of Uyaalawada Narasimha Reddy.

Mumbai: On Friday morning, a fire broke out at Telugu superstar Chiranjeevi's farmhouse on the outskirts of Hyderabad.

According to IANS report, the fire damaged part of a film set. The incident happened near the Gandipet lake. Residents of the respective area noticed the smoke and alerted the police. The police officials said that there was nobody on the sets of the film 'Sye Raa Narasimha Reddy' at the time of an incident. However, a major part of the sets damaged in the fire.

Chiranjeevi's upcoming 'Sye Raa Narasimha Reddy' is a historical war film based on the life of Uyaalawada Narasimha Reddy. It also stars Amitabh Bachchan, Nayanthara, Tamannah and others. The film will be made in Telugu, Tamil and Malayalam.

'Sye Raa Narasimha Reddy' is being produced by Chiranjeevi's son-actor Ram Charan and directed by Surender Reddy.
