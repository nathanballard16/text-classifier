Policeman injured in Srinagar attack airlifted to Delhi for treatment

Srinagar

Policeman, Feroz Ahmad who was injured critically in a militant attack in Chanapora here, was Friday airlifted to AIIMS for treatment, officials said.

Feroz received a critical gunshot wound in the right ribs on the evening of April 26 last month after suspected militants opened fire upon police post-Chanapora. Feroz was immediately shifted to SKIMS for treatment.

“Feroz was airlifted to the All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS) for further treatment today afternoon,” news agency GNS quoted official sources as having said.

A police officer while confirming it said that the seriously injured officer along with his family was airlifted to AIIMS Delhi through an air ambulance arranged by the department.
