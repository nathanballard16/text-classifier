Scolded for liquor addiction, man kills aunt

The accused on Saturday reached the wedding venue in an intoxicated state and started behaving indecently. Raja is a liquor addict and Malabai would often scold him asking him to quit the addiction. Nagpur: A youth murdered his aunt in Pavanshakti Nagar locality under the jurisdiction of Nandanvan police station on early Sunday morning as she would often scold her over his liquor addiction. It is learnt that Malabai was paternal aunt of Raja and his local guardian as his parents stay away. Nandanvan police were informed about the incident, who registered an offence against Raja based on a complaint lodged by Pawan Anantaji Sadmake.
