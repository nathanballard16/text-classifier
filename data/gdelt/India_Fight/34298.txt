Massive Fire at a Factory in Delhi, Three Deaths Reported

New Delhi: Three people, including two women, were killed in a massive fire at a factory in the national capital on Saturday, police said.

The fire broke out at the hardware factory in Shahdara’s Jhilmil industrial area around 9 am, according to fire officials.

Deputy Commissioner of Police (Shahdara) Meghna Yadav said two women and a man died in the fire.

According to the fire officials, the information about the blaze was received at 9.25 am and 31 fire tenders were rushed to the spot.
