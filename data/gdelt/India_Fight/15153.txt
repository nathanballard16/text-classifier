Massive fire at tyre godown, 20 fire brigades rushed

Bhopal: A major fire broke out at a scrap tyre godown at Kabadkhana in Hanumanganj locality early Friday morning. Around 5.30 am on Friday, police received information about fire incident at a tyre godown behind Kundan Namkeen in Kabadkhana. It took 20 fire brigades and water tankers, each, and almost four hours to douse the inferno. Earlier, on wee hours of Tuesday a major fire broke out at a scrap plastic bag godown. The firemen struggled to control the fire due as the heavy smoke was causing suffocation.The highly congested lanes even obstructed movement of the water tankers while they went to refill.
