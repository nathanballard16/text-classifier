Highway robbers’ gang busted, weapons seized

JAIPUR: The city police on Thursday busted a gang of highway robbers and arrested six of its members during raid in Sindhi Camp area. They had bags with them and were planning their next criminal activity when sleuths surrounded them,”an official told TOI. The police said that the accused were brought to the Sindhi Camp police station and interrogated about their suspicious activities. For past few days, suspects carried out shooting at Sanjay circle, Raja Park, and Sanagner areas of the city. A cache of arms and ammunition was also seized by police teams.
