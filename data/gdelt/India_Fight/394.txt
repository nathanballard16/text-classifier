One Indian Army jawan killed in an avalanche in Jammu and Kashmir's Poonch

Image for representational purpose only | Photo Credit: Times Now

New Delhi: One Indian Army jawan killed in an avalanche in Jammu and Kashmir's Poonch. One Army jawan died and another one was injured in an avalanche in Poonch district of Jammu and Kashmir- agencies reported citing Indian Army sources. According to news agency PTI, the two were posted in a unit along the Line of Control (LoC) in Jammu and Kashmir's Poonch district.

Citing local police, PTI reported that the avalanche hit a Rashtriya Rifles post in the Sawjian sector on Thursday morning. The massive avalanche resulted in the burial of two soldiers. The police said that a rescue operation was launched immediately. While one of the jawans died on the spot, the other one was shifted to a hospital- the police told news agency PTI.
