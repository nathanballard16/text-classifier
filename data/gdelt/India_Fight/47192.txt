AP man killed in Saudi Arabia

Hyderabad: A 54-year-old man from Bapatla in Andhra Pradesh was murdered in Saudi Arabia . The Consulate General of India (CGI), Jeddah, informed the victim’s family on Saturday about the incident. “The accused has been detained. The mortal remains are in Al Kharma General Hospital and police investigation is going on,” CGI Jeddah said. Daughter Sameena Shaik took to Twitter to seek help from AP CM and the PM . tnn
