3 killed in blast inside warehouse of chemical storage tanks in India - Xinhua

Source: Xinhua| 2019-12-30 20:06:09|Editor: Shi Yinglun

NEW DELHI, Dec. 30 (Xinhua) -- At least three people were killed and two others went missing Monday in a blast inside a warehouse of chemical storage tanks in the western Indian state of Gujarat, local media reports said.

The blast took place inside the warehouse near Kandla port and oil refinery in Kutch district, about 322 km west of Gandhinagar, the capital city of Gujarat.

"Three people were killed and two missing after a blast took place in the warehouse of chemical storage tanks near Gujarat's Kandla port and Indian Oil refinery," a local television news channel News18 said.

Following the incident, authorities rushed fire tenders to the spot.

The firefighting operation was underway until last reports poured in.
