Pune: 3 killed, 15 injured after bus collides with pick-up vehicle on Kalyan-Ahmednagar highway; rescue ops on

Pune: The bus collided with pick-up transport vehicle on Kalyan-Ahmednagar highway | Photo Credit: ANI

Pune: In a tragic incident, three people were killed while 15 others were injured after a bus and a pick-up transport vehicle collided on Kalyan-Ahmednagar highway near Pimpri Pendhar village in Pune, news agency ANI reported. Rescue operations had commenced at the site soon after the accident, as per the news agency.

Two days ago, on January 16, six children were killed while nine others were wounded in another tragic bus accident. a Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation (MSRTC) bus collided with a truck in the state’s Gadchiroli district on Wednesday morning, causing the death of six children. The accident had occurred at around 8:30 am when the truck and the bus collided bear Gurupalli village in Gadchiroli, which is near 170 km from Nagpur.

The impact of the collision was strong enough to cause cabins of both vehicles to get crushed. After the accident, locals had rushed to the spot to help the victims. Agitated at the death of the children, a few locals later set fire to the truck.
