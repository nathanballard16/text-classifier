Fire At Indore's Timber Market, No Casualties Reported

Fire broke out at Gurunanak Timber market in Indore, 2 shops were gutted

Fire broke out at Gurunanak Timber market in Indore on Monday night at around 9 pm.

Two wood godowns and two shops were gutted in the fire. However, the fire was later brought under control.

No casualties have been reported till now.
