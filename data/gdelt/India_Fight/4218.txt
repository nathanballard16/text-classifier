Man fires in air to support brother in spat with his wife

Ludhiana: A man opened fire in the air with his licencee rifle to support his brother in a spat with the latter’s wife in Chitti Colony of Salem Tabri on Friday evening. He started questioning why she was standing outside the house, and started beating her. However, minutes later, he started pelting stones at her from his brother’s house, which is near his wife’s house. Meanwhile, her son Harpreet, nephew Gurwinder, and sister Ranjeet came to her rescue, after which Harbhajan ran away from there. Then, Gurbhej took out his licencee .12 bore rifle and opened fire in the air.Investigating officer ASI Baljeet Singh said police reached the spot and arrested both the accused.
