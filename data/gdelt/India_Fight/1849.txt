Even Amit Shah is chargesheeted in murder case, nobody calls him murderer: Kanhaiya Kumar

Begusarai (Bihar), Jan 14 (ANI): Former JNUSU president Kanhaiya Kumar, who was today named in the chargesheet of the Delhi Police in 2016 sedition case, also criticised the media trial of branding him as an “traitor”, and even said that the BJP president Amit Shah is chargesheeted in connection with a murder case, however, no one calls him a “murderer”.
