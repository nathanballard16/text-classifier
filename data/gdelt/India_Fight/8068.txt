Dallas Man Shares Story About His Selfie Fall

Doctors at Methodist Medical Center say Tristan suffered injuries to his lung, spleen and pelvis, but they’re amazed that he wasn’t paralyzed or killed by the fall.

Brian Mudd says that there were 259 confirmed deaths between 2011 and 2017 (the most recent numbers available) linked to selfies in the world. The U.S. has the third most such deaths. India and Russia are numbers one and two respectively.
