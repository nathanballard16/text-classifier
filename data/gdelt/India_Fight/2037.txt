PHOTOS: Varun Dhawan is the happiest after working out in the gym and these pictures prove just that

Varun Dhawan is a fitness-enthusiast and the entire nation knows it by now. Be it a hectic day at work, or partying hard till the wee hours of the morning, Varun Dhawan will never ditch his gym. Today, Varun Dhawan was snapped outside the gym and wearing his usual shorts, Tee and cap, Varun Dhawan posed for the paparazzi after his workout session and we totally loved his pictures. Of late, Varun has been rigorously working out in the gym in order to prep up for Abhishek Varman’s Kalank and thanks to social media, this Sui Dhaaga actor makes sure to share pictures with his Instafam.

Today, Varun Dhawan took to social media to wish his Student of the Year co-star Sidharth Malhotra a very happy birthday and alongside a major throwback picture of the two, Varun wrote, ‘Happy Birthday Sidharth.’ Recently, Varun Dhawan, along with other B-town celebs had gone to meet the Prime Minister of India, Narendra Modi to discuss what they think about India as an evolving country and how their films are impacting the Indian culture and basically talk about their views on cinema and what changes they want to see.

Talking about his personal life, Varun Dhawan has been dating his long-time girlfriend Natasha Dalal for a long time now and when Varun appeared on the sixth season of Koffee With Karan alongside Katrina Kaif, the actor admitted to dating Natasha Dalal and said that he would want to get married to her soon. On the work front, after the success of Sharat Katariya’s Sui Dhaaga, Varun is currently shooting for Abhishek Varman’s Kalank which is set in the 1940s and is a period drama starring Varun Dhawan, Alia Bhatt, Madhuri Dixit, Sanjay Dutt, Sonakshi Sinha and Aditya Roy Kapur in the lead roles. Also, Varun Dhawan will be seen playing a cameo in Ali Abbas Zafar’s Bharat and for the same, Varun had recently gone to Abu Dhabi to shoot the part with Salman Khan.
