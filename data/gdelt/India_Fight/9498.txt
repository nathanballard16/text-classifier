PM Narendra Modi chairs Cabinet Committee on Security meet in Delhi

New Delhi: PM Narendra Modi chairs Cabinet Committee on Security meet in Delhi; Defence Minister Nirmala Sitharaman, External Affairs Minister Sushma Swaraj, Home Minister Rajnath Singh, FM Arun Jaitley and NSA Doval attend the meeting.

Cabinet Committee on Security met on Feb 26 morning following after IAF’s Mirage 2000 fighter jets drop 1000-kg bombs across LoC. Balakot, Chakothi and Muzaffarabad terror launch pads across the LOC completely destroyed in IAF air strikes. JeM control rooms also destroyed according to the sources.
