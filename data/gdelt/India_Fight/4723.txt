AP Police decided to transfer Ch Jayaram's murder case to Telangana ?

In a new twist in the murder case of Chigurupati Jayaram, reports are coming that Andhra Pradesh Police decided to transfer the case to Telangana police. AP Police yet to confirm this officially. Since the murder took place in Hyderabad, according to law they have decided to transfer it to Telangana. Since deceased Jayaram's wife Padmasree made serious allegations on AP Police that they have diluted the case, the decision taken by the AP Police grabbed the attention in both the telugu states.

On the other hand, Nandigama police produced the accused in Nandigama Court, where the court sent them for Judicial remand till February 20. Police is planning to file a petition to take them into custody for a week.
