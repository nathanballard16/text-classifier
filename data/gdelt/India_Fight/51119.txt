India Cements reports subdued Q2 profit at Rs 5 crore

India Cements said on Monday its consolidated net profit moved into the black at Rs 5.07 crore during the second quarter ended September 30.

The company had posted a net loss of Rs 5.03 crore for the July to September period in last fiscal year. However, it had reported a profit of Rs 68.98 crore in the preceding quarter (April to June).
