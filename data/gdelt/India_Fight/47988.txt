Man shot dead in Karawal Nagar

New Delhi: A 42-year-old man has been shot dead allegedly by two bike-borne assailants in northeast Delhi's Karawal Nagar area on Thursday. Police have registered a case in this regard and investigation is going from different angles. The deceased, Kishor (42), was a resident of Shiv Vihar area in Karawal Nagar and worked as a financier, police said. Kishor was found in an injured condition at the spot and rushed to a hospital, where he was declared brought dead, the official said. Investigators suspects that few persons came and shot the accused while he was sitting on his scooty.
