Infant wins over rare tumour without chemotherapy

Kolkata: A two-year-old abandoned girl has battled a rare type of tumour without going for chemotherapy at Medical College and Hospital, Kolkata. She was named Meenu by hospital authorities and, at present, she is fine," said Dr Indranil Biswas, medical superintendent and vice-principal of Medical College and Hospital. Hospital authorities had taken help of the police to trace the address of the parents of the infant. "With all the possible help of the hospital authorities she provided adequate medical care. Meanwhile, the hospital authorities are also looking for someone who can adopt the child by going for formal procedure prescribed by the government.
