Dabangg 3: Salman Khan to shake a leg with Prabhudheva in Munna Badnaam Hua? Here’s what we know

Post Bharat, Salman Khan dived straight onto the sets of Dabangg 3, and while the shooting of the film kick-started in Maheshwar, currently, the cast and crew is shooting somewhere in Maharashtra. In Dabangg and Dabangg 2, Salman Khan was seen shaking a leg with Malaika Arora and Kareena Kapoor Khan respectively in the songs- Munni Badnaam and Fevicol Se and it comes as no surprise that in the third installment of the cop franchise, Salman will again set the dance floor on fire with Chulbul Pandey’s dance moves.



While the makers have come up with a song titled Munna Badnaam Hua for Dabangg 3 in which Salman Khan will be seen dancing with Warina Hussain, according to a latest report, the director of the film and ace choreographer, Pranhudheva will be joining Salman Khan in the song. Like every time, this time too Salman Khan must have surely come up with a unique dance step and according to report, he has used his belt to dish out another interesting step. In the song, according to a report in Mumbai Mirror, Prabhudheva’s part will be a special element alongside Salman and Warina and audiences will witness a never seen before avatar of Warina. While Warina is all set to shock everyone with her killer moves and now, according to the theme of the show, Prabhudheva will gesture Warina to call him but Salman will see it and in an attempt to woo her, Salman will challenge Prabhudheva.

Well, it will be interesting to see Salman and Prabhudheva shake a leg together on the screen because while Salman has his own quirky style of dancing, Prabhudheva is surely going to add his own touch. Dabangg also stars Sonakshi Sinha and Arbaaz Khan and while the shooting of the film is around 60 per cent complete, it will hit the screens in December 2019.
