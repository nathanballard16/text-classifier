Indo-Pak armies exchange fire along LoC in Rajouri

Srinagar

After maintaining the silence on Line of Control (LoC) for two days, the India and Pakistan armies exchanged gun fire in Jammu and Kashmir’s Rajouri district.

Reports said that the fire from both the sides started today afternoon in Kalal and Baba Khori areas of Nowshera sector.

However, there were no reports of any damage from either side.
