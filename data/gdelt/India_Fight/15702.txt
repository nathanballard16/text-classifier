Man shot dead by militants in south Kashmir

Suspected militants shot dead a man in South Kashmir’s Tral on Monday evening, Jammu and Kashmir Police said. The man was shot outside his home. He later succumbed to injuries. He was identified as Mohsin, ANI reported.
