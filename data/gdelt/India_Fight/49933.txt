Man fired at, escapes unhurt

Amritsar, November 1Onkar Singh, a resident of Ajnala, was shot at by several unidentified assailants near Dalam village today morning. The victim stated to the police said that he was delivering newspapers from Amritsar to Rajasansi, Kukkranwala and Dalam village. He said unknown armed persons intercepted and assaulted him. He left the van and tried to flee when the accused fired at him, but he escaped. The police have registered a case against unknown persons on the statement of Onkar Singh.
