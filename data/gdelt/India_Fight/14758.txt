Delhi: 24-yr-old shot at in Shastri Park

Follow us on Image Source : PTI Representational

In northeast Delhi's Shastri Park area, a man was shot at on Wednesday, police said.

The victim, Akash (24), a resident of Bhajanpura area, was safe barring a bullet wound on his leg, cops said.

"The incident took place on Wednesday evening at Shastri Park area. The victim and the accused known each other and there seems to be personal enmity among them," PTI quoted Deputy Commissioner of Police (northeast) Atul Kumar Thakur, as saying.

A case was registered by cops. Delhi police is looking to nab the accused person.
