Pune: Man held with gold concealed in rectum

Pune (Maharashtra) [India], May 14 (ANI): The customs department on Monday seized 511.31 grams of gold from a passenger at Pune International Airport here.

A detailed personal and baggage search of the passenger resulted in the recovery of gold concealed in his rectum.

The gold was concealed in the rectum of the passenger, customs officials said.

The recovered gold has been sealed by the customs department.

Further details awaited. (ANI)
