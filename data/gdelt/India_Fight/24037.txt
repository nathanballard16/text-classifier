Major fire at Lamba TVS spares showroom at Nagpur’s Gaddigodam square, lakhs of goods gutted

No casualty was reported in the incident, however, goods worth lakhs of rupees were gutted in the fire. Nagpur: A major fire broke out at a two-wheeler spare parts showroom at Gaddigodam Square, Prabhat Nagar on Nari road early Sunday morning at around 6 am. Sources said that Lamba TVS spare parts showroom owner Rajendra Pal had left for home last night after shutting down the shop. The fire tender rushed to the spot to contain the flames, however by then, the fire had already reduced major quantum of goods to ash. Later in the morning some people noticed the flames emanating from the closed shop.
