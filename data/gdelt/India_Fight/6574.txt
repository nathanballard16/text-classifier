Centre mishandling Kashmir: Samajwadi Party chief Akhilesh Yadav

KANPUR: A day after CRPF jawan Pradeep Singh Yadav lost his life in Pulwama terror attack Samajwadi Party (SP) president Akhilesh Yadav on Friday met his family at his native village Ajan Sukhsenpur in Kannauj district and paid tributes to the departed soul. He said anger was growing in the country over the central government's inability in handling terror. Besides, people are also angry over the way things are being handled in Jammu & Kashmir by the central government. However, here in this house not only a father has lost his son, but sisters too have lost their dear brother. "Anger is growing in the country as the situation is going out of hands in Kashmir.
