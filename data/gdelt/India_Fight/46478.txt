Man murdered his friend

New Delhi: A 22-year-old man murdered his friend on the suspicion of having affair with his girlfriend in Rohini's Kirari Suleman Nagar. Police identified the accused as Monu and his friend Chandan. Investigation revealed that Monu was being accompanied by his close friend Chandan. DCP added that Monu revealed that while supplying mehndi cones, he came in contact with a woman artist and he fell in love with her. Seeking revenge, accused Monu decided to eliminate Raj Kumar.
