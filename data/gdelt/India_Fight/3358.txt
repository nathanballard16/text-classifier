Delhi Youth stabbed to death friend hurt

NEW DELHI: A 20-year-old youth was stabbed to death while his juvenile friend was injured in central Delhi's Patel Nagar on Friday night.The accused has been arrested. The accused, Deepak, got into a fight with the victim, Shanu, for allegedly slapping his friend. Cops said that the incident was the fallout of a personal enmity between two youths who had clashed a few days back.
