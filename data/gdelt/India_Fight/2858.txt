Varun Dhawan kick-starts the shoot of Remo D'souza's dance film - check out the first photos from the location

Varun Dhawan kick-starts the shoot of ABCD 3 in Punjab | Photo Credit: Instagram

Looks like Varun Dhawan is unstoppable! The actor recently winded up shooting for the period drama Kalank, in which he will be playing the male lead and Alia Bhatt will be playing the female lead. And, now he is in Punjab where he started shooting for his next film, ABCD 3 which is going to be a musical drama. The shoot kick-started earlier today and we have got our hands on the first photos from the location where, along with Varun, Bhushan Kumar, Remo D'souza and others are also present.

The actor and the entire crew landed in Punjab yesterday. Varun even went to Amritsar's Golden Temple to seek blessings before he started shooting for his film. And now that the team has started shooting, the happiness is quite visible on the faces of all the cast and crew members. Scroll down to check out the photos. (Also Read: 5 ridiculously expensive things Varun Dhawan owns; his wrist watch is worth Rs 20 lakh – see photos)

The first schedule of Punjab will wrap up in 10 days after which the team will head to London for the next schedule for 40 days. There, the actress of the film, Shraddha Kapoor will also join the team. ABCD 3 also stars Nora Fatehi and Sonam Bajwa in pivotal roles.

One of India’s biggest dance movie, ABCD 3 is being directed by Remo D’Souza and produced by Bhushan Kumar, Krishan Kumar and Lizelle D’Souza. The film is set to hit the silver screen on November 8.
