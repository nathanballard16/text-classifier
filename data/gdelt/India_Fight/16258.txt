India: grenade blast injures 2 policemen

(MENAFN) Police declared that two policemen including an officer were injured on Thursday in a grenade blast in troubled Indian-controlled Kashmir.



The policemen were stormed during a cordon mission at Warpora village of Sopore town in Baramulla district, about 53 km northwest of Srinagar city, the summer capital of Indian-controlled Kashmir.



A police official confirmed: "This morning militants lobbed a grenade at a police party in Warpora Sopore, wounding Station House Officer Dangiwacha and his escort personnel."



"Both of them have been removed to a hospital."
