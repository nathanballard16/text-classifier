Woman’s body found without head and legs

MUMBAI: In the third such case of unidentified bodies being dumped, police on Monday found the headless body of a woman , estimated to be in her late 30s, opposite a state transport bus workshop in Mumbai’s Vidyavihar The remains were wrapped in a bedsheet and first spotted by passersby who alerted police. “We have sent the body for post-mortem and are trying to establish her identity. Her head and legs (from below the knees) are still missing,” said Akhilesh Singh, zonal DCP.Police have registered a case of murder and destruction of evidence. Police said they are scanning CCTV cameras to establish who dumped the body there.“The most important thing is to establish her identity, which will give us the direction for a speedy probe. We are also checking records of missing persons,” an officer said.
