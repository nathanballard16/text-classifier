Bulbul passes state impact contained by govt

'Bulbul', that passed through the coastal districts of the state before finally moving towards Bangladesh, claimed seven lives in different parts of the state so far. The cyclone wrecked havoc on the lives of various crop cultivators in mostly parts of South 24-Parganas, North 24-Parganas and East Midnapore districts. According to state government sources, four people were killed in separate incidents in North 24-Parganas. A man died after being crushed by a falling tree in East Midnapore while another death was reported from South 24-Parganas. Jawed Shamim, Additional Commissioner (I) of the Kolkata Police said four drones have been pressed into service in the affected areas.
