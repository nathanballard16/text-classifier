South Delhi: Pub owner, manager arrested for selling liquor illegally

Investigation also brought to light the owners would mix Indian liquor in foreign liquor The accused persons also stated that they were mixing cheap Indian liquor with foreign liquor and selling it to the guests. A bar in Hauz Khas village was raided after the police officers were informed that the pub was selling liquor to guests without a valid license. When the police officials asked the man at the bar to produce a license, he stated that they did not have a valid liquor license. The owner and the manager of the pub were arrested.
