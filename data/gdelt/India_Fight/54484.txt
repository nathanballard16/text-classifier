Kolkata Police conducts night long raids several eve teasers and unruly bikers held

Kolkata: Kolkata Police, on Friday night, conducted a special drive against eve-teasers, unruly bikers and trouble mongers and arrested several persons and seized many bikes. Based on the information, the Winners team along with the Anti Rowdy Squad (ARS) was instructed to conduct surprise raids in multiple areas including Park Circus, Beniapukur, Park Street and Netaji Nagar. During the raid, the Winners team apprehended 13 eve-teasers and handed them over to the concerned police stations. Police, during the raids, have also seized 51 bikes for not having proper documents and other such violations. It may be mentioned that Kolkata Police regularly conducts raids against drunken drivers in order to prevent accidents in the city.
