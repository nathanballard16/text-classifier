Haryana Congress leader Vikas Chaudhary gunned down in Faridabad

Haryana Congress spokesperson Vikas Chaudhary has been shot in Faridabad on Thursday. Chaudhary was shot at while he was travelling in his car.

The Congress leader later succumbed to his injuries. According to reports, Vikas Chaudhary came out of a gym when he was shot by unknown men.

Around 8-10 rounds were fired at Chaudhary while he was sitting inside his vehicle. He was rushed to a local hospital where he died later.

Police is yet to identify the men who fired the bullets at Congress leader Vikas Chaudhary.

Haryana Congress president Ashok Tanwar said, "It's jungle raj here, there is no fear of law. Same kind of incident happened yesterday where a woman who opposed molestation was stabbed. There should be an investigation."
