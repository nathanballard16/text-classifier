Delhi: Criminal carrying reward of Rs 1 lakh arrested in encounter with police

A criminal, carrying a reward of Rs 1 lakh on his head was arrested during an encounter with the police in Delhi. The incident was reported near Lady Shri Ram (LSR) College in the national capital early on Friday.

The criminal was identified as Iqbal, who reportedly opened fire at the police.

Iqbal had 25 criminal cases registered against him and carried a reward of Rs 1 lakh.
