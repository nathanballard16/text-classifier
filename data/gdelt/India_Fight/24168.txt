Kejriwal appeals to traders to go beyond veil of Modi govt's 'False Nationalism', vote out BJP

Amid the ongoing general elections, Delhi Chief Minister Arvind Kejriwal launched a veiled attack on Prime Minister Modi on Monday. Addressing a press conference in Delhi, the AAP National Convenor said that the nationalism exhibited by PM Modi and his party, the Bharatiya Janata Party, is false and misleading.
