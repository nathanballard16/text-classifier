Delhi communal tension: 3 arrested, including a minor, for vandalising temple

AGENCIES / New Delhi

Delhi Police has arrested 3 people including a minor in connection with the communal clashes that rocked in central Delhi on Sunday night. The clashes had broken out late on Sunday night in Hauz Qazi area in Chawri Bazaar’s Lal Kuan locality between groups belonging to two communities over a parking issue. The issue took a communal turn after a temple was vandalised. The police had appealed to the people to maintain peace and restore normalcy in the area.
