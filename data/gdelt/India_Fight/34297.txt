3 people found murdered inside building compound in Navi Mumbai

The bodies were found inside a building compound in Indira Nagar. (Photo: ANI)

Three people were found murdered inside a building compound in MIDC Industrial Area in Navi Mumbai on Saturday morning, said police.

The deceased have been identified as Irshad Sheikh (20-years-old), Naushad Sheikh (14-years old) and Rajesh (28-years-old).

The bodies were found inside a building compound in Indira Nagar after the police were informed about the incident.

The deceased used to work in the scrap business. Police suspect that an deceased were killed with a sharp weapon.

The bodies have been sent for post mortem examination and the investigation is underway.
