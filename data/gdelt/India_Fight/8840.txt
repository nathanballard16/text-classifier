FM Qureshi calls UAE counterpart, briefs post-Pulwama situation

ISLAMABAD – Foreign Minister Makhdoom Shah Mahmood Qureshi Friday called his UAE counterpart Sheikh Abdullah bin Zayed Al Nahyan to discuss the evolving regional and international situation.

The foreign minister during the telephonic conversation stated that he was looking forward to his upcoming visit to UAE for the OIC Council of Foreign Ministers Meeting (CFM), a Foreign Office statement issued here said.
