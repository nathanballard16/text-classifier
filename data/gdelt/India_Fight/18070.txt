Salman Khan's 'Dabangg 3' goes on floors in Indore

Seven years after 'Dabangg 2' worked its magic on the box-office, the makers of the cop film franchise are back with 'Dabangg 3'.The much-awaited third installment of Bollywood superstar Salman Khan 's most famous franchise 'Dabangg' has started rolling in Indore, Madhya Pradesh . It was earlier reported that the film will go on floors in April this year. The much anticipated 'Dabangg 3' is helmed by ace choreographer and filmmaker, Prabhu Deva . The first installment of the movie series 'Dabangg' (2010) was directed by Abhinav Kashyap while 'Dabangg 2' (2012) was directed by Arbaaz Khan.On Sunday, Salman took to his Twitter handle to share the news that he has landed in Indore with his brother Arbaaz Khan and will begin shooting for the film from Monday. Salman posted a video to announce the news. Salman also shared an emotional connection with the place in the video. Salman said Indore is the birthplace for both the brothers and also said they will be shooting in Mandleshwar and Maheshwar where their grandfather was posted when he was in the police force.'Dabangg 3' will see Sonakshi Sinha and Salman Khan reprising their previous roles from earlier parts. The film will be high on action and is slated to release in December this year.
