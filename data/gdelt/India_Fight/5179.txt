Pakistan breaches truce along LoC in J&K’s Rajouri, jawan hurt

JAMMU: An Indian Army jawan was injured in sniper fire when Pakistan breached truce along the Line of Control (LoC) in Jammu and Kashmir’s Rajouri district on Saturday."Pakistan violated ceasefire at Pokhara Post in Rajouri’s Kalal area in the evening, in which a soldier sustained bullet injuries in a sniper attack. The wounded was airlifted to Command Hospital in Udhampur ," said an official source.
