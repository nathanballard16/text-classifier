Security guard found murdered

Representative image

VADODARA: A private security guard posted in locomotive shed in railway premises at Navayard was found murdered on Monday morning. The deceased identified as Jagjit Jagdish Gajraj was 58-years-old and used to stay in the same locality.

According to the police complaint, Jagjit was posted in the premises to guard the construction material kept there. He began his duty at 10 pm on Sunday and was to be relieved at 8 am on Monday. When Magan Makwana, another security guard, came to resume his duty on Monday morning he found Jagjit dead. The deceased was attacked with sharp weapons on his throat.

The police have begun investigations in the case and is also questioning Jagjit’s friends apart from checking CCTV footages in the locality.
