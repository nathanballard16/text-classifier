BJP worker’s murder: 3 detained

Coimbatore: The rural police on Monday detained three people in connection with the murder of a BJP member on Sunday.

R Santhosh, 26, an astrologer from Kumarasamy Colony on Thadagam Road, was stabbed to death by a six-member gang at Veerakeralam on Sunday afternoon.

Preliminary inquiry revealed that earlier he was the state secretary of Indhiya Desiya Kudiyurimai Katchi. His relatives had forced him to return to the old party six months ago.
