Five workers die while digging well in TN

Chennai: In a tragic accident, five workers engaged in digging a new well in Tamil Nadu’s Tiruvannamalai district were killed on Friday when a rope they had tied to their bodies for safety snapped, police said.

A police officer said that the rope snapped and the five fell down to a considerable depth and died.
