Man shot dead in Rohini

New Delhi, Sep 6 : A 22-year-old man was shot dead by two persons in Rohini's Aman Vihar area, the police said on Friday.

The deceased has been identified as Pushkar, a resident of Aman Vihar, they said.

According to a senior police officer, the deceased was near his house with his pet when two persons came on a black scooter and fired five shots at him.

The victim was rushed to a nearby hospital where he was declared brought dead, police said.

It seems like the incident happened due to personal enmity. The accused have been identified and police are trying to nab them.
