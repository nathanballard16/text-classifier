Governor condemns Naxal attack in Gadchiroli

Vidyasagar Rao has strongly condemned the Naxal attack in Gadchiroli district and conveyed his deepest condolences to the kin of the police personnel who lost their lives in the attack. The reception was cancelled as a mark of respect to the police personnel who lost their lives in the Naxal attack in Gadchiroli district. I condemn the dastardly Naxal attack in strongest words. In a message, the Governor said: “I was shocked and deeply saddened to know about the Naxal attack in Gadchiroli district in which some of our bravest police personnel lost their lives.
