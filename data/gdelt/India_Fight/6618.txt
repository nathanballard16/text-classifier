Gondi police station chief shoots self

AURANGABAD: An assistant police inspector heading the Gondi police station in neighbouring Jalna district committed suicide by shooting in the head with his service pistol on Friday.The incident happened at the official quarters, situated next to the police station, around 11 am. Police have said that the PSI was under pressure after a youth had filed a complaint against him. "It is a case of suicide. "Senior police officers said the API was known to be a cool-headed man. He remained in the chamber till around 11 am and left for home.
