Eight dumpers with illegal sand, mining machines seized

The officials also seized eight dumpers filled with sand.Mining inspector Jaydeep Namdev told TOI that they had received information about illegal sand mining being carried out in backwaters at Badwai village behind Gambhir dam resthouse. INDORE: A joint team of police and Ujjain district administration officials carried out a raid against illegal sand mining being carried out on banks of Gambhir river on Tuesday. The equipment thrown in the river will be fished out soon, Namdev said.“Action against illegal sand mining is being taken as per rules,” Ujjain collector Shashank Mishra said. The team seized three earth movers, two tractors, 1000-metre-long pipeline and two engines from the spot. Five modern set-ups were also put in place for it.
