Kashi Vidyapith student shot dead at Varanasi hotel

Illustration for representational purpose

VARANASI: A student of the Kashi Vidyapith was shot dead by a hotel owner on Monday. The hotel is located near the college of the deceased identified as Shweta Singh.

CO Chetganj Ankita Singh said that the accused hotel owner had been arrested and a team of forensic experts were scanning the spot to collect evidences.

Reports suggested that the girl was known to the accused and was a frequent visitor to the hotel.

The accused claimed that he was in inebriated state at the time of the incident.

According to the parents of the girl, she had come to the town to collect her marksheet and they had talked to her on Sunday. The girl had told her parents that she was staying at her friend's house.
