BHU Student Gaurav Singh Shot Dead On Campus

A Banaras Hindu University (BHU) student died after he was shot at by unidentified men on motorcycle on campus, police said, NDTV reported.

Gaurav Singh, a masters student at the university, was standing with his friends outside the Lal Bahadur Shastri Hostel when unidentified persons on a motorcycle opened fire at him, a police official told PTI.
