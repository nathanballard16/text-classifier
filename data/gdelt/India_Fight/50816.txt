Ludhiana: One dead, another seriously injured in multiple-vehicle collision

LUDHIANA: One person died while another suffered serious injuries after collision between three vehicles, including two heavy vehicles, on Ludhiana-Khanna road near Bija on Saturday.The deceased was identified as 45-year-old Ravinder Singh of Amloh, who was a driver, while the injured person was yet to be identified.As the police got information about the accident they reached the spot and started investigation into the matter.Incharge of the police post said that the driver of a Mahindra pick-up vehicle was booked for causing death due to negligence among other sections and he will be identified soon.
