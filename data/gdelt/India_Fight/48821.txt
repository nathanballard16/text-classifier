UP CM sanctions Rs 15 lakh for Kamlesh Tewari's wife

UP govt announces Rs 15 lakh compensation and a house for Kamlesh Tewari's wife

LUCKNOW: Uttar Pradesh chief minister Yogi Adityanath on Wednesday sanctioned an immediate financial help of Rs 15 lakh to the wife of Kamlesh Tewari , a Hindu outfit leader who was killed last week.A senior state government official said the chief minister also sanctioned a house for the Tewari's kin in tehsil Mehmoodabad in Sitapur district.Adityanath directed officials that those arrested should be tried in a fast track court and strict action should be taken against those involved in the conspiracy.Two suspects, Ashfaq Sheikh (34) and Moinuddin (27), were arrested by the Gujarat ATS on Tuesday evening for allegedly killing Tewari.Tewari, 45, was found dead in a pool of blood at his residence in Naka Hindola area of Lucknow last Friday.
