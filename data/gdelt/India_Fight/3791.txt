Varun Dhawan, Aparshakti Khurana, and Rema D'Souza pose for a picture with the crew after the first schedule of 'ABCD 3'

Bollywood actor Varun Dhawan is currently busy shooting for the third installment of the dance film franchise 'ABCD' directed by Remo D'Souza . The film also stars Shraddha Kapoor Nora Fatehi and Aparshakti Khurana in the lead roles. The latest news from the sets of the film is that the team has finished shooting the first schedule of the movie.According to the latest reports, the team will now head to London for the second schedule which will span for 40 days. The film titled ' ABCD 3 ' is also touted as India's biggest dance movie. The film is slated to hit the theaters on November 8, 2019.Recent reports suggest that Shraddha Kapoor will be playing a Pakistani dancer while Varun Dhawan would be playing a Punjabi boy in the film. Earlier, Katrina Kaif was reportedly roped in to play the character of Shraddha Kapoor in the film. However, later the actress withdrew from the film sighting her busy schedule with the upcoming Salman Khan starrer 'Bharat'.On the professional front, apart from 'ABCD 3', Varun Dhawan will be next seen in Karan Johar's period drama 'Kalank' starring alongside Alia Bhatt, Madhuri Dixit, Sanjay Dutt, and Aditya Roy Kapur. The actor will also make a cameo appearance in 'Bharat' releasing in Eid 2019.On the other hand, Shraddha Kapoor would be seen in the big-budget film 'Saaho' opposite Prabhas. The film is slated to hit the theaters on August 15.
