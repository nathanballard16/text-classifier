Punjab: Body recovered near paddy field; murder case registered

LUDHIANA: Panic gripped Khasi Khurd and Kudyani Kalan villages in Punjab's Ludhiana district after a man was found murdered on Thursday morning. As per the police, the deceased is between 30 to 35 years old and maybe a migrant worker The police officials said that on Thursday morning at around 8.30am, Surinder Singh, a farmer who lives on the Khasi Khurd and Kudyani Kalan road area, called the Meherban police to informed that he saw a body in a pool of blood near his brother’s fields when he was on his way towards his field following which the police recovered the body.SHO Meherban said that they have registered a case under sections 302 (murder), 201 (Causing disappearance of evidence of offence, or giving false information to screen offender) and 34 (Acts done by several persons in furtherance of common intention) of the Indian Penal Code (IPC) against unidentified persons. He added that it appears that more than one person has committed the murder of the deceased.
