Four killed in two accidents in Saurashtra

Representative image

RAJKOT: Four persons were killed in two separate accidents reported from Dwarka of Devbhoomi Dwarka district and Babra in Amreli district since Saturday evening.

The first accident was reported from Dwarka. “Two women of the same family were killed when the car they were travelling in overturned by the roadside when its driver, while trying to save a blue bull, took a sharp swerve,” said an official from Devbhoomi Dwarka police.

The deceased were identified as Raju Manek (22) and Jibai Manek (32). The driver escaped with minor injuries, said police

In Babra, two men were killed when the SUV they were travelling in was hit by a truck on Rajkot-Bhavnagar Road at Galkotadi village. The deceased were identified as Abdul Qureshi (58) and Imran Dodia (30).
