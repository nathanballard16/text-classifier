Rajkot: Leopard attacks farmer

RAJKOT: A farmer was attacked by a leopard in Arjansukh village of Vadia taluka in Amreli bordering Rajkot district . According to forest officials, Ranjit Rathod, was attacked when he was in his farm cutting the fodder for the cattle.The leopard that was hiding behind a tree, pounced on Rathod from behind and injured him. Rathod received injuries on his hand and is under treatment at government hospital.
