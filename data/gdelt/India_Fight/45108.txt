Ludhiana: Gangster arrested with illegal weapon

LUDHIANA: The CIA-1 of Ludhiana police commissionerate arrested gangster Lakhwinder Singh (30) of New Indra Colony in Jassiya village with a .315 bore country-made revolver and two live cartridges. According to cops, the arrested accused is a member of Chotta Lalla gang, and he has almost a dozen cases of attempt to murder, clash, robbery and thefts against him. He was also wanted by Ludhiana police in three cases of attempt to murder, theft and scuffle in Haibowal.
