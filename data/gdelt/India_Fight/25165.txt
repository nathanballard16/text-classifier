'Sacred Games 2', National Geographic posters shot on this smartphone

13 May 2019

OnePlus's first-ever true flagship phone, the OnePlus 7 Pro, is once again making headlines for its camera capabilities.

The National Geographic Magazine has released its first-ever cover that's shot on a phone while Netflix has released two new posters and behind-the-scenes footage of Sacred Games 2. Interestingly, OnePlus 7 Pro was used by both - National Geographic and Netflix.

Here's what makes the phone special.

Let's first talk about OnePlus 7 Pro's all new design

Design

Design wise, OnePlus 7 Pro will feature a bezel-less, notch-less all-screen design achieved by housing the selfie camera in a pop-up module.

This showstopper design will allow the phone to boast an unprecedented screen-to-body ratio of close to around 95%.

Further, an all-glass body, curved screen edges and new color finishes will add to the premium-ness of the upcoming OnePlus flagship.

OnePlus 7 Pro's screen will be a marquee feature

Display

OnePlus 7 Pro will feature a 6.67-inch QHD+ curved AMOLED display with a pixel density of 516ppi and a variable refresh rate of 60-90Hz.

A high refresh rate will allow for smoother UI animations and improved gaming experience.

A recent leak also claimed that the display will be HDR10+ certified, meaning it will offer upto 4,000-nits of peak brightness.

OnePlus 7 Pro will support Netflix HDR as well

Fact

OnePlus has announced that its upcoming premium phone, the OnePlus 7 Pro, will support Netflix HDR. With this, the OnePlus phone joins the coveted club which includes the likes of Huawei Mate 20 Pro and Google Pixel 3 series.

What about the camera specifications?

Camera

As per the leak, OnePlus 7 Pro will offer a triple-lens rear camera comprising a 48MP (f/1.6, OIS) primary sensor, joined by a 16MP (f/2.2) wide-angle lens with 117-degree field-of-view and an 8MP (f/2.4) telephoto sensor with 3x optical zoom.

Up front, the motorized pop-up up camera module is tipped to house a 16MP Sony IMX471 sensor with f/2.0 aperture and EIS.

OnePlus 7 Pro will offer 4K recording at 60fps

Fact

As per the leak, the main camera on OnePlus 7 Pro will support 4K recording at 30fps/60fps, 1080p videos at 30fps/60fps, and super slow-motion 1080p and 720p videos at 240fps and 480fps respectively. The front camera is said to support 1080p video recording at 30fps.

Under the hood

Internals

OnePlus 7 Pro will be backed by a Qualcomm Snapdragon 855 chipset, paired with Adreno 640 GPU, upto 12GB of RAM and upto 256GB of UFS 3.0 internal storage.

Under the hood, it will pack a 4,000mAh battery with 30W Warp Charging support.

Moreover, the phone is tipped to offer improved vibration motor for haptic feedback as well as stereo speakers.

OnePlus 7 Pro: The company's most expensive offering till date

Pricing

Reputed tipster Ishan Agarwal has revealed the India pricing of all the OnePlus 7 Pro models.

The premium phone will cost Rs. 49,999 for the base-end 6GB RAM and 128GB storage model.

Further, the mid-end 8GB RAM and 256GB storage variant and the top-end 12GB RAM and 256GB storage model are tipped to arrive at Rs. 52,999 and Rs. 57,999, respectively.

OnePlus 7 series to launch on May 14

Fact

OnePlus 7 and OnePlus 7 Pro will be officially unveiled on May 14 in India, US, and Europe. In India, the launch event will be held at Bengaluru International Exhibition Centre and it will start at 8:15 pm local time.
