Many injured in bomb blast in northeast India - Xinhua

NEW DELHI, Nov. 5 (Xinhua) -- Many persons, including two policemen, were injured in a bomb blast in India's northeastern state of Manipur on Tuesday, media reports said.

Police cordoned off the area completely soon after the blast. Investigation was on to find out who, or which organization, was behind the bomb blast.

Further details of the incident are awaited.
