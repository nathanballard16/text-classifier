Rohit Shekhar Tiwari's wife Apoorva Shukla detained, taken to undisclosed location for questioning

Rohit Shekhar Tiwari's wife Apoorva Shukla detained | Photo Credit: PTI

New Delhi: Rohit Shekhar Tiwari's wife Apoorva Shukla was on Sunday detained by the Crime Branch of Delhi Police along with two servants in connection with her husband’s death case. Shukla has been taken to an undisclosed location for interrogation, news agency ANI reported.

Rohit, the son of late former Uttar Pradesh and Uttarakhand chief minister ND Tiwari was found dead under mysterious circumstances at his residence in South Delhi’s Defence Colony on April 16. He was brought dead to the Max Hospital in Saket.

On Saturday, Shukla, who is reportedly being seen as a prime suspect in Rohit’s death case, was questioned for hours by the Delhi police.

Rohit’s mother Ujjawala Sharma had earlier said that her son and Shukla had been going through a rough relationship since the first day of their marriage. She also accused Shukla and her family of trying to usurp her son's property, news agency ANI had reported.

"There was tension between Rohit and his wife since the first day of marriage. It was a love marriage; Currently, Delhi crime branch is questioning the wife of Rohit Shekhar Tiwari," Rohit's mother Ujjwala Tiwari had told reporters then.

According to Joint Commissioner Delhi Police Devesh Shrivastava, Rohit suffered a nose bleed which his servants reported to his mother, who was already at Max hospital undergoing health checkup. His autopsy report revealed that he died due to smothering.

On Friday, the Delhi Police had registered a murder case in connection with the death of Rohit, under Section 302 of the Indian Penal Code (IPC).
