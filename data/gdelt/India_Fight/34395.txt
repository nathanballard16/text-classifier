Massive Fire Breaks Out At Ambala's Anaj Mandi

The cause of the fire is yet to be ascertained

A massive fire broke out this morning at Anaj Mandi in Ambala, 45.9 kilometers away from Chandigarh.

Fires tenders have reached the spot and are trying to douse the flames.

The cause of the fire is yet to be ascertained and an investigation is being carried out in this regard.
