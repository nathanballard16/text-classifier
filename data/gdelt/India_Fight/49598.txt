Man found dead at Karaya flat cops to probe property angle in murder

Kolkata: A man was found murdered on Tuesday night at his flat in Karaya. According to sources, on Tuesday, around 8 pm, a person called up Karaya police station and informed that a man has been found murdered. Police found a man, identified as Md Abdul Rafique , who was about 60 years, was lying in a pool of blood. Recently, he had got Rs 26 lakh from the promoter out of which 21 lakh was deposited in his bank account. However, the police are also suspecting his wife could be involved in the murder as after Rafique's death, she would be owner of his property.
