Gujarat: Labourer found murdered in Morbi

RAJKOT: A labourer was found murdered in a farm at Mathak village of Halvad taluka in Morbi district on Saturday.Deceased Bhuro Gujaria (40) was found buried in the farm. His body was dug out by dogs. Farm owner Bharat Makwana saw the body and informed police.Police suspect involvement of Gujaria’s wife Daksha and younger brother Rohan in the crime.“Daksha and Rohan are under our scanner as we suspect that they may be having an extramarital affair and murdered Gujaria,” said an investigating official from Halvad police station. tnn
