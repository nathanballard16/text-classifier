Hit and run: One victim dies

VADODARA: One of the three persons who were injured in the hit and run accident in Fatehgunj died on Wednesday. Dharmesh Mohaniya, 22, a resident of Navayard, succumbed to the severe injuries he received. The police said that the car that mowed down the three, including a 10-month-old baby girl, was identified.Dharmesh and Dhiru Bhuriya, both labourers, were crossing the road near Polytechnic College when the rashly-driven car rammed into them. Cops have registered an offence in the case.
