First female president of UP Bar Council, Darvesh Yadav, shot dead in Agra court

BREAKING: Newly elected UP Bar Council president shot dead in Agra court. (PTI)https://t.co/DklVA2kRqn — ABP News (@abpnewstv) June 12, 2019

Newly elected Uttar Pradesh Bar Council president Darvesh Yadav was shot dead in Agra district court premises, on Wednesday. She was the first female president of the Council in UP. As per the information received, she was shot dead by advocate Manish, who later shot himself as well. He has been admitted in hospital and is receiving treatment. Acting on the information, ADG police along with the force reached the location.
