CM rescues girl asks Mayor to ensure proper medical care

Kolkata: In a humanitarian gesture, Chief Minister Mamata Banerjee on Wednesday rescued an injured woman and sent her to a nearby hospital. The Chief Minister turns a good samaritan for a 20-year-old girl who suffered injuries after on Durgapur bridge. When the Chief Minister was returning after inaugurating the Suruchi Sangha Puja, she noticed the injured the girl lying on Durgapur Bridge. After being instructed by the Chief Minister, the officials took the victim to a nearby private hospital. It may be mentioned here that this is not for the first time that Banerjee came forward to rescue an injured victim.
