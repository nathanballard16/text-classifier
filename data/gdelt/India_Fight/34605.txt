Two prostitution rackets busted; three arrested

RAJKOT: Two prostitution rackets were busted in Bhavnagar on Sunday night in which three persons were arrested.The rackets were busted from Railway Colony and Vadava localities of Bhavnagar.In the raid conducted at Railway Colony, two men aged 24, were arrested, while six women involved in the flesh trade rescued.“The house owner, Firoz Makwana, went absconding after coming to know about the raid,” said an official from Bor Talav police station.In Vadava locality, the officials of Neelambaug police station raided a flat and arrested one man aged 23, and rescued two women.
