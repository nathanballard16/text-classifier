Two killed as motorcycle crashes into 2 two-wheelers in Erode

TIRUPUR: Two people were killed and three others injured after a motorcycle crashed into two two-wheelers near Gobichettipalayam in Erode district on Sunday.Police identified the deceased as Murugan of Atthani and Kumarasamy of Periyakodiveri. A police officer said the incident took place when Murugan was riding his motorcycle on Gobichettipalayam-Savandapur road in the morning.“He lost control over his motorcycle, which hit a bike rode by Kumarasamy and a moped rode by Subramaniam of Ganapathipalayam that were coming from the opposite direction,” the officer said.While Murugan and Kumarasamy succumbed to their head injuries on the spot, Subramanian and his daughter Mangaiyarkarasi, who was riding pillion, sustained injuries. Similarly, Kumarasamy’s relative Bharanitharan, 17, who was also riding pillion, suffered injuries.Other motorists alerted police, who took the injured trio to the government hospital in Gobichettipalayam. The bodies were also sent to the same hospital for postmortem.Police said none of the riders were wearing helmets. They have registered a case and further investigations are on.
