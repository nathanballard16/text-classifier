Haryana Cong leader Vikas Chaudhary shot dead

A A A

Team Udayavani, Jun 27, 2019, 11:45 AM IST

Faridabad: Haryana Congress leader Vikash Chaudhary was shot dead at in Faridabad today by unknown person. However, he later succumbed to his injuries.

Vikas Chaudhary was hit by more than ten bullets by the men after he came out of a gym. He was attacked while travelling in his car in the morning. Ashok Tanwar, Haryana Congress President condemned the gruesome incident and claimed that there is no law and order in the region.

“It is a ‘jungle raj’, there is no fear of law. The same kind of incident happened yesterday, where a woman who opposed molestation was stabbed. There should be an investigation,” Tanwar said.
