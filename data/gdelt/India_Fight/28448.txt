KGF Chapter 2 to be shot at Lalitha Mahal Palace in Mysuru from June 4

Magnum Opus KGF Chapter 2 shoot will be kick started from Mysuru. The film team is planning to shoot at Lalitha Mahal Palace, where some portions of KGF: Chapter 1 was shot. The film which has Yash and Srinidhi Shetty in lead roles is directed by Prashanth Neel Sources tell us that the film shoot begins from June 4, but the portions involving actor Yash will be shot from June 6. In KGF Chapter 1, the sequences of 1980’s Bengaluru was created in Mysuru. Yash(Rocky) meeting Reena (Srinidhi Shetty) was shot opposite to Manasagangothri clock tower. The Lalitha Mahal Palace was converted as palatial house of Garuda (One of the villains) and the sequence of Rocky making first attempt to kill Garuda was canned at Jayalakshmi Vilas Palace by converting it into political party office for shoot purpose. We also hear that makers have approached Bollywood actors Sanjay Dutt and Raveena Tandon to play vital roles in the movie.
