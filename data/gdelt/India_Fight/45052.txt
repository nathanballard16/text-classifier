Priyanka asks MP govt to act in killing of Dalit kids

New Delhi, Sep 26 : Congress General Secretary Priyanka Gandhi took to twitter on Thursday to ask Madhya Pradesh government to act in the killing of two Dalit boys in Shivpuri. She said being a mother, she is very sad with the inhuman act.

She asked the Congress government to act tough against the criminals.

In Bhavkedhi village of Shivpuri in the state, two children were killed for defecating in the open on Wednesday. The children belonged to a Dalit family.

The killers were irked that the children were defecating in front of the panchayat building.

The police said the killers have been arrested and booked for murder.
