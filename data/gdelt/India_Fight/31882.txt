[VIDEO] Delhi Police raids party, arrests 15 members of Kapil Sangwan gang in Goyala Dairy area

Total of 15 people with firearms were apprehended from the place. | Photo Credit: Getty Images

New Delhi: In a major crackdown on criminal gangs in the national capital, the Delhi Police on Monday arrested 15 members of Kapil Sangwan in possession of firearms from outer Delhi's Goyala Dairy area.

Mirror Now reported that the gang has been booked in over 20 cases criminal cases including murder, attempt to murder, extortion and abduction.

The police acted on a tip-off and conducted a raid at a house in Najafgarh area where the accused had gathered to celebrate and party on the release of their leader - Kapil Sanghwan. Additional Commissioner of Police A K Singla told news agency PTI that after they got the tip-off, four police teams were formed which raided the premises at Goyala Dairy. A total of 15 people with firearms were apprehended from the place.

The police officer added that around 50-60 people were partying in the house when the raid was conducted. While the police were conducting the operation, a sub-inspector was stabbed on his left hand.

Reportedly, the accused were identified as 28-year-old Vineet, 23-year-old Vasudev, 22-year-old Sachin, 25-year-old Rahul, 22-year-old Surender Kumar, 22-year-old Chirag, 35-year-old Paramjeet, 22-year-old Pradeep Sehrawat, 23-year-old Abhishek, 25-year-old Naveen, 26-year-old Vicky Dagar, 27-year-old Nishant Dagar, 23-year-old Rohit, 22-year-old Naveen and 22-year-old Deepak.

The prime accused was in Bhondsi jail since 2016 and was released on Monday. However, the police have seized nine pistols and 64 rounds from their possession. Also, 12 out of 15 arrested have criminal records against them.
