Clashes break out in Ludhiana jail, 4 cops, 6 prisoners injured

Clashes broke out at the Ludhiana Central Jail on Thursday afternoon, forcing police to deploy officers inside the jail premises. Four prisoners tried to escape amid the commotion, who were later brought back.

A fire was also started inside the jail during the clashes and the fire brigade was rushed to the jail, reported ANI.

Six prisoners were injured in the clashes while four policemen also sustained injuries.

While four prisoners who tried to escape were brought back, officials will do a final head count in evening.
