Minor dead, 18 YO critical after a knife attack by men

Jaipur: A knife attack by unidentified men on two teenage girls killed a 16-year-old victim on the spot while gravely injured the other victim.

The incident is reported from Rajasthan’s Nagpur District where Vimla died on the spot while her elder sister Mamta(18) is in a critical situation after the two were stabbed by two men when the sisters were going to their farm.

Mamta was rushed to a nearby hospital with critical injuries said Station Hose Officer, Ladnu, Mukut Bihari.

Additional SP Nitesh Arya said the two individual have been detained while the other attackers are on the run.

The victim’s body was sent for post-mortem.
