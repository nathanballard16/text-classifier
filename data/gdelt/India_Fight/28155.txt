SP worker shot dead by six miscreants in Jaunpur dist

Varanasi: A Samajwadi Party (SP) leader Lalji Yadav, 45, was shot dead by six unidentified assailants in Sarai Khwaza area of Jaunpur district on Friday. The police have registered an FIR and investigating the matter.

SP president Akhilesh Yadav has condemned the murder. A probe will be conducted into the incident at party level, according to a SP release issued in Lucknow.

According to reports, Lalji, a resident of Udli village, was going to Jaunpur in his SUV. His vehicle slowed down at a speed- breaker when six masked miscreants on three motorcycles intercepted him and opened indiscriminate fire. According to eyewitness accounts, the miscreants left the place only after they were sure that Lalji had died.

Police reached the spot after getting information and sent the body for postmortem.

Lalji, who worked as a contractor in the district, had several criminal cases registered against him, police added.

SHO Ramesh Kumar Yadav said the killing of Lalji, a SP leader, was being probed from different angles. An FIR has been lodged against unidentified persons in this connection, he added.
