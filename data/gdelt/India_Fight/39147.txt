Key D-company man held at Kerala airport

MUMBAI: A key D gang member, who was a close aide of Anees Ibrahim, was arrested from Kerala on Monday by Mumbai Police ’s anti-extortion cell (AEC).Mohammad Altaaf Saeed holds two Indian passports and used to frequently travel between India and Dubai. He has been handling hawala operations for Anees, police said. He used to live in Vashi.“Acting on specific information, we intercepted Saeed at Kannur airport after he landed from Dubai. He was brought to Mumbai and interrogated. We found his involvement in a 2017-18 case, where a hotelier had been threatened for extortion,” AEC said.Last year, police had arrested Ramdas Rahane, a D gang shooter, for threatening a hotelier from the western suburbs at Anees’ behest. When the hotelier registered a complaint with AEC, four persons were arrested under the stringent Maharashtra Control of Organised Crimes Act (MCOCA); they included a hawala operator.A charge-sheet has already been submitted to court. Saeed was booked in that case and produced before a special MCOCA court, which remanded him to police custody till August 16.
