Suicide attempt over family dispute

Kolkata: Tension cropped up at Jibantala in South 24-Parganas after a person was reportedly shot at his home. Later, it was found that he tried to commit suicide by shooting himself over a family dispute. On Tuesday, the injured youth admitted that he had tried to commit suicide by shooting himself due to a family dispute over his two wives. But she expressed her willingness to return on condition that her husband will have to leave the second wife. Amidst the situation, the injured youth was going through a depression and thus he tried to commit suicide.
