3 terrorists killed in encounter with security forces in J&K; Operations underway at 4 places

An encounter is underway between terrorists and security forces in Shopian district of Jammu and Kashmir and three terrorists have been decimated so far.

Security Forces (Source: PTI)

Two bodies have been recovered so far following the encounter between terrorists and security forces in Keller.

The gunfight broke out in the early hours of Thursday in Shopian’s Keller area.
