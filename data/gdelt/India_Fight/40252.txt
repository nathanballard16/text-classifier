Assam Rifles troops apprehend five PREPAK militants in Nagaland- Review Nepal News





Guwahati: Security personnel had apprehended five militants of Manipur based outfit PREPAK with arms in Nagaland's Mon district on Monday.





According to the reports, the troops of Assam Rifles had apprehended the militants while they entered into India from Myanmar by crossing border at Chen Moho area in Mon district.





A top official of Assam Rifles said that, the militants had entered into India from Myanmar by crossing the international border and they were equipped with arms.





The Assam Rifles personnel had recovered one MQ Rifle along with sling, threeMQ Rifle magazines, 98 rounds 7.62 mm bullets, two Lathode guns, one torch, one wallet and Rs 240 cash in possession from them.
