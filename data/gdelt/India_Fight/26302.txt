Dabangg 3: Mouni Roy Is NOT A Part Of This Salman Khan Starrer?

Salman Khan’s Dabangg 3 is making headlines for several reasons. While the shoot for the first schedule was wrapped up recently in Indore, the news also broke in stating the star, himself, to feature in an item number. Now the latest piece of rumours suggests that actress Mouni Roy is been roped in for item song along with Khan, just joining the league of Malaika Arora and Kareena Kapoor Khan.

Advertisement

A source close to the movie cleared the air by stating, “Rumours have been floating around Mouni Roy being considered for a special dance number in Dabangg 3, but that’s not true”, reports Pinkvilla.

“It’s just a rumour and there is no truth to it. Mouni not been approached for Dabangg 3“, the source adds.

Dabangg 3, being helmed by Prabhudheva, has been locked for release on December 20.

The film, being produced under the banner of Salman Khan Films and Arbaaz Khan Production, also stars Sonakshi Sinha, reprising her role of Rajjo.

Kannada actor Sudeep, who has started shooting for Salman Khan’s “Dabangg 3“, has thanked the superstar for making him feel at home.

“Heat was unbearable, yet couldn’t dominate the energy on set. It was a thrilling day, fabulous unit, fantabulous people, a humongous gym set up on location is an added bonus. The first day of ‘Dabangg 3‘ wraps up with smiles. Thanks, Salman Khan sir for making me feel at home,” Sudeep tweeted.
