POLICE FIRE ON VIOLENT MOB

vinaymishra188@gmail.com

HYDERABAD, May 28 (PTI)—Police opened fire on a riotous crowd believed to be supporters of separate Telangana at Yadula village in Mahboobnagar district last night. The 500-strong crowd indulged in stone-throwing in which three policemen were injured. In Hyderabad city, the house of a municipal councillor, Mr. Yadgiri, who is a supporter of the united Andhra Pradesh, was set on fire by a crowd, which threw stones, smashed lights and window-panes of the house and after breaking open the main door poured petrol on the furniture and set it ablaze. A four-year-old child and a relative of Mr. Yadgiri were hit by stones.
