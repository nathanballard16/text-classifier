Police raids at adulterated Ghee factory, arrest one

Hyderabad: Task Force of Hyderabad City Police raided a ghee Centre which was adulterating ghee . Police seized large quantity of adulterated ghee.

According to the report, P.N. Naveen (24) resident of Uper Basti, Chilkalguda was adulterating ghee with Dalda for the past one year.

He used to claim that the ghee he prepared is pure.

Police seized the machine and a large quantity of adulterated ghee.

Task Force also raided the residence of Naveen and arrested him.

He was produced in the court. The magistrate sent him to jail.
