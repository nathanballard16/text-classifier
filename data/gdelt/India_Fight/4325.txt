2 ASIs hurt in brawl over clearing jam, 2 held

AMRITSAR: An ASI of Punjab Police had to open fire to save his colleague from goons here. Police have arrested Gurpreet, a resident of Khela, and Sukhjinder Singh, a resident of Bhagwan, while the four others managed to flee.Balwinder was admitted to Guru Nanak Dev Hospital here. Sources said that the accused also severely damaged the car of Jassa, who was admitted to a private hospital for treatment. Later, police managed to arrest two persons in this regard.Police said Gehri Mandi police post in-charge ASI Jassa Singh was going to police chowki around 8pm on Sunday when he got struck in a traffic jam at Jandiala Guru chowk. In the melee he also got injured.
