Truck rams into bike, two killed

RAJKOT: Two persons were killed when a truck rammed into the motorcycle they were riding on, on Morbi-Wankaner Highway near Lalpar village of Morbi taluka on Sunday morning.The deceased were identified as Hemant Sahu (37) and his brother-in-law Pradeep Padhiyar (32).“Padhiyar died on the day of his marriage anniversary. Both the victims were from Odisha . Sahu had a packaging contract with a factory, and had called Padhiyar to work with him. Both were going to work when the accident occurred,” said an official from Morbi taluka police station.Meanwhile, the police have launched a manhunt for the truck driver, whose identity could not be ascertained as he fled the spot after the accident.
