Coimbatore: Two brothers killed as car rams bike

COIMBATORE: Two brothers , who were on the way from Tirupur to Coimbatore on a motorcycle, were killed when a car rammed the vehicle at Karumathampatty here on Saturday.Police said the brothers, A Muthukumar, 56, and A Chandrasekar, 49, were natives of Tirupur. “They were dealing in waste cloths from textile units and were travelling to Coimbatore in connection with the business. When they reached Karumathampatty on Avinashi Road, a car rammed the two-wheeler from behind. The brothers died on the way to a hospital,” a police officer said.Police are on the lookout for car driver G Baiju of Chennai.
