Nayanthara to act in RJ Balaji's 'Mookkuthi Amman'?

After the super hit 'LKG', RJ Balaji is currently busy with the pre-production work of his next film 'Mookkuthi Amman'. Vels Films International, producers of 'LKG' are bankrolling this film which will soon go on floors.





The latest update we hear is that Nayanthara will be seen playing a pivotal role in 'Mookkuthi Amman'. It is not official and we are not sure whether she is paired opposite Balaji in the film or not but sources say that she plays an important character taking the story forward.
