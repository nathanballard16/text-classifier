Ajit Doval reappointed as National Security Adviser, gets Cabinet rank

Major Indian Operations under Ajit Doval

India conducted two of its most significant anti-terror operations, the Indian Line of Control strike in September 2016 (Surgical Strike) and 2019 Balakot airstrike, under the supervision of Ajit Doval.

1. In September 2016, the Indian Army conducted the surgical strike on Pakistan in the aftermath of the Uri terror attack.

2. In February 2019, the Indian Air Force (IAF) conducted airstrikes in Balakot, Pakistan after the Pulwama terror attack on February 14.

3. Doval is also credited with solving the Doklam standoff between India and China in 2017 when the two countries were engaged in a bitter deadlock for several months.
