Chennai: 19-year-old student commits suicide by hanging herself at hostel

The deceased was a second-year BSC student. (Picture for representation)

A 19-year-old student allegedly committed suicide on Sunday by hanging herself at her college hostel in Tamil Nadu' Chennai.

Reportedly, the student was found dead inside her hostel room at the Tamil Nadu Central University campus by a friend.

The deceased, identified as Maithili, hailed from Hosur in Tamil Nadu. She was a second-year BSC student.



The police have sent her body for an autopsy and further investigation is underway.
