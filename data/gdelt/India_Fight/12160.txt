Masood Azhar dead? Here's what we know so far

The social media was on Sunday filled with reports suggesting Jaish-e-Mohammed chief Masood Azhar has died.

JeM chief Masood Azhar. FILE Photo: AFP

Several reports have emerged on the social media suggesting Jaish-e-Mohammed chief Masood Azhar has died.

However, there was no confirmation from any authoritative sources.

Reports claimed Masood Azhar was seriously injured in the air strike carried out by the IAF in Balakot.
