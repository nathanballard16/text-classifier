Minor fire breaks out at xerox centre near NEERI

Nagpur: A minor fire broke out in the backyard of a Xerox Centre near NEERI after a rag-picker tossed a burning bidi in the premises on Saturday afternoon. With a thought to utilize the Ramnavami holiday for cleaning purpose, Bhuleshwar Mate, owner of the Xerox Centre had called a rag-picker. As soon as he done with drags, he tossed the bidi in the premises which triggered the fire, said a Fire Official. However the Fire Department reached the spot and douses the flames before it turned severe. Right before starting his work, he lighted the bidi and started enjoying puffs.
