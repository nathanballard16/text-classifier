Fire at Howrah cotton warehouse

Kolkata: A major fire broke out at warehouse full of cotton at Bandhaghat in Howrah on Monday afternoon. According to sources, locals at Bandhaghat area on Monday around 12:30 pm, saw smoke coming out of a warehouse where businessmen store cotton on rent. As the materials inside the warehouse were inflammable, it spread all over the warehouse within minutes. Four fire tenders arrived and fire fighters started operations. Fire fighters were trying to control the fire within the warehouse, but the flames had spread in few other small warehouses adjacent to the main warehouse.
