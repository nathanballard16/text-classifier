Hyderabad: Fire breaks out in IT company, no casualties

A major fire broke out at an IT firm located in Basheerbagh area of Hyderabad on Wednesday. No casualties have been reported till now.

According to Viswa Prasad, DCP, Central Zone, "The fire broke out at the fifth floor of the Khan Lateef Khan building. All the employees have come out from the place of the incident. Attempts to douse the fire are underway."

10 fire tenders are present at the spot along with Greater Hyderabad Municipal Corporation disaster rescue teams.
