Salman Khan and Katrina Kaif’s photo has left Shweta Bachchan lovestruck and we share her sentiments

Today morning, our social media feed reflected an amazing picture of Katrina Kaif and Salman Khan after they wrapped up the shooting of Bharat and soon after seeing the picture, we were left lovestruck. In this picture, Katrina Kaif and Salman Khan are all smiles. Alongside the picture, Katrina’s caption read as, “And that’s a Picture wrap on Bharatits been the most incredible & exciting character for me, the whole process of making this film has been so inspiring .....thank u @aliabbaszafar and @beingsalmankhan @atulreellife ..the bestest boys and Alvira khan ( the bestest girl).”

Now just like all their zillion fans who couldn’t take their eyes off this stunning picture, we had Shweta Bachchan who was left awestruck with this kick-ass picture of Salman Khan and Katrina Kaif as she left a lovestruck emoticon in the comments. Well, this is not the first time that Katrina and Salman are coming together as prior to Bharat, Salman Khan and Katrina Kaif have featured together in a series of films such as Tiger Zinda Hai, Partner, Yuvvraj, Ek Tha Tiger and many more.

Last month, the first teaser of Bharat was released and the audiences were treated to five different avatars of Salman. While the shooting of Bharat kick-started in Malta, the cast and crew have shot in various other locations such as Abu Dhabi, Punjab and Delhi. Directed by Ali Abbas Zafar, the film stars Salman Khan, Katrina Kaif, Tabu, Sunil Grover and Disha Patani in lead roles.
