Ludhiana: Man arrested for allegedly shooting at girlfriend

Dramatic scenes were witnessed at the Main Chowk market in Mullanpur Dakha town of Ludhiana Sunday when a man allegedly opened fire at his girlfriend after chasing her on the street. Watch | Police arrest man in Ludhiana who chased and fired upon his girlfriend. The woman received a bullet injury. He later hid inside a public washroom and also allegedly tried to open fire at the police team before he was arrested. Police said that both were in a relationship but it was on verge of a break-up as the man suspected her of infidelity.
