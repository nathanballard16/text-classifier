4 criminals held after exchange of fire in separate incidents

Ghaziabad: Four criminals were arrested after the exchange of fire in different instances since Wednesday night, police said. SSP Sudhir Kumar Singh said the Sahibabad police, during a routine checking, signalled two bike-borne men to stop near the Farukh Nagar intersection. He has been identified as Zulfikar. In the second incident, the Kotwali police nabbed a goon after an exchange of fire near the Vijay Nagar bypass flyover.He has been identified as Vijay Kumar. In the third incident at 11.15 pm on Wednesday, the Loni police held a goon during a checking at Sewa Dham.
