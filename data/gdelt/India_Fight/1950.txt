Armies of India, Pak exchange fire along LoC in Rajouri

Srinagar

Armies of both India and Pakistan on Tuesday exchanged gunfire along the Line of Control in Sunderbani and Nowshera areas of Rajouri district of Jammu division.

Reports said that the firing started 11 am in Malla Sunderbani area of Rajouri district.

An official said that the armies of the two countries also exchange fire in Kalal forward area of Nowshera. “Small arms firing started at about 1 pm,” said an official.

“The firing started at 1 pm and was going on when last reports came,” he said.

The official said that there was no loss of life or injury to anyone in the firing.
