Kareena Kapoor Khan & Karisma Kapoor strike a pose with mother Babita in this adorable PIC; check it out

Most of the Bollywood stars prefer to go on vacations in order to get away from the monotony of their workload for some time. However, some of the celebrities also continue with their work during their vacation period. This is what Kareena Kapoor Khan and husband Saif Ali Khan have been doing in London right now. While Saif has been shooting for his upcoming movie Jawaani Jaaneman, Kareena, on the other hand, will begin shooting for Angrezi Medium in London soon. Recently, Kareena's sister Karisma Kapoor joined them too on their vacation.

The trio have been seen spending some leisure time in London the pictures of which have been shared on social media. Recently, Karisma Kapoor has shared an adorable picture along with her sister Kareena and mother Babita which will definitely melt our hearts. The three of them were all smiles as they posed together for the picture. Karisma also added a sweet caption along with the picture which read, 'Like mother like daughters.'

On the work front, Kareena Kapoor Khan is currently judging the reality show Dance India Dance 7 because of which she has to keep juggling between her shoot for the show as well as her shoot for the movie Angrezi Medium in which she will feature opposite Irrfan Khan. Recently, she had to make a 12 hour trip from London to Mumbai to shoot for one of the episodes of the show. Meanwhile, Kareena has been receiving a lot of positive response from the audiences for her stint as a judge of Dance India Dance 7.
