Fire breaks out at factory in Delhi's Bawana

Fire breaks out at factory in Delhi's Bawana (Representative image) | Photo Credit: Getty Images

New Delhi: A fire broke out at a factory in outer Delhi's Bawana on Friday morning, an official said.

A call about the fire was received at 9 AM after which four fire tenders were rushed to the spot, a senior official of the Delhi Fire Service said. No injury or casualty was reported so far, he said.
