Passerby killed as criminals fire on police in Delhi

New Delhi: A 50-year-old passer-by was killed after getting hit by a bullet fired by a miscreant at a policeman in Delhi's Nand Nagri area, police said. On inquiry, the car sped away, but the constable tried to chase it. "During the chase, a man from the car opened fire at the constable, but the bullet hit a passer-by.
