One held with 25 cases of liquor

Tribune News ServiceLudhiana, November 3The Dugri police today claimed to have nabbed a liquor smuggler and recovered 25 cases of illicit liquor from his possession. The arrested smuggler has been identified as Kamaljit Singh, a resident of LIG Flats, Phase I, Dugri. Head constable Daljit Singh said yesterday, a police party patrolling the area near Flower Chowk got a tip-off that the liquor smugglers were on their way to deliver a large quantity of illicit liquor to someone. The police raided the spot and nabbed Kamaljit, and impounded the car laden with illicit liquor. The police said the suspects brought the illicit liquor from the neighbouring states and sold it to their clients in Ludhiana.
