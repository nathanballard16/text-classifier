Pakistan Violates Ceasefire Along LoC In Jammu and Kashmir's Poonch

Firing stopped at 8:00 pm in the night.

Pakistan on Tuesday violated ceasefire along the Line of Control in Jammu and Kashmir's Poonch district, Defence spokesperson said. At about 6:30 pm Pakistan initiated ceasefire violation by firing of small arms and shelling with mortars in Poonch sector (J&K). Indian Army retaliated befittingly to the unprovoked firing by the Pakistani side.
