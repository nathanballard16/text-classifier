Nirbhaya convicts should hang at the earliest: Kejriwal

New Delhi, Dec 13 (IANS) Delhi Chief Minister Arvind Kejriwal on Friday demanded that the convicts in the 2012 Nirbhaya gang-rape and murder case should be hanged at the earliest.

"Just like the country, we too want the convicts to be hanged at the earliest. All the pending formalities should be completed fast," he said.
