Cong leader injured as gun misfires

He told police that the gun had fired accidentally and he got shot above the ear in the head. In his statement, Ahir denied that he had attempted suicide. Police said that Ahir owned a petrol pump and was politically active since last 10 years. According to police, Kutch NSUI president and district secretary Mahesh Ahir (32) was rushed to a private hospital in Gandhidham late night on Sunday after he got shot in the head. Initially, it was believed that Ahir had shot himself.He was later shifted to a private hospital in Rajkot where he gained consciousness on Tuesday and recorded his statement with police.
