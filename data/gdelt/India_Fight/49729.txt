Drug smuggler shot dead by motorcycle-borne assailants in Phillaur

amansharma@tribunemail.com

Ashok Kaura

Phillaur, October 31

Panic gripped Phillaur on Thursday evening when three unidentified motorcycle-borne assailants shot dead notorious drug smuggler Harpreet Singh alias Chintu in a crowded market.

Eyewitnesses said Harpreet was standing near a juice shop when the masked assailants pumped bullets into him. Senior Superintendent of the Police (Rural) Navjot Singh Mahal and Phillaur DSP Davinder Singh Attri rushed the spot.

The SSP said nine empty cartridges were found from the spot. An injured Harpreet was taken to a Ludhiana hospital where he was declared brought dead, he said, adding that personal enmity could be the reason behind the incident.

Fingerprint experts and a dog squad were summoned for further investigation. The SSP said the deceased was a drug smuggler and was facing six criminal cases. He was in jail since 2014 and had come out on bail only 10 days ago. The police have registered a case under Section 302/34 of the IPC and the Arms Act.
