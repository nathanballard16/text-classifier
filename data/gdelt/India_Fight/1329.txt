Hyderabad: Husband brutally kills wife, arrested

Hyderabad: Mr. Ravi Kumar, Inspector of Police, Trimulgherry said that a person was arrested under the charges of murdering his wife.

According to the details of the case, N. Krishna, a cab driver married Ashwini in 2011. The couple has a son.

For the past few years, the wife and husband were quarreling.

After quarreling with husband, wife had gone to her parents’ house.

On 29th December, Krishna promised to lead a peaceful life and brought her to his house.

Later, he attacked and killed her. He also dumped a heavy stone on her head.

Police registered a case and produced him in the court. The Magistrate sent him to jail.
