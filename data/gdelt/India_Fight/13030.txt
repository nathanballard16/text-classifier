Fire breaks out at CGO Complex in New Delhi; 24 fire tenders at the spot

Fire at CGO Complex: A major fire broke out on the fifth floor of Pandit Deendayal Antyodaya Bhawan at CGO Complex in New Delhi Wednesday morning. As many as 24 fire tenders are present at the spot to douse the blaze. The building, earlier known as Paryawaran Bhawan, houses many important central government offices. Officials at the spot have not commented on whether there were people inside the building but rescue operations are underway. The 11-storeyed building also houses several ministries including the Ministry of Social Justice Empowerment and Disability and the Ministry of Environment, Forest and Climate Change.
