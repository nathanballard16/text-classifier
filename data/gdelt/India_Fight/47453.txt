Nazmus Sadat arrested in Abrar murder case

Police have detained another accused in connection with Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology (BUET) student Abrar Fahad’s murder.

The detainee is identified as ASM Nazmus Sadat. He is one of the accused named in the case filed over Abrar's death.

Md Masudur Rahman, deputy commissioner of Dhaka Metropolitan Police's (DMP) Media and Public Relations Division, said police arrested him from Katla Bazar area under Birampur Thana in Dinajpur on Monday midnight while he was fleeing to India.

Police have arrested so for 20 persons in connection with Abrar Fahad’s murder case.

Dhaka/Maksud/Mukul
