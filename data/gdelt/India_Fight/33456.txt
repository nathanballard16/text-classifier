Allahabad: History-sheeter shot dead near Iradatganj

ALLAHABAD: Three unidentified armed and bike-borne assailants shot dead a history-sheeter, identified as Piyush Shukla (23), a resident of Bagbana near a brick kiln center, Iradatganj area under the limits of Ghupur police station in Up's Allahabad, late on Saturday night.The police have seized four cartridges of 0.32 bore, and a food packet near the spot.SP (Crime) Ashutosh Mishra told TOI that the history-sheeter was probably attacked by his rivals when he was taking a food packet from a nearby local eatery (dhaba).The attackers might have been tracing him from dhaba and intercepted the slain criminal’s bike near Iradatganj area.Circumstantial evidences revealed that at least four bullets were fired on the victim and the assailants had the lone intention to eliminate him, said police. However, the exact reason behind the killing was yet to be ascertain.Police have formed two police teams to crack down the murder.The SP, meanwhile, said that over a dozen criminal cases, including two murders, were pending against the slain history-sheeter at Naini and Ghurpur police stations of the city.He also added that the slain criminal was placed under history-sheeter– seven list with Ghurpur police station. It was reported that the slain criminal was bailed out two months back.The family members of slain criminal in their FIR lodged with Ghurpur police have named one Shailendra and three others for the killing of the criminal. Slain Piyush had been accused of attacking Shailendra in February last.Besides, the slain criminal’s name also surfaced when he had allegedly threatened the nephew of an ex-BJP MP of dire consequences a few months back in Naini area.
