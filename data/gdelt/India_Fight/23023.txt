Two persons suffer burn injuries in fire after gas cylinder leak

Pune: At least two members from a family suffered burn injuries on Tuesday after a fire broke out due to an LPG leak in Pune.



The incident took place at around 8 am, on the third floor of the building located at Shitala Devi chowk in Guruwar Peth.



A mother (55) and a son (30) were injured in the incident.



The injured have been admitted to Surya hospital for treatment. However, several materials from the house were destroyed in the fire.
