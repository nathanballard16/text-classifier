Two cyclists among three dead in separate road accidents

Nagpur: Two cyclists were among three persons killed in separate accidents in the city on Thursday. An offence was registered at Yashodhara Nagar police station against the truck driver. He was rushed to a private hospital but later shifted to Government Medical College and Hospital (GMCH), where doctors declared him dead. Kalamna police have booked the driver of the killer vehicle, Nikhilesh Gajbhiye.In another incident, cyclist Krishna Barapatre, 43, was killed after a heavy truck crushed him under the wheels near Ita Bhatti Chowk. Sitabuldi police have registered a case of accidental death.
