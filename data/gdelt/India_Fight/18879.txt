Fire breaks out at Air India Building near Delhi Airport Terminal 1

The development comes just three days after a major fire broke out at an illegal plastic factory Samaypur Badli area of the national capital. The fire is under control but the operation (to douse it) is still on," an official was quoted as saying by IANS. The blaze also affected services at the Trauma Centre, which caters to patients from all across the National Capital Region, apart from the city. According to reports, fire tenders have reached the spot and efforts are on to douse the fire. A fire broke out at Air India building near Terminal 1 of Delhi Airport.
