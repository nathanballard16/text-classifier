Security official shoots self inside DRDO premises

BRIEFLYNew Delhi: A 51-year-old official posted with the Defence Security Corps, which provides security cover to Defence installations, allegedly shot himself dead inside the DRDO complex in North Delhi's Civil Lines area on Tuesday, said the police. The security official was identified as Hardeep Singh, a native of Punjab, they said.
