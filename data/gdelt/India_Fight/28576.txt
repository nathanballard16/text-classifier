Journalist murdered in India's Mumbai - Xinhua

Source: Xinhua| 2019-06-04 20:18:44|Editor: Wu Qin

NEW DELHI, June 4 (Xinhua) -- A 38-year-old journalist was found murdered in India's financial capital Mumbai in the wee hours of Tuesday, local media reports said.

Identified as Anand Narayan, the journalist, who worked for a local newspaper, was found dead with his throat slit.

CCTV footage showed three unidentified people entering his house building at around 2:00 a.m. local time. Police have registered a murder case, and are on the lookout for the trio, added the reports.
