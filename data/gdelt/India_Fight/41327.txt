Gujarat: Man fires at estranged wife

RAJKOT: Irked at her for filing a complaint of domestic violence against him, a man shot at his estranged wife in her thigh near Iskcon Mega city in Bhavnagar on Wednesday afternoon. Police identified the accused as Ashok Choithani (25) and the victim as Harvi Moradia (19).“In her complaint, Harvi alleged that Choithani had been angry with her ever since she filed a complaint of domestic violence against him and walked out on him to live with her parents,” said J J Rabari, inspector with Bhavnagar A division police station.Harvi and Choithani had a love marriage. Choithani sells sandwiches in his roadside eatery which Harvi frequented. Both entered into a relationship and decided to get married in February this year. However, barely three months into their marriage, the duo started to squabble frequently, following which Harvi walked out on Choithani and also lodged a complaint against him in July.On Wednesday, Harvi was returning home from college when Choithani waylaid her and opened fire at her from his country-made pistol. Harvi was rushed to Sir T Hospital, where her condition is stated to be out of danger. Choithani is yet to be arrested, said cops.
