Woman cop molested, eight others attacked in 3 cases

Nine cops, including a woman police sub-inspector (WPSI), were attacked while on duty by the miscreants. Nagpur: Police personnel came under attack thrice between Tuesday night and Wednesday morning at Ganeshpeth Mankapur and Hudkeshwar areas. Police have booked the accused for criminal force to deter public servant from discharge of his duty, vandalism and unlawful assembly. They arrested Kamble and Rajurkar while the injured cops were rushed to Mayo Hospital, where they are admitted. Head constable Kishor Dhapke and constable Prakash Sonone were putting jammers on illegally parked vehicles.
