Three cattle smugglers arrested

VARANASI: The police arrested three cattle smugglers and recovered 11 animals from a truck in Rohaniya area on Tuesday evening.According to reports, during a checking drive the police stopped a truck (UP 75 AT 1116) near Lathia crossing and recovered 11 bulls. The police also recovered a fake number plate. The arrested persons were identified as Shahnawaz, Maqsood and Rashid Ali, all residents of Etawah district.The police said that the arrested persons were booked under sections 307 (attempt to murder), 419 (cheating by personation), 420 (cheating and dishonestly inducing delivery of property), 467 (forgery) and 468 (forgery for purpose of cheating) of IPC, Prevention of Cow Slaughter Act and Prevention of Animal Cruelty Act.
