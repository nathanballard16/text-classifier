Union Home Minister Amit Shah allotted former PM Atal Bihari Vajpayee’s bungalow

Union Home Minister Amit Shah has been allotted former PM Atal Bihari Vajpayee’s bungalow, located at Krishna Menon Marg in central Delhi. Mr. Shah, on Tuesday, moved into Vajpayee’s bungalow.

After the demise of former PM Mr. Vajpayee on August 15, the bungalow was lying vacant.

As per the rules, the MPs are required to vacate their bungalows within a month of the dissolution of the Lok Sabha, according to which, they should have been vacated by June 25, as the 16th Lok Sabha dissolved on May 25. As the bungalows were still equipped by the MPs, they were last week, given a deadline of seven days to vacate their government allotted accommodations, mostly, the heritage bungalows located in the posh Lutyen’s zone.
