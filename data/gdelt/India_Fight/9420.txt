VARANASI: A 25-year-old BCom student named Vivek Singh was shot dead outside his hostel in UP College of Varanasi under Shivpur police station on Sunday night.As tension prevailed in the college after the murder, a heavy posse of police and PAC personnel was deployed in and around the campus on Monday. He had issued threats to Vivek.The SSP said, "Nine teams of police were deployed and the case would be worked out soon. SSP Anand Kulkarni said nine teams of police have been set up to work out the case at the earliest.Police said Vivek, a native of Azamgarh district , was shot eight times when he came out of his hostel for some work on Sunday night. The situation in the college is under control."
