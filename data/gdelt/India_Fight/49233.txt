Three die in road accident

Rajkot: Three persons were killed when their two-wheeler was rammed by an unidentified vehicle on an overbridge in Vondh village of Bhachau taluka in Kutch on Sunday morning.

The deceased were identified as Pathaisha Sheikh (24), Yasinsha Sheikh (17) and Sikandar Chauhan (21).

“They were going from Rapar to Bhachau at 2.30am when they met with the accident. They died on the spot,” said A K Makwana, sub-inspector with Bhachau police station.

“We are checking CCTV footages of the toll booth ahead of the spot to identify the vehicle that had caused the accident,” added Makwana. TNN
