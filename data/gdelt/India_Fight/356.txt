Hyderabad man arrested for cheating girlfriend

A man was held on Wednesday for allegedly cheating his girlfriend here at Banjara Hills in the city.

According to the police, the victim, a model befriended Saikrishna while she was returning home from shooting. After some time, the duo continued to be in a live-in relationship. However, when the woman put the marriage proposal, Saikrishna refused to marry her.

Based on the complaint registered by the model, the Banjara Hills police arrested Saikrishna.
