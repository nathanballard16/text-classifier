Pak Drone Shot Down by Indian Su-30MKI Fighter: Reports

Su-30MKI

A Pakistani drone was shot down by an Indian Sukhoi Su-30MKI fighter jet on Monday in Bikaner sector (Rajasthan state) of the Indo-Pak border.

The "spy" drone was detected by a ground-based radar station. Minutes later, one of the Sukhoi jets deployed in the area shot it down using an air-to-air missile, PTI quoted government sources as saying.

In the last week, a Pak drone was shot down by India using an Israeli SPYDER surface-to-air missile system along the Indo-Pak border in Kutch region of Gujarat state, according to NDTV report.
