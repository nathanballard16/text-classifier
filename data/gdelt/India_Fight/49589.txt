Indoo Ki Jawani: Kiara Advani heads back to Lucknow and begins shooting for her upcoming film

Kiara Advani was last seen in Kabir Singh alongside Shahid Kapoor and the actress was showered with appreciation for her perfect portrayal of a simple introvert girl. After having sensationalised the character of Preeti, Kiara shot to fame with four big-ticket films falling into her kitty. The actress is soon going to be seen in Good Newwz co-starring with Akshay Kumar, Kareena Kapoor Khan, and Diljit Dosanjh, on December 27 this year. She is also to feature in Laxmmi Bomb, Bhool Bhulaiyaa 2 and Shershaah, releasing in 2020.

The actress is currently caught up with the filming of her upcoming comedy titled Indoo Ki Jawani, pairing up with Aditya Seal for the first time. The film is slated to hit the screens in 2020 and Kiara has recently headed back to Lucknow once again after completing her shoot schedule for Bhool Bhulaiyaa 2, to begin filming for Indoo Ki Jawani. The young starlet has returned to the City of Nawabs for shooting the second schedule of the film as the team has already completed shooting for the first schedule earlier this month.

The film is set in a small town where Kiara will be seen putting up a bubbly act as she is in search of true love, which she wishes to explore with the help of an online dating app. Helmed by director Abir Sengupta, the film is slated to release theatrically on June 5 next year.
