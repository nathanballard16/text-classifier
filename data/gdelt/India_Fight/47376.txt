Woman found dead with slit throat at in laws residence

Kolkata: A woman was found dead at her in-laws' house at Basanti in South 24-Parganas on Monday morning. The throat of the woman, identified as Sulata Pailan who married Dulal Pailan of Radharanipur in Basanti a few years ago, was silt. Local residents also told the police that the couple used to fight often. The police took the victim to a local health centre at Basanti where the doctors declared her brought dead. The family members of the woman lodged a complaint with the police accusing her husband of murder.
