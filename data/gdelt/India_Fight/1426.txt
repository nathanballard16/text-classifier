Two fire victims still serious

Nagpur: A day after a major fire broke at a building under construction at Kingsway, two people among the nine admitted to hospitals are still serious. The workers, had suffered uneasiness after they accidentally inhaled the smoke while being rescued from the building.The fire had broke out at the multi-storey building at the two-floored auditorium on second and third floor. Six of the workers, who were rescued from the fire, were shifted to Mayo while the rest were sent to Care hospital, after fire teams along with general public and police rescued them from the burning building.According to sources at hospital, among the three taken to Care hospital, two are still serious while the patients from Mayo have been shifted to another private hospital.
