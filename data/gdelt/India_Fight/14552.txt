Mumbai blast: Victims who did not receive relief may benefit under state scheme

Twenty-six years after the serial blasts ripped parts of Mumbai, victims who did not receive compensation may still be compensated under the state’s victim compensation scheme. Following an order passed by the special court over a year ago on September 7, 2017, the District Legal Services Authority (DLSA) has recently started identifying victims who did not receive compensation.
