Salman Khan and Katrina Kaif to shoot a wedding number for 'Bharat'?

Salman Khan starrer 'Bharat' is one of the most awaited movies of the year. The makers released the teaser of the film and fans went gaga over Salman's 5 different looks. This film will also reunite Katrina Kaif and Salman Khan on screen after 'Tiger Zinda Hai'. Directed by Ali Abbas Zafar, the film stars Disha Patani, Sunil Grover, Tabu and Jackie Shroff in other important roles.We all are aware that 'Bharat' will have a number of songs for every occasion, but recently there's a buzz that Salman Khan and Katrina Kaif might be giving us a wedding number of the year and will be shooting for a wedding sequence in a song in Bharat. According to the reports, there are several tracks in the film, featuring on different actors but the one that’s being shot right now is a wedding number. It’s an upbeat song that gives out the festival feel as well. A lot of flowers and other decorations are being used. This will also be Salman and Kat’s second song in the film, after the one they shot in Malta.'Bharat' is slated to release on Eid 2019.
