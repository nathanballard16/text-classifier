Two more youth martyred in occupied Kashmir's Kulgam district

The troops killed the youth during a cordon and search operation at area of the district.

SRINAGAR (Dunya News) - Indian forces on Sunday killed two more Kashmiri youth in their ongoing genocide in the Kulgam district.

The troops killed the youth during a cordon and search operation at area of the district.

Thousands of people participated in the funeral prayers in Kulgam district. The mourners protested and raised pro-freedom and anti-India slogans on the occasion.

Meanwhile, the authorities again suspended the mobile Internet services in south Kashmir. Schools and colleges also remained shut amid fears of further protests against Indian rule.
