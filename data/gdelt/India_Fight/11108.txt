Abhinandan Varthaman: Here's what Pakistan PM Imran Khan said on captured IAF pilot

Pakistan Prime Minister Imran Khan has said that his country will send captured Indian pilot Abhinandan Varthaman to India on Friday as a peace gesture.

Pakistan PM Imran Khan / Image: Facebook

The captured Indian Air Force (IAF) pilot Abhinandan Varthaman will be freed on Friday \"as a peace gesture\", Pakistan Prime Minister Imran Khan announced on Thursday.

The surprise announcement during a joint session of Pakistan National Assembly.

Imran Khan said he is ready to talk to his Prime Minister Narendra Modi to de-escalate Indo-Pak tensions.
