Major fire at hospital building on Kingsway road, people reportedly trapped

Nagpur: A huge cloud of smoke billowing from the Kingsway road suddenly sent everyone on alert as a major fire broke out in a newly constructed building of Sancheti hospital near KP Ground on Wednesday afternoon. The roads along the building located near Bank of India branch on Kingsway road was blanketed with thick black smoke forcing the residents of nearby Mohan Nagar locality out of their houses. 15 people injured however no casualty was reported yet, however few people are said to be trapped inside the building which was not yet operational. The 10 storeyed building was hard to access as fire fighters struggled to douse the flames. The reasons behind the fire were yet to be ascertained however short circuit in electric firing in the building said to have sparked the flames.
