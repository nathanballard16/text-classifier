Mission Mangal song Dil Mein Mars Hai

Starring Akshay Kumar, Vidya Balan, Taapsee Pannu, Sonakshi Sinha, Nithya Menen, Kirti Kulhari and Sharman Joshi in pivotal roles, Mission Mangal is making rounds for all the good reasons. The makers this week released the first track of their film Dil Mein Mars Hai.

The first song from the film shows a phase of planning where Akshay and his team venture out to make the Mars mission happen. Composed and produced by Amit Trivedi, the track has been crooned by Benny Dayal and Vibha Saraf with lyrics penned by Amitabh Bhattacharya. Akshay shared the song on Twitter, and wrote, "Teamwork makes the dream work! Watch the team work towards their dream, #MissionMangal in #DilMeinMarsHai!Song out now.
