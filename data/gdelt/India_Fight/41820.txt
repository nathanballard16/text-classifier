Bihar: Murder convict celebrates birthday in jail, seven officials suspended

SITAMARHI (BIHAR): Seven officials of a Sitamarhi jail were suspended in connection with hosting of a birthday party by Pintu Tiwari who is serving life sentence for murdering two engineers in Darbhanga.A murder convict had cut a cake and served mutton curry to other inmates inside the jail to celebrate his birthday, and the 'invitees' video recorded the programme on mobile phones, a jail official said on Sunday.A video of the party, which has gone viral on Saturday night, shows Tiwari cutting a cake and sharing sweets with other inmates who presented him with gift-wrapped packets, Sitamarhi jail superintendent Rajesh Kumar Rai said.A platter of rice and mutton curry was also served at the gathering while some of the inmates whipped mobile phones out of their pockets and clicked selfies with Tiwary, he said.Tiwary, a sharp-shooter of the Mukesh Pathak gang, had carried a reward of Rs 50,000 on his head. He was arrested from Patna three years ago.It was not clear when the birthday party was held.Rai said the revelry took place in violation of the jail manual and the entire episode was captured on mobile phones, the entry of which is banned inside the prison.He said Inspector General of Police (Jail) Mithilesh Mishra on Sunday ordered the suspension of seven prison officials.
