Bank manager shot in his car, documents missing

ALLAHABAD: A manager of a nationalised bank was shot dead by three men near Markhamau under Mauaima police station in the city on Friday morning.Anik Kumar Dohre (50) was a resident of Dhoomanganj. He was on his way to the bank’s Banka Jalalpur branch along with a colleague, Naveen Kumar, when the attack took place.According to police, Dohre and Kumar were travelling in the former’s car when three men on a motorcycle waylaid them and shot him. They fired at the victim’s chest, snatched a bag containing important documents lying in the car and fled.Police rushed the grievously injured bank manager to Swaroop Rani Nehru (SRN) Hospital, but he was declared brought dead.DIG (Allahabad range) KP Singh said the attack took place occurred around 10.15am and they were yet to ascertain the motive behind it. “Two police teams have been formed to crack the case. Kumar is still in shock and could not explain how it all happened. The assailants are said to have escaped towards Pratapgarh,” the DIG said.Police said Kumar wasn’t sure whether there were two or three attackers, but he did till tell them that the men had not covered their faces.
