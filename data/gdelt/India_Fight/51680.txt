Secretary of Niranjani Akhara commits suicide in Allahabad

Representative image

ALLAHABAD: The secretary of Niranjani Akhara on Sunday committed suicide by shooting himself with licensed pistol on the campus under the limits of Daraganj police station.

Prayagraj SSP, Satyarth Anirudh Pankaj said, "Preliminary investigations have revealed that Ashish Giri (40) took this extreme step after being fed up of his prolong illness."

SSP said that the incident took place between 8am and 9am on Sunday morning. When he did not turn up for morning breakfast on the campus, his disciples looked for him and found him profusely bleeding at his room.

Upon being alerted, senior police officials along with the forensic experts rushed to the spot and carried out investigations.

President of Akhil Bharatiya Akhara Parishad, Narendra Giri also rushed to the spot and inquired about the incident.

Police said that the deceased was associated with Niranjani Akhara for many years. He was also suffering from high blood pressure along with stomach and liver infections.
