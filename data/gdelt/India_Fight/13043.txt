15 students injured in clash

Three party workers were injured, including one from the PTI and two from the ANP PHOTO:AFP

HYDERABAD: Over a dozen students of Sindh Agriculture University were injured in a clash between two groups of students outside the Qaimkhani hostel on Thursday. Police said that they reportedly clashed over occupying seats in the varsity’s bus. They used batons and bricks. According Liaquat University Hospital officials, 15 injured students were brought to the hospital.



Published in The Express Tribune, March 1st, 2019.
