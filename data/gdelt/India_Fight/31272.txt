Terrorist killed in encounter in Baramulla

SRINAGAR: A terrorist was killed in an encounter on Saturday with security forces in Baramulla district of Jammu and Kashmir , police said.Security forces launched a cordon and search operation in Boniyar area of Baramulla district in the early hours following information about presence of the terrorists in the area, a police official said.During the search operation, the terrorists opened fire at security forces who retaliated, he said."In the exchange of fire, one unidentified ultra was killed," he said, adding the identity and group affiliation of the slain terrorist was being ascertained.
