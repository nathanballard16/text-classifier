No headway in MSU student’s murder probe

Vadodara: Vadodara rural police have made no headway in the investigation into the murder of a third-year commerce student of MS University.

Local crime branch, special operations group and cops from local police station have been trying to find out the killer of Khusboo Jani (20) whose body was found in the pond at Chansad village on the night of December 14. “We are looking at all probabilities but so far we have not been able to find out either the murder motive or the killer,” said assistant superintendent of police Ravindra Patel.

Body of Jani, who was missing since December 11, was sighted by residents of Chansad tied to a wooden log and wrapped in a quilt and plastic.
