Three Hizb terrorists killed in encounter in J&K's Shopian

#UPDATE Shopian encounter: Two more terrorists killed, weapons and warlike stores recovered; Operation underway. https://t.co/2zKAd9rtgs — ANI (@ANI) 1556867965000

SRINAGAR: Security forces on Friday killed three Hizbul Mujahideen terrorists in an encounter in J&K's Shopian Lateef Tiger, associated with the "Burhan Wani brigade", was one of the three terrorists killed in the encounter. After the killing of Lateef, the security forces have been successful in eliminating all the 11 members associated with Wani.Following the encounter, security forces launched a cordon and search operation in the Imam Sahib area of Shopian district in south Kashmir in the morning, a police official said.He said a brief exchange of firing took place between the two sides after contact with the militants was established.The area has been put under cordon and further details were awaited, the official said.The operation is currently underway.
