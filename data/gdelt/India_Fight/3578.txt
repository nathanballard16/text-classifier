Man dies after car drags him to 70 feet in hitandrun

The man was crossing the road when a car rammed into him and dragged him for over 70 feet. Surat: A 37-year-old man was killed in a hit-and-run accident at Magdalla Ichhapore Road here on Sunday night. He could not stop the vehicle immediately on seeing the victim and rammed into him, dragging him to some distance. He is suspected to have escaped fearing attack by locals after the accident,” police said. He was identified as Mohammad Haji Malek, 55, a developer, and arrested by police on Monday.“Preliminary investigation shows that the accused was driving the car in very high speed.
