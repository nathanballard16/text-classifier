Two nabbed for killing gangster

New Delhi, July 2Two persons were arrested on Tuesday for allegedly killing a 40-year-old gangster and his neighbour last month in the Nand Nagari area. The accused have been identified as Lokesh (39) of Mandoli and Ankur Malik (25) of Shamli district in Uttar Pradesh, the police said. Two pistols and 14 live cartridges were recovered from their possession. On June 14, Khurvesh and his neighbour Kanchi were shot dead by the accused in the afternoon near the Nand Nagari area. “Lokesh and Malik were arrested with weapons in the wee hours in the Nand Nagari area,” said Atul Kumar Thakur, Deputy Commissioner of Police.
