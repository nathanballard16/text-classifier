Cousin arrested for man’s murder

Ludhiana: Model Town police claimed to have solved the murder of a labourer, Zari Lal of Bihar, who was murdered on March 23. According to police, Lal’s cousin Kundan Kumar was the mastermind and three more are accused in the case. He though did admit that they had put acid on his face to hide his identity after the murder. He was stabbed to death and the accused left his body in a field. Around 9.30pm, Zari answered the call and asked Kundan to prepare dinner, saying that he is coming back, but Zari didn’t.
