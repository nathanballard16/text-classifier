Man found murdered in Khatodara

Representative image

SURAT: Body of a unidentified man was found on Soma Kanji Estate II road near Udhna-Magadalla Raod on Wednesday morning. He was stabbed to death by some unidentified person. Police suspect that he was murdered after being robbed.

Police recovered a bicycle near the body but no identity documents were found.

Police have shared images of the deceased in the area to establish his identity. It is suspected that his purse was looted. Police are examining CCTV footages of the area to find out suspicious movements.
