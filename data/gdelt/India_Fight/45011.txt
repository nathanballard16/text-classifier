Nagpur: Fomer Union Minister Hansraj Ahir Survives As Convoy Meets With Accident, 2 Dead

New Delhi:

A security vehicle part of the former Union Minister Hansraj Ahir's convoy met with an accident in Maharashtra on Thursday morning. According to reports, a speeding truck hit the CRPF vehicle on the Chandrapur-Nagpur highway. One Central Reserve Police Force jawan and a Maharashtra Police official who was driving the vehicle were killed in the accident. Ahir, who was not in the vehicle, escaped unhurt while three others are in a serious condition. Ahir was heading towards Dr Babasaheb Ambedkar International Airport in Nagpur at the time of the accident.
