Elderly found dead at home in Gariahat

Kolkata: An elderly woman was found murdered on Thursday morning at her home on Garcha Road in Gariahat. Her domestic help saw her lying on the drawing room and informed her neighbours. According to sources, Jhund lived with her younger son and his family at a house on Garcha Road. When she went inside she saw Jhund was lying in a pool of blood in the drawing room. It is suspected that the accused person knew that the elderly woman lived alone and planned to rob the house.
