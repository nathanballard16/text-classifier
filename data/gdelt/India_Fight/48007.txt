Ex-JKLF man held over 1990 IAF-killings link

NEW DELHI: Former Jammu and Kashmir Liberation Front (JKLF) commander Javed Ahmad Mir was arrested by the CBI in connection with the probe in the killing of four Indian Air Force (IAF) personnel in a terrorist attack in Srinagar in 1990. CBI officials said Mir was arrested from his Srinagar residence on October 15 on the basis of a non-bailable warrant issued against him earlier this year. He was granted bail by the court the next day.In March, the J&K HC had allowed the transfer of proceedings and the writ petitions in a few cases to its Jammu wing after the CBI moved a plea. These cases included the killing of the four IAF personnel in Srinagar in 1990 and the December 8, 1989, kidnapping of Rubaiya Sayeed, daughter of then home minister Mufti Mohammad Sayeed.
