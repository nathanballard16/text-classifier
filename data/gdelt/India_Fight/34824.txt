Youth killed in hit-and-run, driver traced

Ludhiana: An 18-year-old youth was killed in a hit-and-run case on Hambran Road on Monday. PAU Police have traced the driver of the car, which had hit the youth’s motorcycle, and have booked her for causing death by negligence.The victim has been identified as Rishi Rai of Kapil Park area. He was a worker at a dairy complex on Hambran Road.The car driver, identified as Minakshi Chhabra, 32 of Gold Link Apartments near Hambran Road, had escaped after the accident while the injured was rushed to the hospital where he succumbed to his injuries, police said.In his statement to police, Rishi’s father Mintu Yadav said on Monday afternoon he along with his son were returning home from Hambran. His son Rishi was on another bike. When they reached near the crossing of Gold Link Apartments, the woman in the car took a sharp turn towards right and hit Rishi’s bike. The woman escaped, leaving Rishi injured on the spot.ASI Richard Masih, investigating officer, said the police have registered a case against woman under Sections 279 (rash driving) and 304-A (causing death by negligence) of the IPC. The woman is yet to be arrested.
