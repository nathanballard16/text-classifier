Fire breaks out at factory in Delhi

A fire broke out on Tuesday morning at an auto parts factory in Delhi.

According to the sources, the fire broke out at around 5 am at the factory located in Delhi’s Peeragarhi area.

The blaze that started at the ground floor, quickly spread to the first and second floors.

Fire tenders were rushed to the spot to douse the flames.

The cause of the fire is yet to be ascertained.

No injuries or casualties into the incident have been reported so far.

Further details are awaited.

On May 8, a fire broke out at a plastic factory near Vardhman Mall in Delhi’s Bawana area.

The fire that started at the basement, spread to the ground and three floors of the plastic factory.
