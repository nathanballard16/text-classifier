Indian, Pakistani troops open fire on Kashmir LoC

(MENAFN) Troops of India and Pakistan have exchanged heavy fire and targeted each other's positions on Line of Control (LoC) dividing the disputed Kashmir region.



Both sides exchanged fire in Poonch sector, about 180 km southwest of Srinagar, the summer capital of Indian-controlled Kashmir.



Indian officials reported no loss of life in the skirmishes on the Indian or Pakistani side.



Earlier in the day a similar exchange was reported in Nowshera sector of adjacent Rajouri district.
