2 Terrorists Killed In Encounter In Jammu And Kashmir's Kupwara

Kupwara encounter: Firing has stopped and the security forces are combing the area.

Two terrorists have been killed in an encounter in Jammu and Kashmir's Kupwara. A security personnel was also injured in the gunbattle in Handwara in the north Kashmir district.

Bodies of both the terrorists have been recovered from Kralgund village of Langate area and searches were underway to sanitise the place.

Security forces launched a cordon and search operation in Babagund area of Kupwara following information about presence of terrorists there, the officials said.

"The hiding terrorists opened fire at the security forces, triggering an encounter," a police officer said.

Firing has stopped and the security forces are combing the area.
