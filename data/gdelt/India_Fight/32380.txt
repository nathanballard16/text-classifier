Salman Khan and Alia starrer ‘Insha Allah’ to be shot in Florida

Actor and producer Salman Khan is all set to reunite with director and producer Sanjay Leela Bhansali for ‘Insha Allah’. The film also stars Alia Bhatt as the female lead and is scheduled to arrive in theatres during Eid 2020.

And while Salman Khan and Sanjay Leela Bhansali have previously worked together in ‘Khamoshi’, ‘Hum Dil De Chuke Sanam’ and ‘Saawariya’, this will be the first time that Alia Bhatt will be working with both SLB and SK.

There was buzz that the film would be extensively shot in Orlando, Florida. However, the latest reports suggest that the makers will kick-start shooting for the film in Varanasi and then jet off to Florida.

According to a Times of India report, Sanjay Leela has been working in full swing on the film. He even recently visited Varanasi and Haridwar for a recce and attended the famous Ganga Aarti. He also took part in the aarti in Haridwar and visited a few houses too.

If reports are to be believed, Khan plays a 40s something businessman in the film while Bhatt plays an aspiring actress. The film is a love story and the actors will be romantically paired opposite each other.
