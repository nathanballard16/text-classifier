: Heavy rains and thundershower lashed parts ofincluding water-starved northern districts on Wednesday evening even as a private weather site predicted that slowly and steadily,2019 is inching closer to make onset over Kerala.The monsoon was earlier predicted to hitby June 7.According to Skymet Weather, initial indications have already started developing in Arabian Sea. All thanks to the formation of a cyclonic circulation over Southeastand adjoining areas of Lakshadweep Islands.The India Meteorological Department () said in view of the strengthening and deepening of cross equatorial flow and the development of an East-West shear zone in the mid-troposphere levels across south Arabian Sea and Maldives - Comorin area, the southwest monsoon has further advanced into some more parts of south Arabian sea.The IMD also predicted fairly widespread rainfall with isolated to very heavy falls likely over Assam, Meghalaya, Nagaland, Manipur, Mizoram and Tripura during next five days.While conditions are likely to become favourable for advance of southwest monsoon into some parts of Northeastern states during next three to four days. However, heat wave to severe heat wave conditions are likely to prevail over Rajasthan, Madhya Pradesh and Vidarbha during next three to four days, IMD said.
