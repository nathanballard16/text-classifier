Youth injured in an acid attack in Sadashiv Peth, attacker kills himself

In a shocking incident, an unidentified person threw acid on a 25-year-old man, identified as Rohit Kharat while he was talking to a girl on Tuesday evening on Tilak road. Two rounds were also reportedly fired during the attack, but this was yet to be verified, the police said.



Kharat was seriously injured in the attack and has been rushed to the Poona Hospital by his family and neighbours. He could not identify the culprits who reportedly ran away to hide on the terrace of a nearby building in Sadashiv Peth.



The attacker who hid in the duct of a nearby building reportedly fired at the police and later shot himself dead. He was a resident of Akkalkot. No policemen were injured during the firing.
