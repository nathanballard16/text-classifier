HC grants anticipatory bail to min Panse

Bhopal: A single-judge bench of MP high court, on Tuesday, granted anticipatory bail to PHE minister Sukhdev Panse, who has been accused of murder of Pardi couple in Multai town of Betul district. The court, however, exonerated then SDM Sakkle but rejected petition of the rest of seven accused for exoneration. The incident was probed by CBI at the behest of M P high court. Bordu Pardi and his wife Dodal Pardi were killed in the incident. The CBI had thoroughly probed the case and gave them clean chit, they argued.
