Hyderabad: Man brutally murdered in Old City

Hyderabad: In Charminar Police Station Jurisdiction, an incident of murder occurred near Bhagwan Devi Hospital.

According to the details, Ravi alias Peter (45) was brutally murdered. It is reported that he was the resident of Ghansi Bazar area. He was a leader of TDP also.

On receiving information, clues team of Hyderabad City Police reached the spot and started collecting evidence.

Police sources indicated that the deceased had developed extramarital relationship with a woman police constable. Police suspects that it could be the reason behind killing.

Police registered a case and started investigation.
