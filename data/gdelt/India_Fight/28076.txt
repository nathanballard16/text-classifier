Grenade attack on CRPF camp in Tral, Kashmir

Follow us on Image Source : INDIA TV Grenade attack on CRPF camp in Tral, Kashmir

Terrorists on Friday attacked a CRPF camp in Jammu and Kashmir's Tral. The terrorists hurled grenades at CRPF camp of 180 battalion.

The attack happened at Midoora. According to reports, CRPF personnel retaliated with aerial firing.

Earlier on Friday, security forces gunned down three militants in two gunfights in Jammu and Kashmir's Shopian and Kishtwar districts. The security forces started a cordon and search operation in Marwah village in Kishtwar in Jammu region following information that some militants were hiding there.As the cordon was tightened, the militants opened fire, triggering an encounter. Two policemen have sustained injuries in the encounter.

In another gunfight in Dragad-Sugan village of Shopian district in the Kashmir Valley, police said the body of a militant was recovered. Earlier, the police recovered two bodies of militants along with their weapons. The gunfight took place in an orchard in Dragad-Sugan village after security forces surrounded the area.
