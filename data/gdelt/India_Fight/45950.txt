3 criminals held after brief exchange of fire

New Delhi: The Special Cell of Delhi Police in a joint operation with UP Police arrested three criminals after a brief exchange of fire in Meerut on Thursday. Police said that all three criminals sustained bullet injuries on their legs. Deputy Commissioner of Police (special cell) Pramod Singh Kushwah said that the incident took place around 9.15 PM in the area of TP Nagar in Meerut. A team under ACP (special cell) Attar Singh was involved in the operation. "Saddam got bullet injuries on both legs whereas Usman and Dalip also sustained injuries in their legs," police said.
