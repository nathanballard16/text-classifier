Man gets life term for murdering wife

COIMBATORE: A 40-year-old man was sentenced to life imprisonment by the additional district judge court here on Friday for murdering his wife over a family dispute at his house in Kullakkapalayam near Pollachi in January 2016.Kumar, a construction worker from Raja Street at Kullakkapalayam got married to Uma Maheswari, 33, and the couple has two sons. Kumar often fought with his wife after getting drunk. On January 11, 2016, Kumar asked his wife to visit their native village during the Pongal festival. But she refused to go and told her husband that their elder son had gone to Sabarimala and she was not also having enough money to visit her native place. Kumar fought with his wife and in a fit of rage, poured kerosene over her and set her ablaze. She was admitted at the Coimbatore Medical College and Hospital.Police received a complaint from her and registered a case against Kumar under section 307 (attempt to murder) of IPC. She later died without responding to treatment after which Kumar was charged with section 302 (murder) and arrested. He was lodged at Coimbatore central prison.
