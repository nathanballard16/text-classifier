RAC jawan posted outside Finance Ministry in Delhi shoots himself

New Delhi:

A Rajasthan Armed Constabulary (RAC) jawan, who was posted outside the Finance Minister in Delhi, has shot himself, reported ANI news agency. The jawan was rushed to the hospital where he succumbed to the injuries. The reason behind the RAC jawan's move to commit suicide has not been ascertained.
