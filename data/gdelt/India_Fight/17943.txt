India: unknown gunmen shoot civilian dead

(MENAFN) Police declared that unknown armed men have gunned down a civilian, aged 35, Saturday in unrest Indian-controlled Kashmir.



The victim has been attacked in Baramulla town, about 70 km west of Srinagar city, the summer capital of Indian-controlled Kashmir.



A police official confirmed: "Unidentified gunmen this evening shot dead a youth namely Arjumand Majid Bhat in the main town."



"He was shot at his shop. Though he was removed to hospital but doctors there declared him brought dead."
