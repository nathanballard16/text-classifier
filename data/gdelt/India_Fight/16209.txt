Deer hunt creates tension at Hyderabad University, probe ordered

Unknown persons hunted a deer in Hyderabad Central University shooting range premises under Gachibowli police station limits. The incident came to light on Thursday when the students of HCU heard bullet firing sounds and in tension informed the forest officials and police.

Officials of forest and law and order Police rushed to the spot and found that a deer killed by the unknown hunters with bullets. They have collected clues and based on the postmortem report are going to take action on the culprits. By filing a case police are investigating.
