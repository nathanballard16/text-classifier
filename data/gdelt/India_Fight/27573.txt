SP ex-MP found dead, poisoning suspected

Former Bulandshahar MP and Samajwadi Party leader Kamlesh Balmiki (51) was found dead in mysterious circumstances at his house in Khurja area of Bulandshahr Police suspect it to be a case poisoning and are waiting for the post mortem report to ascertain the cause of death. According to police, the former MP’s nephew Lalit Balmiki made a distress call to the police station claiming that his uncle was lying dead inside his room. His cousins had come down to check on him after he did not revert their calls.“They had to force themselves through the first two doors, bolted from outside, before they could enter the room where Prajapati was lying dead,” Manish Mishra, Bulandshahr SP said.
