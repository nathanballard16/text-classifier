Indian troops martyr seven more youth in occupied Kashmir

In occupied Kashmir, Indian forces have martyred seven more youth in fresh incident of state terrorism in Kupwara district today.

Indian Army's Srinagar based Spokesman Colonel Rajesh Kalia in a media interview said the youth were killed in Keran area of the district.

Meanwhile, with the recovery of two more dead bodies in the debris of a house collapsed during cordon and search operation in Pandoshan area of Shopian, the death toll mounted to four in the village.

A human rights group based in Srinagar, Voice of Victims, sought the intervention of United Nations and the heads of Western and Muslim countries in the wake of a new wave of state terrorism unleashed by India in the occupied territory.
