Maharashtra Exit Polls Live: BJP to win 109-124, Sena 57-70. Congress to win 72-90, NCP 40-50

Maharashtra exit poll: How parties stand

And, here are the party-wise numbers. The BJP is expected to be the No. 1 party in Maharashtra with the India Today-Axis My India exit poll projecting 109 to 124 seats for the party.

Ally Shiv Sena is expected to win between 57 and 70 seats, which means that the BJP will have the upper hand in the resultant government.

Meanwhile, the Congress is predicted to win 32-40 seats while its ally NCP is likely to win 40-50 seats.
