Fire breaks out at Mumbai’s Oshiwara housing society

Mumbai, Jan 05 (ANI): A fire broke out at an office building in Swati Society in Mumbai’s Oshiwara on Friday night. The fire broke out on the building’s ground floor at around 10:20 pm. Three fire engines were sent to the spot. The fire was declared level 0 (minor) and doused immediately. Now, fire is under control at the spot. No casualties have been reported so far in this incident. Reason of fire has not been ascertained yet.
