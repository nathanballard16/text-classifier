Bus rams into three 2-wheelers, two dead

HYDERABAD: Two persons were killed in Kukatpally when a Narsimha Reddy Engineering College bus rammed into a scooter and two bikes on Saturday. The deceased were identified as Aravind (23) and his colleague Ananthalaxmi (22), private employees and residents of Nizampet "The bus was overspeeding and three others were injured. Also, the driver fled from the accident spot," said KPHB Colony inspector Laxmi Narayana . We have launched a manhunt to nab the driver," he added. A case was registered.
