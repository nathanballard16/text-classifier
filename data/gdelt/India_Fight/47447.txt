Gujarat: Three killed in collision between police jeep and auto

RAJKOT: Three people were killed while three other suffered injuries after a police jeep collided with an auto rikshaw near Barvala circle of Botad district on Monday late night.The incident took place near Samadhiyala on Barvala-Botad highway. The deceased, traveling in rikshaw, were identified as Vinod Sadaria, Mohmod Shah and Meji Vaghela. Three other occupants of the rikshaw received serious injuries and were rushed to a hospital in Botad.
