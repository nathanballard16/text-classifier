Surgical Strike 2 on Pakistan: Swara Bhasker says Jai Hind after Pulwama revenge attack

Swara Bhasker tweeted in support of the armed forces

In a pre-dawn strike, Indian Air Force jets crossed the Line of Control (LoC) in Pakistan to drop 1000 kg bombs on Jaish-e-Mohammed terror camps in Muzafarabad sector.

The attack was launched 13 days after the Pulwama attack, when a convoy of vehicles, carrying security personnel on the Jammu Srinagar National Highway was attacked by a vehicle-borne suicide bomber in the Pulwama district, Jammu and Kashmir.

The attack has been lauded by many Bollywood celebs on social media, From Akshay Kumar to Ajay Devgn, many celebs took to Twitter to praise the India Air Force for the surgical strike.

Swara Bhasker too took to Twitter to salute the armed forces. The actress wrote, "Salute to the our armed forces. Jai Hind."

Akshay Kumar took to Twitter to show his support. He wrote, "Proud of our #IndianAirForce fighters for destroying terror camps! Quiet no more! #IndiaStrikesBack."
