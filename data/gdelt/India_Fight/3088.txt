Fire breaks out at yarn manufacturing firm

SURAT: A major fire erupted in a yarn manufacturing company’s godown at Bagumara near Palsana locality, some 20km from Surat on Thursday evening.The fire broke out at Eagles Fibres Ltd and spread to JPB Fibres Ltd. The fire brigades from Surat, Palsana and Bardoli were pressed into service.However, when the last report came in, the fire was still raging. Sources from the fire brigade said that a huge quantity of raw materials, finished products and machineries were all gutted.There was also a report of four workers going missing. Police said the search would be possible only when the fire was brought under control.According to some workers, the four missing include the son of the labour contractor. However, there was no official confirmation in this regard.
