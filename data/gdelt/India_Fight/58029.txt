Magazine photoshoots are a common thing for every Bollywood actor. While some actor's cover shoot grab the limelight for breaking stereotypes, others make news for their style. So, today, we have compiled 10 such stylish photoshoots of the leading men of B-town that are unmissable.



Aditya Roy Kapoor who is known to be a heartthrob of many turned digital cover-star for MensXP. Breaking all stereotypes, the actor was seen wearing an ample amount of make-up for the photoshoot. He posed for the lens wearing a blue co-ordinated pantsuit. The actor looked dapper in the striped ensemble.
