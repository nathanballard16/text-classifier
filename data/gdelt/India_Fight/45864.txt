3 arrested for killing man

New Delhi, October 2Three men were arrested for allegedly killing a man in Rohini's Prem Nagar area, the police said on Wednesday. They were identified as Manish (26), Uday (25) and Neeraj (19), all residents of Prem Nagar, they said. According to the police, one Ashish (22) was admitted to a hospital with gunshot injury Monday night. His father had evicted him from his home and he was living with his girlfriend and selling liquor, they said. A country-made pistol, along with four cartridges, was seized, the police said.— PTI
