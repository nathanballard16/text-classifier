Fire breaks out in tyre godown in Navi Mumbai's Nauda Fata area

A massive fire broke out in a tyre godown in Nauda Fata area in Navi Mumbai on Tuesday. Rescue operations underway.

Rescue personnel and fire brigade are on the spot.

Massive blaze with dark fumes was seen billowing out of the incident site.
