Sealdah Court grants DNA test permit in Tangra twin murder case

Kolkata: The Sealdah Court on Thursday granted permission to the city police to conduct DNA test on Lee Wan Sang, who has been accused of murdering his wife Mi Ha and his father Ka Sang. In the course of the investigation, the city police urged the court seeking permission to conduct DNA test on the accused. During the investigation, police had found some portion of the nails of the accused inside the palm of the deceased woman. After starting an investigation, police arrested Lee Wan in this connection. The father of Lee Wan rushed to the spot and found the woman lying in a pool of blood.
