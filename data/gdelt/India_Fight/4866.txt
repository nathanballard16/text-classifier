Three murders in a day shock Diamond City

SURAT: Three murders have been reported from different areas of the Diamond City on Wednesday in two seperated incidents. The attackers stabbed Vala multiple times leading to heavy blood loss. His nephew Jayanti, a diamond polisher, lodged the complaint of the attack. Umesh Rai, a labourer, was going home after his job when the robbers tried to snatch his phone. Rai resisted the robbery during which he was stabbed by sharp weapons.
