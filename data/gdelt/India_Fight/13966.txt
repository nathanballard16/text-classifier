Coming soon: First state-run shooting range

Gurgaon: The city’s first state-run shooting range will soon come up at Tau Devi Lal Stadium. Anyone can come and get free training at the range, officials said.“The shooting range will be built at a cost of Rs 2-2.5 crore. GMDA has put forth a proposal to make a 50m shooting range in the city that will be run by a government agency. “This will be the first shooting range in the state which will be government-run. A government shooting range is the need of the hour,” said 11-year-old Tiyana Phogat, who got first position in .177 air pistol sub junior women category at an all-India event held in Ahmedabad in October last year.
