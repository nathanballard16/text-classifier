Police officer hurt in Baruipur group clash

Kolkata: A sub-inspector of police from Baruipur was among the seven persons who suffered injuries in a clash that broke out between the two groups at Kachari bazaar area of Baruipur late on Wednesday night. Dipankar Das, the police officer was injured when he reached the spot to stop violence. He has been undergoing treatment at Baruipur sub-divisional hospital with serious injuries on his body. The situation turned ugly when members from each group attacked their opponents. Six persons received injuries and they were taken to the Baruipur sub-divisional hospital.
