Ganja farming busted; farm owner arrested

RAJKOT: In a major seizure, more than 100 kg of ganja (marijuana) was seized and one person arrested after an entire farm full of ganja cultivation was raided in Khampalia village of Muli taluka in Surendranagar district on Saturday.Police said the accused, Prabhu Koli (50), confessed that he had been cultivating ganja to earn quick bucks since long.“Koli himself is a ganja addict. So far we have seized more than 100 kg of ganja, but the quantity likely to increase as we are still counting the marijuana plants,” said an official from Surendranagar special operation group.
