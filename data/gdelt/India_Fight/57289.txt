Tejashwi Prasad Yadav, opposition leaders booked for protests

PATNA: Police arrested at least 40 people for Saturday’s violent clash between two rival groups at Phulwarisharif in Patna during the RJD-led Bihar bandh against Citizenship (Amendment) Act and National Register of Citizens. Police also booked several opposition netas, including Tejashwi Prasad Yadav , for blocking roads, illegal use of loud speakers, and staging protests without seeking permission.Altogether, 27 people from RJD, Congress and other opposition parties have been named in the FIR. Apart from Tejashwi, RJD state president Jagdanand Singh and MLA Abdul Bari Siddiqui were also named. City SP (central) Vinay Tiwari said two separate FIRs had also been lodged with Kotwali police station in connection with the attack on journalists.
