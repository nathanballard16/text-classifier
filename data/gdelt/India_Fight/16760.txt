Rivalry may have caused labourer’s murder

* Post-mortem revealed the labourer had died 10-12 days ago. Ludhiana: The post-mortem report of Zari Lal (36), a labourer from Model Town, has revealed he was stabbed several times on his head, eyes, and stomach. The autopsy report reveals Zari was killed brutally, with rivalry suspected as the reason. So far, they have not come across any person who had a personal rivalry with Zari Lal.MURDER MOST FOUL* The Model Town labourer had gone missing since March 11. After the autopsy report , now police are suspecting rivalry to be the reason behind the murder.Zari Lal was found murdered in Bagha Kalan village in Ladhowal on Saturday morning.
