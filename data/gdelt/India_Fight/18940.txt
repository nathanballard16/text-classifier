Tailor killed over illicit relationship in Katargam

SURAT: A tailor was killed by a diamond worker for having illicit relationship with his wife in Katargam area of the city on Friday. Balwant Makwana was stabbed to death by Chetan Jadhav who later surrendered at Hari Om Nagar police chowky . He was admitted to a hospital for treatment of his injuries sustained during fight with Makwana.Katargam police said the accused had warned the victim a number of times, but he didn’t take the warning seriously.DJ Chavda, assistant commissioner of police, D division, said, “We are yet to question the accused. Both of them were from the same village in Saurashtra and lived in Katargam. Makwana was a tailor and had a shop in Udaynagar.”
