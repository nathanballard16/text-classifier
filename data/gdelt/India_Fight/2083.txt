Delhi: 24-year-old man killed in hit-and-run

Just as we reached the DND loop near Maharani Bagh, an unidentified vehicle hit us from behind. Police said they are looking at CCTV footage of the surrounding area for clues about the errant vehicle. A case was registered under IPC sections 279 (rash driving), 337 (causing hurt) and 304-(a) (causing death by negligence) at Sunlight Colony police station. A professional photographer was killed and his friend sustained injuries after their motorcycle was hit by an unidentified vehicle in southeast Delhi’s Sunlight Colony on Tuesday, police said.
