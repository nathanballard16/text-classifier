2 arrested for killing business partner

New Delhi, July 10Two persons were arrested on Monday for allegedly killing their business partner in east Delhi’s Yamuna Vihar in January, said the police on Wednesday. The duo was identified as Kamaljeet Singh (44) and Sunil Malik (29). Both were carrying a reward of Rs 20,000 each in connection with the murder of Kamaljeet’s business partner Subhash Rawat, said the police. The police said the two-wheeler and the pistol used to murder Rawat were also recovered from them. Interrogation revealed that Kamaljeet and Rawat were involved in the real estate business.
