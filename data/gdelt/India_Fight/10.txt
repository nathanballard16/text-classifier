Fire in Mangal Bazaar building kills two

VADODARA: Two persons were killed after fire broke out in a building at Mangal Bazaar in the early hours of Monday.Fire broke out in the basement of Satyabhava Complex in the narrow lane of Mangal Bazaar which spread to the first and second floors of the building sometime before 5 am. Firemen could not bring the fire engines right up to the building due to narrow lane of Mangal Bazaar and illegal encroachment. While Soni is a resident of Mangal Bazaar, Chandiwala is a resident of Nagarwada. When the firefighters reached there, the fire had spread to second floor and the basement was filled with smoke. Their family members also could not say why they had gone to the building in the wee hours.
