Nimta murder Two more arrested

Kolkata: Two more youths were detained by the police in connection with the Nimta murder case on Monday morning. Sources claimed that one of the detained youths is suspected to have supplied the murder weapon. Though the police refused to comment on the case, sources claimed that investigating officials suspect that one of the youths had supplied the murder weapon. The police are at present interrogating them to recover the murder weapon.
