Seven arrested for stealing cement-laden lorry after attacking driver

A special police team has arrested seven persons in connection with stealing a cement-laden lorry after allegedly attacking its driver at Tirumandurai village in the district last month.

The lorry was parked along Chennai–Tiruchi national highway when unidentified persons attacked the vehicle driver, Jeevarathinam, and drove away with the truck along with the consignment.

Mangalamedu police registered a case of robbery on a complaint from the lorry driver. A special police team, which was constituted to crack the case, arrested seven persons.

The stolen lorry and 520 bags of cement were recovered. The arrested persons were sent to judicial custody, a police press release said.
