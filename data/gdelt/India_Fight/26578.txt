Terrorists hurl grenade at a CRPF post in Pulwama

Pulwama (Jammu and Kashmir) [India], May 21 (ANI): Terrorists on Tuesday evening hurled grenade at a Central Reserve Police Force (CRPF) post situated at State Bank of India (SBI) in Jammu and Kashmir's Pulwama district.

No loss of life or injury has been reported yet.

On May 18, three terrorists affiliated with proscribed terror outfit Hizbul Mujahideen were killed in an encounter in Pulwama, Jammu and Kashmir police had said. (ANI)
