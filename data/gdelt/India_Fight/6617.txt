Pulwama attack: Rail roko protest in Nalasopara

MUMBAI: A group of protesters blocked the railway tracks at Nalasopara on Saturday morning to protest against the terrorist attack in Kashmir’s Pulwama that killed over 40 CRPF men.The protesters, including office goers got onto the tracks at around 8 am. Government Railway Police (GRP) and Railway Protection Force (RPF) personnel are in the process of evacuating the protesters from the tracks. Train services have been affected on the route.The protesters were also joined by some office-goersProtesters also raised slogans against the terrorists.At Vasai , state transport buses and auto rickshaw were prevented from plying in the morning.Political parties like Shiv Sena has called for a bandh in Vasai-Virar region.
