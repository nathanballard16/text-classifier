Cotton trader among two killed in accident

Rajkot: Two persons were killed and two others injured , when an SUV collided with a diesel tanker on Ahmedabad-Rajkot highway, about 3km from Limbdi town on Tuesday morning. The deceased were identified as Vinu Dangar (50), a cotton trader from Dhrol in Jamnagar and Vinodgar Gosai (52). While Gosai had died on the spot, Dangar succumbed to his injuries. The accident took place when Dangar and others were returning in an SUV after purchasing a truck from Ahmedabad. tnn
