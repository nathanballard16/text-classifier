IMFL worth Rs 14.32 lakh seized in Rajkot

RAJKOT: A total of 4 ,104 bottles of Indian Made Foreign Liquor (IMFL) worth Rs 14.32 lakh were seized in two different raids conducted at Navagam on the outskirts of Rajkot and Rajkot Old Market Yard on early Wednesday morning. In Navagam, a total of 3,204 IMFL bottles worth Rs 10,75 lakh were seized.Cops raided an open plot in Navagam after they received a tip-off that bootleggers were gathered there to divide the share of IMFL bottles. However, when they got there, bootleggers fled.
