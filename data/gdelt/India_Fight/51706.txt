Allahabad: Secretary of Niranjani Akhara mahant shoots himself dead

ALLAHABAD: Ashish Giri (40), secretary of Niranjani Akhara on Sunday committed suicide by shooting himself with a licensed pistol on the akhara campus under the limits of Daraganj police station Prayagraj SSP Satyarth Anirudh Pankaj said: “Preliminary investigations revealed that Giri had taken the extreme step to end his lie after being fed up of his prolong illness”.SSP said that the incident took place between 8am and 9am on Sunday morning. When he did not turn for morning breakfast on akhara campus, his disciples looked for him and found profusely bleeding at his room which was opened.Upon being alerted, senior police officials along with forensic experts rushed the spot and carried out investigations. Akhil Bharatiya Akhara Parishad President Narendra Giri also rushed to the spot and inquired about the incident.Police said that deceased mahant was associated with Niranjani Akhara for many years. He was also suffering from high blood pressure along with stomach and liver infections.
