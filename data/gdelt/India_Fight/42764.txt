Woman dead husband kids injured in road mishap

Kolkata: A 25-year-old woman was killed and her husband and two children were severely injured after they were hit by a speeding truck. The incident took place on National Highway 34 at Sagardighi area of Murshidabad on Sunday. The incident led to traffic congestion on the National Highway 34 for sometimes. When they were crossing Ratanpur area on National Highway 34, a loaded truck hit their motorcycle from behind. The police are conducting raids to nab the truck driver.
