Bengaluru: FIR registered against person for shooting dog with air gun

Bengaluru (Karnataka) [India], Nov 12 (ANI): An FIR has been registered against a person for shooting a dog with an air gun.

The accused has been identified as Shamsundar, who is a resident of Jayanagar.

Three pellets were found in the dog's body.

The canine is being treated at a pet's clinic.

Further details are awaited. (ANI)
