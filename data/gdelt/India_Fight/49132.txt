Terror attack in Srinagar: 6 CRPF personnel injured as terrorists hurl grenade in Srinagar

Srinagar: 5 CRPF jawans injured in grenade attack

SRINAGAR: Terrorists hurled a grenade at a Central Reserve Police Force CRPF ) team, injuring six personnel, in the Karan Nagar area of Srinagar on Saturday, police said.The CRPF team was manning a checkpoint when the grenade attack took place.Six personnel were injured, they said.The grenade exploded with a bang causing panic in the area, police said.The security forces fired some shots in the air in retaliation, officials said.
