Uttar Pradesh: Four dead as AC bus catches fire at Agra-Lucknow expressway

Four people died after an AC bus caught fire at the Agra-Lucknow expressway in the wee hours of Monday.

The ill-fated bus was travelling from Delhi's Anand Vihar to Lucknow, police informed.

Om Prakash, assistant superintendent of police (ASP), Mainpuri said, "Around 1 am in the night a bus caught fire at Firozabad- Mainpuri border near the 76th milestone. The bus had four passengers and three staff members out of which two passengers and one staff was saved while four others have died."

Police also said that further action is being initiated in the accident.
