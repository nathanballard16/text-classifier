Pakistani UAV shot down in Rajasthan

Jaipur: A unmanned aerial vehicle (UAV) which intruded into Rajasthan’s Ganganagar was shot down by the Army here, Saturday, defence sources said.

“One UAV intrusion in Ganganagar sector around 7.30 pm. The drone was engaged and brought down,” a defence source said. No further details were shared.

A fighter jet of the Indian Air Force shot down a Pakistani military drone March 4 in Bikaner sector of the Indo-Pak border using an air-to-air missile. Another Pakistani drone was shot down by India February 27 along Indo-Pak border in Kutch in Gujarat.

The downing of the drones came in the midst of heightened tension between India and Pakistan after IAF struck a terrorist training camp in Pakistan’s Balakot and subsequent retaliation by Islamabad.
