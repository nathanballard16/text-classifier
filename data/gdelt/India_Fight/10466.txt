Good news for all the Mahesh Babu fans, Maharshi finally has a release date - details inside

Good news for all the Mahesh Babu fans

The wait is finally over! Fans have been waiting with bated breath for Mahesh Babu's next release, Maharshi. After rumours doing the rounds, the makers have finally confirmed that the film will release on April 25. Directed by Vamshi Paidipally, the film is being produced by Sri Venkateshwara Creations, Vyjayanthi Movies, PVP Cinemas. But above it all, Maharshi is Mahesh's 25th film in the industry which makes this film even more special.

Over the last few months, Mahesh and the makers of the film have been on a tight schedule to complete the last few scenes. Except for two songs, the entire film's shoot is to be completed by March 15. With post-production going on simultaneously, Mahesh missed his wife, Namrata Shirodkar's birthday celebrations too.

Taking to Twitter, the makers of the film wrote, "An update about # Maharshi... Shooting part will be completed by March 15th except for 2 songs. Post Production is going on simultaneously in full swing. All set for April 25th Release."

Mahesh will be teaming up with Mohenjo Daro actress, Pooja Hegde for the first time while Allari Naresh will be essaying a pivotal role in the film. A few weeks ago, images of the superstar from the sets of the film began doing the rounds on social media only leaving the fans asking for more.

After a long schedule in the United States of America last year, the team is currently shooting in Pollachi in Tamil Nadu. As per reports in Indian Express, the team will also be shooting in Abu Dhabi and Hyderabad.
