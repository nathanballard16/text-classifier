Police recover murder weapon

After a day-long search, the Maradu police on Friday evening recovered a knife, which was allegedly used to commit a murder earlier this month, from a waterbody in the Maradu-Kundannoor stretch.

The police had arrested Johnson Xavier, 31, of Nettoor, who is accused of murdering Anil of Maradu on February 3, from Bengaluru on Thursday.

The police said he confessed to throwing the knife into the waterbody following which a five-member diving team was deployed from Fort Kochi who fished it out by evening.

The knife will now be sent for forensic examination.
