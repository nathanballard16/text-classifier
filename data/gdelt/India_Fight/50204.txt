Man opens fire outside BJP MP Hansraj Hans' office in Rohini, arrested

Delhi Police has arrested a man who allegedly opened fire outside the office of BJP MP Hansraj Hans in Rohini area on Monday evening. The assailant was later identified as Rameshwar Pehalwan, 51, a resident of Bawana village. The office was locked and no one was inside at the time of the incident, therefore, no injuries were reported. #WATCH Delhi: A 51-year-old, Rameshwar Pehalwan fired 2 shots outside the office of BJP MP Hans Raj Hans in Rohini, today.
