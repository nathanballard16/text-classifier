42-yr-old man shot dead in Karawal Nagar

New Delhi, October 18A 42-year-old man has been shot dead allegedly by two bike-borne assailants in North-East Delhi’s Karawal Nagar area, the police said on Friday. The deceased, Kishor, was a resident of Shiv Vihar area in Karawal Nagar and worked as a financier, they said. A senior police official said information regarding the incident was received at around 8 pm on Thursday. Kishor was found in an injured condition at the spot and rushed to a hospital, where he was declared brought dead, the official said. A case under Section 302 of the IPC and Section 25 of the Arms Act has been registered, the police said.
