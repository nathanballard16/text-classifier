Watch: Truck catches fire at petrol pump in Kanpur, several other vehicles gutted

A truck parked near a petrol pump in Kanpur district of Uttar Pradesh caught fire before being exploded. The truck caught fire suddenly which rapidly spread to other vehicles standing near the truck. The incident took place on Saturday in Barra - a neighbourhood in Kanpur city of Uttar Pradesh. The incident also exposed the emergency fire fighting preparations of the petrol pump where, as per eyewitnesses, not ever water was available to extinguish the fire. Bystanders informed local fire brigade authorities about the fire which later sent four engines to douse the flames.
