Pakistan foreign minister says India preparing another attack this month

Pakistan has "reliable intelligence" that India will attack again this month, Foreign Minister Shah Mahmood Qureshi said on Sunday, as tension over a February standoff between the two neighbours had appeared to ease. The attack could take place between April 16 and 20, he said.
