Shah Rukh Khan and Suriya to play cameos in R Madhavan's Rocketry: The Nambi Effect? Find out

Rocketry: The Nambi Effect starring R Madhavan is one of the most awaited films of the year. The film has already created quite the buz, specially for Madhavan's look which bears an uncanny resemblance to Indian scientist Nambi Narayanan. The actor has left no stone unturned to get into the skin of the character and latest reports suggest that Madhavan is likely to get King Khan Shah Rukh Khan and Tamil star Suriya Sivakumar on board for the project.

According to a report in India, Bollywood superstar Shah Rukh Khan is likely to play a cameo in the Hindi version while Tamil star Suriya Sivakumar will play the same role in Tamil version of the multilingual film. Shah Rukh Khan and Maddy have a good rapport and it is highly possible that SRK would have given his nod for the cameo. Same can be said about Suriya, who has earlier played cameos in his friends' films, stated the report.

Rocketry: The Nambi Effect will see the actor essay the role of the protagonist in the film. The film is also Madhavan's directorial debut and revolves around the life story of scientist and engineer Nambi Narayanan. The ISRO scientist was falsely charged with espionage and arrested in 1994. However, two years later, the charges against him were dismissed by the CBI and the Supreme Court of India declared him not guilty in 1998.

The film will be a trilingual in English, Hindi and Tamil and is shot extensively across India, Princeton, Scotland, France and Russia. The film is slated to release worldwide in the second half of 2019.
