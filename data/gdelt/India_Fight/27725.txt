Major fire in plastic company at Dadra and Nagar Haveli

SURAT: A major fire broke out in Hamilton Plastics Limited at Raholi in the Union territory of Dadra and Nagar Haveli on Tuesday. The fire, which started at 11.30am, within minutes engulfed the entire factory premises on 135 sq metre area near here. A truck that arrived with plastic goods also caught fire and was gutted along with plastic and cardboard material in the factory.Firefighters along with six fire vehicles from Silvassa, Vapi, Daman, Bhilosa and Alok fire stations doused the fire with assistance from a couple of fire tenders from private companies.Officials said the cause of fire was not known but there were no casualties. Hamilton Plastics Limited manufactures plastic items. The assessment of loss is yet to be made, they added.
