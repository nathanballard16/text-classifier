Ex-students Alia Bhatt, Sidharth Malhotra and Varun Dhawan to groove with Tiger Shroff in SOTY 2?

Alia, Sidharth, Varun to groove with Tiger Shroff in SOTY 2 | Photo Credit: Instagram

Student Of The Year marked the Bollywood debut of the three of the shining stars of Bollywood. Now the makers will soon come with the second instalment of the film which stars Tiger Shroff and debutante Ananya Panday and Tara Sutaria. The shooting of the film is going on with a full swing and it is set to release in May 2019.

The film will be a college drama just like the first instalment and fans are eagerly waiting for it. Now the report has it that ex-student Alia Bhatt will be seen grooving alongside the current student Tiger Shroff. The reports even have that Sidharth Malhotra and Varun Dhawan too will join the actress and the student Tiger.

As per the report by Mumbai Mirror, “Farah Khan will be choreographing the song which only features Alia and the film’s team is figuring out ways to incorporate her co-stars from the first film, Varun Dhawan and Sidharth Malhotra, in the sequel. Both the actors are expected to shoot their portions in the coming weeks too. Alia and Tiger’s song will be shot over one week on a grand set that is being put up at a suburban studio.”

Well, this is an interesting detail and we cannot wait for the ex and the first ever batch collaborate with the recent batch for a fun dancing number. The film is directed by Punit Malhotra and produced by Karan Johar. TRhe star cast was recently seen on the couch of Koffee With Karan Season 6.
