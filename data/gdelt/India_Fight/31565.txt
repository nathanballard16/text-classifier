India: Muslim youth beaten by Hindu extremists succumbs to injuries in Jharkhand

In India, a Muslim youth, who was mercilessly beaten by a mob, succumbed to injuries in Jharkhand on Monday.

According to reports, he was subjected to torture in full public view while being forced to chant “Jai Shri Ram” and “Jai Hanuman” in Kharsawan district.
