Family helps criminal escape

Amritsar, July 24A police party of Karnal district in Haryana that came to arrest a person, Dalbir Singh, who was wanted in robbery and theft cases, was attacked by the members of accused family. The city police have now registered a fresh case against the accused and his members of family under Sections 353, 186, 224, 506, 148 and 149 of the IPC. The Karnal police was headed by ASI Jaipal Singh. He said that the Haryana Police wanted Dalbir Singh alias Babbu in theft and robbery cases, which were registered with the Karnal city . He said on Tuesday the police raided his residence and arrested Dalbir Singh.
