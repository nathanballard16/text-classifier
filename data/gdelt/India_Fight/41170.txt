Rebuked, jawan shoots havildar, kills self in Tamil Nadu

CHENNAI: A 22-year-old jawan from the Jammu & Kashmir Rifles shot dead a havildar who reprimanded him for not being alert on guard duty at Pallavaram and then killed himself early on Tuesday, police said. Police said havildar Parveen Kumar Joshi, 39, was on night rounds checking on the men on duty when he found rifleman Jagseer Singh, 22, sitting in a corner when he was supposed to be standing guard at the armoury.The havildar reprimanded the jawan and went to his tent to sleep. Around 3.45am, Jagseer Singh entered the tent and opened fire with his 5.56mm INSAS rifle on Kumar, police said. Alerted by the gunfire, when other army personnel saw Singh coming out of the tent and tried to nab him, he shot himself in the stomach.
