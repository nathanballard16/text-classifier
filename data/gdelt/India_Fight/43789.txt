Here's when Ram Charan will re-join the sets of RRR!

RRR is undoubtedly one of the most-talked-about films of this year. SS Rajamouli, who is directing the film, is successfully bringing together Jr NTR and Ram Charan together to share the screen space. As already known, the film’s shoot has been going on in Bulgaria where the makers are busy filming action scenes of Jr NTR. The first schedule of the film was wrapped up in December and the second schedule was all about Ram Charan. The actor took a break from the shoot due to his ankle injury and then got busy with his magnum opus film, Sye Raa Narasimha Reddy.

Now, according to reports, Ram Charan is all set to be back on the sets of RRR. The next schedule of the film will reportedly start in Hyderabad after a gap of few days. The report in the leading media also states that the actor will be having a busy September and October as he will be juggling between RRR shoot and Sye Raa promotions. Let’s see how well he is able to manage his time.

RRR narrates the story of freedom fighters Alluri Sitarama Raju and Komaram Bheem. The film is slated for a July 30, 2020 release. It will see Bollywood stars Alia Bhatt and Ajay Devgn playing special roles. Meanwhile, Ram Charan is busy with the pre-release event of Sye Raa Narasimha Reddy!
