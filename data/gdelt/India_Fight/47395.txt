Mall fence, guard’s booth on govt land demolished

Varanasi: The security staff booth and fence of a city mall, allegedly raised on land of Cantonment Board and revenue department, were demolished by a joint team of officials of the district administration and police on Monday.The joint team led by additional city magistrate-IV Shubhangi Shukla raided the JHV mall. As the team started measuring the area of the mall, its authorities turned up with maps and other related documents. Shukla said that during the entire exercise it was found that the JHV mall owner and authorities had raised the fences and a booth for its security guard on the government land. Later, on orders of the ACM (IV) both the illegal structures were demolished.Shukla’s one and half-year-old son was also with her as she led the drive.
