Tigress found dead in Pench reserve, first death this year

NAGPUR: A female tiger cub was found dead in Khursapar tourism zone under Pench Tiger Reserve (PTR) on Thursday.This is first tiger death of 2019 and third in the last four days. Two tigers died on December 30 & 31 in Paoni range of Umred-Karhandla Wildlife Sanctuary in Nagpur district As per the latest estimation, there are 38 adult tigers in Pench.This is the third tiger death in the last four days. It is a case of internal fight with clear canine marks on the neck region, probably by a dominant male. Being close to Pench, Khursapar has high density of tigers.
