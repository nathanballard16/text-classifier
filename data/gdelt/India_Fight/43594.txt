Delhi: Serial offender James Bond 007 held in capital

Krishna alias 'James Bond 007' was arrested by Delhi Police for his involvement in murder and robbery cases in Delhi. Police also arrested his aide Vinod.

During the interrogation, it came to light that Vinod, who is Class VIII pass, was fond of costly cigarettes and liquor and that is why he entered the crime world, however, Krishna is a cab driver and a daily drinker who needs money for his addiction.

"He always wears a shirt with 007 logo and prefers being addressed as James Bond 007," Anto Alphonse, Deputy Commissioner of Police (Dwarka) said.
