Vijay Sethupathi and Antony Varghese to join the sets of 'Thalapathy 64' in December

Actor Vijay has been shooting in Delhi for the second schedule of his next film tentatively titled ' Thalapathy 64 '. The film is directed by Lokesh Kanagaraj of 'Maanagaram' and 'Kaithi' fame. While the film's heroine Malavika Mohanan has completed her portions in the schedule and has returned to Mumbai, the schedule itself will be wrapped up in a few more days.The college portions involving students and lecturers in the film have been shot in this particular schedule. According to the sources, Vijay will see him play a college professor, who fights against the corruption in the education system in the country.While actor Vijay Sethupathi , who is said to be playing the villain in the film as already shot a major portion of his scenes during the first schedule in Chennai, the star actor is expected to join the team for shooting in the next schedule along with Antony Varghese , who is also part of the cast.The film features Malavika Mohanan as the female lead, while Antony Varghese, Shantanu Bhagyaraj, Sriman, Srinath and Sanjeev will be seen in important roles. The film is produced by Xavier Britto under the banner XB Film Creators. On the technical front, the film has music by Anirudh Ravichander, cinematography by Sathyan Sooryan, and editing by Philomin Raj.
