2 held in Jhilmil factory fire case

New Delhi: A day after three persons, including two women, were killed in a massive fire at a hardware factory in the Jhilmil area of Shahdara, the Delhi Police have arrested factory owners two persons in the case. Deputy Commissioner of Police (Shahdara) Meghna Yadav said they have arrested Adnan and Naeem in the case. According to Delhi Fire Services (DFS) officials, the fire broke out at a rubber-plastic factory in Shahdara's Jhilmil Industrial Area around 9 am. Delhi Chief Minister Arvind Kejriwal visited the factory in Jhilmil area where a major fire broke out that killed three workers as he extended condolences to the family of the deceased. Brave Delhi Fire Service warriors saved two lives in Jhilmil factory fire.
