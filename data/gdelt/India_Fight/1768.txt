ACP dog attacks kite flyer, youth dies

HYDERABAD: In an unpleasant incident, a youth died after jumping from the second floor of his building while flying kite.

According to the reports, the incident took place on Sunday, 13 January when the deceased, who identified as Syed Imran alias Khalid Omer along with his friend Shabaz went up to his home for flying kites.

A dog of his neighbour, Assistant Commissioner of Police suddenly attacked them and to escape from it, both jumped off the second floor.

They both were rushed to the hospital where Imran, (27), a resident of Bholakpur succumbed to the injuries while his friend suffered injuries but is out of danger now.

Police registered a case and started the investigation.
