Alia Bhatt to expose a fake godman in Sadak 2

Mahesh Bhatt’s Sadak 2 is one of the most anticipated films of the year. With this film, the filmmaker Mahesh Bhatt returns to direction after a whopping 23 years. It was on Bhatt’s 70th birthday that Alia Bhatt took to social media to announce as she wrote, ““SADAK 2 @MaheshNBhatt @PoojaB1972 @duttsanjay #AdityaRoyKapur #MukeshBhatt @VisheshFilms #Sadak2.”

According to the report of Mirror, Alia Bhatt will be seen exposing a fake guru who runs an ashram. “Alia’s character is out to expose a fake guru who runs an ashram. She is accompanied by Sanjay Dutt’s character in this journey. Sanjay has already started prep with Bhatt saab. They start shooting in a fortnight,” as quoted by Mirror.
