Delhi: Man shot for objecting molestation of female friend

New Delhi [India], Feb 11 (ANI): In a gruesome incident, a man was shot at by two unidentified men in capital’s Kamla market on Sunday night after he objected to his female friend being molested by them.

The miscreants later fled away with the victim's car.

Both the men were later arrested by the police and weapons were recovered from their possession.

More details are awaited. (ANI)
