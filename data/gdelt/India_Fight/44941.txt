SHRC seeks report on police encounter

A day after a history-sheeter was gunned down by the police here, the State Human Rights Commission (SHRC) has sought for a detailed report into the incident from authorities concerned.

SHRC chairperson Justice T. Meenakumari took suo motu cognisance of media reports, including that of The Hindu and called for a detailed report with documents from Public Secretary (Law & Order) into the incident within four weeks.

According to media reports, the history-sheeter, a native of Villupuram district was shot dead by police in Chennai on Tuesday night.
