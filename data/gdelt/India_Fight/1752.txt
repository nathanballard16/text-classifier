Is Baahubali star Anushka Shetty really quitting films to get married? Details inside

Anushka Shetty | Photo Credit: Instagram

Baahubali star Anushka Shetty is away from films for almost a year now and while fans are wondering where the actress is, rumours suggest that the actress is planning to quit films and get married. Since a couple of days, speculations of her leaving the film industry are doing rounds and people are believing the same as well since she has not been shooting for any films for a very long time now.

The news of her doing a film with Madhavan, Silence which will go on the floor sometime in March also came recently but looks like fans believe the rumour of her quitting films more. Buzz has it that her family is looking for a suitable groom for the 37-year-old actress and recently they performed some ritual at Kedarnath as well. (Also Read: This video of the rumoured couple Prabhas and Anushka Shetty at SS Rajamouli's son's wedding is going viral)

While speculations suggest that she is actually planning to bid adieu to the film industry, India Today has reported that sources close to the Baahubali actress have revealed that the rumours are not true hence fans can stop worrying about it.

Earlier, the news of Anushka being in a relationship with co-actor Prabhas also came but recently the latter denied it on the Koffee With Karan show. He said, “If any two people work together for two years, of course, they will be linked. But, I wasn't dating Anushka. Ask Raj (director Rajamouli), if you want.”
