Jammu & Kashmir: Woman shot dead by terrorists in Pulwama

SRINAGAR: Terrorists shot dead a woman and injured a man on Wednesday in Pulwama district of Jammu and Kashmir, police said."Terrorists fired upon a man and a woman at Singoo-Narbal in Pulwama district today. While the lady succumbed to injuries, the injured man has been rushed to a hospital," a police official said.He said the deceased woman has been identified as Nigeena Bano.Further details are awaited, he added.
