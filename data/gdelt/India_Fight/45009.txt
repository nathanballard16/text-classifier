Coimbatore: Navy man found dead on railway track

COIMBATORE: A 22-year-old trainee soldier of the Indian Navy was found dead on railway track near the Madukkarai Railway Station on Tuesday. Investigation revealed that he had succumbed to injuries after falling from a moving train.The deceased was identified as Ankit Kumar Sharma, a resident of New Delhi. The Podanur Railway Police were alerted by the public about the presence of the body on the tracks between Ettimadai and Madukkarai Railway Station. An identity card recovered from the body revealed that the deceased was a trainee soldier in the Indian Navy and was undergoing training at the Indian Navy Camp in Ernakulam , Kerala. He had left the camp on Sunday by train after completing the training session.The body was sent to the Coimbatore Medical College Hospital for postmortem.
