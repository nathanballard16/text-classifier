Madhya Pradesh: 20 days after kidnapped twins murdered, 5-year-old kidnapped and killed in Satna

BHOPAL: Twenty days after the twins who were kidnapped at gunpoint from their school bus in MP's Satna district were murdered, another five-year-old child who was abducted on March 12 was murdered on Wednesday afternoon. Later, on February 23, bodies of the twins were found from Yamuna river in Banda district of Uttar Pradesh. Later after hours of searching, they got it announced from the local village mosque, but the kid could not be found. Subsequently, police started investigations and five people were rounded up, but to no avail, added sources.SP Satna Santosh Singh Gaur confirmed that ransom call was received. The kin then rushed to Nagaud police station and informed about it.
