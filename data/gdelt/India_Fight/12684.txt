Celebrating highest opening of career with 'Luka Chuppi', Kriti Sanon distributes Bareilly Ki Barfi

Riding high on the success of her recent release 'Luka Chuppi', 'Bareilly Ki Barfi' actress Kriti Sanon celebrated her highest opening by distributing sweets to media.Minting Rs 7.75 crores on its first day, Kriti Sanon's 'Luka Chuppi' marked her highest opening film. Owing to the overwhelming response and love to the film, the actress distributed sweets at a famous eatery in the suburbs of Mumbai.Kriti Sanon has been garnering accolades for her apt portrayal of a small town girl Rashmi who drives the film with her idea of a live-in relationship against a marriage.Earlier, the actress received praises for her bold and beautiful character Bitti in 'Bareilly Ki Barfi'.Kriti Sanon has been treating the audience with varied characters giving a testimony of her versatility. With multiple films in her kitty, Kriti Sanon is currently busy promoting as well as shooting for her upcoming projects.Marking her first release of the year with 'Luka Chuppi', Kriti will be next seen in 'Arjun Patiala ', 'Housefull 4', and ' Panipat '.
