Khatua murder: SIT records statements of 7

Bhopal: The ten-member SIT constituted to probe GCF officer S C Khatua murder case has recorded statements of seven people in Jabalpur on Friday. Officials say the murder case is directly linked with the CBI investigations into supply of the Chinese parts in Indianised bofors.“We are looking into various angles related to the murder. Call detail records are being analysed,” said Sanjeev Uikey, SIT head. Four CBI officers visited Khatua’s house on January 10 and questioned him for three hours in connection with the supply chain.Khatua was last seen at 8.54 am on January 17 in footage collected from traffic cams. Police also contacted a lawyer Khauta had contacted before he disappeared.
