Kareena Kapoor Khan wore this designer gown to hang out Priyanka Chopra

After celebrating her birthday this weekend, Kareena Kapoor Khan is back to work. Yesterday, the actor was spotted shooting for the latest episode of Dance India Dance, for which she arrived in a pale pink gown by Kuwait-based designer, Yousef Al Jasmi. She completed her look with a silver choker by Bvlgari that accentuated the neckline of her gown. She was joined on set by none other than Priyanka Chopra who wore a blazer dress by Versace.
