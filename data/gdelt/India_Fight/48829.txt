Army bids adieu to personnel martyred in Rajouri - Jammu Kashmir Latest News

JAMMU: Army paid tributes on Wednesday to Naib Subedar Valte Sunil Ravsaheb who got martyrdom in a gun battle with terrorists along the Line of Control (LoC) in Rajouri district.

Wreaths were laid by Brig Vikrant Kulkarni, Officiating Station Commander of Jammu, Brig AS Berar, Group Capt Sandeep Sharma, Col Sandeep Sharma and several other Army officials.

The mortal remains were taken from Jammu to Delhi in a service aircraft and will be further taken to Pune by civil airlines, a defence spokesperson said.

The mortal remains will be taken by road from Pune to his village in Ahmednagar district for last rites. (AGENCIES)
