Sadhvi Pragya welcomed by Muslims in Bhopal during roadshow; says "reflects unity between communities"

Pragya Singh Thakur, conducted a roadshow in Hathi Khana area of Bhopal, on Saturday.

Malegaon blast accused and BJP’s Lok Sabha candidate from Bhopal, Sadhvi Pragya Singh Thakur, received grand welcome by people of Muslim community during her road show in Bhopal on Saturday. Sadhvi held a roadshow in old Bhopal which is pre-dominantly a Muslim locality. An overwhelmed Pragya, said that the response from the people was an evidence of unity between communities. Two days ago Pragya in her statement regarding Muslims had said “Muslims are with us and those who are conspiring will never succeed.”
