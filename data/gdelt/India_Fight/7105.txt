Eyes glued to cellphone boy falls in lift duct

Singh’s friend Navin (21) who was on second floor rushed to the fourth floor with other friends on hearing his screams. He kept climbing stairs while chatting and on reaching the fourth floor stumbled against the half-erected wall. LUCKNOW: Texting while on move proved perilous for 24-year old MA student, Sumit Singh, who fell into an under-construction lift duct of a four-storey apartment in Aliganj on late Saturday night as his eyes were fixed on his smartphone . “We tied up few bedsheets and used it to descend in the duct to bring out Sumit. But, after going inside the duct, I couldn’t bring him up,” he added.Navin’s brother Amit and other friends rushed to the ground floor and found carpenters having dinner there.
