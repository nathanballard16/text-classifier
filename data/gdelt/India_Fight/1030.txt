Delhi woman killed for refusing to pay extortion money, accused arrested

A woman in her 50s was found dead outside her house in Dwarka's Uttam Nagar on Monday. The Crime Branch unit of the Delhi Police apprehended a juvenile for the alleged murder of the woman on the intervening night of January 1 and 2.

The accused, an aspiring rapper, was allegedly a member of a gang involved in crimes. The victim, 55-year-old Shama Devi, was killed for refusing to pay extortion money.

The incident was recorded on CCTV camera, which helped the police to identify the culprits.

Police laid a trap to arrest him from Najafgarh Road on January 5. During questioning, the boy said that he was influenced by rap artistes and wanted to become one himself. He told police that he had become a drug addict and began committing crimes for money.

Following a complaint by the victim's family, a case of extortion was registered against the accused at the Uttam Nagar police station on January 3.
