CGST officials raid another health club in Surat

SURAT: The CGST department raided yet another health club in the Diamond City for evasion of tax on Saturday.This is second consecutive raid by the CGST department on health clubs in the city. Three days ago, the CGST department had unearthed Rs2.50 crore worth of tax evasion by a well-known gym and fitness centre located at Piplod Sources said the CGST officials raided the club located on VIP Road on Saturday following information regarding tax evasion on membership fees.GST rate on health and gym centre is computed at 18%. The owners of the gym and fitness centre would deduct tax from the customers, but it was not being deposited with the GST department. The CGST officials seized documents, including account books and statements from the club for scrutiny.
