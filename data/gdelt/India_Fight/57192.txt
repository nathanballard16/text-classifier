PM Modi targets opposition for spreading false information on CAA, NRC

In what is seen as the launch of Bharatiya Janata Party's (BJP) campaign for the upcoming Assembly polls in Delhi Prime Minister Narendra Modi on Sunday addressed a rally in Delhi's Ramlila Maidan.

Starting his address with 'Bharat Mata ki jai', Modi said that the Centre had worked for the welfare for the people of Delhi by regularising unauthorised colonies.

Attacking the opposition leaders for misinforming people, he said that some people intentionally disseminated fake information to spread hatred.
