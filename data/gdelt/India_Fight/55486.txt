Patiala: Jailed gangster escapes after associates open fire at police team

PATIALA: Bhawan Singh alias Gaggi , an accused in a murder case, managed to give police slip after his accomplices opened fire at the police escorting him to the Moonak court for a case hearing.Gaggi, who was lodged at the Bathinda Jail, was brought to the Moonak court on Friday by a team of three policemen from Bathinda in connection with a hearing of a case registered under Section 323 of the IPC. They had reached the court in a Ritz car and after they left the court complex, Gaggi complained of nausea prompting the police team to halt the vehicle.As he stepped out of the vehicle along with the cops, at least 5 men who were travelling in a Brezza opened fire at the police team in which constable Charanjit Singh was injured as a bullet hit him in his leg.The police team also retaliated and opened fire at the criminals, but they managed to flee from the scene. The police team immediately sounded an alert and check barriers were erected at all strategic locations in and around Moonak.It is being suspected the accused drove towards Haryana . It was learnt that the accused had mudded the number plates of the vehicle to avoid identification. The police said a case under the relevant sections of the law including 307 of the IPC has been registered at the Moonak police station and a probe has also been ordered in the case.Gaggi who hails of Ladal village in Sangrur district is facing a number of cases including murder and was presently lodged at the Bathinda jail. An alert has been sounded across the state asking the security to maintain a vigil for a white Brezza vehicle in which five to six persons are travelling.
