BJP steps up attack on Cong over murder of two leaders

Bhopal: The state BJP on Sunday stepped up its attack on the Congress government in the wake of five killings, including the murder of two BJP leaders, during the last one week. Public relations minister PC Sharma said t BJP leaders were killing each other due to personal rivalry. BJP leaders are being killed and criminals have become fearless. Thakre was killed in Barwani, which is the home district of home minister Bala Bachchan on Sunday morning.However, Bachchan clarified on Sunday that it was an internal feud between BJP leaders which has resulted into the gory crime. “Allegations levelled by the saffron party against the Congress government were motivated and false”, he said.
