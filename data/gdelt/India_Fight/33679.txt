Mandana Karimi has been her fan's favourite ever since she appeared on the controversial reality show Bigg Boss 9. From undergoing a drastic weight loss transformation to making her Bollywood debut with Bhaag Johnny in 2015, Mandana never fails to remain in the headlines. This time, the diva gave yet another reason for her fans to admire her. Sporting fancy headgears and glittery ensembles, Mandana was luxury personified as she posed in designer Rocky Star's ensembles for the latest shoot.
