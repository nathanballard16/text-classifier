In February, Kolkata Police and CBI officials fought a pitched battle over the latter's attempt to quiz Kolkata police commissioner Rajeev Kumar over the Saradha scam. Indian depositors have lost massive amounts of money in ponzi schemes like Saradha's.

Synopsis

With the poor and the financially illiterate repeatedly tripped by fly-by-night operators, the government lays down severe punishment for duping investors. But there are grey areas in the new law. As the global experience shows, there are no easy remedies.
