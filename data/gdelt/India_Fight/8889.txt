Amid rising Indo-Pak tension Vice Chief of Army visits border areas in Gujarat

Bhuj:Amid rising tension between India and Pakistan after Pulwama terror attack, Lieutenant General Devraj Anbu, the Vice Chief of Army Staff (VCOAS) today reviewed the border security in Kutch district of Gujarat.

He also flew in army chopper over the sensitive Sir Creek Area.

Defence Sources said that Vice Chief of Army arrived here in the morning and then went to Kunvarbet by chopper.

From their he flew to Koteshwar and then to Lakhpat.

He also met with soldiers deployed for the border security.

During a meeting presided over by him the Border Security Force (BSF) officials and senior police officials from Narayan Sarovar and Lakhpat area also participated.
