Kanpur Couple baby girl killed in accident near Nawabganj

KANPUR: A young couple and their one-month-old daughter were killed after the car they were travelling in was hit by another vehicle near Nawabganj bird sanctuary on Kanpur-Lucknow national highway in Unnao on Thursday. The police said that Harvardhan and his family, residents of Nawabganj, had left home for Firozabad when the accident occured. According to the police, all the three died on the spot.The deceased were the close relatives of block head of Nawabganj, Arun Singh. The police are yet to identify the vehicle which hit the car causing death of the entire family.Some passersby informed the police about the accident. According to the eye-witnesses dense fog is suspected to have acused the accident.
