Sunny Leone celebrates her birthday on the sets of the reality show

Age is just a number for Sunny Leone who is celebrating her 38th birthday today. The actress is currently in Jaipur shooting a popular youth reality show . Sunny and Rannvijay Singha will be seen hosting the dating reality show. The shooting schedule somewhat kept her busy on her birthday, however her family and the production team had planned for a small party on the sets post the shoot.Her family and co-host Rannvijay surprised her with a special birthday cake. This is not the first time that the actress has celebrated her birthday on the sets.Earlier also the show team had arranged a special surprise for the actress. It has noticed from past couple of seasons now that the actress’ birthday usually coincides with the schedule of the reality show.
