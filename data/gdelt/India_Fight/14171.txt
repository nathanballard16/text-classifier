Health department raids Unsafe food colours impurities found

People are not aware that most food they are consuming might be adulterated or impure.From using unsafe synthetic food colours to compromising with food purity, health department’s food safety officers have found that all during their raids conducted in January and February. JAIPUR: Recently conducted raids at city’s restaurants, food item godowns and food processing units have brought shocking results to the fore. Besides, the health department is awaiting more test report of samples of food items during their raids. There are provisions under Food Safety Standard Act, which do not permit sale of spices without packaging. The health department had seized three such restaurants in past two months for violating FSSA norms.
