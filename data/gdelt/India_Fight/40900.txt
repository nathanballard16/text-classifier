Raima Sen begins shooting for 'Love. Sleep. Repeat'

Mumbai, Aug 25 : Actress Raima Sen has started shooting for the new web show "Love. Sleep. Repeat" Sharing the news, Raima took to social media and wrote: "love sleep repeat . The journey begins. Coming soon." Along with the post, she also posted a photograph of herself posing with the crew on the set.

"Love. Sleep. Repeat" will stream on Zee 5.

The series also features actress Priyal Gor and actor Puneesh Sharma of "Bigg Boss" fame.
