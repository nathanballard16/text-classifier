Nimta mastermind Prince aide arrested

Kolkata: In a major breakthrough, the Barrackpore police on Saturday arrested Prince Singh, the mastermind behind the murder of Debanjan Das who was found dead in North 24-Parganas' Nimta on Nabami night. According to sources, the Barrackpore police came to know about his whereabouts after interrogating his aunt and his niece. Incidentally, earlier in the day, the police arrested Prince's aide Bishal Maru, a co-accused in connection with the murder. The investigating officers came to know that Maru had spoken to Prince several times over phone after the crime was committed. In the preliminary investigation, the cops came to know that Prince had asked his friend Maru to visit the pub to keep a tab on Das.
