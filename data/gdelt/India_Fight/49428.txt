Three killed in Murshidabad crude bomb explosion

Kolkata: Three persons were killed in an explosion at Jalangi in Murshidabad on Monday evening. It is alleged that miscreants had stored crude bombs at an abandoned house at Taltali in Jalangi, which accidentally exploded. All of a sudden locals heard sound of a high-intensity explosion. It is suspected that the trio died due to explosion of crude bombs. Sources informed that two groups were involved in aconflict over the issue of controlling the Taltali ferry in Jalangi.
