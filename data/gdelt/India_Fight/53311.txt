As India demands justice for Telangana doctor, CM KCR attends high profile wedding

Four days after the Telangana doctor was raped and murdered, Chief Minister K Chandrashekhar Rao KCR, who has not uttered a word on the brutalily or visisted the victim's family, was seen attending a high profile wedding of TRS MLA's daughter in Hyderabad.

CM K Chandrashekar Rao along with his cabinet colleagues attended Khanapur MLA Rekha Naik's daughter wedding and blessed the couple.

Minister Satyavathi Rathod, Rajya Sabha MP Kesav Rao, J. Santosh, Mahabubabad MLA Shankar Nayak and another party leader also attended the big fat wedding in Hyderabad today.

A 26-year-old veterinarian was brutally gangraped and killed by four lorry workers before they burned her body on Wednesday night in Telangana's Shamshabad.

The gruesome rape and murder case, which is being perceived as another 'Nirbhaya', has sparked national outrage.

The Telangana veterinary surgeon's charred body was found on Thursday morning under a culvert in Chatanpally on the Hyderabad-Bengaluru national highway.

The Cyberabad Police arrested four men on Friday in connection with their involvement in the rape and murder of the Telangana doctor. The accused have been identified as Mohammad Areef, Jollu Shiva, Jollu Naveen, and Chintakunta Chennakeshavulu. Mohammad Areef (25), who works as a driver, is the prime accused in the Telangana rape-murder case.

Neighbours and relatives of the victim have been protesting in demand for justice for the 26-year-old.

Meanwhile, Union MoS Sanjeev Balyan was allowed to visit the victim's family and spoke to them today. He said will raise this matter with Prime Minister Narendra Modi on Monday.
