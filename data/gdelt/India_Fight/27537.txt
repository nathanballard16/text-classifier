Man killed in north Kashmir

SRINAGAR: A 39-year-old man was shot dead by terrorists in north Kashmir’s Kupwara district on Monday evening. He was a fruit merchant.Police said unknown gunmen shot Abdul Majeed Shah (39) outside his home in Babagund area of Handwara . He was critically injured and rushed to a nearby hospital. He succumbed to his injuries on the way.Police they said have taken cognizance of the matter and investigations are on. A hunt was launched to hunt to nab the gunmen.
