AIIMS MBBS Open Counselling 2019 now on August 26, 27: All you need to know

The All India Institute of Medical Sciences has rescheduled the counselling dates for its MBBS programme from August 20 and 21 to August 26 and 27. "Considering the large number of candidates and guardians participating in the open round of counselling for MBBS 2019, under the prevailing circumstances, it has been decided by the competent authority that for adequate arrangement and ensuring safety, the open round of counselling has been rescheduled from August 20 and 21 to August 26 and 27," says an AIIMS notice.
