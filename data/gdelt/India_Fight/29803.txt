Actor Varun Tej meets with accident

Hyderabad: Telugu film actor Konidela Sai Varun Tej, who was travelling to Bengaluru from Hyderabad for a film shooting, has met with an accident on Wednesday night at Rainpeta stage in Wanaparthy district.

Sources said Tej and his friends were in three different cars. When the Audi car Tej was travelling in reached Rainpeta stage an Indica car rammed it. Reportedly, the driver of the car was was in a inebriated condition. Two persons sustained small injuries and were taken to a hospital. Meanwhile Tej posted on social media, “Got into a car accident and thankfully everybody is safe and sound.No injuries whatsoever. Thanks for the concern and your love (sic)”.
