Suspect In Westerville Homicide In Dayton Police Custody

A suspect in a Westerville homicide has turned himself in to police in Dayton.

Westerville police say44-year-old Ranae Baxter was killed at a home on Kanpur Place on Friday. Police say 42-year-old Tyrone Lauderdale is being held in Dayton on a murder charge. Court records also show Lauderdale called his daughter and said he stabbed and killed his girlfriend. An investigation by local and state authorities is underway.
