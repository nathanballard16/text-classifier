Alia Bhatt who was recently in Ooty to shoot for her upcoming film Sadak 2, turned her work trip into a family vacation with her mother Soni Razdan and sister Shaheen Bhatt by her side. The actress took to Instagram to share a picture of herself playing on a swing. She captioned it as ".. don’t forget to play". Alia's elder sister Pooja Bhatt who starred in the original film will also be a part of the sequel. The film is scheduled to release on July 2020.
