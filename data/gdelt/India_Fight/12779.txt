India: 2 militants die in clashes with troopers

(MENAFN) Officials declared that two militants have died on Tuesday in an intense clash with soldiers in troubled Indian-controlled Kashmir.



The clash broke out on Monday night at Mir Mohalla-Tral of Pulwama district, about 39 km south of Srinagar city, the summer capital of Indian-controlled Kashmir.



A senior Indian army official confirmed: "Two militants were killed today in a gunfight with troops in Tral,".



The official added: "The gunfight started last night and continued until this morning,"
