2 killed, 20 injured in road accident in Maharashtra

Raigad (Maharashtra) [India], May 20 (ANI): Two people were killed while 20 others were injured when two buses collided on the Mumbai-Pune highway near Khalapur, Raigad on Monday.

State police and emergency units are carrying out the rescue and relief work.

The injured have been rushed to a nearby hospital.

Further details are awaited. (ANI)
