J&K: Blast at Ghanta Ghar near Lal Chowk in Srinagar, second attack under 24 hrs

Militants carried out two grenade attacks on security forces in Kashmir, including one in the Lal Chowk area of the city, on Friday but there were no reports of any casualty, police said. (Express photo by Shuaib Masoodi)

There was no loss to life or property reported after an explosion occurred Friday at the Ghanta Ghar in Lal Chowk in Srinagar, J&K police told ANI in a statement. This is the second explosion in the city under 24 hours.

In a separate incident, militants lobbed a grenade at a police camp in Gagran area of South Kashmir’s Shopian, news agency ANI reported. Police have registered cases for both the incidents.

Thursday’ attack took place less than 100 metres from the National Conference headquarters and the local office of the All India Radio.
