Chandigarh: Following a domestic discord, a 35-year-old man killed his wife and minor daughter at Amar Enclave in Azad Nagar of Hisar district, Haryana on Saturday morning. The accused apparently used a sharp weapon to murder his family members.

According to a report in The Times of India, the police have arrested the accused under Section 302 (murder) of the Indian Penal Code. The deceased were identified as 35-year-old Sarala Devi and her 16-year-old daughter Pooja.

The police officials stated that Shravan who was a resident of Dobhi village in Hisar had rented the Amar Enclave house about six months ago. He was reportedly living on the first floor along with his wife, daughter and his son Manjeet. While, the owner named as Vinod, lived on the ground floor of the house.

On Saturday morning at around 11:30 am, Sarla and Pooja were screaming loudly, hearing their cries for help Vinod and his wife rushed upstairs to check what was happening. They found the door closed and no one opened it even after repeated knocks. Meanwhile, other neighbours broke one of the glass windows and the entered the room. They found the women lying in a pool of blood.

However, the neighbours handed Shravan to the police. He has been booked under relevant sections of the Indian Penal Code while further probe in the matter is underway.
