NEW DELHI: The Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) has released the online admit card for private candidate for compartment examinations 2019. The admit cards were released on the official website of the CBSE Board - http://cbse.nic.in/The class 10th and 12th private candidates who have applied for the CBSE compartment exam 2019 are advised to visit the official website of the Board to check and download their admit card online.The students can follow the steps provided below to check and download their admit card via official website of the Board:1) Click on the direct link provided here - CBSE Online Admit Card for Private Candidate for Comptt Examination 2019 2) Now select your region - Other Region or Delhi Region3) Next, select the search option, there are three options provided on the page - Application No. or Previous Roll no. and year or Candidate name4) Enter your details in the box and then click on Proceed5) Your online admit card will be displayed on the screen6) Download your admit card and also take a print out of the same for future useThe candidates need to bring the print out of the admit card to the exam centre. Students without admit card will not be allowed to enter exam hall.
