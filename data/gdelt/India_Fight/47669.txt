Massive fire at Hotel Dreamland near Pune railway station, no casualties

Hotel Dreamland in Pune was engulfed in flames on Wednesday. | Photo Credit: Times Now

Key Highlights The reason behind the fire has not been ascertained yet

Pune: A massive fire broke out at a hotel near the Pune railway station late on Wednesday night. Hotel Dreamland, which lies opposite Pune Railway Station was engulfed in flames. However, the reason behind the fire has not been ascertained yet.

According to preliminary reports, there have been no casualties.
