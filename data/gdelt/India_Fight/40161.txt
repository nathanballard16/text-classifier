Couple found murdered in UP's Tharwai village

ALLAHABAD: A couple was found murdered at their residence at Hasanpur Kokari village under the limits of Tharwai police station on early Monday morning.The deceased have been identified as Santosh Prajapati (30) and his wife Seema (25).The bodies of husband and wife were spotted on separate cots with their throats slit.Upon being alerted, senior police officials along with forensic experts rushed to the spot and carried out investigations.SP trans Ganga N K Singh told TOI that bodies of the couple were spotted by neighbours in a pool of blood and they informed the police. The motive behind the killing is yet to be ascertained.
