Gold worth Rs 18L seized by customs at Chennai airport

CHENNAI: Customs officials at the city airport seized gold worth 18 lakh from a passenger flying in from Bongkok on Saturday.Sekh Mohammad Maim, 23, of Hooghly from West Bengal, was intercepted soon after he landed in the city. During search, officials found that small gold cylindrical rods were concealed in the wheels of his stroller. The rods weighing 203g, valued at 8 lakh, were recovered.He also carried gold rubbery spread concealed inside a sanitary pad in his underwear. On extraction, 270g of gold valued at 10 lakh was recovered. Total 473g of gold valued at 18 lakh was seized from the passenger. The customs officials said further investigations are on.
