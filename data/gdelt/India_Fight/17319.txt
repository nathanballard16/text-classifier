College student critically injured in collision with truck

Ludhiana: College student Lovedeep Singh (17) — who was riding a white-coloured two-wheeler was critically injured when a truck hit him on a turning in Dugri Phase 3 near Bal Bharti School around 2.20pm. A passer-by who saw the incident said the truck driver was turning in a wrong direction, which led to the collision with the two-wheeler. We have got all the evidence from the accident spot, and have seized the truck. The truck driver fled from the spot immediately after the accident.Shubh, another college student who was on the spot at the time of the accident, said: “I was coming from my college after writing an exam, when I saw people gathering around the accident site. When I saw the boy, he was kind of half-dead, as the truck had hit him badly.
