Mastercard continues to battle rising challenge of digital fraud

Financial services major Mastercard acknowledges the problem and is focusing on mitigating digital fraud, which has been on the rise in India and the world as more and more people switch to digital transactions. “More than 70 per cent of the detected frauds are from digital transactions, which are 35 per cent of our volume.
