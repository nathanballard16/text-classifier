2 criminals arrested after separate encounters with police in Ghaziabad head constable injured

Ghaziabad: Two criminals carrying rewards on their arrest have been nabbed after separate encounters with police here, in which both of them and a constable sustained bullet wounds, police said on Saturday. Head Constable Pankaj sustained a bullet shot injury, following which the police party returned fire, officials said. One of the suspects, identified Dharmendra, alias Tindi, was injured in the police fire and arrested, while the other managed to flee, they said. Head Constable Pankaj and the two criminals were sent to nearby hospitals for treatment, the SSP said. Two countrymade pistols and two used and four live cartridges were recovered from the arrested duo, he added.
