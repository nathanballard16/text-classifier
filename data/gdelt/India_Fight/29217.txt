Days after Aligarh case, minor raped by 6 men in Uttar Pradesh over family dispute

Police have registered a case against six accused under relevant sections of the Indian Penal Code | Photo Credit: ANI

Lucknow: Days after the brutal Aligarh murder case, a 12-year-old girl was allegedly raped by six men in Kushinagar district in Uttar Pradesh. The incident took place in the Ahirouli Bazar police station area on Friday evening. Reportedly, the police have arrested four accused in the case and two others are still absconding. The police are trying to nab the rest of two accused.

According to news agency PTI, Superintendent of Police R N Mishra said that they have registered a case against six accused under relevant sections of the Indian Penal Code, Protection of Children from Sexual Offences Act and the SC/ST Act. Meanwhile, the survivor has been sent for a medical examination.

Just like the Aligarh case which has sparked tensions in the area, similarly, the accused in this case had a dispute with the survivor's family over the construction of the drainage channel on Friday morning. By evening, they dragged the girl in front of the family and raped her. The accused thrashed the family members when they resisted.

However, survivor's mother filed a written complaint to the police on Saturday and the case was registered on Sunday.
