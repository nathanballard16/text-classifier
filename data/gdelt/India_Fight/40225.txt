Sovan Chatterjee confident of win against Mamata

Former Calcutta mayor Sovan Chatterjee, who had defected to the BJP from the Trinamul Congress, said on Tuesday that he was ready to take on his former mentor Mamata Banerjee in an electoral battle, in which he was confident of victory.

Chatterjee visited the Bengal BJP office on Tuesday with his associate Baishakhi Banerjee. The duo were greeted by state BJP chief Dilip Ghosh.
