Police Open Fire on Telengana Agitators: One Killed

sanjiv@tribunemail.com

HYDERABAD, June 26 (PTI)—One person was killed on the spot and an 18-year-old college student sustained injuries when police opened fire on a violent crowd of Telengana agitators at the Gowliguda area of the city this evening, according to sources. The injured student was admitted to hospital. He had received a bullet injury. Trouble started when a large number of agitators stoned a police party stationed here. The same locality was the scene of firing yesterday when a crowd tried to snatch away rifles and ammunition kept in a shop.
