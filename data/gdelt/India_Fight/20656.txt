Arjun Kapoor Starring India's Most Wanted Teaser Out Tomorrow

From the director of No One Killed Jessica and Raid, here comes an untold inspiring story of true events based espionage drama "India's Most Wanted", the teaser from the movie will be out tomorrow.

The makers of the film just dropped the official poster on social media along with confirmation that the first official teaser will be out on 16th April 2019.

The Fox Star Hindi handle dropped the poster and wrote, "An untold story inspired by true events. Presenting #IndiasMostWanted, from the director of No One Killed Jessica and Raid. Teaser out tomorrow. @arjunk26 @rajkumar_rkg @raapchik_films #IMW"

The film is reportedly about a secret mission that leads an arrest of a terrorist and it will be shot primarily in Nepal and Delhi. Arjun Kapoor will be seen playing the role of an intelligence officer who is on the chase.

Arjun Kapoor also shared his excitement about the poster and upcoming teaser. He tweeted, "I am proud to present to all of you #IndiasMostWanted, an unbelievable story inspired by true events. Teaser out tomorrow. @rajkumar_rkg @foxstarhindi @raapchik_films #IMW"

India's Most Wanted is an upcoming action thriller film directed by Raj Kumar Gupta, also starring Amrita Puri in pivotal role. The film is scheduled to release in 24 May 2019.
