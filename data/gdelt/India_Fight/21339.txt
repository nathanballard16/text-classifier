Encounter breaks out in Baramulla, J&K; 1 terrorist gunned down

The attack was done on convoy of 32 Rashtriya Rifles patrolling party. As per reports, 2-3 terrorists attacked the Army convoy, after which Army jawans killed one terrorist in a retaliatory firing; whereas the rest of the terrorists were successful in fleeing. After this incident, the security officials cordoned the area and again initiated a search operation on Saturday morning.
