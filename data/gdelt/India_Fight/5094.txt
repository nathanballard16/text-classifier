BJP MPs trader cousin shot at in Moradabad

LUCKNOW: A cousin of BJP MP from Sambhal, Satya Pal Singh Saini, was shot at by unidentified assailants outside his shop in Moradabad on Friday evening. His condition was stated to be stable.The incident took place around 6pm when Jai Prakash reached his shop in Pakhwara market. There were reports Jai Prakash was on his way to his shop after meeting the inspector in charge of the local police station when the incident took place. The 32-year-old victim was shot thrice and doctors found a bullet lodged in his stomach. The condition of Jai Prakash, who's undergoing treatment at a private hospital in Moradabad, was stated to be stable and doctors were yet to perform a surgery and remove the bullet from his stomach.
