J&K: Truck Driver Killed By Terrorists In Shopian; Jawan Dies In Landmine Blast In Nowgam

“Two desperate terrorists targeted a Rajasthan registered truck and its driver Sharief Khan, who was shot dead by them,” news agency ANI quoted sources in Jammu and Kashmir police.

A Rajasthan truck driver and a jawan were killed by terrorists in Shopian and Kupwara districts of Jammu and Kashmir respectively, in separate incidents, on Monday. Two unknown terrorists including one from Pakistan attacked and killed a truck driver in Shopian district. An apple orchard owner was also attacked and beaten up in Shirmal, agencies reported. In the preliminary investigation, ut came to the fore that the truck was from Rajasthan which had come to carry fruits from Kashmir. It was already laden with fruits when the incident took place.
