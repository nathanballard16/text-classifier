Trader his neighbour shot dead for resisting robbery bid in Ghaziabad

Ghaziabad: A 40-year-old trader and a youth from his neighbourhood were shot dead by unidentified robbers for resisting a robbery attempt in Tronica city area of Ghaziabad in the early morning on Monday. Cops said that another youth also sustained bullet injuries and was admitted to hospital in a critical condition. According to police, the deceased were identified as Dharamveer Sharma (40), grocery shop owner and owner of the house where the robbery was being committed and his neighbour Sonu Verma (22). The incident occurred around 3 am on Monday when nearly half a dozen robbers barged into Dharveer's house. Meanwhile, two youths, Sonu and Babu, reacted to the sound of gunshot and rushed for help.
