Head-on collision leaves two seriously injured

Ludhiana: Two jeep-borne middle-aged persons suffered serious injuries after their vehicle collided with a truck near Walio village on Friday night. They have been identified as Kulwant Singh (50) and Kulwinder Singh (45) of Bhagnor village.

The police said Kulwant and Kulwinder were going from Samrala to Machhiwara in their jeep on Friday night for some work. They said their vehicle collided with a truck coming from the opposite side near Walio village. The police said the duo had suffered serious injuries. Passers-by informed the police and called on the ambulance service.

The police said they were rushed to the Samrala civil hospital. They said doctors later referred them to the PGI, Chandigarh.
