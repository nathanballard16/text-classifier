Bhaiyyu Maharaj Was Blackmailed, Pressured to Marry By Secy: Cops

Madhya Pradesh Police on Sunday, 20 January, said spiritual leader Bhaiyyu Maharaj, who allegedly shot himself in 2018, was being blackmailed by his personal secretary to marry her.

Police have arrested three people on the charges of abetting to suicide and for “secretly” giving Maharaj "high doses of medicines" to "control" him, a senior police official said on Saturday, 19 January, according to PTI.
