Kejriwal condemns terror attack on CRPF

New Delhi, Feb 14 : Delhi Chief Minister Arvind Kejriwal on Thursday condemned the terror attack on a CRPF convoy in Jammu and Kashmir's Pulwama district that left 10 troopers dead.

"Extremely shocking... I strongly condemn the terror attack on CRPF convoy in which many casualties are feared. India must stand united in this moment of grief," he tweeted.
