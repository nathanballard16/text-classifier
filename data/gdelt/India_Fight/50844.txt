Ayodhya verdict: Police readied riot gear for SC security

NEW DELHI: Just like journalists, Delhi Police , which is entrusted with apex Court security, was expecting the Ayodhya verdict next week and was jolted with the SC’s late evening announcement on Friday that Saturday will be judgment day in the historically sensitive case.Within minutes of the announcement at 9 pm on Friday, DCP S K Tewari swung into action by requisitioning sufficient riot gear from Police Lines for a core team from among the thousand-strong contingent chosen to provide security to the court complex.When reporters reached the SC more than two hours before the judgment, which was scheduled to be pronounced at 10.30 am on Saturday, it was a sight with police in riot gear deployed strategically inside the complex. Outside, the police blocked entry of the general public.
