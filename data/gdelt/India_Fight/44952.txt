3 sharpshooters nabbed

New Delhi: Three sharp shooters of gangster Sandeep alias Dheelu were arrested in a shootout with Delhi police early morning on Wednesday in Dwarka's Dhansa Najafgarh road on Wednesday. Notorious gangster Sandeep alias Dheelu may also be accompanying them. While returning back they would take Main Dhansa Najafgarh Road to reach their hideout in South Delhi, said DCP Special Cell, Sanjeev KumarYadav. "Acting on a tip-off, a team of Special Cell laid a trap was on main Dhansa Najafgarh Road and the Honda Accord car was intercepted. "Surjeet alias Polu sustained gunshot wound in right hand while Parmod alias Sonu was shot in right leg.
