BJP alleges attack by 40 TMC workers in Kolkata

Several Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP) workers were on Thursday evening allegedly attacked by Trinamool Congress (TMC) workers in Netaji Nagar area of south Kolkata in West Bengal.

The incident took place at the Regent Park where 40 TMC workers attacked them, alleged the saffron party.

Following this, the supporters of the BJP surrounded the Regent Park police station and held protests.
