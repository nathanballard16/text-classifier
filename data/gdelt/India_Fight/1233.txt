Armies of India, Pak exchange fire along LoC in Keran sector

Srinagar

Armies of both India and Pakistan exchanged gunfire and shells in Keran sector near Bulbir post in Athmuqam area of Kupwara district, along the Line of Control (LoC) on Wednesday.

Reports said that both the armies exchanged gunfire in Keran sector, Bulbir post in Athmuqam, however, there were no reports of causality from either side.

Earlier, there were reports of exchange of small arms fire and heavy shells along the Line of Control in Poonch district of Jammu.
