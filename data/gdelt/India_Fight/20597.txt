Three of a family killed in accident

RAJKOT: Three persons of the same family were killed when the car they were travelling in turned turtle in Kukavav of Amreli district on Sunday night. The deceased were identified as Bharti Jotania (29), her mother-in-law Geeta (52) and her five-month-old nephew Het.According to police, the Jotania family, which lives in Sitaram Society on Kotharia Road in Rajkot, had gone to Chalala village of Amreli taluka to participate in a religious ritual. They were returning home on Sunday. “The car was being driven by Sanjay Jotania, husband of Bharti. The real cause of the accident is yet to be known as Sanjay is not in condition to speak,” said an official from Kukavav police station.
