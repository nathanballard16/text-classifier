NKDA chalks out elaborate plans to battle vector borne diseases

Kolkata: New Town Kolkata Development Authority has chalked out elaborate measures to control the spread of vector-borne diseases. One particular day of the week is fixed for spray in a particular block. One particular supervisor is also fixed for a particular block. NKDA app is provided to supervisors and the representatives of the block with the direction that the supervisors will post snap shot in the Whatsapp. NKDA officials maintained that the measures have helped to bring down the number of vector borne diseases considerably.
