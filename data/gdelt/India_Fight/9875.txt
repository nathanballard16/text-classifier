IAF strikes in Pak: Will support forces in their bid to end terrorism, says Congress after all-party- The New Indian Express

By PTI

NEW DELHI: Leaders of different political parties on Tuesday congratulated the Indian Air Force at an all-party meeting here on operation targeting a terrorist camp in Pakistan, with the Congress saying that it will support security forces in their endeavour to finish terrorism coming into India from outside.
