Salman Khan and Sonakshi Sinha spend time with special children in Rajasthan amidst Dabangg 3 shoot : Bollywood News

Salman Khan and Sonakshi Sinha are currently shooting for the last leg of their upcoming film Dabangg 3 in Rajasthan. The actors took some time off from their schedule to visit special children of Umang to brighten up their day.

Photos shared by Bina Kak on her social media handle show the actors having a gala time with the kids. Salman Khan even shook a leg with the kids. Meanwhile, Sonakshi Sinha was all smiles as she posed with the children.

Sharing the picture of Salman Khan dancing with the kids, Bina wrote, “Dancing on the sets ...Salman khan spending quality time with children of #umangjaipur on the sets of #dabangg3 ..Special tent was put up for children n they were treated with patties, wafers n pastries by the crew. They all had a ball with their favorite Bhaijaan.”

Salman Khan also surprised Umang School by bearing the cost of solar power for their building in Jaipur.

Meanwhile, Dabangg 3 also stars Arbaaz Khan, Pramod Khanna and South star Kiccha Sudeep. The film’s shooting had kicked off in Madhya Pradesh and then moved to Mumbai. Sudeep will be the antagonist in the film and Dabangg 3 will have two parallel tracks of Chulbul’s past and present. The action sequences between Salman and Sudeep were shot in Mumbai.

The film will be released on December 20 in Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, and Kannada.
