The Government of India today introduced the scrapping of Article 370 and Article 35A.

To protest against the central government ‘s unilateral decision on J&K, several organisations and concerned citizens organised a demonstration today at Jantar Mantar, New Delhi at 3 pm.

The citizens asserted that this move without taking into account the voices of people of Jammu and Kashmir is undemocratic and Illegal.

They shouted slogans demanding ‘Immediate release of all opposition leaders in Kashmir!’

‘Restore Article 370 and 35A!’

‘Stop meddling with the Indian Constitution!’
