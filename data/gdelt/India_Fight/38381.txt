Fire At Hyderabad’s Gandhi Hospital

Hyderabad: A fire broke out at the state run Gandhi Hospital here on Thursday evening.It took place in the Children’s ward on the third floor of the hospital.

Patients and attendees were in panic watching the flames at the spot.The reason behind the fire is said to be a short circuit that escalated to the level of a fire.There are no causalities reported as the staff responded quickly at the sight of the incident.
