BSF Officer injured as India, Pakistan armies exchange fire along LoC in Poonch

Srinagar

An assistant sub-inspector of the Border Security Force (BSF) was injured after armies of India and Pakistan exchanged fire at Krishna Ghati sector in Poonch district late last night.

An official said that the ASI Satyapal Singh received splinter injuries after Pakistan army attacked Indian army posts in the area.

The injured ASI was shifted to Rajouri hospital for treatment where his condition is said to be stable.
