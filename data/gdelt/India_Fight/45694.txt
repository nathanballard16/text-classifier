Kerala ISRO scientist found murdered in Hyderabad apartment

By IANSHYDERABAD: A scientist with the National Remote Sensing Centre (NRSC) of the Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) was Tuesday found murdered in his apartment here, police said. S. Suresh, 56, was allegedly killed by unknown persons at his flat at Annapurna Apartment in Ameerpet area in the heart of the city. Suresh's wife along with some other family members rushed to Hyderabad and approached the police. and said they were scanning the CCTV footage of the apartment complex to probe the case. His wife was also working in the city but was transferred to Chennai in 2005.
