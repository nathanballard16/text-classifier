Criminals chase down man in Delhi, shoot him. And then they celebrate | Watch

A man was shot by six gangsters inside his house in Mahendra Park area of Jahangirpuri in Delhi.



The shooutout, which is said to be a result of a gangwar, has been caught on camera, where the criminals can be seen shooting a man inside his house.



The victim has been identified as Naresh and is being treated in the hospital.



The shocking CCTV footage shows the victim, running to save his life, while six men, unaffected by the presence of onlookers, chase and fearlessly shoot at him from the entrance of his house as soon as he gets in to hide.



The criminals fired multiple rounds and left the scene. The CCTV footage shows the criminals celebrating after the shooutout.
