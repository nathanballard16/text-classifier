Elephant found dead

Coimbatore: A wild elephant was found dead in Anaikatti near Thudiyalur range on Wednesday.

Forest department officials said that the carcass of the female elephant, estimated to be about 15 years old, was found in a decomposed state. Residents of the Kootupulikadu tribal settlement had spotted the carcass after foul smell emanated from the area. The cause of the animal’s death was yet to be ascertained. Forest officials said that a postmortem would be conducted.

In July, a female elephant was found dead near Thoomanur tribal settlement in Anaikatti. TNN
