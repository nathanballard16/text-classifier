CRPF constable shoots dead 3 jawans after altercation in J&K camp, then shoots self

The alleged fratricide incident happened at around 10 pm when constable Ajit Kumar shot three of his colleagues from his service rifle.

In a shocking incident, a CRPF personnel allegedly killed three of his colleagues after a feud broke out between them on Wednesday and then allegedly shot himself. The accused CRPF personnel named Ajit Kumar is critical in a hospital. The three personnel who were fired at by their colleague, died at the 187th battalion camp of the force in the Battal Ballian area in Udhampur. The personnel first got into an altercation after which the shooting took place.
