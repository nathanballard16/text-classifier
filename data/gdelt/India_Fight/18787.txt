Raid on arms factory

Greater Noida: Police raided a factory that allegedly manufactured illegal arms and ammunition and arrested one person on Thursday.The accused has been identified as Ranjit, from Kasna village in Greater Noida.Around 1am, police raided the building in the industrial area, Site V of Kasna, Greater Noida, and recovered equipment used in manufacturing country made pistols, along with some arms and ammunitions. “We conducted a raid and arrested the person who was running the factory. He has been named in various crimes, including arms smuggling, in the past as well,” said Sujeet Upadhyay, SHO, Greater Noida police station.The police claim to have recovered 14 country made pistols, one pistol, iron pipes and other items.
