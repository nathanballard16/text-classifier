Mumbai fire: Flames engulf commercial building in Andheri; several feared trapped

Fire broke out at a commercial building in Mumbai's Andheri on Monday afternoon. Flames engulfed the sixth floor of the Peninsula Park building located off the Veera Desai road in Andheri West.

Several people are feared trapped in the 22-storied building. Meanwhile, there are no reports of any casualties yet, India Today reported.
