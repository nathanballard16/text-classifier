Man wanted in murder case arrested

New Delhi, July 29A man, who was absconding after killing a person in Punjabi Bagh area earlier this month, was arrested by the special cell of the Delhi Police, said the police officials on Monday. The police said accused, Arif alias Ikram, was a resident of Sultanpuri. According to the police, he was wanted in a case of murder earlier this July wherein he had attacked a person named Raees and his brother Salim, who was driving a car. The killing was a result of an ongoing tussle between Arif and Raees, a resident of outer Delhi’s Nangloi, a senior police officer said. Arif is previously found to be involved in 35 cases, including that of murder, extortion, robbery and motor vehicle thefts, said the police.
