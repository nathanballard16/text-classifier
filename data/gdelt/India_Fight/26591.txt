Grenade hurled at SBI guard post in Pulwama

Srinagar

Unknown persons on Tuesday evening hurled a grenade at a Central Reserve Police Force (CRPF) bunker situated at State Bank of India in south Kashmir’s Pulwama district.

Reports said that the unknown persons lobbed grenade on the CRPF bunker located in the premises of State Bank of India in Pulwama, but it missed the target and exploded outside.

However, there were no immediate reports of any injury or damage.

Soon after the attack, forces cordoned off the area and launched a hunt for the attackers.
