Kareena Kapoor Khan and Saif Ali Khan to resume their work soon after their Tuscan vacation

A few days back Kareena Kapoor Khan and Saif Ali Khan were spotted at the airport with their little munchkin Taimur heading to London to spend some time off their busy work schedule and pictures from their vacay started making rounds on the internet.Now that the couple is soaking in the Tuscan sun with their son, the couple would soon be back and would resume their hectic work schedule. As per a report, the couple is currently staying in a six-bedroom villa in Tuscany with some great amenities like a private pool and a chef.After their six-day stay, the couple will be heading to London wherein Saif will be shooting for his upcoming film 'Jawaani Jaaneman'. Post which Bebo will be returning back to India for a day to shoot for her upcoming dance reality show as she too will start shooting for 'Angrezi Medium' in London soon.Meanwhile, Kareena will next be seen in films like, ‘Takht’, ‘Good News’ and ‘Angrezi Medium’. Saif, on the other hand, has films like, ‘Bhoot Police’, ‘Jawaani Jaaneman’ and ‘Tanhaji: The Unsung Warrior’.
