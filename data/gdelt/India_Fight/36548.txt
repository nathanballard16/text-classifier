27-year-old man murdered during a birthday celebration in Ghatkopar

Ghatkopar

A 27-year-old man who was celebrating his birthday, was killed by a group of people on Sunday night.The incident took place late in the night inThe man was killed using a sharp weapon by a group of seven to eight people.The exact reason behind the incident is yet to be identified.However, Police Inspector Pratap Bhosle said, "Prima facie, the deceased had an altercation with some people four to five days ago, and he was killed in connection with that."
