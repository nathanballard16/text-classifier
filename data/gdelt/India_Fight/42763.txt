Four days after youth dies kin refuse to accept body in Rajasthan

KOTA: Four days after the custodial death of one Giriraj Mali (20), who allegedly consumed poison in police custody in Mangrole police station, Baran his family refused to accept the body. They are demanding that the accused police officers be booked for murder and that there should be one more postmortem by a high-level medical board.Meanwhile, Baran-Jhalawar MP and BJP leader Dushyant Singh and Kiran Maheshwari on Sunday joined protesters MLA Madan Dilawar, ex-MLA Lalit Meena and Hemraj Meena. They staged a dharna before Mangrole tehsil office supporting the family’s demands. tnn
