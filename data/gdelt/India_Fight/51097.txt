Photos: Aamir and Kareena party on sets of ‘Laal Singh Chaddha’ in Chandigarh

Mumbai: Kareena Kapoor and Aamir Khan’s ‘jodi’ has given us blockbuster hits like ‘3 Idiots’ and ‘Talaash’. Post a span of seven years they are coming on the silver screen together with ‘Laal Singh Chaddha’. The senior actors are shooting for the film in Chandigarh, but are also wearing their party shoes. For work doesn’t stop them for enjoying. The duo was seen partying with their team members.

The soiree also saw Aamir’s wife Kiran Rao though his son Azad Rao Khan and Kareena’s baby boy Taimur were unseen.

Previously Kareena’s first looks photos have been leaked online. In the pictures, she is seen wearing a traditional beige colored Kurti with a purple colored ‘dupatta’. Along with Kareena, a picture of Aamir from the film set has also gone viral. In the photo, Aamir can be seen seated on grass with his laptop as the shooting of the film was taking place.

'Laal Singh Chaddha' is the Hindi official remake of Tom Hank’s 1984 Hollywood film Forrest Gump. The film is set to release on December 25, 2020.
