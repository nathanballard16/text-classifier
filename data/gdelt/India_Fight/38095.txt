Man combating 4 criminal cases shot

New Delhi: A 25-year-old man, who had a criminal background, was stabbed several times and shot dead barely 50 metres away from his house in north-east’s Nand Nagari area on Monday evening. The deceased has been identified as Vicky and was battling four criminal cases registered against him. The police have identified four suspects and have detained few of them for interrogation. Vicky was married, the police said.
