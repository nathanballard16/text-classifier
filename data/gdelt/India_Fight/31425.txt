608gm gold seized at MIA

MANGALURU: Customs officers at Mangaluru International Airport (MIA) on Friday seized 608gm of gold from a passenger who landed here from Dubai on Air India Express flight 184.The passenger had stashed the 24-carat gold in his rectum. The passenger was nabbed on the basis of close profiling, customs officers said. The gold was concealed in the form of paste.The passenger was arrested, a tweet from Customs stated.
