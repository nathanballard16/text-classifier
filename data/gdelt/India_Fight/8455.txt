Encounter breaks out in Sopore, North Kashmir; 1 terrorist killed

The area has been cordoned off by security forces.

The police have imposed section 144 in the area and the internet services have been suspended to ensure the terrorist cannot communicate. As per sources, the terrorists belong to Jaish-e-Mohammad terror outfit, which were responsible for the deadly Pulwama attack, which claimed the lives of 40 Indian soldiers on February 14.
