Priya Prakash Varrier's wink film to release in Kannada

One of the most popular faces on social media is Malayalam actress Priya Prakash Varrier , who shot to instant fame from her ' wink sequence video' from Oru Adaar Love. The clip from the high school love story went viral and Priya was instantly dubbed as one of the national crushes by the online users. Now, Kannada fans will get to see the film that had this sequence in their language, as the makers are now releasing the film dubbed in Kannada shortly as Kirik Love Story . Priya was in Bengaluru recently for the audio launch of the Kannada version. She revealed that she hasn't received any offer from a Kannada film yet, she is aware of KGF and Yash when it comes to Sandalwood.
