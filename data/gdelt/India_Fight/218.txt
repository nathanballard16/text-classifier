Alia Bhatt trolled on social media for her airport look!

Bollywood stars are often spotted at the airport, and so, they make sure, they have a host of 'airport' looks they ready. While some of her fans were impressed by the outfit, there were many social media users who had a field day trolling Alia for her choice of outfit.
