Batla House Trailer: John Abraham To Unfold The Truth Behind 2008 Delhi Encounter In This Cop Drama

When it comes to making patriotic movies with a message, no one does it better than John Abraham. And now, John is back to tell another story.

The trailer of Batla House was unveiled today and after striking gold with back to back films like Romeo Akbar Walter, Satyameva Jayate and Parmanu, this upcoming film will attempt to unravel the truth behind the 2008 encounter with alleged terrorists that took place at Batla House in Jamia Nagar in Delhi.

Screengrab

Just to jog your memory a little, it was on September 13, 2008, a series of bomb blasts in Delhi claimed over 26 lives and injured over 133 people.

An operation was conducted on September 19 by the Special Cell of the Delhi Police, who raided a flat in Batla House. Two alleged terrorists, Atif Amin and Mohammad Sajid, were shot dead by the police.

Starring John Abraham as Sanjeev Kumar Yadav who spearheaded the encounter in Operation Batla House, the film has been directed by Nikhil Advani, who has worked on movies like D-Day, Kal Ho Naa Ho and Satyameva Jayate among others.
