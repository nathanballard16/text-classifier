Pakistan allows import of life-saving drugs from India

The Ministry of Commerce releases SRO after approval of federal government.

ISLAMABAD (Dunya News) – Government on Monday allowed import of life-saving medicines from India. The export of life-saving drugs to India was also allowed, Dinya News reported.

According to sources, the Ministry of Commerce has issued the SRO in this regard to amend Export Import Order, 2016. The Ministry of Commerce releases SRO after approval of federal government.

Last month, Pakistan suspended its trade relations with India, in retaliation against New Delhi s decision to revoke Article 370 that gave special status to Jammu and Kashmir.
