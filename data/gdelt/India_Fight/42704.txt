Delhi: History-Sheeter Shot Dead By 10-15 Assailants; Police Suspects Gang Rivalry

In a broad daylight shooting in New Delhi's Narela area, a person named Virendra Maan alias Kale was shot dead by a gang of unidentified assailants on Sunday, agencies reported. The deceased person had 13 criminal cases registered against him and had also earlier unsuccessfully contested the municipal polls from Bahujan Samaj Party in 2013. As per police officials, the man named Virendra was a known criminal of the area
