Territorial Army jawan visiting home on Eid holiday shot dead by terrorists in south Kashmir

SRINAGAR: Suspected terrorists shot dead a Territorial Army jawan , who was home on leave for Eid, in Anantnag district of south Kashmir on Thursday, officials said.Police said unidentified gunmen came to the residence of Manzoor Ahmad Beg in Sadoora village in Anantnag district Thursday evening and shot at him.An critically injured Beg was rushed to the district hospital where he was declared dead on arrival.Beg was posted at neighbouring Shopian district and was attached with 34 Battalion of the Rashtriya Rifles Eid was celebrated on June 5.
