Delhi man killed by lightning in Goa, wife critical

A man from Delhi was killed by lightning and his wife sustained severe injuries on Friday evening in Candolim beach in North Goa, police said.

Chaitanya Nagpal (35) died on the spot while his wife is critical in hospital, Inspector Nolasco Raposo of Calangute police station said.
