Samsung Galaxy M40 goes on open sale in India

Samsung launched the Galaxy M40 smartphone earlier this month in India and the smartphone went on sale starting June 19th in flash sales via Amazon.in. Today, Samsung has officially announced that the Galaxy M40 will now be available on open sale along with launch offers from Airtel, Vodafone Idea and Jio.

The smartphone will be available to purchase on open sale via Amazon.in and Samsung’s online store. Users will now be able to purchase the Galaxy M40 anytime from these online e-retailers.

To recall, the Galaxy M40 has a 6.3-inch Full HD+ Infinity-O LCD screen with 91.8% screen-to-body ratio, is powered by Octa-Core Snapdragon 675 with 6GB of RAM, runs Android Pie with Samsung One UI, features a 32-megapixel rear camera with f/1.7 aperture, secondary 5-megapixel camera for portrait shots and an 8-megapixel 123° ultra wide-angle lens. It also has a 16-megapixel front camera. It uses screen sound technology that uses the phone’s screen as an earpiece and the smartphone is backed up a 3500mAh battery with 15W fast charging.
