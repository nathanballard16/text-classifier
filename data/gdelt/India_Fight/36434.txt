Five of his family killed in road accident

Five persons of a family were killed on Sunday when their car crashed into a road divider in Pagaria area in Sehore district of Madhya Pradesh, police said.

The deceased persons were travelling to Bhopal from Indore when the incident occurred near Mukati warehouse under Astha police station area, around 42 kms from Sehore, said Additional Superintendent of Police Sameer Yadav.

They are identified only as Ankush, his wife Sharda, their daughters Isha and Priya, and his brother Anurag, he said, adding that other details like their complete names and ages are not known yet.

The exact cause behind the accident is investigated.
