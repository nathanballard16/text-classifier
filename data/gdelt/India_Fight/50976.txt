RJ Balaji – Nayanthara’s Mookuthi Amman to arrive for summer 2020!

After the massive success of LKG, RJ Balaji has already announced his next film which will be starring Nayanthara too alongside him. Titled Mookuthi Amman, the film has RJ Balaji doubling up as a writer and a director as well. Balaji announced the title with a simple yet attractive poster during the India vs Bangladesh game on Sunday.

Mookuthi Amman will be going on floors soon, with shoot to take place at Chennai and some other locations. The film is bankrolled by Vels Film International, who recently produced LKG and Comali.
