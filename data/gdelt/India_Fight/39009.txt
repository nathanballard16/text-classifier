Major fire breaks out at Gandhi Nagar market in Delhi; 21 fire tenders at spot

Fire at Gandhi Nagar market in Delhi | Photo Credit: ANI

New Delhi: A major fire broke out at Gandhi Nagar market in Delhi on Tuesday morning.

As many as 21 fire tenders have reached the spot and are making efforts to douse the flame.

No loss of life or injuries has been reported so far.

More details in this regard are awaited.

Gandhi Nagar market situated in Shahdara area of New Delhi is one of Asia's largest textile market. It is believed that the market has a footfall of around 10,000 to 20,000 people every day.
