AKP Phnom Penh, February 18, 2019 --

Cambodian Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs and International Cooperation H.E. Prak Sokhonn sent here this morning a condolence letter to H.E. Ms. Sushama Swaraj, Minister of External Affairs of India over a recent bomb attack in Pulwama, Jammu and Kashmir.

“I would like to express my deepest condolences to Your Excellency on the loss of many security officers in a suicide bomb attack which took place in Pulwama, Jammu and Kashmir on Feb. 14, 2019,” said H.E. Prak Sokhonn in his letter.

“Cambodia strongly condemns this terrorist attack and stands in solidary with India at this moment of profound grief. Cambodia is firmly committed to contributing to the fight against terrorism in all its forms and manifestations,” underlined the Cambodian foreign minister.
