Jammu and Kashmir: IED blast in Pulwama's Tral; civilian injured, road damaged

Follow us on Image Source : INDIA TV IED blast in Pulwama's Tral

One civilian was injured after an IED blast took place in Pulwama's Tral, Jammu and Kashmir. A road in the area was also damaged in the repercussion. According to reports, the blast occurred at nearly 3 am, early on Saturday in Amlar area, following which the area witnessed panic.

A team of police reached the spot soon after the report of the blast and investigation in the matter was underway.

IED was planted to target patrolling vehicles of the security forces patrol vehicles, sources said, adding it seems to have exploded prematurely.
