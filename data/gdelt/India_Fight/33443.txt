Fire in chemical godown at Ankleshwar

SURAT: A massive fire broke out in the warehouse of a chemical company in Ankleswar GIDC on Saturday morning. No casualty was reported.Sources said that the fire broke out in the chemical storage warehouse of Sajjan India limited. Short circuit is believed to be the primary reason for the fire.As many as 10 fire tenders were rushed to bring the fire under control. It took more than four hours for the fire tenders to gain control on the blaze.Sources said the manufacturing units located in the vicinity of the chemical company were asked to shut as precautionary measure. A fire officer said, “We received a call of fire at about 9 am and our tenders were rushed to the spot. The foam spray was used to gain control on fire. There was no casualty.”
