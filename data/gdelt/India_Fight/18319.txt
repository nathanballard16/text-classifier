Pakistan foreign minister Qureshi urges US to play role in resumption of Pakistan dialogue with India

Qureshi spoke over telephone with US Secretary of State Michael Pompeo, amid tensions with India following the Pulwama terror attack and the following aerial strike by Indian jets on a training camp of the Jaish-e-Mohammed in Balakot

Shah Mehmood Qureshi (Source: PTI)

Qureshi asked the United States to intervene and play its role in resumption of Indo-Pak dialogue

Qureshi spoke over telephone with US Secretary of State Michael Pompeo

He informed Pompeo about the de-escalatory measures taken by Pakistan including the handing over of the Indian pilot.
