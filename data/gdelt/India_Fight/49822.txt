Ludhiana: Youth Akali Dal remembers victims of 1984 anti-Sikh riots

LUDHIANA: The Youth Alkali Dal remembered victims who lost their lives in the 1984 anti-Sikh riots . Youth Akali Dal district president, Gurdeep Singh Gosha, said that on the directions of Rajiv Gandhi , a large number of Sikhs were killed in Delhi and other areas. The Sikh community will never forget the killings and Gandhi family who is responsible for it.Gosha said that even after 35 years of the killings, the culprits were not punished. In a little relief to the victims, Congress leader Sajjan Kumar was arrested but others were still roaming free. Gosha also expressed his solidarity with the families of the 1984 riot victims.
