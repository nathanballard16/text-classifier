Terrorists barge into Army jawan's home, shoot him dead

SRINAGAR: An Army jawan was on Saturday shot dead inside his home in the Sopore area of Baramulla district in Jammu and Kashmir by terrorists , police said.The terrorists barged into the residence of Mohammad Rafi Yatoo, an Army jawan, and fired at him indiscriminately, a police official said.He said Yatoo was rushed to a hospital but succumbed to injuries.
