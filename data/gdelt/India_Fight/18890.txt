545 liquor bottles seized in Krishna district of Andhra Pradesh

The police on Friday seized as many as 545 bottles of liquor worth Rs 50,000 from a house in Moguluru village in Andhra Pradesh’s Krishna district, said the police.

The police raided the residence of K Venkat Rao after Nandigama Deputy Superintendent of Police Subhash Chandra Bose gave orders.

A case has been filed under relevant sections and the accused has been taken into custody.
