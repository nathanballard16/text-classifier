Delhi police recovers fake currency worth 1.73 crore, five arrested

The Delhi Police on Thursday busted a fake currency racket and confiscated Rs 1.73 crore that included Indian as well as foreign currency. The Special Cell of the Delhi Police, based on specific inputs, raided the place and where fake currency was printed for some time. Along with the currency notes, a laptop and other equipment that was used by the gang to print fake money bills were also seized. Notes worth Rs 2000, 500, 100, 50 and US dollars were being printed. So far, the gang has printed about 6 crore worth of fake currency.
