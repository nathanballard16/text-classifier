Murder accused shot dead by police in Chennai – Chennaionline

A criminal, facing multiple cases of murder, was shot dead here on Tuesday when he attacked a police team trying to arrest him, police said.

According to police, Manikandan from Villupuram has eight murder cases against him.

The Villupuram police got a tip-off that Manikandan was hiding in Korattur here and a team came to arrest him.
