Jammu: 60-year-old man killed in attack by wild bear in Poonch

India

pti-PTI

Jammu, May 11: A 60-year-old man was killed in an attack by a bear in Jammu and Kashmir's Poonch district on Friday night, officials said.

Fayaz Ahmed Chounhan was attacked by the wild animal in Gali Kalaban village which came out of a nearby forest, they said. His body was recovered from the spot later, officials said.
