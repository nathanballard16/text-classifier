Pakistan can’t fight direct war against India: Rajnath Singh

AMN / NEW DELHI

Paying tributes to martyrs of Kargil War in Lok Sabha, Defence Minister Rajnath Singh says India’s bravehearts have defeated Pakistan thrice; Pakistan can never fight a direct war against India.

Defence Minister Rajnath Singh hailed the valour and courage of armed forces during wars with Pakistan.

Speaking in Lok Sabha, the Defence Minister said that due to lesson taught by armed forces, Pakistan will never think of going into full fledge war with India, rather it remains involved in proxy war.
