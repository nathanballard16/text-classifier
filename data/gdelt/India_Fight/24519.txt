14 rebels shot dead by Indian police

(MENAFN) A senior district official declared that over 14 gunmen rebels, known as Naxals, have died in India's southwestern state of Maharashtra.



Maharashtra Police in the Gadchiroli district's Bhamragarh area have launched a mission against the rebels at nearly 9:30 a.m. local time.



Shekhar Singh, district magistrate of Gadchiroli declared: "A few of the policemen who carried out the operation received injuries, but there are no deaths (of cops)".



He added, "The process of pulling out the dead bodies of the Naxals from the forested area is on, and the final number of the dead would be known once the search and combing operation is finished."
