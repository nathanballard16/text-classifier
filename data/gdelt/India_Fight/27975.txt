Beef served on hacked BJP Delhi website as Modi 2.0 takes oath

The website of Delhi wing of Bharataiya Janata Party has been hacked. The hackers replaced several pages of the website with recipes of Beef dishes, accompanied with the message: Hacked by 'Shadow_V1P3R'.

On the homepage, the navigation bar has been edited to replaced word BJP with the word BEEF. For example, About BJP has been edited to About BEEF, BJP History has been changed BEEF History. Rest of the contents of the homepage remain untouched.

The hacking attack took place while the Narendra Modi government is taking oath to office in Rasghtrapati Bhavan in New Delhi.
