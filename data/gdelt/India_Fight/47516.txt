Man arrested for killing infant son

New Delhi: A 27-year-old man was arrested for killing his infant son in Gazipur area. Deputy Commissioner of Police (East) Jasmeet Singh said that during the quarrel, he hit the child on his forehead. "The cause of death was injury due to the stick hit by child's father," said DCP East. During the investigation, the accused was arrested from West Bengal. He was hiding in a relative's house in West Bengal.
