Woman, son run over by truck in Kutch

RAJKOT: A 30-year-old woman and her four-year-old son were killed when their two-wheeler was rammed by a truck on Bhuj-Mandvi Road near Khatri Talav in Kutch district on Sunday. The deceased were identified as Jigna Pandya and her son Om. Pandya’s elder son, Rudra (8), who, too, was riding pillion “The victims were going to Bhuj from Mandvi when they met with the accident. The victims lived near Ashapura temple in Bhuj,” said an official from Mankuva police station.
