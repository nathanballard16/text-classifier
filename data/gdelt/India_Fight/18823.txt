Madhya Pradesh: Fire Breaks Out In Mhow Army Range, 2 Army Personnel Killed And 5 Injured

2 army men were killed after a fire broke out in the Mhow Army Range on Thursday, 5 personnel in the camp also attained injuries.

The Incident happened in the Bercha Range at Mhow, a place near Indore.

PRO Colonel Aman Anand said, “Two Army men were killed in the fire incident at the Army’s Mhow-based Bercha Range and five others were seriously injured.”

Officials said that the fire was caused due to a spark during the firing practice.
