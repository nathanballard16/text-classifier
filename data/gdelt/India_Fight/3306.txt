Armies of India, Pak exchange fire along LoC in Poonch

Srinagar

Indian and Pakistani armies on Saturday exchanged fire along the Line of Control in Mankote in Krishna Ghati sector of Poonch district in Jammu division.

Reports said that the exchange of fire started in Mankote area of the district at around 11:10 am in the morning. However, no casualty or damage was reported.

When last reports came in the exchange of firing was going on.
