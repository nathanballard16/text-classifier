Bike rams into railway level crossing, 2 killed

RAJKOT: Two persons riding a motorcycle were killed after they rammed into a bar at the railway level crossing near Kukma village of Bhuj taluka in Kutch district on Monday night.The deceased were identified as Deepak Charan (23) and Suresh Charan (25) both residents of Ganesh Nagar in Gandhidham.According to police, the duo were on their way to Mata No Madh, about 150km from Gandhidham. the accident took place when the rider lost control of the bike and rammed into the metal bar at the level crossing while trying to avoid collision with a vehicle coming from the opposite side.
