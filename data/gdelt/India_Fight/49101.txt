Mangaluru: Nursing student from Kerala ends life

Mangaluru, 26 Oct: In a case of suicide due to suspected love failure, a nursing student was found dead at her rented accommodation at Attavar on October 25, Friday.

The deceased is Anupama (23) of Kerala origin. Her body was found hanging from the ceiling fan of her flat at Sridevi Apartment. She was a student of a college near Kankanady.

It has been gathered that her boy friend who worked for a merchant navy at Chennai had turned down her proposal for marriage.

Sources said she had also left behind a death note.

Pandeshwar police visited the spot, scrutinized the area and undertook investigation.
