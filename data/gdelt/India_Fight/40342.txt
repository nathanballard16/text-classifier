Akshay Kumar and Rana Daggubati to get into qawwali face-off in THIS film

MUMBAI: The comedy film Housefull has left people in splits. The film became so popular that it has turned into a franchise. Now, its fourth instalment, Housefull 4, is one of the much awaited films.

Housefull 4 is also touted to be one of the biggest and the costliest films of the year. After watching and enjoying the earlier films, fans can’t wait for Housefull 4. The film deals with the theme of reincarnation and now, according to the latest reports, actors Akshay Kumar and Rana Daggubati have shot a qawwali song with the cast members. According to Mumbai Mirror’s source, the song sequence is a face-off between Akshay and Rana at the center along with the rest of the cast including Riteish Deshmukh, Bobby Deol, Kriti Sanon, Kriti Kharbanda, Pooja Hegde, Johny and Jamie Lever, Ranjeet and Chunkey Panday. The source told the daily, “It is at a crucial juncture and is on the lines of a fun conversation between the protagonist and antagonist. The song is set in the present era and was shot at a studio in Mumbai.”
