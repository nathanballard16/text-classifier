Man hit and killed by train in Kamptee

Nagpur: A married 38-year old man was hit and killed by a train on the track near Ramanagar in Kamptee. The incident came to light around 9.30 am on Wednesday. The deceased has been identified as Heeralal Magan Tayde, resident of Ramanagar. It could not be verified which train struck Heeralal.

On being informed about the incident, a team of New Kamptee police reached the spot and sent body of Heeralal to government hospital for autopsy.

Cops have registered a case of accidental death and investigating the matter further. The deceased is survived by wife, mother, brother and a host of relatives to mourn the loss.
