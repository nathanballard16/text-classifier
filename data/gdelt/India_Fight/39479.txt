Salman Khan wishes his fans on the occasion of Independence Day & Raksha Bandhan; View Post

The whole country is celebrating two special occasions today - Independence Day and Raksha Bandhan. Our B - Town celebs have also celebrated the special occasions and have wished their fans through social media. Right from Amitabh Bachchan to Ranveer Singh, Varun Dhawan, Alia Bhatt, Taapsee Pannu, Kartik Aaryan and others, everyone have wished their fans on the two special occasions and have also celebrated the same.

Salman Khan, who is currently in Jaipur for the shooting of his upcoming movie Dabangg 3, has recently wished all his fans through his social media handles. The Dabangg actor has shared a video from the sets of the movie in which he is seen sporting his Chulbul Pandey look with the iconic moustache. In the video, Salman wishes his fans on the special occasions and also hilariously says 'Happy Rains' as we can clearly see in the background that it is raining in Jaipur.
