Medical student dies in car crash

The speeding Ford EcoSport crashed into an electricity pole outside Daulat Ram College and turned turtle. Two women passengers, who allegedly fled after the accident, were also travelling with Sharma and his friend Abhinav, who was driving. Passersby pulled out the two youths inside and rushed them to a nearby hospital where the co-passenger, Sharma, was declared dead. New Delhi: A 21-year-old medical student died while his friend was injured when their speeding car turned turtle after hitting hit an electricity pole in Delhi University’s north campus early on Sunday.The deceased has been identified as Himanshu Sharma and police said a case has been registered.Police said the incident happened around 3.30am, when the two were returning after attending a fest at SRCC.
