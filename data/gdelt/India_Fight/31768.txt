Delhi: Head constable of RAC shoots himself dead outside Finance Ministry

New Delhi: In a shocking incident, a head constable of the Rajasthan Armed Constabulary (RAC) deployed outside the Union Finance Ministry allegedly shot himself dead with his service weapon on Tuesday afternoon. The deceased identified as Jai Narain (48) was posted on guard duty near gate number 2 of North Block, police said. "The incident took place at around 12.40 pm. In the CCTV footage, the victim can be seen shooting himself with his service carbine," said Deputy Commissioner of Police (New Delhi) Madhur Verma. He was rushed to a nearby hospital where he was declared brought dead, news agency PTI has reported. No suicide note has been found from the spot, the officer said.
