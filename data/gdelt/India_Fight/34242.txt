Knitwear worker found murdered

TIRUPUR: A 34 year-old knitwear worker was found murdered in his house near Veerapandi on Thursday. Deceased was identified as K Noorul Shah, hailing from Ramanathapuram district . The man was staying in a house in Palvanjipalayam, along with six other men, who were also natives of Ramanathapuram.On Thursday, his neighbours grew suspicious after a stench began to emanate from his house which had been locked from outside for the last few days.The neighbours along with the house owner broke-open the door only to find Shah lying dead. Police have confirmed that it as a case of murder as the deceased had suffered multiple injuries to the head. The police also found a hammer with blood stains from the spot.
