174 Indian soft shelled turtles rescued from Serampore

Kolkata: In a joint raid by Wildlife Crime Control Bureau and State Forest department, 174 Indian soft shelled turtles were rescued from Serampore late on Thursday morning. It was informed that they will deboard a train at Serampore railway station and will go to Kolkata by a car. On Thursday morning officials of WCCB and Wildlife Crime Control Unit (WCCU) of West Bengal Forest Directorate were waiting at Serampore railway station. The duo was detained and taken to the Government Railway Police (GRP) in Serampore railway station. The turtles were taken to wildlife rehabilitation centre in Salt Lake for rehabilitation and to be released in the wild.
