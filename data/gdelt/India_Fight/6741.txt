Ludhiana: 18-year-old alleges rape by neighbour, probe on

LUDHIANA: An 18-year-old student accused her neighbour of raping her for the past nine months.The girl has alleged that the 22-year-old accused also clicked her nude photos and recorded the video of the act to sexually exploit her. "The police have registered a case immediately after receiving the compliant. The police have launched a manhunt to nab him," investigating officer Arshdeep Kaur Grewal said. The police team has raided the house of the accused. However, he is absconding.
