63.3% polling for 59 seats in sixth phase of LS elections

The ruling Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP) had won 45 of these 59 seats in the 2014 Lok Sabha elections. It had won seven of the eight seats in Bihar, eight of 10 in Haryana, all four in Jharkhand, seven of eight in Madhya Pradesh and 12 of 14 in Uttar Pradesh. It had won all the seven seats in Delhi.
