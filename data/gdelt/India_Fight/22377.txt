A revolutionary verdict by a five-judge bench of Supreme Court decriminalised homosexuality in 2018, taking us one step closer to equality for all. While there are many ways you can promote equal rights, a trio of youngsters came up with a unique initiative, The Pink List. The online portal compiles a list of Lok Sabha candidates who support the LGBTQ community. Though the list has many politicians, we are highlighting 10 of them below. Let's have a look at the first one. Radha is contesting the election as an independent candidate from Chennai South, Tami Nadu. The politician who welcomed the Supreme Court's decision also said that there's still a lot more work to do.
