Police investigating death of man found shot in East Price Hill

The Cincinnati Police Department’s Homicide Unit is investigating the death of a man found shot in East Price Hill on Sunday night.

First responders discovered Taher Taher, 32, in the 3300 block of Bassett Road after being dispatched there about 8 p.m. on a report of a shooting, police said.

Police said Taher was pronounced dead at the scene.
