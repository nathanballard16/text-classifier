From Emergency hero to Defence Minister: 5 facts about George Fernandes

George Fernandes, a socialist to the core and one of the most popular defence ministers, passed away on Tuesday at the age of 88. Fernandes, who had been in public life for nearly six decades, had been suffering from Alzheimer's disease for a long time and was down with swine flu for the last few days as well.

The socialist leader was India's defence minister in the NDA government headed by Atal Bihari Vajpayee and he was the industry minister under the Jan Sangh government, which defeated Indira Gandhi in 1977.
