2-wheeler riders killed in accident

COIMBATORE: Two men including a migrant labourer , were killed after a tipper truck rammed the moped they were travelling on near Chithirai Chavadi on the outskirts of the city on Tuesday.One of the deceased was identified as M Veeramuthu, 28, a native of Pudukkottai. He was working in a tea stall at Madampatti for the last 15 days.“Veeramuthu, who was riding the moped, tried to enter the main road from a pocket road, but failed to notice the truck, which rammed into the motorcycle. The duo, who did not wear helmets, were thrown off from the two-wheeler. Veeramuthu suffered severe head injuries and died on the spot. The unidentified pillion rider was sent to the Coimbatore Medical College and Hospital where he died without responding to the treatment,” a police officer saidPolice have taken photographs of the migrant labourer and are planning to issue pamphlets to migrant labourers working in the nearby areas to trace his identity.Alandurai police have registered a case. Further investigation is on.
