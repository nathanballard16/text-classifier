Priyanka Chopra and Farhan Akhtar's The Sky Is Pink gets a release date - read on for details

Priyanka Chopra, Zaira Wasim and Farhan Akhtar | Photo Credit: Instagram

After a gap of three years, global icon Priyanka Chopra is all set to make her Bollywood comeback. The actress will be seen sharing the screen space with Farhan Akhtar in the upcoming biographical drama, The Sky Is Pink. The movie is being directed by Shonali Bose and also features Zaira Wasim and Rohit Saraf in vital roles. Recently, the release date of the film was announced.

Trade analyst Taran Adarsh took to Twitter and wrote, "Release date finalised... #TheSkyIsPink to release on 11 Oct 2019... Stars Priyanka Chopra, Farhan Akhtar and Zaira Wasim... Directed by Shonali Bose... Produced by RSVP and Roy Kapur Films."

The Sky Is Pink marks Priyanka and Shonali's first-ever collaboration. However, this is the second time that Farhan and PeeCee have teamed up for a film. The duo has previously worked together in Zoya Akhtar's Dil Dhadakne Do. They were also praised a lot by the audience for their sizzling chemistry in the film.

The upcoming release is based on the life story of motivational speaker Aisha Chaudhary, who was diagnosed with pulmonary fibrosis at the age of 13. It is being co-produced by the 36-year-old actress' Purple Pebble Pictures along with Siddharth Roy Kapur Roy Kapur Films and Ronnie Screwvala's RSVP Productions.

A report in Mumbai Mirror has stated that the cast of the movie will kickstart the last schedule in March and it will be shot in Mumbai. Following this, a wedding sequence will also be shot in the Andaman and Nicobar islands. It will hit the theatres on October 11.
