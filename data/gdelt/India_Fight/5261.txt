TMC MLA's murder: BJP demands CBI probe

Trinamool MP Abhishek Banerjee will visit Hanskhali on Monday.BJP state president Dilip Ghosh demanded a CBI probe in the MLA's murder, to stop slander against BJP. Only a CBI probe will help to nab the killers, as CID is biased under Trinamool regime, Ghosh said. So his role is also under probe.Trinamool secretary general Partha Chatterjee blamed BJP for the murder. Ghosh claimed the murder to be a result of inner party feud of Trinamool. KOLKATA: Nadia police has arrested two persons in connection with the murder of Krisnagunj MLA Satyajit Biswas, who was shot dead from a close range on Saturday at Hanskhali near his home.
