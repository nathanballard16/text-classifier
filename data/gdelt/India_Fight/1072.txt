Farmers to join for nation-wide strike called by central trade

In India, two day nationwide general strike by the trade unions started from today to protest the policies of Narendra Modi-led BJP government.

According to General Secretary of All India Trade Union Congress Amarjeet Kaur, the strike is quite visible in different states including Assam, Meghalaya, Karnataka, Manipur Bihar, Rajasthan and Goa.

The trade unions having complete support of all major independent federations of central employees, state employees and the employees of banks, insurance and telecom sectors are observing "rail roko and road roko" strike against the government.
