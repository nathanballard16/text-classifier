Sartaj Singh gets new look

However, he does plan to come to India for the promotions of Scared Games 2.

Saif Ali Khan, who won the audience’s hearts as Sartaj Singh in Sacred Games, is gearing up for Season 2.

The actor has become leaner for the role. A source close to the project reveals, “Saif had lost weight for Seaosn 2 of Sacred Games, and the character required him to look much leaner. There is much more action in the second season, and Saif, who usually follows a healthy lifestyle, lost a few kilos to get into the skin of the character.”

Meanwhile, Saif is currently in London shooting for his next film Jawaani Jaaneman. However, he does plan to come to India for the promotions of Scared Games 2.
