Fire breaks out at MSRTC garage in Pune

Pune: Fire broke out at a garage of Shivneri luxury buses here on Wednesday.

The garage of Shivneri luxury buses is owned by Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation (MSRTC).

“Two fire tenders along with two water tankers are present at the spot,” officials told ANI.

However, no casualties have been reported so far.

Further details in the matter are still awaited.
