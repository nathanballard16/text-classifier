India, Pakistan armies trade fire along LoC in Poonch

Srinagar

Armies of Indian and Pakistan on Friday exchanged shells along the Line of control (LoC) in Poonch district of Jammu and Kashmir.

Reports said that Pakistani troops today morning fired towards forward posts and villages along the Line of Control in Poonch. The mortar shelling and small arms firing from across the border started in Sawjiyan sectors around 8:00 in the morning.

However, there was no report of any casualty from either side of the border till now.

Meanwhile, news agency ANI tweeted; “Jammu and Kashmir: Pakistan violated ceasefire in Sawjian sector of Poonch district at about 0830 hours today by resorting to shelling with mortars & firing of small arms along LoC. Indian Army is retaliating.”
