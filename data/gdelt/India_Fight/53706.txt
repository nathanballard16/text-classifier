Body parts of man found stuffed in suitcase at Mumbai beach

MUMBAI: Chopped body parts of an unidentified person were found in a suitcase at Mahim beach early on Tuesday.The police cordoned off the area after receiving the information. The police found a chopped hand and a leg inside the suitcase.The police have sent the body parts to JJ Hospital for medical examination. A murder case against unidentified accused has been filed in this regard. Police are trying to ascertain the identity of the victim.
