IMFL worth Rs 54.67L seized in two raids; 7 held

Rajkot: A total of 16 ,144 bottles of Indian-Made Foreign Liquor (IMFL) worth Rs 54.67 lakh were seized in two raids carried out in Junagadh GIDC and Limbdi in Surendranagar district early on Friday morning. Seven bootleggers were arrested in this connection.In Junagadh GIDC, a total of 8,268 IMFL bottles worth Rs 33.25 lakh were seized from a truck and two bootleggers arrested. Police said the accused, identified as Manish Bishnoi (35) and Ramrakh Bishnoi (23), were ferrying the liquor cache in the truck from Jodhpur to deliver it to Junagadh. “We are investigating as to who had uploaded the liquor cache on to the truck and the liquor supply was meant to be delivered to whom,” said R K Gohel, sub-inspector with Junagadh local crime branch.In Limbdi, a total of 7,475 IMFL bottles worth Rs 22.42 lakh were seized from a truck and five bootleggers arrested. Police are yet to ascertain the identities of the accused.
