Narendra Modi’s anti-Muslim bill risks carnage in Assam

Assam, the state in northeast India famous for its tea plantations, erupted into protests last week, and violence continued yesterday in other states. The unrest has been triggered by a law bringing religion into citizenship that has backfired in the faces of Narendra Modi, the prime minister, and his Hindu nationalist Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP).

The BJP, fuelled by its own self-interest and lack of self-awareness, failed to anticipate the reaction to the Citizenship Amendment Bill (CAB) in northeast India.
