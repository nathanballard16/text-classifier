Pedestrian killed in collision

Article content

A pedestrian was killed after a collision with a transport truck in Delhi on Saturday morning.

The transport was travelling eastbound on Highway 3 when the driver attempted to turn southbound onto Mill Street at which point the pedestrian was fatally injured.

The collision occurred at about 6:30 a.m.

The pedestrian died at the scene. Police have not released the person’s name.

The truck driver, who was not injured, was co-operating with police, Norfolk OPP said in a media release.

Highway 3 was closed between Western Avenue and Church Street for several hours to allow for investigation.
