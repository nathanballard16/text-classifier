Thousands attend funeral of Baramulla youth

ISLAMABAD: Thousands of Kashmiris attended the funeral prayers of a youth on Friday who was killed by Indian forces during a violent cordon and search operation in the Baramulla district of occupied Kashmir.

The youth, Aamir Rasool Kaboo, was killed along with another Kashmiri by Indian troops during the operation in Kalantra area of Baramulla on Thursday night. His body was handed over to the family shortly before midnight.

Witnesses said thousands of people assembled at Kaboo’s home in Arampora to take part in his last rites. The mourners raised full-throated pro-freedom and anti-India slogans as they marched with his body to the graveyard. Meanwhile, complete shutdown was observed across the Sopore town and its adjacent areas against Kaboo’s killing.
