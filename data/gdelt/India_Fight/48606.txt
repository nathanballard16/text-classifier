Begumpet: Auto diver’s throat slit, head smashed with boulders

Hyderabad: Unknown assailants brutally murdered an auto-rickshaw driver in the broad daylight in Begumpet area today.

The deceased was identified as Mohd Wajid a resident of Rasoolpura locality in Begumpet, TT reports.

Cops suspect personal enmity was behind the gruesome murder. The assailants slit his throat and later smashed his head with boulders.

The Begumpet police, forensic experts of the CLUES team rushed to the crime scene and shifted the body mortuary.
