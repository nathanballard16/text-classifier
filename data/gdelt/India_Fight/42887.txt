Leopard attacks elderly woman in Khambha

RAJKOT: An 80-year-old woman was attacked by a leopard during wee hours of Monday in Jivapar village of Khambha taluka in Amreli According to foresters, the victim, Monghiben Parmar,was attacked from behind when she went out of the house to attend nature’s call. She suffered injury on her head. She was first taken to Rajula hospital and later shifted to Amreli civil hospital. \
