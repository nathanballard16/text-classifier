'Reliance Group got projects worth Rs 1 lakh crore during UPA'

NEW DELHI: Anil Ambani 's Reliance Group on Sunday slammed Congress over Rahul Gandhi 's continuing attacks by saying it had bagged projects worth Rs 1 lakh crore from UPA "We would like to remind Rahul Gandhi that it was during the 10-year Congress-led UPA regime between 2004 and 2014 that the Anil Ambani-led Reliance Group was awarded projects of over Rs 1 lakh crore across diverse, key nation-building infrastructure sectors by none other than Rahul Gandhi's own political party - Congress," said a spokesperson for the group.The Reliance Group asked Rahul to clarify if the UPA was supporting an alleged crony capitalist and dishonest businessman.The group in its statement said, "Rahul Gandhi, in his typical cavalier fashion, has continued his campaign of disinformation, distortion and malicious lies. Yesterday (in an interview to ET ), he singled out Anil D Ambani as allegedly a crony capitalist and dishonest businessman - all obviously patently untrue statements."
