Four booked for encroaching Parsi graveyard in Rajkot

RAJKOT: Four persons were booked on Tuesday night for allegedly encroaching and vandalizing a graveyard belonging to the Parsi community in Rajkot.Police said the accused, Ashiq Dalwani, Dhiraj Bawaji, Jagdish Bawaji and Shanipari Bawaji had allegedly encroached the graveyard at Bheelwas behind Eagle Petrol Pump in Rajkot on May 26 and vandalized the property.“The accused had not only vandalized the graveyard but also allegedly constructed eight cabins there. When the complainant, Kusman Tamboli, registered his protest, the accused allegedly threatened to kill him,” said an official from Pradyumannagar police station.
