AAP minister opens mohalla clinics, shown black flags by BJP

New Delhi, June 29Labour Minister Gopal Rai on Saturday inaugurated two mohalla clinics in his Babarpur constituency amid a protest by BJP workers over the "neglect" of the Assembly segment. According to the minister, BJP workers staged a protest against the inauguration of a mohalla clinic in North Ghonda. “The most disgusting kind of politics at display in Delhi... BJP leaders opposing the inauguration of a mohalla clinic for the poor. There is already a mohalla clinic which is not functioning properly,” he said. The AAP government has set a target of setting up 1,000 mohalla clinics across the national capital, a move aimed at providing primary healthcare facilities in the neighbourhood, the statement said.
