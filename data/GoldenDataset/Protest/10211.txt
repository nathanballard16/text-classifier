Parliament session live: Left MPs protest over GST; government to table key bills

As Parliament convened on Wednesday, members of Parliament from CPM and other Left parties staged a protest in front of Mahatma Gandhi statue, demanding withdrawal of Goods and Services Tax (GST) on job works. While the Lok Sabha is slated to take up bills like the New Delhi International Arbitration Centre Bill 2019 and the Dentists Amendment Bill 2019, the Rajya Sabha will discuss the Central Education Institutions (Reservation in Teachers’ Cadre) Bill 2019.
