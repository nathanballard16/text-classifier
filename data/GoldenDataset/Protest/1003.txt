Left fronts stage protest against Citizenship Bill in New Delhi

New Delhi, Jan 14 (ANI): Left fronts staged a protest against the Citizenship (Amendment) Bill, 2016 in New Delhi on Monday. Protestors termed the bill as undemocratic. All India Students Association (AISA), Communist party of India (Marxist-Leninist) Liberation (CPI-ML), All India Central Council of Trade Unions (AICCTU) took part in the protest.
