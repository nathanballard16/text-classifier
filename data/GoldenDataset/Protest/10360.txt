Protest over fuel prices in Odisha

BHUBANESWAR: The state wing of the Samajwadi Party on Saturday staged a protest against the union budget's imposition of cess and additional duty on petrol and diesel, which increased fuel prices across the country.BHUBANESWAR:Around hundred party members under the leadership of Samajwadi Party state president Rabi Behera organised a rally and reached out to governor Ganeshi Lal. They submitted a memorandum seeking intervention of the President.
