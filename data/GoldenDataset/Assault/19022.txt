2 minors abducted sexually assaulted in Kalighat 2 held

Kolkata: Two minor girls were allegedly abducted and sexually assaulted in Kalighat by a youth and two minor boys on the wee hours of Thursday. According to sources, on Thursday between 2am and 3am, two minor girls who reside on the footpath in front of Kalighat temple were abducted by the three accused persons, who were known to them. The trio took the girls to the bank of Adi Ganga near an ashram, where they allegedly assaulted the girls sexually and left them at the spot. Police sources informed that as the accused persons were known to the girls, they did not hesitate to go with them. Another accused minor boy is still absconding and a search is on to nab him.
