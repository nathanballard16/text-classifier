YS Vivekananda Reddy Murder Case: Rs 125 Cr Property Involved:- Ex-Chief Minister YS Rajasekhar Reddy's brother YS Vivekananda Reddy has been brutally killed in his residence during the early hours of March 15th and this incident created a sensation all over. Special Investigation Team (SIT) has been formed and the process revealed several unknown facts. They came to know shocking truths about the case. Parameshwar Reddy and Y Gangi Reddy have been taken into custody in relation with this case. SIT came to a confirmation that a Bengaluru based land settlement which is worth Rs 125 crores may be the reason for YS Vivekananda Reddy's murder.

Meanwhile, YS Jaganmohan Reddy filed a petition in AP High Court requesting a CBI probe in this case. He claimed that TDP is using YS Vivekananda Reddy's murder for political benefits. He said that he has no trust on SIT. The Special Investigation Team (SIT) nabbed couple of crucial suspects in the case which may be cracked soon. There are talks that some professional killers have been hired to assassinate YS Vivekananda Reddy. SIT came to the conclusion that the cops are not informed after the murder and his personal assistant along with others brought out his body from washroom to the bedroom and cleared all the blood marks.
