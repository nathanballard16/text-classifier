Pollachi sexual assault case: FIR registered against MK Stalin’s son-in-law

Chennai (Tamil Nadu) [India], Mar 14 (ANI): The Crime Branch of Chennai Police has registered an FIR against DMK president MK Stalin’s son-in-law Sabareesan.

Tamil Nadu Deputy Speaker Pollachi V Jayaraman had lodged a police complaint stating that rumours were being spread against him and his family on the instructions of Sabareesan on social media and some channels stating that his son is involved in Pollachi sexual assault case.

Jayaraman has demanded police to take necessary action against the Sabareesan. (ANI)
