Youth arrested on rape charge

The Elamakkara police on Monday arrested a person on the charge of rape.

The arrested was identified as Imran, 24, of Kolkata, who is now a resident of SRM Road.

He was arrested on a petition filed by a 24-year-old woman, also from Kolkata.

She had alleged in the petition sthat she was sexually abused by him, promising to marry her.

According to the police, the two had been in an affair for the last couple of years before the relationship turned sour.

The police have slapped the accused with IPC 376 (rape).

He will be produced in court on Tuesday.
