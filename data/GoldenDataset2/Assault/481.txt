Gujarat: 3-year-old girl sexually assaulted in Hazira

SURAT: Around 3-year-old girl was allegedly sexually assaulted by some unidentified person on Thursday in Hazira. She was brought to her home by around 19-year-old teenager who informed the family members that some other youth brought the girl to him. The family then contacted 108 ambulance service and shifted the girl to hospital.The girl went missing at around 3 pm while playing near home. The family realized that she is missing almost after an hour and they started searching for her. She suffered injury in private parts and was admitted to hospital for treatment.The minor was found around a kilometre away from her home in Hazira village .
