Lawyers protest against Delhi Police

Garhshankar, November 6Lawyers here, led by Bar Association president Pankaj Kripal, held a protest in the Court Complex on Wednesday accusing the Delhi Police of firing and beating of lawyers of Delhi. Pankaj said if lawyers took off their black coats, there would be none to bring justice to the public. Only the lawyers and the courts save the public from the rage of the police. He said to provide protection to lawyers, the Advocate Protection Act should be made and lawyers should be constitutionally declared as officers of courts. Sanjeev Kalia, secretary, Bar Association, Garhshankar; cashier Surinder Pal, Gurdeep Saini, Harpreet Singh, Sanjeev Banga and Pawan Kumar, were present.
