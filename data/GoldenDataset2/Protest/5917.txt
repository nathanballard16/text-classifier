Indo-Arab League to hold protest meeting on April 20 in Hyderabad

Hyderabad: Indo-Arab League will hold a protest meeting in Hyderabad to register its protest against the occupation of Golan Heights by Syria. The meeting will be attended by many Arab ambassadors and higher officials.

It may be mentioned that since its inception in 1967, Indo-Arab League has been playing a key role in establishing strong contacts between India and Arab countries. It also mobilized public opinion.

Chairman of Indo-Arab League was given a prestigious award in recognition of his services.

The Golden Jubilee celebrations of the League will be held at the end of this year.
