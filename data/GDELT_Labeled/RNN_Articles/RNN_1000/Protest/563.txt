MLC Ivan D’Souza begins hunger protest against merger of Vijaya Bank with BoB

Mangaluru, 08 Jan: MLC Ivan D’Souza began hunger protest against PM Modi-led union government for merging Gujarat-based Bank of Baroda (BoB) with local Vijaya Bank after garlanding the statue of Mahatma Gandhi, at the city park, here on Tuesday, January 8.

He will fast along with his well wishers in this regard from 10.00am to 5.00pm on Tuesday, January 8.
