Kolkata bound bus looted in Jharkhand s Dumka

Dumka (Jharkhand): Armed men beat up passengers of a Kolkata-bound bus coming from Bihar's Bhagalpur, looted over Rs 4 lakh in cash and mobile phones from them before fleeing the area in Jharkhand's Dumka district, police said on Wednesday. Dumka SP YS Ramesh told reporters that the incident took place near Massanjore in the district on Tuesday night. "Nine armed men boarded the bus in Dumka town. When the vehicle reached Bagnal More near Massanjore, they overpowered the driver and took control of it at around 11 pm," he said. Ramesh said the armed men beat up the passengers and looted Rs 4.2 lakh in cash and 13 mobiles from them.
