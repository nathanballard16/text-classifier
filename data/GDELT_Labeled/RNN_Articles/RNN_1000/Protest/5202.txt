Krypton Industries Ltd receives order of Rs. 23.10 crores

Krypton Industries Ltd have received an order of Rs. 23.10 crores, to be fulfilled in between March 2019 to March 2020 for Hospital Equipment (Wheelchairs) through our dealer against Tender from ALIMCO, a Government of India Undertaking.



Shares of KRYPTON INDUSTRIES LTD. was last trading in BSE at Rs.14.2 as compared to the previous close of Rs. 14.14. The total number of shares traded during the day was 890 in over 12 trades.



The stock hit an intraday high of Rs. 14.2 and intraday low of 14. The net turnover during the day was Rs. 12476.
