Fraudster sweeps Rs 2 lakh using cloned ATM cards in MIDC

Nagpur: As many as seven people have ended up losing a total of over Rs 2 lakh from their respective bank accounts, using cloned ATM cards from March 1 to March 8. MIDC police have registered case of fraud under section 420 of the Indian Penal Code and started the probe The incidents have baffled MIDC police as the concerned ATM booth from where the frauds took place falls under their jurisdiction. The police have registered a case of fraud against unidentified miscreants of looting group of people to the tune of lakhs in just 8 days. According to the complainants, the fraudsters made several transactions from their account in quick succession.
