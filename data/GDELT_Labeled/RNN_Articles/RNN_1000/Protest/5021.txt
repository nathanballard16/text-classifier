Employees of A.P Financial Corporation protest, demand for bifurcation

Hyderabad: The employees of A.P Financial Corporation registered their protest for not bifurcating the organization even after five years of separation of T.S.

The employees appealed to the Governor, Mr ESL Narasimhan to intervene to safeguard their interests. They also shouted slogans against the autocratic attitude of the MD and raised the slogans “Andhra Down Down.”
