CAA: Bhim Army leader Chandrashekhar detained, 10 more held

New Delhi, Dec 21 : Bhim Army chief Chandrashekhar Azad, who was protesting at the Jama Masjid against the Citizenship Amendment Act (CAA) and National Register of Citizens, was detained Friday late night, a police official said.

Ten more people were also arrested in Daryaganj area in violence related incidents, he added.
