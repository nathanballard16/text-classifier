Reconstruction of Guru Ravidas Temple sought

Our CorrespondentPhagwara, September 8Hundreds of Dalit activists burnt the effigy of Prime Minister Narendra Modi here on Saturday in protest against the demolition of Guru Ravidas Temple in Delhi last month. On the concluding day of their six-day relay fast-cum-dharna organised at the Tehsil Complex, the protesters demanded the reconstruction of the Ravidas Temple in the Tughlqabad area in Delhi. The dharna was held under the banner of the Bahujan Samaj Front, Punjab. The protesters raised slogans against the BJP-led NDA government, the RSS and Modi. The call for the six-day protest was given by the Guru Ravidas Sadhu Sampradai Society.
