Kerala Congress MPs stage protest at Parliament demanding farm loan waivers

Members of Parliament from the Kerala Congress on Tuesday staged a protest at the Parliament House in New Delhi.

The MPs were holding a protest in front Mahatma Gandhi's statue at the Parliament, over various issues concerning farmers such as farmers committing suicide, demanding loan waivers for them.



Both houses, the Lok Sabha and the Rajya Sabha, were to discuss the motion of thanks to the President's address at the joint session of the Parliament.

The Rajya Sabha began its proceedings with an obituary reference to Rajasthan BJP state president and sitting Rajya Sabha MP Madan Lal Saini.
