500 protesters detained in Chennai during stir against Citizenship Act

By Express News ServiceCHENNAI: Around 500 protesters were detained in the city for protesting against the Citizenship Amendment Act on Saturday. This included 200 protesters who gathered near the Raj Bhavan in Guindy. The protesters were mostly from student bodies such as the All India Students Federation and Students Federation of India, besides some members of the public. In a similar incident near Beach Station in North Chennai, around 100 protesters were detained by the police around 1 pm. The AISF and SFI also conducted protests near Central Railway station at 10 am and around 200 people were later detained by the police.
