City students come out in support of protest at JNU

Tribune News ServiceAmritsar, November 23Student bodies in the city have come out in support of students of the Jawaharlal Nehru University, New Delhi, who are protesting against the fee hike by the Central Government. They said any kind of fee hike should not be implemented. If the fee hike is implemented, students from the Below Poverty Line will not be able to better their life and society through their education. Ravinder Kaur, member of Punjab Students’ Union (Lalkaar), said: “This seems to be an attempt tat privatising educational institutions. Our union supports the protest by JNU students.”Some of the GNDU research scholars have also written a letter in support to students to the JNU authorities.
