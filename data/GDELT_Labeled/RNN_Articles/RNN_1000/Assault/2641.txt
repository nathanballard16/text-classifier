Impact of Pulwama attack: Miscreants assault Muslim youth in Hyderabad

In one such incident, a Muslim youth was assaulted by miscreants on the suspicion that he raised pro-Pakistan slogans. Following the heated arguments, a group of five miscreants attacked him. Mr. Jaffar told the media persons that he is an Indian and did not raise any pro-Pakistan slogans. In his complaint, he mentioned that miscreants attacked him seeing his namaz cap. The miscreants alleged that the youth had raised the slogan, ‘Pakistan Zindabad’.
