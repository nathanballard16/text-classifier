Rape accused tries to commit suicide, arrested

Rajkot: A transporter, Vanrajsinh Parmar, accused in abduction and rape of a 27-year-old woman, attempted suicide at his office in the Surendranagar on Monday. He was admitted to a private hospital for treatment where the police has kept him under detention. They will formally arrest him after discharge from hospital.The accused and his uncle Dharmendrasinh Parmar are accused of kidnapping and raping Vanrajsinh’s ex-girlfriend in Rajkot. Based on the survivor’s complaint lodged on Saturday last, the University police arrested Dharmendrasinh.Vanrajsinh had consumed poisonous substance at his office in Surendranagar late evening on Monday and shifted to a private hospital. His condition is stable. As soon as Rajkot police got information about Vanrajsinh, they asked their Surendranagar counterparts to detain him at the hospital.According to police, the woman had an affair with Vanrajsinh. But when she came to know that Vanrajsinh had concealed information that he was married, she broke up with him. But after break-up, Vanrajsinh threatened to kill the woman and her family if she did not marry him. The woman’s family came to know about the criminal history of Vanrajsinh’s uncle Dharmendrasinh and they initiated efforts to settle the issue.When the woman’s family came to meet Dharmendrasinh on April 2, Vanrajsinh kidnapped the survivor in his car and confined her at various places for two days.On the third day, Dharmendrasinh took the survivor to a stable where he allegedly raped her. The survivor also sustained severe injuries on her chest, said police.The woman managed to escape from confinement and lodged a complaint on Friday last, based on which police arrested Dharmendrasinh (42) on Saturday.
