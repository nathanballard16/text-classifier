Rashtrapati Bhavan staff rapes woman inside staff quarters

An MSc student in Delhi has been allegedly raped by a staff member in the Rashtrapati Bhavan. Police have registered an FIR against the accused.

The accused, Nishant Yadav, had allegedly raped the girl on the pretext of marriage. He called the university student to the staff quarters of the Rashtrapati Bhavan and raped her on Tuesday night.

The police have lodged an FIR against Nishant Yadav, who is absconding.
