‘Kerala govt failed entirely, it’s daylight rape on Hindu people’: Union Minister Hegde on Sabarimala row

New Delhi, Jan 02 (ANI): Union Minister of State for Skill Development & Entrepreneurship Anantkumar Hegde sparked controversy yet again while commenting on Sabarimala row. “Kerala govt failed entirely. It’s daylight rape on Hindu people”, said Hegde while talking to ANI. His comments came after two women below the age of 50 claimed that they entered Sabarimala Temple premises today.
