Factory makes tiles with anti-Pak slogan

Rajkot: A ceramic tiles maker from Morbi town has started manufacturing tiles with design of a Pakistan national flag and slogan ‘Pakistan Murdabad’ as a mark of protest against the Pulwama attack. The ceramic unit named Saxena Ceramic on Morbi national highway has made 25 boxes of tiles on which ‘Pakistan Murdabad’ has been written below the Pakistan national flag. The unit owners said that they will fit this tiles in the toilets of the people at their own cost.
