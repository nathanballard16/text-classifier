K’taka: People protest against rising crimes against women

A+ A-Bengaluru: Local residents here on staged a protest against the increasing number of crimes against women in the country. The protestors titled the protest as ‘#NirbhayaDishaMe’, as a reference to the rape and murder of the two victims in Delhi and Telangana respectively. Recently a rape victim succumbed to her injuries in New Delhi after she was set ablaze by four men including the rape accused in Unnao, Uttar Pradesh. As per the National Crime Records Bureau (NCRB) report for 2017, 3.5 lakh cases of crime against women were reported in the country. Majority of cases under crimes against women were registered under ‘Cruelty by Husband or his Relatives’ (27.9 pc) followed by ‘Assault on Women with Intent to Outrage her Modesty’ (21.7 pc), ‘Kidnapping and Abduction of Women’ (20.5 pc) and ‘Rape’ (7 pc).
