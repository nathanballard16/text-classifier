Greater Noida: Class 12 students assault minor after he refuses to 'salute' them

However, the police officials have lodged a complaint against the accused under the sections of the Indian Penal Code. The further investigation in the case is underway. | Photo Credit: Thinkstock

Lucknow: In an appalling case reported from Greater Noida, Uttar Pradesh, a Class 10 student of a private school was allegedly thrashed by his seniors on Friday after he declined to 'salute' them. The police officials have lodged a non-cognisable report in the matter.

According to a report in Hindustan Times, the incident happened on Friday around 12 pm, outside the school. The victim went to a nearby stationery shop, where he was stopped by his four seniors. The senior students then assaulted him using sticks and blunt objects.

The victim told the HT that these were same students whom he declined to 'salute' in the morning. Meanwhile, the victim's elder brother told the national daily that the Class 12 boys were standing at the gate while his brother was entering the school.

They stopped him and asked why he did not salute them, as they were seniors. Later, they threatened him of dire consequences.

In the afternoon, they got an opportunity and assaulted him. The victim has received injuries on his head due to the attack, added victim's brother. The victim's family approached the police after their son returned with bruises.

However, the police officials have lodged a complaint against the accused under the sections of the Indian Penal Code. The further investigation in the case is underway.
