Uttar Pradesh: Minor gang-raped, filmed by stepfather, uncle: accused absconding

The victim's mother got married to accused-Waseem after her first husband passed away, in October last year. | Photo Credit: Thinkstock

Lucknow: In a bizarre case reported from Muzzafarnagar, Uttar Pradesh, a 16-year-old girl was allegedly raped by her stepfather and his cousin in Shamli district. The minor's ordeal did not end here, the accused men also recorded a video of their act.

According to news agency PTI, the Circle Officer Rajesh Kumar Tiwari mentioned that the incident took place on Saturday and the police had registered a case. The accused were identified as Waseem, the victim's stepfather, and his cousin Tanvir.

Reportedly, both accused have been absconding after committing the crime. The case was lodged under various sections of the Indian Penal Code (IPC) and the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences (POCSO) Act.

The PTI report stated that the complaint was lodged by the victim's mother. She mentioned that the accused also recorded a video of their act and threatened to make it viral if the girl narrated her ordeal to anyone. The victim's mother got married to accused-Waseem after her first husband passed away, in October last year.

However, the police officials have launched a search operation in order to nab the accused men. In a similar case reported from Shamli district, a 16-year-old boy, who worked as a truck helper was allegedly sexually assaulted by the driver and beaten up when he tried to put up a resistance. PTI reported that the Circle Officer Ashok Kumar said that the incident took place on Saturday and the police had arrested the truck driver.
