World urged to break silence on ethnic cleansing of Muslims in IOK

Pakistan's Ambassador to Turkey Syrus Sajjad Qazi has urged the international community to break silence on the ongoing ethnic cleansing of Muslims in the Indian Occupied Kashmir.

He was speaking at an event organized by Pakistan's Embassy in Ankara in connection with the Black Day' commemorations and to protest against Indian recent illegal move to change special status the Occupied Kashmir.

The Ambassador also highlighted the sufferings of Kashmiri people after India locked down Kashmir in the wake of its recent illegal decisions.

The event concluded with the lighting of candles by all the participants, commemorating the Kashmiri martyrs and pledging strong solidarity with the people of Indian Occupied Kashmir.
